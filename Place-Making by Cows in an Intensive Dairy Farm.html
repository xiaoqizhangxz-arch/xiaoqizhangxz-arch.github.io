Place-Making by Cows in an Intensive Dairy Farm: A Sociolinguistic Approach to Nonhuman Animal Agency

Leonie Cornips and Louis van den Hengel

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，集约化养殖场中的奶牛，尽管其物理自由受到极端限制，但它们依然通过“场所营造”（place-making）的过程展现出一种复杂的语言能动性（linguistic agency）。作者们挑战了将语言视为人类独有认知能力的传统人类中心主义观点，转而提出一种后人类主义的社会语言学框架。在该框架下，语言被重新定义为一种分布式的、具身的（embodied）、由人与非人行动者、物质与环境共同构成的“物质-符号集合体”（material-semiotic assemblage）中涌现的社会实践。因此，奶牛与围栏、食槽等物质环境的互动，本身就是一种创造意义、协商权力关系、构建归属感的语言行为。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要来源于对荷兰南部一个集约化奶牛场的民族志田野调查。关键证据包括：第一，对现有文献的梳理，证实奶牛具备复杂的认知、情感与社会能力，这为它们拥有能动性提供了科学基础。第二，田野观察中的具体案例，如一位农场主关于母牛“Mientje”学会用舌头打开门闩的叙述，这直接展示了非人动物解决问题与意向性行为的能力。第三，也是最核心的证据，是对“牛犊舍”（jongveestal）中牛犊和青年母牛行为的细致观察与音视频记录。作者特别分析了在喂食期间，牛犊们通过铁栏同步伸头、咀嚼的集体行为。这种与物质环境（铁栏）共同产生的有节奏的“身体同步”（bodily synchronizing）及其发出的碰撞声，被作者诠释为一种独特的“语言”。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程分为三个步骤。首先，通过追溯亚里士多德、笛卡尔、海德格尔乃至乔姆斯基的理论，系统地解构了西方哲学与语言学中将语言（logos）与纯粹的声音（phonè）对立、从而确立“人类例外论”的思想根源。其次，构建了一个新的理论综合框架，融合了社会语言学（强调语言的社会实践性）、批判后人类主义（特别是Alastair Pennycook的“后人类主义应用语言学”）以及新唯物主义（特别是Jane Bennett的“能动集合体”理论），从而将语言的发生场所从人类大脑“重新定位”到更广阔的物质-社会网络中。最后，作者将此理论框架应用于民族志田野数据，将牛犊在围栏前的同步进食行为从简单的动物行为，重新诠释为一种复杂的语言实践——一种“双语能力”（bilingualism）。在这种“双语”中，一种语言是牛犊自身的发声和身体语言，另一种则是由“铁栏与身体”共同创造、依赖于特定场景的物质-符号语言。这一诠释最终支撑了其核心论点：即使在极端约束下，非人动物的能动性也能通过与物质世界的创造性互动而涌现。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用质性的民族志研究方法（ethnographic fieldwork）。研究方法包括在荷兰一个集约化奶牛场进行的为期数周的沉浸式观察、系统的音视频记录以及与农场主的访谈。研究的独特性在于，它并非旨在量化动物行为，而是通过后人类主义和新唯物主义的理论视角，对观察到的现象（特别是人-动物-物质的互动）进行深度的理论化诠释（theoretical interpretation）。这种方法使得作者能够将看似平常的动物行为（如进食）分析为具有深刻社会语言学意义的“场所营造”实践。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

核心案例是位于荷兰南部的“农场1”（Farm 1），一个拥有约150头奶牛的集约化养殖场。研究焦点集中在名为“jongveestal”（牛犊舍）的建筑内，其中36头不同年龄的牛犊和青年母牛被铁栏分隔在四个区域。本文最具说服力的分析，正是围绕这些被限制在牛犊舍中的牛犊展开的。通过对它们在喂食时与铁制饲喂围栏（iron feed fence）互动的详细描述，作者展示了它们的身体如何与围栏共同作用，通过同步的运动和发出的有节奏的声响，创造出一种共享的社会性（sociality）和意义，从而将一个纯粹的物理空间转变为一个有意义的“场所”。

11.1 Introduction

    If, as Eva Meijer asserts (2016, 73), in this tradition “humans are viewed as radically different from other animals,” then language is commonly seen as “one of the main ways in which this difference is expressed.” (Cull 2015, 19)
  • 文章引用阿甘本的“人类学机器”概念，批判了将人类定义为“言说存在”而将动物排除在外的二元对立，这种机制通过物种等级划分来不断地创造人类自身。

    Specifically, we will explore how dairy cows, as caged living beings or what sociologist Rhoda Wilkie (2010, 115) has called “sentient commodities,” negotiate their environment as a material-semiotic resource in the production of a meaningful world.

  • 最后，作者引用哈拉维的观点，强调研究非人动物的意义在于与它们“共存”，而不仅仅是将它们作为思考的工具。

11.2 Language and the Politics of Human Exceptionalism

本章系统性地批判了西方哲学和语言学两大领域中将语言作为人类特权标志的传统。作者首先追溯了从亚里士多德将人类的理性言说（logos）与动物的声音（phonè）对立的观点，到笛卡尔视动物为无思想的机器，再到海德格尔认为动物“贫于世界”（weltarm）的哲学谱系。这些思想家共同构建了一个排斥非人动物的政治与语言领域。接着，文章将批判矛头指向当代语言学，分析了乔姆斯基的生成语法如何将语言定义为抽象的、非社会的“内在心智工具”，从而延续了笛卡尔式的身心二元论和人类中心主义。最后，作者指出，尽管主流社会语言学仍以人类为中心，但其强调语言的具身性、多模态和情境性，为发展一种包容非人行动者的、更具前景的语言学研究路径提供了可能。

  • 作者分析了亚里士多德在《政治学》中通过区分logos（理性言说）和phonè（声音），将政治生活与语言能力严格绑定，从而将动物排除在政治共同体之外。
    Aristotle, as Derrida explains in The Beast and the Sovereign (2009, 343–349), thus makes a categorical distinction between human and nonhuman animals to posit an inextricable link between language and the political sphere.
  • 文章指出，后世许多哲学家继承了亚里士多德的观点，否定动物的语言和认知能力。
    Many later philosophers have followed Aristotle in rejecting the linguistic and cognitive abilities of nonhuman animals (Meijer 2016, 75–76).
  • 作者讨论了笛卡尔的观点，即动物因为不能言说所以没有思想，只是物理机器。
    ...Descartes maintained that other animals “cannot speak as we do: that is, they cannot show that they are thinking what they are saying” (Descartes 1985, 140).
  • 海德格尔的观点被引用，他认为动物虽非“无世界”（如石头），却是“贫于世界”，因为它们被本能所困，无法像人类一样通过语言来“形成世界”（world-formation）。
    ...an animal “behaves within an environment but never within a world.” (Heidegger 1995, 239)
  • 海德格尔将人类语言视为揭示世界意义的唯一途径，从而强化了人与动物的区分。
    “Language alone,” he writes, “brings what is, as something that is, into the Open for the first time.” (Heidegger 1971, 73)
  • 文章批判乔姆斯基的生成语法理论将语言视为人类独有的心智构造，并认为其主要功能是服务于思维而非外部交流。
    The generative framework, advanced since the late 1950 s by Noam Chomsky and others, theorizes language as a human mental construct... (Chomsky 2002, 2006)
  • 乔姆斯基将语言视为一种独立于发声器官的“内在心智工具”，这延续了人类中心主义。
    This view consolidates the older assumption that the primary function of language is for human thought rather than for external communication. (Berwick and Chomsky 2016, 164)
  • 乔姆斯基明确将语言能力视为人类与其他动物之间的“鸿沟”，是“人类本质”的体现。
    ...language constitutes “a yawning chasm between what we [humans] can do and what other animals cannot” (Berwick and Chomsky 2016, 110)... “the ‘human essence’, the distinctive qualities of mind that are, so far as we know, unique to man (sic)” (Chomsky 2006, 88).
  • 尽管乔姆斯基承认动物与人类在言语能力上存在连续性，但他仍坚持人类语言在“递归”或“创造性”方面是独一无二的。
    ...Chomsky has acknowledged that “available data suggest a much stronger continuity between animals and humans with respect to speech than previously believed” (Hauser et al. 2002, 1574), he nonetheless maintains his faith in a uniquely human property of language... (Chomsky 2006, 6)
  • 哈拉维指出乔姆斯基将语言独特性视为一个可检验的假说，而非纯粹的哲学预设，但作者认为该框架仍阻碍了对非人动物语言能力的研究。
    And while Chomsky, as Donna Haraway notes, has been cautious enough to present the idea of linguistic uniqueness as “a testable hypothesis, not an assumption rooted in premises of human exceptionalism” (Haraway 2008, 373 note 44), there is no doubt that the tradition of anthropocentric thought... has been a serious obstacle...
  • 与生成语法相反，社会语言学将语言视为认知与社会文化相互关联的社会建构。
    In contrast to Chomsky’s non-social view of language, sociolinguistic research has built on the pioneering work of William Labov and others to theorize language as both dependent on cognition and interconnected with the workings of society and culture. (Labov 1994, 2001)
  • 作者认为社会语言学中将语言视为具身、多模态和多感官现象的趋势，为非人语言研究提供了潜力。
    This view converges with recent approaches to language as an embodied (Bucholtz and Hall 2016), multimodal (Müller et al. 2013) and multisensory (Pennycook and Otsuji 2015) phenomenon...

    ...bodies, in turn, are themselves part of the semiotic landscape as they are “imbricated in complex arrangements that include nonhuman as well as human participants, whether animals, epidemics, objects, or technologies” (Bucholtz and Hall 2016, 186).
  • 作者认为，将语言概念扩展到包括多模态实践，有助于将非人动物的感官能力（如嗅觉）视为其语法的一部分，而不是将其视为缺陷。
    It thus provides a useful framework to analyze differences between and among human and nonhuman animals in terms of grammatical possibilities and expressions instead of simply ascribing deficiencies to the latter (Kulick 2017, 373).

    For example, if a cow in an indoor dairy farm steps back and withdraws her face through the iron bars when humans approach her, this bodily movement...may be analyzed equally to how human animals phrase negation as in the sentence do NOT approach me (Cornips and van Koppen 2019).

11.3 Cows as Social and Linguistic Beings

本章旨在为奶牛的语言主体性建立基础，首先通过梳理科学文献，证明奶牛是具有鲜明个性、丰富情感和高度社会复杂性的智慧生物。接着，作者批判了以“牲畜”为框架的研究视角，指出这种视角服务于集约化养殖，并严重限制了奶牛展现其天性的可能，从而呼吁以奶牛自身的视角来理解它们。随后，通过一个农场主讲述的奶牛“Mientje”开锁的故事，生动地展示了奶牛在受限环境中的能动性与反抗。最后，本章核心性地引入并阐释了“场所营造”（place-making）这一社会语言学概念，并结合Alastair Pennycook的后人类主义语言学思想，将其重新定义为一种分布式的、由人、动物、物体和空间共同参与的物质-符号实践。这为后续分析奶牛如何通过与环境的互动来“言说”奠定了理论基础。

  • 作者指出，挑战人类中心主义的语言观，能帮助我们更好地理解动物和它们的语言。
    ...challenging an anthropocentric view of language can help us see animals of other species, and their languages, differently” (Meijer 2016, 74).
  • 研究表明，人类语言与非人交流系统之间可能没有绝对的界限，这动摇了人类例外论的基础。
    ...there may not be a “sharp divide between human language and nonhuman communicative systems” (Evans 2014, 258; see also Meijer 2019). This does not mean that human and nonhuman forms of communication are the same, but, as Alastair Pennycook (2018, 82) notes, “it is an argument against human exceptionalism.”
  • 作者将语言重新定义为一种社会、空间和人造物的资源，并引用Pennycook的理论。
    By theorizing language in terms of material-semiotic assemblages and spatial repertoires (Pennycook 2017), we wish to avoid an anthropocentric definition of language...
  • 科学证据表明，奶牛拥有复杂的认知、情感和社会能力，包括社会学习。
    Measured assessments of cows’ cognitive, emotional and social abilities provide scientific support for what people familiar with cows already know... (Marino and Allen 2017; Colvin et al. 2017).
  • 奶牛能形成强大的社会联结，特别是母牛与牛犊之间。
    ...mother cows and calves sharing an especially powerful emotional connection... (Marino and Allen 2017, 484).
  • 奶牛具备形成和分类心智概念的能力，而不仅是对外部刺激做出反应。
    These abilities show that cows do not merely respond to external stimuli but engage in the formation and categorization of mental concepts (Colvin et al. 2017, 7).
  • 奶牛表现出的情绪反应，如对学习成功的兴奋感，被认为反映了自我意识和共情等高级心理能力。
    ...cows display emotional reactions to their own learning and in response to each other’s feelings, which has been suggested to reflect sophisticated levels of psychological capacities such as self-awareness and empathy (Hagen and Broom 2004; Marino and Allen 2017, 482–483).
  • 集约化养殖对奶牛造成的痛苦被详细描述，包括其行为受限和遭受的痛苦程序。
    As caged living beings...cows raised for food in factory farms experience “unnatural conditions from birth to slaughter” (Marino and Allen 2017, 474)...
  • 在商业化养殖环境中，奶牛形成社会联结极为困难，严重影响其福祉。
    ...social bonding formation is extremely difficult to establish and maintain (McLennan 2013), which has devastating consequences for their well-being and welfare.
  • 作者批判了主流奶牛研究的应用科学视角，认为其主要服务于人类的养殖实践。
    As the scientific literature on cow psychology and behavior is dominated by an applied science perspective...there is a felt need to understand, and relate to, cows on their own terms. (Marino and Allen 2017, 475)
  • 作者引用了Despret的观点，认为奶牛被困于工业化生产的“装置”中。

    ...what is typically labelled as a language is reconceived as a linguistic resource that only becomes socially meaningful in combination with other material-semiotic resources distributed across people, places and environments (Pennycook 2017).
  • 作者引用Cowley和Pennycook，将语言理解为由人与非人行动者互动产生的新兴属性。

    This shift in thinking corresponds to the critical posthumanist “turn” that has been put on the linguistic research agenda recently by Alastair Pennycook, who urges us “not just to broaden an understanding of communication but to relocate where social semiosis occurs” (Pennycook 2016, 446).
  • 作者再次引用Pennycook，强调语言资源是空间分布的，而非仅仅存在于头脑中。
    Once we acknowledge that, as Pennycook (2018, 51) notes, “linguistic and other semiotic resources are not contained in someone’s head...but are spatially distributed,” we can begin to explore how dairy cows, such as Mientje, engage in linguistic place-making...

11.4 Linguistic Place-Making in an Intensive Dairy Farm

本章是文章的核心实证部分，详细阐述了奶牛如何在集约化养殖场中进行语言性的“场所营造”。作者首先将奶牛的场所营造视为一种“知识疆域”的构建，并通过梳理文献指出奶牛通过声音、视觉、嗅觉、触觉乃至游戏等多种模态与世界互动。接着，文章详细描述了田野调查地点——“农场1”及其“牛犊舍”（jongveestal）的物理布局和管理方式，强调了其对奶牛社会结构和行为的极端限制。在这一背景下，作者通过图片和观察记录，展示了牛犊们如何通过“身体同步”（bodily synchronizing）这一行为（尤其是在喂食时）来创造社会性。最终，作者提出了创见性的论点：牛犊们在进食时与铁栏的同步互动，以及由此产生的有节奏的声响，构成了一种独特的、非声音的“第二语言”。这种由“身体与铁栏”共同产生的语言，与它们自身的发声构成了“双语”（bilingualism）实践，这正是它们在被监禁环境中进行场所营造、构建意义和能动性的关键方式。

  • 作者将场所营造视为一个建立有意义联结的过程，对社会生活至关重要。
    Just as for people, we assume that the formation of meaningful bonds between cows and a place is “a powerful factor in social life… and is often based on the social relationships that are enacted in a place” (Schieffelin 2018, 35).
  • 场所营造的过程对权力动态和不对称性是敏感的。
    ...“processes of place-making and place itself are always sensible to power dynamics and asymmetries” (Schieffelin 2018, 34)...
  • 牛将一个地方转变为“知识的疆域”。
    First, a cow becomes connected to her place as a “territory of knowledge” (Schieffelin 2018, 30, citing Århem 1998) through her verbal practices.
  • 研究表明，牛犊的叫声可以编码年龄等信息，并用于与母亲交流。
    One study showed that calf calls encode age, but not sex... when separated from their mothers and preceded suckling (Padilla de la Torre et al. 2015, 58).
  • 圈养奶牛的叫声可以表达饥饿、性兴奋或挤奶延迟等特定状态。
    ...indoor housed cows signal verbally that they are hungry, sexually aroused, and experience milking delay in distinctive ways (Jahns 2013, 247).
  • 有证据表明，奶牛能通过尿液中的应激激素气味感知同伴的压力。
    ...there is evidence that they can detect the scent of stress hormones present in the urine of fellow cows (Marino and Allen 2017, 475–476).
  • 玩耍行为，如奔跑和与物体互动，是动物愉悦感和创新能力的重要标志，并构成了复杂社会能力的基础。
    While play is an important indication of an animal’s pleasure, curiosity and capacity to innovate, and as such it “forms the basis for complex object-related and social abilities” (Marino and Allen 2017, 481)...
  • 工业化养殖场对奶牛的圈养和按年龄分组，阻止了它们形成自然的、以母系为主的社会结构。
    The assignment to specific physical places in distinct housings prevents the calves, heifers and cows from forming a natural herd that would include a matrilineal social structure... (Marino and Allen 2017, 488).
  • 糟糕的通风环境使嗅觉灵敏的牛犊不得不面对氨气等有害气体的 omnipresent 气味。
    ...calves and heifers, whose sense of smell is far superior to that of humans, deal with omnipresent scents of ammonia, carbon dioxide, methane and hydrogen sulfide (Vallez 2013, 12)...
  • 在自然条件下，奶牛间的物理距离反映了它们的社会关系和偏好。
    The spatial distance that cows establish between each other is affected by their relationship and proximity might indicate the existence of a social bond (McLennan 2013, 26).
  • 在更自然的环境中，奶牛会与特定的个体建立自愿的联结。
    Under more natural conditions, cows seem to engage with particular individuals with whom they prefer to spend their time, creating voluntary bonds... (McLennan 2013, 49–50).
  • 在拥挤的圈养环境中被迫与不喜欢的邻居靠近，可能对动物福利产生负面影响。
    ...a calf or heifer who is a non-preferred partner may stand, lie down or eat in closer proximity than would naturally occur, which may lead to feelings of uneasiness and has been suggested to have negative consequences for animal welfare (McLennan 2013, 52).
  • 作者引用Frans de Waal的“认同”（identification）概念，将身体同步视为一种“将自我映射到他者”的过程，是模仿和共情的前提。
    As a material-semiotic resource, bodily synchronizing can be seen as part of what Frans de Waal has described as “identification” with the other, a process of “bodily mapping the self onto the other...which not only relates to a capacity for shared neural representation, but also forms “a precondition for imitation and empathy” (De Waal 2012, 123).
  • 作者借用Argent对马与骑手同步运动的描述，来形容牛犊在喂食时集体运动的社会性。
    Within this joint “embodiment of movement,” a collective form of sociality in which each calf will instantaneously “follow and lead” (Argent 2012, 120), the calves engage one another and the space around them in bodily acts of identification...
  • 作者将牛犊的进食行为描述为通过动觉、触觉和近身互动等交流模式进行的创造性场所营造活动。
    ...from a sociolinguistic perspective it also entails creative acts of place-making through what Argent calls “kinesic, haptic, and proxemic communication modes” (Argent 2012, 119).
  • 作者将牛犊的“双语”实践定义为一种复杂的行为，它利用了两种结构上差异极大的语码。
    ...bilingualism is to be understood as a “complex set of practices” (Heller 2007, 15) which draw on linguistic resources that belong to two codes which are structurally maximally divergent (see Auer, forthcoming, 8)...
  • 在社会语言学中，不同的语言或语言变体与社会差异和社会不平等的建构过程相关联。
    ...different languages, or language varieties, are associated with diverging “processes of construction of social difference and social inequality” (Heller 2007, 15).
  • 语言被理解为一系列互动对象、人和地点共同作用下产生的分布式效应。
    As an embodied and embedded practice, linguistic place-making...is produced as a “distributed effect of a range of interacting objects, people and places” (Pennycook 2017, 278).

11.5 Conclusion

本章结论部分重申并深化了核心论点。作者首先批判了传统人文主义视角，该视角将圈养动物视为双重受限（本能与物理空间）的被动存在，从而使其“失声”。与此相对，本文的后人类主义视角则揭示了一个悖论：正是这种监禁状态（unfreedom），特别是作为物质环境一部分的铁栏和围栏，反讽地成为了奶牛进行语言性场所营造的“社会空间人造物资源”。随后，作者强调，在工业化养殖这一“自然”与“文化”界限已然崩塌的人类世情境中，能动性（包括语言能动性）不应被理解为个体属性，而应被看作是在不平等的“能动集合体”（agentic assemblages）中涌现的分布式力量。最终，文章通过将语言和能动性重新概念化为一种分布式的、在人-非人-物质的纠缠中产生的过程，旨在打破将非人动物视为“无声的野兽”的人类例外论，并期望促进人类对其他动物作为智慧、社会性和言说存在的更深层次的认可。

  • 作者批判传统世界观将自然和物质视为被动和可被人类利用的惰性存在。
    ...it reinforces a traditional mechanistic worldview where both nature and matter are considered to be passive and inert, available for manipulation by humans and exploitable for profit (Merchant 1992, 48–55).
  • 文章重申，动物能动性是其行为的内在组成部分。
    In drawing attention to the linguistic agency of dairy cows, we do not wish to reiterate the familiar observation that “agency is intrinsic to the way animals behave” (Špinka and Wemelsfelder 2011, 34)...
  • 作者引用哈拉维关于自然与文化“内爆”的观点，来描述工业化养殖场中人、动物、机器界限模糊的状况。
    In the context of industrial dairy farming...a condition exemplifying the “implosion of nature and culture” (Haraway 2003, 16) that marks the Anthropocene...
  • 文章引入Jane Bennett的“能动集合体”概念，将能动性理解为在人与非人行动者网络中涌现的力量。
    ...we must account for how different forms of agency...emerge from within what political theorist Jane Bennett (2010, 107) has called “agentic assemblages,” that is, networks of human and nonhuman actors living together in relations of systemic inequality.
  • 集合体描述了事物被组合在一起并以新方式运作的过程，为思考分布式能动性提供了途径。
    Assemblages, as Pennycook (2017, 278) notes, “describe the way things are brought together and function in new ways”...
  • Bennett认为能动性分布在一个本体论上异质的场域中，而非仅仅局限于人类身体或集体。
    Bennett, indeed, suggests that we think of agency as “distributed across an ontologically heterogeneous field, rather than being a capacity localized in a human body or in a collective produced (only) by human efforts” (Bennett 2010, 23).
  • 最终，作者将能动性的概念与Bennett的“活力唯物主义”和Pennycook的“后人类主义应用语言学”联系起来，挑战了将能动性与人类意图绑定的传统观念。
    This conception of agency, rooted in what Bennett (2010) calls a “vital materialism” and what Pennycook (2018) describes as a “posthumanist applied linguistics,” disturbs the traditional understanding of agency as the capacity for self-willed action...
