Unravelling non-human agency in sustainability transitions

Maria Contesse, Jessica Duncan, Katharine Legun, Laurens Klerkx

全文概要总结

作为一名长期致力于可持续性转型研究，特别是关注多层次视角（MLP）与行动者网络理论（ANT）交叉领域的学者，我认为本文对转型研究领域做出了及时且深刻的理论贡献。它直面了长期以来转型研究中存在的“人类中心主义”偏见，通过严谨的理论综合与扎实的经验案例，系统地阐释了非人类行动者（actants）在推动社会-技术系统转型中的能动性（agency）。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：非人类行动者（如本文案例中的害虫）并非仅仅是转型过程中的被动背景或物质条件，而是拥有真实能动性的“行动者”（actants），能够主动地催化、塑造甚至驱动可持续性转型。这种非人类能动性是关系性的（relational），它通过 destabilizing 现有制度（regime）、重组行动者网络、改变人类行动者的角色与关系，从而对转型轨迹产生决定性影响。这一论点直接挑战了MLP中将“景观层”（landscape）视为缺乏能动性的宏观背景的传统观点，并强调了各层次之间边界的流动性。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的主要论据来源于对智利蔬菜种植系统中“非洲裔画 Bug”（Bagrada hilaris）入侵事件的质性案例研究。具体的经验证据包括：(1) Bagrada害虫的入侵直接破坏了以化学农药为核心的现有病虫害管理制度（regime），使其经济上和技术上都难以为继；(2) 害虫的出现促使原本分散的农民组织起来，成立了历史上第一个“兰帕农民协会”；(3) 害虫“召集”了一个跨部门的公私合作平台（Mesa），将农民、政府官员（农业部、卫生部）、研究人员等先前割裂的行动者连接起来；(4) 在这个新网络中，政府机构（如SAG）的角色从单纯的监管者转变为知识传播和赋能的促进者；(5) 害虫的存在使得替代性的小众创新（niche innovations）——如综合害虫管理（IPM）和生物防治（如致病真菌）——从边缘走向中心，获得了研究支持和农民的采纳意愿。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者巧妙地构建了一个“MLP + ANT”的整合分析框架。首先，使用MLP作为一种“启发式结构”（heuristic structure），将案例中的元素定位在景观层（Bagrada作为外部冲击）、制度层（基于农药的病虫害管理）和小众层面（IPM等替代方案）。接着，运用ANT的核心概念，尤其是“追随行动者”的方法论和“转译”（translation）四阶段理论，来深入剖析转型“进行中”（in-the-making）的动态过程。论证逻辑如下：Bagrada作为景观层行动者，首先通过其破坏性行为“问题化”（problematization）了现行制度；随后，它通过制造危机，引发了农民、政府、研究人员等各方行动者的“兴趣”（interessement）；这导致了各方被“征召”（enrolment）进新的网络（如Mesa），并协商新的角色与行动方案；最终，新成立的农民协会和Mesa成为了网络的“代言人”，开始“动员盟友”（mobilizing allies）以争取更多资源。通过这一过程，文章清晰地展示了非人类行动者如何通过一系列关系性行动，重塑网络、改变权力关系，并推动制度变迁。

4. 方法 (Method)

本研究采用的是质性单案例研究方法。其方法论的核心特色在于借鉴了ANT的“追随行动者”（following the actor）原则，将研究焦点从预设的人类行动者转移到非人类行动者——Bagrada害虫身上。数据收集是多源的，包括2018-2019年间在智利的田野调查，具体手段涵盖了：(1) 对关键行动者网络“Mesa”会议的参与式观察；(2) 对公共机构组织的两次相关研讨会的观察；(3) 对受影响的农民、政府官员、研究人员等6位关键人物的半结构化访谈；(4) 对新闻报道、政府决议等二手文献的分析。数据分析则结合了演绎编码（源于MLP和ANT的理论概念）和归纳编码（源于经验观察），并使用Atlas.ti软件进行辅助。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

核心案例是自2016年起入侵智利中部地区（特别是兰帕市）的农业害虫Bagrada hilaris（俗称Chinche Africano）。这一案例被视为一个“进行中的转型”（transition-in-the-making）。具体而言，Bagrada害虫对十字花科蔬菜造成了毁灭性打击，导致了严重的经济损失，并使得传统的、高度依赖化学农药的病虫害管理制度失效。这一外部冲击迫使当地农业系统不得不寻求向更可持续的综合害虫管理（IPM）和生物防治方法转型。这个案例的精妙之处在于，它不是一个由人类主导的、有预设目标的转型项目，而是一个由一个“不速之客”（非人类行动者）意外引发的、充满冲突、协商和重组的真实转型过程。

1. Introduction

本章首先确立了“能动性”（agency）在可持续性转型研究中的核心地位，并指出既有研究绝大多数都局限于人类能动性，忽视了非人类行动者的作用。作者认为，考虑到转型研究对基础设施和物质文化的重视，对非人类能动性的忽视是令人惊讶的。因此，本文旨在通过引入行动者网络理论（ANT），以智利农业害虫Bagrada hilaris为例，探究非人类行动者在塑造可持续性转型过程中的具体角色，从而填补这一理论空白。

  • 总结了能动性议题在可持续性转型及相关领域研究中的兴起。
    While agency has received considerable attention in recent sustainability transitions studies, as well as in the literature on socio-ecological systems and sustainability transformations, the focus has been on the agency of humans. (Wittmayer et al., 2017; de Haan and Rotmans, 2018; Westley et al., 2011, 2013)
  • 指出早期转型研究因忽视能动性和权力斗争而受到批评，这种批评促进了对转型中行动者角色的新理论探讨。
    Sustainability transitions studies were initially criticized for an over-functionalistic approach and for neglecting issues of agency and power struggles (Duygan et al., 2019; Fischer and Newig, 2016; Grin et al., 2011; Shove and Walker, 2010; Smith et al., 2005).
  • 概述了当前研究对不同行动者群体（如社会运动、政策制定者、中介者）在转型中作用的经验性发现。
    Empirical studies have shown how diverse actor groups can contribute to transitions; including: social movements (Ornetzeder and Rohracher, 2013; Seyfang et al., 2014); incumbents (Späth et al., 2016; Musiolik et al., 2012; Turnheim and Geels, 2013), technology manufacturers (Budde et al., 2012; Musiolik and Markard, 2011); policy makers (Quitzau et al., 2012), and intermediary actors (Hargreaves et al., 2013; Kivimaa, 2014).
  • 介绍了用于理解人类能动性的主流理论概念，如“制度创业”和“制度工作”，及其包含的具体行动（构建愿景、建立联盟等）。
    Understandings of agency in sustainability transitions have been based on concepts such as (collective) institutional entrepreneurship (Duygan et al., 2019; Hassink et al., 2018; Heiskanen et al., 2019; Hermans et al., 2013; Jolly et al., 2016), and institutional work (Fuenfschilling and Truffer, 2016; Rogers et al., 2015).
  • 强调了中介行动者在连接不同参与方中的关键作用。
    Other studies have indicated the role of intermediary actors as relevant agents that catalyze transitions due to their mediating function among dispersed, diverse or previously disconnected actors... (Bush et al., 2017; Fischer and Newig, 2016; Kivimaa et al., 2019a).
  • 指出能动性是分布式、集体性的，而非“英雄式”的个体行为。
    Lawrence et al. (2013) have argued that agency is a task distributed across several actors. This is aligned with studies with an institutional work perspective, that emphasize the role of collective actors in transition, explicitly refraining from heroic formulations of sole actors... (Jolly and Raven, 2015; Smink et al., 2015).
  • 明确提出本文的核心研究问题，即非人类行动者Bagrada hilaris在塑造智利可持续食物系统转型中的作用。
    What is the role of the non-human actor Bagrada hilaris in shaping the transition-in-the-making towards more sustainable food systems in Chile?
  • 引入“进行中的转型”（transition-in-the-making）概念，以描述那些尚不确定最终结果，但已展现出制度 destabilization 潜力的过程。
    ...we consider the role of non-human agency in relation to human agency in a case of sustainable food systems transition, by analyzing a so-called ‘transition-in-the-making’ (Elzen et al., 2011).
  • 指出本文对农业食品系统转型研究的贡献，特别是在该领域日益认识到生物和物质关系重要性的背景下。
    We also further contribute to studies addressing sustainability transitions in the agricultural sectors or the agri-food system..., and the need to take into account (biological) material relations in sustainability transitions processes and analysis... (Darnhofer, 2020; Klerkx and Begemann, 2020; Ollivier et al., 2018; Pigford et al., 2018)
  • 预告了将结合使用MLP和ANT作为分析工具，以探究非人类能动性。
    In order to do so, we make a complementary use of two theories: the Multi-level Perspective (MLP)... and, Actor-Network Theory (ANT) which allows us to account for non-human agency (Shim and Shin, 2019; Shin, 2016).

2. Theoretical framework

本章详细阐述了研究的理论基础，即多层次视角（MLP）与行动者网络理论（ANT）的互补性结合。作者首先定义了“进行中的转型”，强调其过程性与不确定性。随后，系统介绍了MLP的三个分析层次（景观、制度、小众），并特别指出了传统MLP模型中景观层能动性的缺失。接着，详细解释了ANT的核心思想，包括其对非人类行动者（actant）能动性的对称性处理、关系性本体论、以及“转译”（translation）过程的四个阶段。最后，作者论证了将MLP的宏观结构性视野与ANT的过程性、关系性视角相结合的合理性，认为这种结合能够更有效地揭示非人类行动者在跨层次互动中的作用。

  • 将“进行中的转型”定义为一个过程，其中新的实践、网络和框架出现，挑战并动摇现有制度，但其最终能否导致全面的制度转型尚不确定。
    We refer to a transition-in-the-making as a process that we cannot be certain will progress to an overall transition..., but in which we can see the emergence of (new) practices, networks and framings that question and destabilize an existing regime, having the potential to start and lead to a broader transition (Elzen et al., 2011).
  • 提出将MLP作为一种启发式工具来定位转型中的不同行动者，而ANT则提供了分析行动者（相互）行动的分析透镜。
    While the MLP can be a useful heuristic to position different actors involved in a transition at different levels (Diaz et al., 2013), the theory does not provide the analytical lens to analyze actor (inter)actions (de Haan and Rotmans, 2018; Diaz et al., 2013; Elzen et al., 2012; Ingram, 2015).
  • 阐述了MLP的景观层概念，并指出其通常被视为一个缺乏能动性的背景，行动者只能对其作出反应而无法在短期内影响它。
    The general assumption is that actors can only respond to this level, and that there is no room for agency (Raven et al., 2012) or at least actors cannot influence it in the short run (Geels and Schot, 2007).
  • 引入ANT的核心主张，即任何物质或社会世界的元素都可以是行动者（actant），行动是行动者网络合作的产物。
    Instead, ANT claims that any element of the material and social world can be an actor, or in their parlance, an actant. ... As such, a change agent (or mediator in ANT terms) is any ‘entity’ – whether this is ‘social’, ‘natural’ or ‘technical’ – that problematizes or destabilizes a network... (Latour, 2005; Sayes, 2014).
  • 澄清ANT并不为非人类赋予“意图性”，而是认为意图性只是行动的一种类型，并且人类的意图性行动本身也依赖于外部能动性。
    To be clear, ANT does not attribute intentionality to non-humans neither does it presuppose that agency implies intentionality (Sayes, 2014).
  • 详细解释了ANT中网络形成的核心过程——“转译”（translation），及其四个阶段：问题化、兴趣、征召和动员盟友。
    In ANT, the process by which actants make relations is referred as translation. Translation involves four phases: i Problematization... ii Interessement... iii Enrolment... iv Mobilizing allies...
  • 回应了对MLP和ANT结合的潜在批评（如本体论不兼容），并强调ANT作为一种描述关系和行动的方法，其重点在于网络如何形成，而非仅仅描绘网络结构。
    Some scholars have pointed-out that it is unclear how relational ontologies –including ANT– can be useful to analyze transition dynamics beyond the empirical mapping of cases (Geels, 2010), and to the incompatibility between MLP levels and ANT’s flat ontology, which denies the analytical usefulness of ‘levels’ (Geels, 2011).

3. Case description and methods

本章介绍了研究的具体案例背景和研究方法。案例聚焦于2016年首次在智利被发现的入侵性害虫Bagrada hilaris，它严重破坏了当地以大量使用化学农药为特征的病虫害管理制度。作者将向综合害虫管理（IPM）的转变视为一个小众创新，并指出这种转变需要根本性的文化规范变革。研究方法上，作者遵循ANT的路径，通过在2018-2019年间进行的质性数据收集来“追随”Bagrada害虫，包括参与多方利益相关者圆桌会议（Mesa）、访谈和文件分析。

  • 描述了案例的背景：2016年Bagrada hilaris害虫首次在智利被检测到，并迅速传播，它被怀疑是通过陆路或国际空运抵达的。
    In Chile, the Bagrada was first detected in late 2016 in Estero Las Cruces, Metropolitan Region (Faundez et al., 2016), very close to the case study area (Lampa), from where it spread to other Chilean regions (Faundez et al., 2018). It is suspected that the pest arrived by land or international air transport (Faundez et al., 2016).
  • 指出了智利现存病虫害管理制度的问题，即过度使用农药及其导致的蔬菜残留物超标。
    This pest management regime is based on the high use of chemical pesticides (Zolezzi, 2012; Muñoz-Quezada et al., 2016); and external inputs in general. The problem of high-pesticides use in Chile has been made public by researchers (Elgueta et al., 2017; Muñoz-Quezada et al., 2012, 2016)...
  • 定义了综合害虫管理（IPM）作为一种替代性小众方案，并承认它与有机农业相比不那么“激进”，但仍是传统农民向生态实践过渡的重要途径。
    Still, IPM remains relevant because it forms part of several conventional farmers transition trajectories towards ecological practices; including organic agriculture (Lamine, 2011).
  • 将Bagrada案例情境定位为一种“内生更新情境”（endogenous renewal context），即制度内部成员为应对外部压力而进行的、通常是增量式的创新。
    The transition-in-the-making process undertaken by the Bagrada and its related network can be linked to what Smith et al. (2005) coined as an ‘endogenous renewal context’.
  • 详细说明了数据收集的方法，包括对“Mesa”会议和两次政府研讨会的观察、与6名关键参与者的半结构化访谈，以及对新闻和官方文件的分析。
    Qualitative data was gathered between September 2018 and June 2019 in Chile, following similar methods from studies examining nonhuman agency through ANT (Shim and Shin, 2019; Shin, 2016), or complementing ANT with MLP (Diaz et al., 2013).

4. Findings

本章详细呈现了研究发现，分为两部分。第一部分（4.1）追溯了由Bagrada害虫引发的网络构建过程，展示了它如何将农民、政府机构（SAG, INIA）、地方政府、农药、乃至生物防治制剂（如致病真菌、信息素）等不同层次的行动者“征召”进一个新的网络。这个过程催生了两个新的正式组织：兰帕农民协会和Mesa圆桌会议。第二部分（4.2）深入分析了Bagrada在这个网络中扮演的多重角色。它既是所有其他行动者的“敌手”（antagonist），又是催化剂、连接者和中介者，维系着整个网络的运作。研究发现，Bagrada的出现不仅改变了农民、政府机构等人类行动者的角色，也改变了农药等非人类行动者的角色（从有效工具变为昂贵且不可靠的方案），从而为IPM等小众创新方案的崛起创造了条件。

  • 描述了Bagrada作为景观层行动者，如何通过其破坏性行为，将制度层行动者（如农民、政府机构）“征召”进其网络，并促使了两个新网络的形成。
    Since its arrival, the Bagrada has problematized the issue of ‘pest management’, enrolling a whole range of different actants from the regime into its network, while also triggering the formation of two new regime actors networks... The Bagrada can be seen as a landscape actant: an external shock putting pressure within regime actors...
  • 展示了最初的应对措施是制度性的和路径依赖的：测试和注册了19种化学农药以进行紧急控制。这一行动由农业部的三个相关机构（INIA, SAG, FIA）合作完成。
    This first measure corresponds to regime practices associated to pest management; so far, most farmers have depended on pesticides for managing the Bagrada, and pests in general.
  • 揭示了农药滥用引发了农民与SAG（监管机构）之间的紧张关系，这反过来推动了对替代方案的探索。
    Many farmers were fined by the SAG, and tensions among the SAG and farmers emerged due to the, at times, excessive or improper use of pesticides to control the Bagrada...
  • 识别出INIA和SAG扮演了“混合行动者”（hybrid actors）或“基于制度的中介者”（regime-based intermediaries）的角色，他们连接了制度与小众创新。
    We consider INIA, and partly the SAG, as ‘hybrid actors’: they belong to the regime level food system agencies..., while also being the actors connecting and translating niche innovations (IPM, biological control) to the regime (Elzen et al., 2012).
  • 记录了兰帕农民协会的成立过程，这是在地方市长的建议和支持下，农民为增强其向公共机构提出诉求的力量而组织起来的。
    The advice and support of the mayor resulted in the creation of the first ‘Asociación de Agricultores de Lampa’ (Lampa’s Farmers Association) in history, which now has 74 members, all of which are conventional farmers (regime actors) from Lampa.
  • 阐述了Mesa圆桌会议的诞生，它是一个多部门平台，旨在协调各方力量共同应对Bagrada问题，并将之前关系紧张的行动者（如农民和SAG检查员）聚集在一起。
    The Mesa is a multi-sectoral roundtable gathering together private actors (farmers) and public agencies from the local, regional and national level.
  • 分析了Bagrada扮演的看似矛盾但实则统一的角色：它既是网络的“敌手”，又是将所有行动者维系在一起的中心概念和“中介”。
    That is, as an antagonist, we see that the Bagrada has reorganized all other network actants (human and non-human), while also being an intermediary among these actants’ relations, semiotically holding them together.
  • 论证了Bagrada如何改变了SAG的角色，从一个纯粹的检查员转变为一个促进者，通过提供培训来缓和与农民的紧张关系并传播知识。
    This change of role of the SAG has also helped to smooth farmers-SAG relations, which in turn have allowed them to better collaborate against the Bagrada...
  • 揭示了Bagrada如何通过其生物特性（高繁殖率）使得化学农药失效，从而改变了农药在网络中的角色，并为生物防治（如致病真菌）创造了机会。
    ...we can see that the Bagrada changes the function of chemical pesticides, by translating them from an easy pest control to one that is expensive and unreliable. ...niche biological control actants –such as entomo-pathogenic fungus– are starting to be more appealing for farmers...

5. Discussion

本章对研究发现进行了深入的理论反思，主要围绕三个核心主题展开。首先，探讨了非人类能动性在转型中的具体行动和角色，指出其行动（如破坏制度、建立联系）与先前研究中确定的人类能动性行动有相似之处，但非人类能动性是通过其物质性来“调节”而非“决定”人类的行动空间。其次，强调了能动性的关系性本质，认为转型并非源于个别“英雄式”行动者的战略规划，而是多重、异质的行动者（包括人类和非人类）之间复杂互动和依赖的结果。最后，讨论了这些发现对MLP理论的启示，认为研究结果挑战了MLP的三个基本假设：a) 转型是自下而上（从小众到制度）的线性过程；b) 景观层缺乏能动性；c) 各层次之间边界清晰。本文证明了能动性可以来自任何层次，且层次间的边界是流动的。

  • 总结了Bagrada所扮演的多种能动角色，如破坏制度、中介关系、改变其他行动者角色等，并指出这些角色与之前文献中对（人类）中介者的描述相符。
    Some of the agentic actions and roles played by the Bagrada are in line with those identified in previous studies about agency in transitions. These included connecting previously disperse (human) actors (Fischer and Newig, 2016; Kivimaa et al., 2019a, 2019b) from the regime, triggering the creation of alliances and networking (Musiolik et al., 2012; Kivimaa et al., 2019a, 2019b; Westley et al., 2013)...
  • 指出Bagrada作为“支持者”的角色不同于传统定义，它本身就是一个变革性的行动者，通过改变其他行动者的角色和关系来推动转型。
    In contrast, also as a supporter the Bagrada is a transformative agent, which emerges in several ways from the findings. We see the Bagrada as a transformer in that it has transformed other actants roles and practices...
  • 强调“改变行动者角色”本身应被视为一项关键的能动性功能，并应被纳入“制度工作”的范畴。
    ...we consider that the capacity of changing actants roles... should be consider as a relevant agentic role within studies and frameworks about agency in transition, and also addressed as part of the set of actions of ‘institutional work’. (Wittmayer et al., 2017)
  • 指出非人类能动性并非物质决定论，而是通过限制、赋能或支持来“调节”（condition）人类的能动性。
    The Bagrada does not determine human agency, but as we have shown in our findings, it has the capacity to condition other actors’ agencies; limiting, enabling or supporting their possible actions. (Latour, 2005; Legun and Henry, 2017; Müller and Schurr, 2016; Svensson and Nikoleris, 2018)
  • 批判了将转型能动性局限于人类的、有意的、理性的战略行动的观点，强调了非意图性行动和其他形式能动性的重要性。
    These findings illustrate that we should not limit our understanding of agency in transitions to human agency through intentional, rational and strategic actions, as has so far been the focus of most transition and transformation studies about agency (Brown et al., 2013; Farla et al., 2012; Geels, 2020; Werbeloff et al., 2016; Westley et al., 2013).
  • 警告不要过度强调转型的共识性和可控性，指出冲突、偶然性和不可预测性是转型过程的内在特征。
    These findings reinforce previous studies that caution against too much emphasis on sustainability transitions as consensual or deliberative processes that can be intentionally managed into a certain normative direction (Avelino, 2009; Hargreaves et al., 2013; Meadowcroft, 2009; Rossi et al., 2019; Shove and Walker, 2007).
  • 提出本研究对MLP的第一个启示：转型能动性并非局限于特定层次，而是由跨越景观、制度和小众三个层次的行动者网络共同作用的结果。
    These findings support Jørgensen (2012) who argues that agency for transitions is not necessarily confined to any particular level, while (human) actors engaged at all levels are not working in isolation.
  • 提出对MLP的第二个启示：景观层并非没有能动性，本文证明了景观层的非人类行动者可以是关键的变革推动者。
    ...the landscape no longer appears as a level without agency, void of actors (or actants) performing functions that help to advance transitions (Fischer and Newig, 2016), nor as a level in which its material aspects lack autonomous causal efficacy (Svensson and Nikoleris, 2018).

6. Conclusion

本章总结了全文的核心贡献和建议。理论上，文章证明了非人类行动者可以是重要的变革推动者，它们通过多种行动和角色来破坏现有制度、强化转型过程，并调节人类能动性。这对MLP理论提出了两大修正：转型网络跨越所有三个层次，并且景观层并非没有能动性。方法论上，作者推荐结合使用MLP和ANT等关系性理论，以更好地捕捉转型过程中人类与非人类行动者之间的复杂互动。实践上，文章建议转型实践者应识别并致力于改变网络中关键行动者的角色及其相互关系，并承认和利用多样的能动性来源，包括非人类的能动性。

  • 重申了非人类行动者可以成为重要的变革推动者，能够同时扮演多种角色来瓦解制度并调节人类的行动。
    ...we have shown that non-humans can be relevant change agents in provoking and catalyzing a process of transition, simultaneously doing and performing varied agentic actions and roles that disrupt the regime and reinforce a transition process, while also conditioning human agency...
  • 提出未来研究应超越对战略性、意图性人类行动的关注，转向探索异质行动者之间的关系与互动动态。
    Furthermore, research on agency in transitions should not be limited to strategic intentional human action... but on the relational and interactional dynamics among multiple and diverse agents –human and non-human– involved in transition processes (echoing Darnhofer, 2020; West et al., 2020).
  • 明确指出研究对MLP的两大理论启示：一是转型过程涉及跨越所有三个层次的行动者网络；二是景观层并非没有能动性。
    Therefore, with regard to the MLP as an heuristic to understand and structure transitions our study has two main theoretical implications. First, that a whole network of both human and non-human actants distributed across the three MLP levels... are involved in the transition process. Secondly, that considering non-human agency implies that the landscape is no longer a level where there is no agency.
  • 建议未来研究可以通过MLP与ANT或其他关系性方法（如“组合”理论）的交叉使用来深入探索能动性问题。
    Given this, research about agency in transition may benefit from cross-overs with ANT or other relational approaches such as assemblages approaches (Darnhofer, 2020; Legun and Henry, 2017; Shim and Shin, 2019; West et al., 2020)...
  • 为转型实践者提出建议，强调识别和改变行动者角色与关系的重要性，并应在干预中考虑和利用多元的能动性来源。
    A first reflection for those willing to steer processes of sustainability transitions is the importance of identifying changes in actants roles and in the relations between actants needed for advancing sustainability transitionsg...
