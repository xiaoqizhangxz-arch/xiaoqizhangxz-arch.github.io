Ontology Matters: The Relational Materiality of Nature and Agro-Food Studies

David Goodman

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，主流农业食品研究（agro-food studies）因其理论根基——即源自古典马克思主义的“劳动过程”（labour process）概念——而深陷于现代主义的“自然-社会”二元论本体论（dualist ontology）之中。这种本体论将自然视为被动的、外在于社会的存在，导致该领域在理论上无法充分把握和分析由农业生物技术引发的、以“关系性物质性”（relational materiality）为核心的新型生物政治（bio-politics）议题。因此，作者主张，农业食品研究必须进行本体论层面的反思与更新，并提出行动者网络理论（Actor-Network Theory, ANT）作为一种更具解释力的替代理论框架，因为它通过“关系唯物主义”（relational materialism）的视角，超越了主客二分，能够更好地捕捉人与非人行动者（non-humans）共同建构的、复杂的生态-社会网络。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者主要通过以下几方面论据支撑其论点：首先，通过思想史梳理，揭示了“劳动过程”概念在“新农村社会学”和农业政治经济学中的核心地位及其理论谱系，指出其将分析焦点限定在生产关系和社会结构变迁上，而系统性地忽视了自然的能动性。其次，作者指出，当代围绕转基因生物（GMOs）等农业生物技术展开的生物政治行动主义，其核心关切已超越传统的阶级、生产结构问题，转向了物种边界、生态风险、身体健康等涉及自然与身体物质性的伦理与安全议题，而传统框架对此解释力不足。再次，作者引介了生态马克思主义（ecological Marxism）内部对“劳动过程”概念的重构尝试（如Ted Benton），承认其进步性，但认为其仍未彻底摆脱人类中心主义。最后，作者详细阐释了ANT的核心概念（如异质性工程、对称性原则），论证其如何提供一种非二元论的分析路径，将自然、技术和身体视为网络中活跃的行动者，从而为理解新的生态-社会混合体（socionatural assemblages）提供了强大的理论工具。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

本文的论证逻辑循序渐进，极具说服力。首先，文章从农业食品研究的内部理论困境（回应Marsden的评论）出发，确立了“本体论选择至关重要”这一元理论前提。接着，通过对“劳动过程”概念的谱系学考察，诊断出该领域长期以来未被审视的本体论“盲点”——即现代主义的自然-社会二元论。随后，文章将这一理论诊断与经验现实相结合，以农业生物技术引发的生物政治浪潮为例，具象化地展示了现有理论框架的“失语”状态。在批判的基础上，作者展开了建设性的工作：他先探讨了马克思主义内部的“绿色化”改良路径，承认其价值但点明其局限；然后，系统地引介ANT作为一种更为彻底的本体论替代方案，并详细论述其“关系唯物主义”如何能够捕捉到人与非人行动者共同编织的“无缝之网”（seamless fabric）。最后，文章回应了对ANT“缺乏批判性”的指责，论证其通过解构既定本体论和揭示权力网络的建构过程，本身就蕴含着深刻的批判潜力和伦理向度，从而有力地完成了其核心论证闭环。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的主要研究方法是理论思辨、概念史分析和批判性文献综述。作者并非进行经验研究，而是对一个学术领域（农业食品研究）的理论基础进行深度“诊断”和“治疗”。他通过追溯核心概念（劳动过程）的源流与演变，揭示其内含的本体论预设。通过对不同理论范式（古典马克思主义、生态马克思主义、行动者网络理论）的比较分析，展现了它们在解释“自然”问题上的差异与优劣。这种方法体现了作者深厚的理论素养，通过对一个领域的元理论（meta-theoretical）反思，推动其议程的更新与发展。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

贯穿全文的核心案例是“农业生物技术”（agro-biotechnologies），特别是转基因生物（GMOs）的兴起及其引发的全球性“生物政治行动主义”（bio-political activism）。这个案例被用来论证多个层面的观点：1）它揭示了当代农业食品系统中最前沿、最具争议性的议题，这些议题（如物种跨界、基因漂流、食品安全、身体完整性）直接指向自然的物质性和能动性。2）它凸显了传统以“劳动过程”为中心的政治经济学分析框架的局限性，因为该框架更关注资本渗透、农场结构等社会生产关系问题，而对上述生态、伦理和身体政治议题缺乏敏感度。3）它为引入ANT等新理论提供了经验基础，因为这些新技术创造了前所未有的“自然-文化混合体”（nature-culture hybrids），恰好是ANT理论所擅长分析的对象。

The labour process and agro-food studies

本章节追溯了“劳动过程”作为农业食品研究中核心中层概念的历史地位。作者论证，自1970年代“新农村社会学”和“农业新政治经济学”兴起以来，源自古典马克思主义（特别是其列宁主义变体）的演绎认识论和以生产为中心的分析范式，使得“劳动过程”成为分析农业变迁的基石。从“土地问题”的经典辩论到全球农业食品重构的当代讨论，这一概念虽形态各异，但其本体论内核——即将自然视为被动背景而非能动参与者——始终未变，甚至在后结构主义转向中也被不加批判地继承下来。

  • 批判了早期“新农村社会学”的演绎认识论，并指出了后来对其的挑战。
    This centrality reflects the twin foundations of the ‘new rural sociology’ and ‘new political economy of agriculture’ in the deductive epistemology of classical Marxism, which was not seriously questioned until the later 1980s (Long et al. 1986; Vandergeest 1988; Marsden 1988; Buttel and Goodman 1989; Buttel and McMichael 1990)
  • 指出农业食品研究中采用的劳动过程概念源于列宁和考茨基的政治辩论，而非马克思的自然主义思想。
    Rather, the labour process concept widely adopted in the ‘new’ rural sociology, and which posits the mimetic equivalence of agriculture and industry, derives from the politically charged context of Lenin’s debates with the Narodniks and Kautsky’s exchanges with the German ‘reformists’ (Hussein and Tribe 1981).
  • 考茨基分析的复杂性曾被列宁主义正统所遮蔽，但近期得到重新发掘。
    Although the subtleties of Kautsky’s analysis more recently have been reclaimed from the Leninist orthodoxy (Watts 1996; Goodman and Watts 1997), “the Russian, and specifically Leninist, contribution . . . (came) . . . to define the terms of the ‘classical’ debate..." (Goodman et al 1987, p. 145).
  • 第三世界农民研究的理论辩论（如生产方式衔接、商品化）进一步强化了这种理论谱系。
    This lineage of theoretical and macrostructural concerns was further strengthened by re-interpretations of the classical tradition, and notably the ‘agrarian question,’ in the neo-Marxist development literature on the fate of the Third World peasantries... (Newby 1985).
  • 农业新政治经济学的核心议题是农业社会结构的资本主义转型，这是一种以生产关系为中心的分析。
    As already suggested, the recovery of the deductivist legacy of classical Marxism also extended to its central problematic: the capitalist transformation of agrarian social structures (Goodman and Redclift 1981; Buttel and McMichael 1990).

    Its imprint as foil can clearly be seen in critical moves to formulate actor-oriented perspectives of agrarian change (Long et al. 1986).

    The immediate farm-level social and technical relations of production similarly are privileged in analyses of agrarian exceptionalism, that is, agriculture as a special case or anomaly within capitalism, arising from the singularities of its natural production process (Mann and Dickenson 1978; Goodman et al. 1987; Mann 1990).

    Recourse to the conceptual frame of the labour process can be tracked across analyses of capitalist restructuring, the characterization of Fordist/post-Fordist production in agriculture, ‘farming styles’ actor-oriented perspectives, and the food regimes literature (Goodman and Watts 1994).

The labour process and agricultural biotechnologies.

本章聚焦于“第一代”农业生物技术分析，论证了它们如何将“劳动过程”框架应用于新技术。这些分析（如Kloppenburg；Goodman, Sorj, Wilkinson；Boyd等人）虽然深刻揭示了资本如何通过商品化（如种子）和“占有主义”（appropriationism）来渗透和改造农业生产，但其视角始终局限于生产的社会关系和资本的单向能动性。作者批判这种框架将自然视为被动的、被改造的对象，而未能捕捉到生物技术所创造出的新生命形式和生态-社会混合体的复杂能动性。这种单向度的、人类中心主义的分析，使得它们无法有效回应当前生物政治行动主义的核心关切。

  • “第一代”生物技术分析将议题置于农业与工业的边界问题上，但仍采用资本渗透和商品化农业劳动过程的经典框架。
    What may be termed ‘first generation’ analyses of agricultural biotechnologies extended the horizon of agro-food studies by problematizing the boundaries between ‘agriculture’ and ‘industry’ (Kloppenburg, Jr. 1988; Goodman, Sorj and Wilkinson 1987).
  • Kloppenburg的分析建立在农业作为“顽固部门”的经典马克思主义论述上，并将Braverman的理论应用于植物科学，视其转变为“资本主义财产”。
    The work of Kloppenburg, Jr. (1988), for example, is first situated generally on the classical Marxist terrain of agriculture as a ‘recalcitrant sector,’ before taking up the specific theme of the vectors of capitalist penetration of plant science and its gradual reconfiguration as “capitalist property” (Braverman 1976, p. 156).
  • Goodman, Sorj, and Wilkinson (1987) 提出了“占有主义”(appropriationism) 概念，描述了工业资本如何逐步将农业生产过程中的自然元素转化为工业活动。
    The concept of ‘appropriationism’ is used to designate the historically discontinuous, piecemeal “. . . but persistent undermining of discrete elements of the agricultural production process, their transformation into industrial activities, and their re-incorporation into agriculture as inputs . . .”(Goodman et al 1987, p. 2).
  • 这些作者都强调了杂交技术在开启植物改良私有化和推动综合性作物管理系统中的关键作用。
    In common with Kloppenburg, Jr. (1988), these authors recognize the pivotal importance of hybridization in opening up opportunities for proprietary plant improvement...
  • 对这些“第一代”分析的近期扩展，如Boyd等人借鉴马克思价值理论，区分了自然的“形式 subsumption”和“真实 subsumption”。
    In a recent critique and extension of these ‘first generation’ analyses, and including Susan Mann’s work (Mann and Dickenson 1978; Mann 1990), Boyd et al. (2000) borrow from Marx’s value theory to propose a distinction between the formal and real subsumption of nature.
  • 这些分析都呼应了Yoxen早前被忽视的贡献，他将“科学视为劳动过程”，揭示了资本主义社会关系如何内嵌于科学劳动过程及其技术设计之中。
    With their insistent emphasis on the social agency of capital in making nature into a productive force, Boyd et al. (2000) echo a much earlier formulation by Yoxen (1981). (Referencing also Radical Science Journal Collective)
  • Yoxen深入分析了分子生物学的历史，揭示了生命如何被还原论地理解为“程序化物质”，以及资本如何通过基金会、国家和工业间接控制这一领域。
    Yoxen’s principal analytical themes are the following: the emergence of the reductive, ‘information’ theoretic view and the forces behind “the apprehension of nature as programmed matter” (pp. 69–71)...
  • Yoxen总结道，在资本控制下，生物技术成为“占有活生生的自然的一种特定模式”，使生命本身成为一种生产力。
    Under the control of capital, biotechnology “is a specific mode of the appropriation of living nature – literally capitalising life,” which “. . . becomes a productive force, playing a growing role in reconstituting the social relations of a new capitalist order” (Yoxen 1981, p. 112).
  • 作者总结，这些基于劳动过程的分析框架，其能动性是单向的，即人类主体作用于一个外在的、被客体化的自然，无法理论化新技术实践中出现的“活生生的实体”。
    It is equally clear, however, that agency in these approaches is unidirectional and uniquely identified with human intentionality and praxis. Human agents act ‘upon’ an external, objectified, but now less ‘recalcitrant,’ nature, rather than in social partnership with nature.

The materiality of nature: reconstructing the labour process

本章探讨了重构“劳动过程”概念以纳入自然物质性的可能性，主要围绕生态马克思主义的尝试展开。作者指出，农业食品研究领域普遍忽视了这一重要的理论发展。他介绍了以Ted Benton为代表的学者如何试图通过恢复马克思早期著作中的自然主义和唯物主义前提，来修正劳动过程理论，强调其作为“社会对自然的占有”这一被忽略的维度。这一重构旨在为整合社会正义与环境正义（红绿联盟）提供理论基础。然而，作者也强调，这种依赖于未经审视的“劳动过程”概念的做法，实际上是默许了现代主义的自然-社会二元论，从而限制了对农业食品网络中“活生生的生态-社会共生产物”的理论把握能力。

  • 作者批评尽管农业食品研究声称已与演绎主义决裂，但其核心概念（如劳动过程）的本体论基础却从未被审视。
    However, this shift seems to have been more about problematics, especially of the ‘agrarian question’ variety, than a root-and-branch interrogation of its ontological foundations and core concepts. (Referencing Buttel and McMichael 1990; Arce and Marsden 1993)
  • 该领域对平行学科中关于“将自然带回”社会理论的丰富讨论（如红绿辩论、生态马克思主义）保持沉默。
    By problematizing the modern ontology of the social sciences with its dualistic separation of nature and society, these initiatives interrogate the ability of inherited conceptual structures to address the materiality of nature–society interactions (Benton 1991, 1995).
  • Alfred Schmidt的经典研究开启了对马克思自然观的重新审视，激励了生态马克思主义的建构。
    This challenge, initiated by Alfred Schmidt’s classic study, The Concept of Nature in Marx (1971) has encouraged theorists to resuscitate the naturalistic, materialist premises of Marx’s early writings as the foundations of an ecological Marxism.
  • 生态马克思主义辩论的核心议题包括马克思的“普罗米修斯主义”以及资本主义的“第二重矛盾”。
    As observed previously, points of discussion include the Prometheanism of Marx’s conceptualization... and under-theorization of ‘the ecological crisis-tendencies of capitalism’... the so-called second contradiction of capitalism (Benton 1989, 1992; Grundmann 1991; Watts 1995; O’Connor 1988; Burkett 1998a,b).
  • 马克思主义地理学也参与了这一讨论，扩展了“自然的生产”论题。
    This expanding programme to construct an ecological Marxism and integrate Red/Green concerns also has been taken up in Marxist geography, where Castree (1995) has sought to extend the ‘production of nature’ thesis (Smith 1984) and subsequent debates (Redclift 1897; FitzSimmons 1989 a,b).
  • Benton试图通过强调劳动过程的“社会对自然的占有”维度，来“绿化”马克思的理论，以平衡其社会关系维度。
    To summarize drastically, Benton’s purpose vis-ávis both Marx’s ‘mature’ economic theory and contemporary labour-process debates originating from Braverman (1974) is to ensure that the complex duality of labour processes is recognized... to complement the social-relational aspects of exploitation and valorization by restoring to equal prominence the second aspect of labour processes as forms of social appropriation of nature (Benton 1989).
  • 这种重构旨在为连接地方性环境斗争与更广泛政治项目的生态社会主义政治提供分析线索。
    In this respect, Castree (1995) envisions ecological Marxism as the analytical thread for an ecological socialist politics linking local, place-based environmental struggles into a wider political project, anticipating Harvey’s call to find ways to connect ‘militant particularism’ and global ambition (Harvey 1996).
  • 依赖未经重构的劳动过程概念，意味着默许了自然与社会的分离，无法理论化自然的能动性。
    To spell out the disturbing corollary of this argument, reliance on the unreconstructed concept of the labour process involves, at the very least, complicit (because unexamined) acceptance of the modernist separation of nature and society... (Referencing Castree 1995).
  • 作者观察到，农业食品研究领域开始出现一种反思性的“转向”，开始关注自然，并探索ANT和惯例理论等新视角。
    It is particularly encouraging, therefore, to see the growing engagement in agro-food studies with theories which urge that ‘the status of nature,’ as Marsden et al. (2000) put it, be reconsidered... including work on consumption and food quality (Lockie and Kitto 2000), and parallel explorations of ant (Busch and Juska 1997; Murdoch 1997a,b; Whatmore 1997; Goodman 1999), convention theory (Wilkinson 1997; Murdoch and Miele 1999) and... complementarities between these two perspectives (Marsden et al. 2000).
  • Benton指出，社会学学科的建构本身就排除了关于社会与其“自然”基底关系的问题，这种二元思维根深蒂固。
    As Benton (1994) observes... “. . . these dualistic modes of thought go very deep. They are not mere superficial devices which can simply be eliminated from the discipline whilst leaving everything else in place. They are in a very important respect organizing categories . . .” (p. 29, original emphasis).

Actor-Network Theory and relational materialism

本章系统阐述了行动者网络理论（ANT）作为一种替代性本体论的潜力。作者指出，ANT通过其“关系唯物主义”和“广义对称性”原则，从根本上消解了自然/社会、主体/客体的二元对立。它将世界视为由人类与非人类行动者通过“异质性工程”共同建构的、流动的网络。通过Latour等人的论述，作者强调ANT的分析焦点在于揭示社会与物质交换属性的“盲点”，看到“自然-文化混合体”的形成过程。最后，本章回应了对ANT缺乏批判性和政治立场的批评，认为ANT通过揭示世界的建构性、赋予以往被消声的非人类以“发言权”，本身就内含一种深刻的关系伦理和批判潜能，能够为理解和参与当代生物政治提供强有力的工具。

  • ANT提供了一套启发式概念，主张诸如自然/社会等本体论范畴并非先验给定，而是异质性网络建构过程的事后“效应”。
    Ant elaborates a set of heuristic concepts and methodological premises to propose that ontological categories, such as nature/society or structure/agency, cannot be theorized and defined a priori.
  • ANT的分析旨在描述网络形成过程中，不同实体间跨越自然/社会鸿沟的属性交换。
    In this heterogeneous engineering, no line is drawn a priori between classes of phenomena “by distinguishing those that drive from those that are driven” (Law 1994, p. 12).
  • Latour聚焦于“社会与物质交换属性的盲点”，揭示世界是由“自然与文化的混合体”构成的“无缝之网”。
    In rejecting the purified or fetishized categories of ‘nature’ and ‘society,’ Latour (1994) focuses on “the blind spot where society and matter exchange properties” (p. 41). ... In these processes of mediation, the natural and social worlds have been woven into “a seamless fabric.” (Referencing Latour 1993, p. 10)
  • Latour的历史分析揭示了“非人类的社会化”过程，即过去的社会-物质互动如何通过“技术代表”凝结下来，支撑着现代社会。
    These “technical delegates” of past sociomaterial interactions, earlier outcomes of the “delicate shuttle” of relational networking across the nature/society ‘divide,’ hold society together and provide the foundation for modern technosciences (Latour 1994, p. 40).
  • Callon和Law指出，ANT挑战了将能动性定位为个体（特别是人类个体）的观念，认为能动性是网络的集体产物。
    In other words, ant goes beyond attentiveness to the material foundation of human action. Its premise is that agency is always a collective, networked outcome, performed by nondualist sociomaterial associations. (Referencing Callon and Law 1995, p. 502)
  • Law强调ANT的独特性在于其“关系唯物主义”，区别于其他仅承认物质基础但仍保持本体论分离的社会学。
    As Law (1992) argues, materialism and social relations interact in other sociologies but what distinguishes ant is its relational materialism. Failure to take this further step invites asymmetrical analyses... (Referencing Murdoch 1997a,b; Benton 1989).
  • Haraway批判了马克思主义人本主义中“人与自然的中介”理论，呼吁建立一种人类不再是万物尺度的对话。
    Haraway (1992) raises similar issues in her critique of Young (1985) for adopting “a theory of mediations between man and nature,” in which “nature could only be a matrix or product, while man had to be the sole agent” (p. 83).
  • 尽管ANT因其“广义对称性”原则而被批评为丧失规范立场、显得价值中立。
    Shapiro (1997), for example, objects that ant’s insistence on ontological symmetry involves the loss of normative positioning. (Referencing also Lee and Brown 1994; Koch 1995; Collins and Yearly 1992; McClellan 1996).
  • 但许多学者也在ANT中发现了一种“关系伦理”，认为其强调属性交换的世界构建过程本身就具有伦理意涵。
    Thus, Whatmore (1997) suggests that “Latour’s notion of hybridity as nature–culture collectives” (p. 47) exposes connectivities erased by dualistic modernist perspectives and can be used to construct a relational ethics. (Referencing also Kovach 2000; Koch 1995; Shapiro 1997).
  • Murdoch等人认为，ANT的真正价值在于尝试将新的伦理关怀融入社会科学，即使它不符合传统“批判理论”的定义，它也履行了一项批判性任务。
    These theorists “force us to look afresh at the categories, divisions, and boundaries that frequently divert our attention away from the nonhuman multitudes which make up our world... ant does not qualify as a ‘critical theory’ but we should recognize that it performs a critical task . . .” (Murdoch 1997b, p. 753).
  • 作者引用Pickering的后人类主义视角，认为批判不必局限于人类中心轴线，而可以指向特定的人类/非人类组合。
    From a posthumanist perspective, however, as Pickering (1997) argues, “it is no longer clear that critique and reflection have to move along this axis. Posthumanism directs our attention to the multiple intertwinings of the human and nonhuman, and critique might, for example, direct itself towards specific human/nonhuman assemblages . . .” (p. 191).
  • FitzSimmons的批判地理学工作也强调，解构被实体化的“自然”概念，对于挑战整个支配性话语至关重要。
    Challenging radical geographies to discard the Enlightenment invention of primordial nature, FitzSimmons urges that “We must recognize that externalised, abstracted, Nature-made-primordial provides a source of authority to a whole language of domination." (FitzSimmons 1989a, pp. 108–109).
  • ANT通过对网络如何组装和稳定的“厚描”，也提供了理解如何拆解权力网络的概念工具。
    Furthermore, with its ‘thick’ descriptions of the assembly, coordination and durability of powerful networks, ant provides conceptual tools to understand how these “translation regimes” (Callon 1991) might be dismantled... (Referencing also Latour 1992, 1993; Haraway 1997).

Conclusion

结论部分重申了文章的核心主张：本体论选择对于学术分析和政治实践至关重要。作者总结道，社会科学深受二元论本体论的束缚，而农业食品研究领域继承的“劳动过程”概念，正是这一问题的集中体现，它将分析视角局限于生产领域，无法有效应对由生物技术引发的、关乎自然、身体与伦理的新型生物政治。作者呼吁，农业食品研究需要进行理论更新，将不同的分析立场（如重构的马克思主义与ANT）带入对话，以更好地支持反对不受约束和不民主的技术科学的社会斗争。这场理论更新才刚刚开始，但生物政治行动主义的兴起使其变得尤为迫切。

  • 作者指出，社会科学背负着沉重的本体论包袱，各种“绿化”项目正是为了纠正这种本体论“盲点”。
    Reflecting their early disciplinary and institutional raison d’ être, the social sciences are burdened with heavy ontological baggage whose original seals are still very much in place (Benton 1991, 1995).
  • 实体化的本体论范畴会遮蔽其背后的政治议程，而生态重构项目和ANT等视角则有助于打开这些“黑箱”。
    Here, ecological reconstructive projects and perspectives such as ant have a significant critical role to play by opening up these ‘black boxes,’ as Vogel (1996) has argued. (Referencing also FitzSimmons 1989a)
  • 作者再次强调，继承自马克思主义传统的“劳动过程”概念，其分析入口几乎没有为绿色政治议题留下空间。
    Benton (1989), Castree (1995) and others, both within and outside the Marxist tradition, also have emphasized that the analytic point of entry privileged by the inherited concept of the labour process leaves little or no space for the issues of green politics.
  • 结论呼吁将不同分析立场（如马克思主义和文化研究）进行对话，以推动农业食品研究的理论革新。
    The important point, however, is to bring different analytic positions into conversation in order to promote the theoretical renewal of agro-food studies and support social struggles against unfettered and undemocratic technoscience. (Referencing Castree 1995 for a similar point on bridging theories)
