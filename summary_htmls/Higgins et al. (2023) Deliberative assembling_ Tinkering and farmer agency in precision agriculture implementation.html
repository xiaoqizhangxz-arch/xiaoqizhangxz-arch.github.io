Deliberative assembling: Tinkering and farmer agency in precision agriculture implementation

Vaughan Higgins, Daniël van der Velden, Noemie Bechtet, Melanie Bryant, Jane Battersby, Melissa Belle, Laurens Klerkx

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，“修补”（tinkering）这一概念为理解农民在精准农业（PA）实施过程中的“审慎组装”（deliberative assembling）行为提供了一个强有力的分析透镜。作者们认为，农民并非被动地接受技术，而是主动地通过一系列复杂的“修补”实践，将精准农业技术与他们现有的知识、惯例、优先级以及支持网络进行整合，从而使其在农场中变得“可行”（workable）。这些“修补”实践——具体表现为“断开连接”（disconnection）、“实验与试错”（experimentation and trial-and-error）以及“权衡与妥协”（trade-offs and compromises）——重塑了农民的能动性（agency）。这种能动性并非固有或绝对的，而是在与技术、顾问、经销商等多元行动者构成的关系网络中被动态地分配和协商的，既可能为农民开辟新的选择空间，也可能对其构成限制。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的论据建立在对澳大利亚、荷兰和法国三个国家 arable farming systems（旱作农业系统）的比较案例研究之上。通过对稻米种植者（澳大利亚）、作物农民（荷兰）以及使用决策支持工具（DST）的小麦和油菜籽种植者（法国）进行的大量半结构化访谈，作者们收集了丰富的经验证据。这些证据生动地揭示了农民在面对PA技术时的具体行动策略：例如，澳大利亚农民通过依赖可信赖的农艺师来弥补机械经销商服务的不足；荷兰农民积极改造旧设备并与本地农技公司合作；法国农民则不得不在分散的咨询服务和硬件支持之间进行协调。这些跨国案例的经验材料，共同构成了支撑其核心论点的坚实基础。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑层次分明。首先，它定位在农业食品研究中的“关系转向”（relational turn）和“组装思维”（assemblage thinking）的学术脉络中，指出现有研究虽强调了超越农场的社会物质关系，却忽视了农民自身的“审慎组装”工作。其次，为了填补这一理论空白，文章引入了后行动者网络理论（post-ANT）中源自John Law等人“关怀的编排”（choreography of care）思想的“修补”概念，并将其操作化为三个分析维度：维持整合（holding together）、制造分离（separations）和实验（experimentation）。接着，文章将这一理论框架系统地应用于三个国家的案例分析，展示了“修补”在不同制度和市场环境下的具体表现形式。最后，在讨论部分，作者们将三个案例的发现进行综合提炼，归纳出三种关键的“修补”实践，并深入阐述了这些实践如何重新分配农民的能动性，从而有力地回应了研究问题，并深化了对数字农业社会动态的理解。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用了一种严谨的比较案例研究方法。遵循Bartlett和Vavrus（2017）的思路，作者们采取了一种“同源横向案例比较”（homologous horizontal case comparison）策略，即在不同背景下研究相似群体（使用PA的作物农民），以追踪PA这一现象如何被地方化地实践。数据收集主要依赖于在三个国家进行的半结构化访谈，采用目的性抽样（purposive sampling）选取了不同PA使用经验的农民及其他相关行动者（如顾问、研究员）。分析过程始于为每个案例撰写独立的叙事报告，然后通过作者间的集体讨论和比较，识别出跨案例的共同主题和趋势，最终形成了文章的核心发现。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文章通过三个鲜明的国家案例来具体阐释其理论。澳大利亚案例聚焦于稻米种植者，他们通过“改造”（retrofitting）现有机械来规避对单一品牌（如John Deere）的“技术锁定”（technological lock-in），并策略性地“断开”与不可靠的机械经销商的联系，转而严重依赖本地农艺师的“可信赖”建议来“维持整合”PA技术。荷兰案例展示了农民更强的技术自主性，他们不仅改造旧设备，还与本地农技公司合作修改新设备，展现了高度的“实验与试错”精神；但他们在数据解读方面遭遇了传统顾问的知识瓶颈，不得不依赖自身的“农民感觉”。法国案例则凸显了一个“碎片化”的支持系统，农民在购买了由合作社销售的DST软件后，必须自行解决与不同品牌硬件的连接问题，这迫使他们通过组织顾问和经销商的会面来进行协调，体现了为使PA可行而进行的“权衡与妥协”。

2. 将“修补”理论化为审慎组装 (Theorising tinkering as deliberate assembling: holding together, separations, and experimentation)

本章详细阐述了本文的核心理论框架——“修补”（tinkering）。作者将其界定为人类行动者为使新技术在特定情境下可行而进行的审慎工作。借鉴John Law关于“关怀的编排”（choreography of care）的论述，作者将“修补”分解为三个相互关联的维度：1）通过惯例和技艺来“维持整合”（holding together）不同元素；2）创造“分离”（separations）以管理不兼容的实践；3）具有“实验性”（experimental）的试错过程。这个框架为后续的案例分析提供了清晰的分析透镜。

  • 将“修补”与“组装”进行比较，强调“修补”更侧重于人类行动者在特定情境下为使新技术与现有知识和目标相适应而进行的审慎工作。
    Where tinkering builds on these concepts is in its explicit emphasis on the deliberative work of human actors in making new practices or technologies workable in the context of ‘existing knowledge-practices and farming goals’ (Higgins et al., 2017, p. 200).
  • 将“修补”概念化为一种“关怀式”工作，即农民需要将外部的“非本地关怀”（如粮食安全、环境管理、智能技术）与自身的“本地关怀”（现有知识、实践、优先级）相整合。
    Significantly, tinkering is also conceptualised as care-ful work (Mol et al., 2010; Singleton and Law, 2013) in that it is oriented towards farmers engaging with practices and priorities beyond what they care for intrinsically or immediately, also what have been called ‘non-local cares’.
  • 引用Krzywoszynska (2016)的观点，指出“修补”是使非本地实践变得“更可接受和有效”的核心。
    In this sense, tinkering is central in enabling farmers to make non-local practices, techniques, or technologies ‘more acceptable and effective’ (Krzywoszynska, 2016, p. 305).
  • 引入John Law (2010)的“关怀的编排”概念，作为系统化理解“修补”工作的理论工具。
    We argue that Law’s work on ’care as choreography’ is particularly useful for agri-food scholars in conceptualising the tinkering work ‘that allows situated action’ (Law, 2010, p. 67) on the part of farmers...
  • 阐述“修补”的第一个维度：“维持整合”。这依赖于惯例、模式化实践以及基于情境的专业知识和“技艺”。
    First, tinkering involves the use of routines (Law, 2010) or patterned practices (Singleton and Law, 2013) for holding together different elements and objects... These practices rest on situated expertise (Krzywoszynska, 2016; Legun et al., 2022) and forms of skilled craftwork...
  • 指出“维持整合”过程中可能出现的张力，即外部实践可能与本地实践发生冲突，或导致农民的经验知识被“殖民化”或“隐形化”。
    Such wider, non-local, practices may produce ways of knowing that collide with or rub alongside local practices and ways of working (Gill et al., 2017), or they may not recognise local practices as being important at all (Higgins et al., 2018). ...farmers may find their experiential knowledge and practical skills colonised, punctuated, disembodied, and rendered invisible (Singleton, 2010, p. 250).
  • 阐述“修补”的第二个维度：“创造分离”。农民需要通过“断开连接的仪式”来管理不兼容的元素，将某些事物排除在农场实践之外。
    Second, tinkering also involves creating distance and separations between local farming practices and wider practices... These ‘disconnection rituals’ (Singleton and Law, 2013, p. 269) shape what is to be kept outside of or away from their on-farm practices.
  • 引用Higgins et al. (2018)的研究，以澳大利亚肉牛养殖者的生物安全实践为例，说明农民如何通过“流体工程”创造物理和空间上的分离。
    For example, Higgins et al. (2018) draw on the concept of ‘fluid engineering’ to identify two key practices used by Australian beef producers to establish distance...
  • 引用Singleton and Law (2013)的观点，指出某些分离实践也可以被理解为一种“实践抵抗”，通过创造和关注“他者性”来实现。
    ...Singleton and Law (2013, p. 272) argue that some separation practices may also be understood as a form of practical resistance to wider practices by enacting forms of otherness within, and ‘attending to that otherness without attempting to capture it’ (emphasis in original).
  • 阐述“修补”的第三个维度：“实验性”。整合与分离的惯例本身就是一种试错过程，涉及创造新的实践来处理张力。
    Third, routines for holding together and separating on-farm practices and wider practices are experimental in character... they are themselves a form of trial and error, involving the creation of new practices for separating and handling tensions between different subjectivities and objectivities’ (Law, 2010, p. 68).
  • 引用Mol et al. (2010)的观点，强调“修补”涉及人与工具之间的相互适应。
    This reflects the observation by Mol et al. (2010, p. 15) that tinkering involves ‘people willing to adapt their tools to a specific situation while adapting the situation to the tools’.
  • 引用Higgins et al. (2017)的研究，以澳大利亚稻米产业的PA实施为例，说明农民如何通过改造技术和使用他人设备来“修补”。
    Higgins et al. (2017) apply this approach to tinkering in their study of PA implementation by growers in the Australian rice industry...these forms of tinkering are crucial for growers in negotiating and working around wider modes of ordering ‘that they see as constraining their capacities to implement new technology’. (Higgins et al., 2017, p. 200).

3. 方法论 (Methodological approach)

本章详细介绍了研究方法。作者采用了Bartlett和Vavrus（2017）的比较案例研究方法，旨在追踪精准农业（PA）这一现象在不同国家背景下的具体实践。这种方法强调对过程和关系的关注，允许在研究过程中涌现新的发现。研究涉及澳大利亚、荷兰和法国三个案例，主要通过对农民进行半结构化访谈来收集数据。通过对各案例进行独立叙事分析，然后进行跨案例比较，识别出共性和差异，从而构建出关于农民如何使PA可行的整体论述。

  • 采用Bartlett和Vavrus（2017）的比较案例研究方法，以理解农民如何使PA可行。
    We employ a comparative case study approach (Bartlett and Vavrus, 2017) in order to understand how farmers make PA workable and how they implement PA technologies on their farm.
  • 指出这种方法与农业食品研究中的“过程-关系转向”相契合，允许追踪现象并保持对案例中新出现的现实持开放态度。
    This way of doing a comparative case study fits well with the process-relational turn in agri-food studies (Darnhofer, 2020)... This takes different forms across the cases, which shows the attention to particularities of local contexts, but also shows similarities across the cases on the use of PA... (Bartlett and Vavrus, 2017, p. 10).
  • 明确研究设计为“同源横向案例比较”，即在不同背景下研究具有相应特征的相似群体。
    This form of analysis following Bartlett and Vavrus’s (2017, p. 52) classification can be seen as a homologous horizontal case comparison (taking similar groups as cases in dissimilar contexts that share corresponding features).
  • 概述了三个案例的具体情况：澳大利亚（水稻种植者，多种PA技术）、荷兰（作物农民，多种PA技术，直至“农业4.0”）、法国（小麦/油菜籽种植者，专注于一种决策支持工具DST）。
    The Australian data...The Dutch data...Finally, the French data...
  • 解释了分析过程：首先为每个案例撰写简短的叙事报告，然后通过作者间的讨论和比较，识别出关键主题和趋势。

4.1 澳大利亚案例 (Australia: the role of local agronomists in holding together PA with grower knowledge and practices)

本章分析了澳大利亚水稻种植者使PA可行的两种主要实践：标准化机械和改造现有机械。核心发现是，由于对单一品牌（如John Deere）的技术锁定和糟糕的售后服务感到担忧，大多数农民选择“改造”（retrofitting）。在这一过程中，由于机械经销商支持不足，农民严重依赖他们信任的本地独立农艺师。这些农艺师虽然不一定是PA技术专家，但他们的独立建议被视为整合新技术与现有农场实践的关键。因此，农民通过依赖农艺师来“维持整合”，同时策略性地“断开”与不可靠商业支持的联系。

  • 少数农民采用“标准化”策略，即统一使用单一品牌的机械，以简化操作和确保兼容性。
    It’s less complicated [using one company’s system]. It’s compatible. Like you can take it out of the tractor and put it in the header, and then take it out of that tractor and put it in another one, and it’s just all you have to do is plug it in. (CIA7)
  • 大多数农民对标准化持谨慎态度，主要担忧是技术锁定风险和对单一公司的过度依赖，这会损害他们的自主性和灵活性。
    John Deere like to keep everything in-house so they won’t share, they don’t share …. Because we’re dealing with electronics and codes and things like that, it’s so easy to lock the technology up so you’re captive to their product’ (MIA8).
  • 对机械公司售后服务的不良体验加剧了农民的担忧，他们认为在关键时刻得不到及时有效的技术支持。
    Very rarely do [technicians] come out and fix the problem... We are getting to the stage where we cannot work without it, and that’s a big problem. (MIA10)
  • 作为替代方案，大多数农民选择“改造”现有机械，即在旧设备上安装或修改PA技术，这被认为成本更低、更灵活。
    As a consequence of poor service and concerns about trade-offs, most growers opted to instead retrofit PA software on existing machinery.
  • 农民对成功改造设备感到自豪，认为这体现了他们的适应能力和技术手艺。
    I’ve got a little tractor which has got the steering wheel and an early screen, I take it out of that and put in my 2000 year model Case header, so I’ve got John Deere GPS in the Case header, it works alright. It’s just a matter of adapting things. (MIA7)
  • 由于缺乏来自机械经销商的可靠支持，农民在实施PA时严重依赖现有的独立支持网络，主要是顾问和农艺师。
    The lack of support from machinery dealers and companies meant that growers tended to rely heavily on existing independent support networks, primarily advisors and agronomists, in making PA workable.
  • 尽管农民承认农艺师可能缺乏专业的PA工程知识，但他们最看重的是独立于商业利益的可信赖建议。
    ‘They’re not necessarily up to speed on the engineering side of things...’ (MVIA18); and, ‘their business is weeds and chemicals and fertilisers’ (MVIA21) rather than PA. Nevertheless, it was the trusted independent advice separate to the commercial interests of transnational machinery companies that was most valued...
  • 农艺师的角色是与农民现有的技术合作，而非推销升级，这种支持方式受到农民的高度评价。
    He’ll come out and he might suggest what technology you’ve already implemented to use it while you’re there, but he’s not trying to get you to upgrade or do anything to improve your technology. He’ll work with what you’ve got. (CIA4)

4.2 荷兰案例 (The Netherlands: disconnections between farm advisors and farmers in the interpretation of PA data)

本章探讨了荷兰作物农民使PA可行的三种实践：标准化、改造和利用外部支持。与澳大利亚相似，多数农民因成本和灵活性问题而拒绝标准化。他们更倾向于“改造”旧设备和“修改”新设备，并在此过程中与机械经销商和本地农技公司（如DAKON）紧密合作，展现了高度的实验性和能动性。然而，一个显著的“断裂”出现在数据解读环节：农民普遍认为传统顾问在解读PA数据方面“知识水平糟糕”，导致他们不得不依赖自己的“农民感觉”来做决策。这揭示了硬件支持与农艺解读支持之间的严重脱节。

  • 少数农民选择标准化，以解决兼容性问题，尽管这限制了他们购买设备时的灵活性。
    Well, the simple thing is, I am a John Deere man... I would constantly be playing catch-up because you have two systems running at the same time. That wouldn’t work. (CF9)
  • 许多农民强调简便性（如“三击法则”），认为PA软件应该像消费级应用一样易于使用。
    The three-clicks-rule is a rule that my employees have implemented. If it’s more than three clicks [to use a piece of software] they won’t use it. (CF2)
  • 大多数农民积极参与改造旧设备和修改新设备，显示出强烈的自己动手（DIY）精神和技术自信。
    [CF14] Well I modified them quite heavily actually. That might be my own stubborn side, because I believe I should be able to do it myself. So I built them myself, all the auto steering I built myself...
  • 农民通过网络搜索和现有知识来完成复杂的改造工作，表明了高度的自学和实验能力。
    [CF14] Well, it is mainly looking it up on the internet, through Google. There is always someone who has done it before. […] And a lot of the variable rate applications, well it’s just all electric motors that drive it and you can easily vary their speeds. (CF14)
  • 本地化的国家级农技公司（如DAKON）因其能快速响应荷兰农业的具体挑战而被农民高度评价，优于大型跨国公司（如Trimble）。
    Trimble is American, a fairly decent program. But well, you can only ask so much as a Dutch crop farmer. And then there are companies like DAKON, which are very active on the Dutch market, and they see our challenges and can switch much faster. (CF4)
  • 在数据解读方面，农民与顾问之间存在巨大鸿沟。农民直言顾问的知识水平“糟糕”。
    [CF5] Their knowledge level? […] Terrible. Yes. They might know how to make a variable rate map, but they haven’t got a clue of how to use it.
  • 由于缺乏有效的农艺解读支持，农民最终只能依靠自己的经验和“农民感觉”来决策。
    You have soil scans, organic matter, and you want to do something, but which algorithms should you use? And then I often do it based on my own farmers’ feeling. But it’s not very scientific then. (CF14)

4.3 法国案例 (France: integrating different sources of support to fill gaps in local advisory services and make PA workable)

本章聚焦于法国农民使用决策支持工具（DST）进行施肥优化的实践。这里的核心挑战源于一个“碎片化”的市场结构：农民从他们信任的本地咨询机构购买DST软件，但必须自行将其与现有硬件连接。这迫使农民进行一种特殊的“改造”，即解决软硬件兼容性问题。由于传统顾问缺乏必要的技术能力，农民不得不转向机械经销商寻求硬件支持。这种顾问与经销商之间缺乏协调的状况，要求农民主动进行“修补”，甚至需要亲自组织会议来解决问题。在数据解读上，农民的行动则呈现多样性：一些人基于对顾问的信任而严格遵循建议，另一些人则利用农民讨论小组的集体智慧来调整建议，显示了不同支持网络如何塑造PA的最终实践。

  • 与前两个案例不同，法国农民的“改造”主要是为了连接从不同来源购买的软件和硬件，而非出于避免技术锁定的策略。
    Contrary to the Australian and Dutch case studies, retrofitting does not seem to emerge from explicit strategies of farmers. Instead, it results from the structure of the DSTs’ market.
  • 农民需要具备一定的电子和计算机技能才能成功连接设备，这是一种技术门槛。
    Oh, it takes a little bit … how shall I put it: you have to have some electronics and computer skills, to make it easy to implement. (F25)
  • 农民报告了连接过程中的诸多困难，例如旧机器无法读取推荐图，导致应用精度下降。
    We make an average dose [of what the DST software recommends]... we make it only manually. (F10)
  • 一个核心问题是，提供软件的本地咨询机构往往缺乏解决硬件连接问题的能力，导致农民感到失望。
    F8: Yes, absolutely. That was what was essential for me. And the lack here is what made me stop [buying] the [DST] service... I thought he was going to find me solutions. But in the end he didn’t. He didn’t know!
  • 因此，农民被迫依赖专门销售硬件的机械经销商来解决连接问题。
    We had the help of the machinery dealer to make the spreader work. (F27)
  • 最能体现“修补”工作的是，农民需要主动协调互不沟通的顾问和经销商，以解决技术问题。
    Finally we solved the problem at [name of local fair] where I had gathered myself [name of the advisor from the Chamber of Agriculture], the manufacturer of the console and the manufacturer of the tractor distributor. (F8)
  • 在数据解读方面，与顾问有牢固信任关系的农民倾向于严格执行软件建议。
    F10: It was a lot with the technician, yes we are almost buddies, we get along very well so I trusted him on the system... ‘We put the USB key [on the machine], it’s the USB key that does everything’ (F10).
  • 而参与农民讨论小组的农民则会利用同行的经验来调整施肥剂量，展现了集体知识在“修补”过程中的作用。
    We have the advantage of being [in a local group] where we are about fifteen farmers... we discuss everything that works for us, what doesn’t work, and we pool our efforts. (F23)

5. 讨论 (Discussion)

讨论部分系统地综合了三个案例的发现，并将其与现有文献对话，以回应研究问题。作者首先强调，“修补”的核心是“维持整合”农民的“本地关怀”（如经验知识、信任、自主性）与PA技术所蕴含的“非本地关怀”（如对商业建议的依赖、数据驱动知识）。其次，文章识别出三种关键的“修补”实践来分配农民的能动性：1）“断开连接”（如澳大利亚农民与经销商），2）“实验与试错”（如荷兰农民改造设备和法国农民协调支持），3）“权衡与妥协”（如为简化操作而接受技术锁定，或在支持不足时依赖自身经验）。最后，作者认为，像“改造”这样的实践具有潜力，可以为农民创造“另类呼吸空间”，构成一种对PA技术殖民化趋势的“实践抵抗”。

  • 维持整合的努力与挑战: 农民的“修补”工作旨在将他们的本地关怀（如经验知识、信任和自主性）与PA技术中固有的非本地关怀（商业依赖、数据知识）结合起来。
    ...much of the tinkering work performed by farmers is aimed at holding together what they value – e.g., local cares such as experiential farming knowledge (Higgins et al., 2017; Ogunyiola and Gardezi, 2022), trust (Gardezi and Stock, 2021; Rijswijk et al., 2023), and farmer autonomy (Forney and Epiney, 2022) – with non-local cares inscribed in PA technology.
  • 对支持网络的复杂依赖: 尽管支持网络对PA可行性至关重要，但本研究揭示了这些关系的复杂性和矛盾性，深化了关于顾问在数字化转型中角色的新兴文献。
    Previous research highlights the importance of support networks... (Eastwood et al., 2017, p. 2)... our results also reveal that these relationships are more complicated than what is documented in the existing research... this holding together is often partial and is also the result of compromises and trade-offs in accessing appropriate support...
  • 能动性的三种分配实践：断开连接: 澳大利亚案例显示，农民通过与被认为无益的行动者（机械经销商）“断开连接”来行使能动性，但这可能反过来增加对其他（技术上不专业的）行动者的依赖。
    Evident in our Australian case study is the disconnecting (Law, 2010; Singleton and Law, 2013) from those actors who are judged by farmers as making limited or poor contributions in making PA workable for them...
  • 能动性的三种分配实践：实验与试错: 荷兰和法国的案例表明，农民通过“实验”来弥补支持系统的不足，这既可以是一种创造性的策略（如组织会议），也可能是在别无选择下的无奈之举（如依赖自身经验）。
    Our French and Dutch case studies highlight how farmers turn to forms of experimentation or trial and error (Law, 2010) for addressing gaps in support.
  • 能动性的三种分配实践：权衡与妥协: 所有案例都显示出农民在“权衡与妥协”中组装PA，这是一个在现有组装研究中被忽视的重要维度。例如，为了简便而接受技术锁定，或在顾问专业知识有限的情况下继续依赖他们。
    All our case studies point to the significance of trade-offs and compromises by farmers in assembling PA. This is something that is little recognised in existing agri-food research using assemblage thinking.
  • 改造作为“实践抵抗”: 跨案例的“改造”和“修改”实践，展示了农民技艺在审慎组装PA中的重要性。这些实践可能构成了Singleton和Law（2013）所说的“另类呼吸空间”或“实践抵抗”，使农民能够与PA技术的殖民化现实保持一定距离。
    Indeed, these practices seem to provide what Singleton and Law (2013, p. 272) call ‘alternative breathing spaces’ or a form of ‘practical resistance’ for farmers that enable a degree of separation from the otherwise colonising realities but also inaccuracies of PA technology (Brooks, 2021; Carolan, 2020a; Clapp and Ruder, 2020; Stock and Gardezi, 2021; Visser et al., 2021).

6. 结论 (Conclusion)

结论部分重申了本文的核心贡献。作者认为，本文通过引入“修补”（tinkering）的理论视角，成功地深化了对农民在精准农业（PA）中“审慎组装”实践的理解。研究表明，农民并非被动地被技术决定，而是通过技艺和实践判断，在复杂的支持网络中积极行动。然而，他们的能动性是高度情境化和关系性的，通过“断开连接”、“实验”和“权衡”等实践，既可能被增强，也可能受限。最终，文章主张“修补”是一个有价值的分析工具，能帮助农业食品学者更深入地揭示农民的审慎组装实践，以及这些实践如何在与其他关系和行动者的互动中变得可行。

  • 本文通过考察农民的“修补”形式及其对能动性的影响，为组装方法在农业食品研究中的应用做出了贡献。
    This paper contributes to the application of assemblage approaches in agri-food studies, and research on PA specifically, by investigating the forms of tinkering used by farmers to assemble PA...and what these engender for farmer agency.
  • 农民的“修补”实践（如技艺和实践判断）表明他们是积极的行动者，而非被动的技术接受者。
    Our analysis highlights the significance of practices of skilled craftwork and practical judgement in managing PA implementation in the context of localised farming routines, knowledge, and experiences.
  • 农民的能动性是偶然的，取决于他们如何驾驭各种支持网络，这些网络既是机遇也是挑战。
    At the same time, our research points to how that agency is contingent on the complex ways in which farmers navigate various support networks.
  • 总结了农民的“修补”实践——断开连接、实验和权衡——如何既能开辟也能限制他们的选择。
    This gives rise to a range of tinkering practices – disconnecting, experimentation, and trade-offs – that can both open-up or foreclose options for farmers in making PA workable.
  • 最终论点：“修补”是一个概念上连贯且有价值的方法，能够揭示农民审慎组装实践的细微之处以及其能动性的关系性本质。
    As such, we conclude that tinkering provides a valuable approach for enabling agrifood scholars to tease out in greater depth farmers’ deliberative assembling practices and how these are made workable in the context of other relations and actants.
