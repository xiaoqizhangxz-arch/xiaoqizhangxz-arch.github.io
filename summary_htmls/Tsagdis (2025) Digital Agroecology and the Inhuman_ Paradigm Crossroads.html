Digital Agroecology and the Inhuman: Paradigm Crossroads

Georgios Tsagdis

全文概要总结

作为一名长期关注技术哲学与环境人文学科交叉领域的学者，我认为本文对“数字生态农业”（Digital Agroecology）的分析极具洞察力。作者Georgios Tsagdis并未停留在对生态农业与“农业现代主义”（Agromodernism）之间表层对立的描述，而是深入其哲学根基，揭示了两者共享的一个更深层次的、通常被忽视的本体论前提。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：尽管生态农业与农业现代主义在话语框架（discursive frames）和心态（mindsets）上看似截然对立，但它们都受制于现代技术科学的“世界图像”（world-picture）。在这个图像中，世界被视为一个可供人类主体无限建构和操控的对象。因此，为了避免生态农业被农业现代主义同化，作者倡导一种另类的、“非人的”（inhuman）生态农业。这种生态农业应欢迎那些能够抵抗对土地进行全面控制的、总体化倾向的技术，并优先考虑超越人类价值观和利益的存在。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据主要建立在两个层面。首先，在社会学层面，他通过对联合国粮食及农业组织（FAO）等国际机构政策辩论的“框架分析”（frame analysis），清晰地展示了生态农业与农业现代主义在权利、知识论证据和技术观三个子框架上的具体分歧。其次，在哲学层面，他征引了马丁·海德格尔（Martin Heidegger）、让-弗朗索瓦·利奥塔（Jean-François Lyotard）和弗雷德里克·奈拉（Frédéric Neyrat）的核心理论。海德格尔的“世界图像”理论被用来揭示两大范式共享的现代性基础；利奥塔的“非人”概念被用来区分两种“非人”——一种是技术发展带来的非人化，另一种是抵制这种发展的、作为想象力来源的“非人”；奈拉的“不可建构之物”（unconstructible）则为构建一种新的生态政治学提供了理论出口。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑层次分明。首先，它通过框架分析，将生态农业与农业现代主义的对立从模糊的印象具体化为可分析的话语结构，确立了两者在表面上的不可调和性。接着，文章巧妙地转换视角，从“外部”的框架分析转向“内部”的心态分析，探讨了采纳生态农业心态的主观因素。在充分铺陈了二者的差异之后，文章引入了海德格尔等哲学家的思想，进行了一次深刻的哲学“釜底抽薪”，指出这些差异最终都统一在现代技术科学的“座架”（enframing）之下。最后，基于这一哲学诊断，文章提出了“非人”生态农业作为一种超越现有二元对立的、更具革命性的未来路径，从而完成了从批判到建构的整个论证闭环。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文的方法论是跨学科的典范。它始于社会科学的**话语/框架分析**（Discourse/Frame Analysis），系统地梳理了现实世界中的政策话语和意识形态冲突。随后，无缝过渡到**现象学与后结构主义的哲学批判**（Phenomenological and Post-structuralist Philosophical Critique）。这种方法的精妙之处在于，它没有将社会学分析和哲学思辨割裂开来，而是用前者揭示“是什么”（what is the conflict），用后者追问“为什么会这样”（why is it so），最终导向“可以是什么”（what it could be）的规范性构想。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

核心案例是关于将尖端数字技术融入生态农业的当代趋势，这一趋势得到了联合国和欧盟等超国家行为体的推动。具体的分析场景是联合国世界粮食安全委员会（CFS）高级别专家小组（HLPE）报告的咨询过程，这一过程生动地展现了生态农业和农业现代主义话语框架的激烈碰撞。在文章结尾，作者引用了福冈正信（Masanobu Fukuoka）的“无为农法”（"do-nothing" agriculture）作为“非人”生态农业的一个实践范例，这种农法强调人的退让与审慎，让自然本身发挥主导作用。

Introduction

本章引出核心议题：在应对人口增长和生态系统压力下，由数字技术驱动的“第四次农业革命”正在兴起。联合国等机构提倡将数字技术与生态农业相结合，但作者质疑这种结合在根本的范式层面上是否可行。文章旨在探讨生态农业与“农业现代主义”的差异，并进一步追问它们是否共享同一个技术科学的“世界图像”，最终为一种另类的“非人”生态农业铺路。

Agroecology: Scope and Evolution

本章界定了生态农业的内涵和外延，指出其多样性和不断演变的特性。它不仅仅是一套技术，更是一门科学、一种实践和一个社会运动。作者强调了生态农业面临的核心挑战：一方面，它因其对可持续性和社会公正的承诺而逐渐进入主流；另一方面，这种主流化也带来了被主导市场的“创新命令”所收编的巨大风险，这反映了第三次农业革命的负面历史教训。

  • 指出生态农业概念的多元性，被不同价值观和意图的行动者不断重塑。
    ...there are many “agroecologies” constantly re-signified and co-produced, from actors with different values, intentions and worldviews (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 2; Loconto & Fouilleux, 2019; Méndez et al., 2012; Rivera-Ferre, 2018).
  • 引用联合国高级别专家小组（HLPE）制定的十三个生态农业原则作为其核心内容的里程碑。
    A milestone was set when the UN’s HighLevel Panel of Experts (HLPE) for Food Security and Nutrition encapsulated the agroecological approach in thirteen principles... (HLPE, 2019).
  • 提及生态农业原则的持续发展，以涵盖更多实践和文化维度。
    Since then, additional principles have been proposed in literature, aiming to incorporate aspects such as specific practices, sociocultural dimensions and farming systems (Wezel et al., 2020).
  • 举例说明了新提出的原则，如培养生态农业心态。
    One such example... is the addition of the principle of fostering agroecological mindsets (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023).
  • 将生态农业定义为一个跨学科方法，融合了科学、实践和社会运动。
    Agroecology can be moreover understood as a “transdisciplinary approach that embraces science, a set of practices and a social movement” (Wezel et al., 2020).
  • 强调了“养活世界”的叙事被用来支持工业化农业，而贬低生态农业。
    Agroecology has been routinely dismissed as unrealistic and unviable, with the “feed the world” narrative used to privilege industrial agriculture over ecological production of lower yields. (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 2; cf. Fouilleux et al., 2017, Bellwood-Howard & Ripoll, 2020).
  • 指出生态农业进入主流后面临被市场和政策主导者收编的风险。
    ...this development invites fears of co-optation by dominant market and policy agents threatening to rob the movement of its transformative potential (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 2; cf. Levidow et al., 2014; Laforge et al., 2016).
  • 引用熊彼特的创新定义，指出创新是现代发展范式的核心。
    Thematised by Joseph Schumpeter as “doing things differently” (2005, p. 84), innovation has always been integral to the modern developmental paradigm.
  • 提及OECD的“创新命令”，这是一种不创新即灭亡的最后通牒。
    In recent years, its significance has been encapsulated in OECD’s “innovation imperative” (2015), virtually an ultimatum to innovate or perish (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 3).
  • 描述第三次农业革命的负面后果：尽管农场规模和技术力量大增，但农民收入反而下降。
    ...the reduced numbers of mechanised, large-scale farmers that survived to reap the benefits, earned less than their great-grandparents when adjusting for inflation (Shepard, 2013).
  • 批判第四次农业革命中对技术社会和生态效益的宣称常常缺乏证据。
    Critique points out that claims of social and ecological benefits of technologies are often unsubstantiated (Miles, 2019)...
  • 指出新技术对劳动力和农业社区的负面影响常被淡化或忽略。
    ...while negative impacts, including impacts on labour and agricultural communities are downplayed or altogether ignored (Barrett & Rose, 2021).
  • 总结认为，对许多人来说，创新与农业现代主义的“生产主义和积累逻辑”紧密相连，因此与生态农业不相容。
    Nonetheless, innovation remains for many attached to an agromodernist “logic of productivism and accumulation” and thus incompatible with agroecology (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 3).

Frames and Sub-Frames: Agroecology and Agromodernism

本章运用框架分析理论，深入剖析了在联合国世界粮食安全委员会（CFS）的政策辩论中，生态农业与农业现代主义两大话语框架的对立。这种对立具体体现在三个子框架上：1）权利：农业现代主义强调市场权利（选择产品的权利、知识产权），而生态农业强调更基本的人权和粮食主权。2）证据：农业现代主义依赖可量化的经济指标（GDP、农场收入），而生态农业呼吁更全面的、包含社会和生态福祉的整体考量。3）技术：农业现代主义将创新等同于技术进步，而生态农业则强调社会创新的重要性，视农民为知识创新的主体。

  • 引用HLPE (2019) 的报告作为分析农业政策中话语竞争的案例。
    An exemplary contestation of agroecology and agromodernism over agricultural policy took place during the consultation process initiated by HLPE in the Committee on World Food Security (CFS)... which ultimately led to the evocatively titled report “Agroecology and Other Innovations” (HLPE, 2019).
  • 采用框架分析理论来理解不同政治意识形态议程。
    To examine this discursive contestation, frame analysis (Snow & Benford, 1992; Steinberg, 1998) provides invaluable insight into the political-ideological agendas that informed the discussion...
  • 描述了农业现代主义与生态农业在权利子框架上的对立，前者关注市场权利，后者关注人权与粮食主权。
    To these “market” rights, agroecology opposes more fundamental human rights, foregrounding the rights of the most affected and striving for food sovereignty (Loconto & Fouilleux, 2019).
  • 指出HLPE最终报告采纳了生态农业的观点，将人权作为可持续粮食系统的基础。
    This was reflected in HLPE’s final report, which after the consultation round promoted human rights into the “basis for ensuring sustainable food systems” (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 11).
  • 强调生态农业在社会和生态层面与智能农业、可持续集约化等农业现代主义运动的区别。
    The above makes clear that socially, as much as ecologically, agroecology is distinct from agromodernist movements such as smart agriculture and sustainable intensification (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 12; Pimbert, 2017).
  • 揭示在证据子框架下，生态农业因缺乏社会、文化等方面的量化指标而受到批评。
    ...agroecology is commonly critiqued as failing to provide measurements for social, cultural, political and ecological outcomes (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 6).
  • 指出即便承认农民福祉的重要性，缺乏量化指标也导致这些考量被边缘化。
    Even when the significance of aspects such as the farmers’ well-being is acknowledged, the absence of corresponding quantifiable indicators is used to sideline such considerations (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 7).
  • 描述农业现代主义将农民视为“企业主”，而生态农业将其视为“生态管家、社区成员和知识生产者”。
    ...opponents of agroecology prioritised the measurable indicators of GDP and farm income, considering farmers primarily as “business owners,” rather than “ecological stewards, community members, and knowledge producers” (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 7).
  • 展现生态农业倡导者对指标作用的批判性反思，呼吁关注心理健康、营养价值等更全面的因素。
    In contrast, proponents of agroecology called for critical reflection on the role indicators play in informing policy over more holistic considerations, such as mental health, nutritional value, biodiversity and resilience (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 7).
  • 阐明在技术子框架下，农业现代主义将创新等同于技术进步，并认为技术决定社会变革。
    For agromodernism, innovation is nearly synonymous with technological progress, which conditions in turn social change (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 8).
  • 对比生态农业的观点，即创新与技术脱钩，农业创新是与社会生态持续对话数千年的过程。
    For agroecology, in contrast, innovation is decoupled from technology, as agricultural innovation has been at work for thousands of years, in an ongoing dialogue with social and ecological developments (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 9).
  • 举例说明生态农业的立场常被歪曲，例如被“康奈尔科学联盟”诋毁为反科学和反理性。
    A straw man is often made of agroecology’s reserve towards technology, as when, for example, the “Cornell Alliance for Science,”... denigrated agroecology as hostile to science and rationality... (Conrow, 2020).
  • 指出农业现代主义如何利用生态农业的价值观（如适应当地条件）来推广其自身的技术（如基因改造）。
    To that effect, genetic modification, fiercely rejected by agroecology, was asserted as a conducive path towards agroecological goals (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, p. 8).
  • 揭示生态农业的反驳：农业现代主义将创新与道德责任等同，是为了推广自上而下、利润驱动的技术。
    Agroecology responds that the persistent equivocation of the innovation imperative with a moral imperative is sustained by the agromodernist discourse in order to promote top-down, profit-driven technologies...
  • 强调生态农业将社会创新置于技术创新之上，视农民为知识创新的核心，并主张创新目标和方式应由集体决定。
    In contrast, agroecology considers social innovation as potentially more important than technological innovation, foregrounding farmers as knowledge innovators... (Anderson & Maughan, 2021, pp. 9–10).

Viewing the Frame: The Agroecological Mind

本章将分析从客观的话语框架转向主观的“心态”（mindset）。作者探讨了个体采纳生态农业世界观背后的深层价值观、信念和情感倾向。他识别出五个核心因素：与自然的连接、地方感、身份认同、自我/社会效能感以及自豪感等“溢出效应”。作者认为培养这种心态是实现从“经济人”到“生态人”转变的关键。然而，他敏锐地指出，即便是这种崇高的目标，依然将人类置于中心位置——通过管理我们自身来服务于自然，这暗示了其与农业现代主义共享的、以人为中心的建构主义根源。

  • 采用“心态”一词来指代驱动个人决策和行动的规范性价值观、信念、动机和态度。
    In their research, Eija Soini Coe and Richard Coe adopt the term “mindset” to refer to the normative values, beliefs, motivations, and attitudes that underlie a person’s decisions and actions (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, p. 2).
  • 将心态定义为塑造我们理解世界方式的深层、假定性思维模式。
    They define mindsets as “deep, assumed patterns of thinking that shape how we make sense of the world” (FrameWork Institute, 2020)...
  • 引入“合理行为模型”，该模型包含四类影响因素：动机性、社会性、情境性和认知资源。
    ...the model of justified behaviour entails four types of factors exerting influence: i. motivational..., ii. social..., iii. contextual... and finally iv. epistemic, as well as other resources... (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, p. 3).
  • 阐述心态转变的可能触发因素：深刻的积极体验、危机，或经验的累积。
    ...a mindset transformation can destabilise “worldviews and frames of reference,” which may be precipitated by: “a deeply touching positive experience...; a crisis...; a cumulation of experience...” (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, 4).
  • 提出第一个核心因素“与自然的连接”，这是一种感知到自我与自然相互关联的特质，与将自然视为资源的农业现代主义心态对立。
    Connecting with nature creates and enforces “symbiotic cognitive, affective, and experiential traits that reflect... a sustained awareness of the interrelatedness between one’s self and the rest of nature” (Zylstra et al., 2014).
  • 强调同理心和同情心在塑造对其他生命形式的关怀倾向中的作用。
    ...empathy—adopting nonhuman perspectives—and compassion—instinctively helping nonhumans—fashion an affective disposition of care towards other life forms and ecosystems (Jax et al., 2018).
  • 提出第二个因素“地方感”，它能激发对地方的守护，但也可能服务于农业现代主义的进步观。
    A sense of place, especially when strong enough to inform one’s identity, is likely to make one attentive to signs of degradation (Chapin et al., 2012) and thus motivate stewardship of a place.
  • 提出第三个因素“身份认同”，即与环保或生态农业群体的认同能强化相应的价值观和行为。
    Identification with environmentalist or agroecological groups... reinforces corresponding values and behaviours (Fritsche et al., 2018).
  • 提出第四和第五个因素“自我效能感”和“溢出效应”（如自豪感），这些因素可以巩固生态农业精神。
    Even small achievements... can have the “spillover effect” of enhancing a sense of self-efficacy (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, p. 6)... affects such as pride and satisfaction in doing “the right thing,” can have spillover effects, consolidating an agroecological ethos.
  • 指出这些情感也可能因确认已做出足够贡献而阻碍进一步行动。
    However, such affects may also thwart further action if taken as confirmation of sufficient contribution to the problem (Meijers et al., 2013).
  • 提及一个反对观点，即重要的不是心态而是“双赢”实践的采纳。
    It may thus be objected that what matters is not mindsets, but the adoption of practices (Foguesatto et al., 2019, p. 405).
  • 建议将“培养生态农业心态”作为第十四条生态农业原则。
    To that effect, Soini Coe and Coe propose the addition of fostering such mindsets to the aforementioned thirteen widely accepted agroecological principles (Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, p. 7).
  • 描述培养心态的目标：将“经济人”转变为从自然中获得同情、尊重和灵感的“生态人”。
    What they aspire to... is the transformation of homo economicus into homo ecologicus, an ethically virtuous person that sympathises, respects and draws inspiration from their experience of nature (Becker, 2006).
  • 对“生态人”概念提出最终批判，认为即使在这种高尚的愿景中，自然的福祉也依赖于人类对自身的生产和管理，这暴露了其与农业现代主义共享的建构主义基础。
    However, even in this noble aspiration, the welfare of nature is conditional upon the cultivation, that is the production and management of ourselves, as humans (Massy, 2017; Soini Coe & Coe, 2023, p. 9).

The Technoscientific Operation: Constructing the World and the Human

本章是全文的哲学核心，旨在揭示生态农业和工业农业共同的、未被言明的预设。作者通过三位哲学家的思想来展开论证。首先，借助海德格尔，他指出，现代技术科学将世界转变为一幅可被人类主体表征和操控的“图像”（picture），这使得人类成为万物的尺度。其次，通过利奥塔，他区分了两种“非人”（inhuman）：一种是技术发展所带来的、追求效率而脱离肉身现实的非人化进程；另一种是与肉身、情感和想象力相连的、作为童年特征的“非人”状态。最后，他援引奈拉的“不可建构之物”（unconstructible）概念，认为真正的生态政治挑战在于抵制将世界完全“人化”的建构主义冲动，为那些拒绝被塑造和改造的“非人”维度留出空间。

  • (Heidegger) 论述现代科学旨在驾驭世界的变易，预测未来并追溯过去。
    ...modern science is marked by a constant activity, which does not merely aim to understand what is immutable in the world, but to steer the world’s becoming, predicting the future and retrodicting the past of all that exists (Heidegger, 2002, p. 63).
  • (Heidegger) 指出技术科学的循环操作使其活动本身成为目的。
    Research is thus able to bring beings under an unprecedented level of control and, proving itself time and again successful in its results, gradually turns its incessant activity into an end in itself (Heidegger, 2002, p. 74).
  • (Heidegger) 阐明这一过程的双重结果：世界成为图像，人成为主体。
    For Heidegger the result of this process is twofold: on the one hand, the world... becomes picture, while on the other, the human becomes subject (Heidegger, 2002, p. 69).
  • (Heidegger) 定义世界图像并非对世界的描绘，而是世界作为图像呈现给主体。
    Accordingly, a world-picture is not one among many pictures of the world, but rather the world understood as picture offering itself to a human subject (Heidegger, 2002, p. 67)...
  • (Heidegger) 将世界观（Weltanschauung）解释为主体从其位置对世界图像的看法，这与前文的框架和心态分析相呼应。
    Accordingly, a worldview [Weltanschauung], is the view of the world-picture that a subject enjoys from their position (Heidegger, 2002, p. 70).
  • (Heidegger) 批判价值观是物化过程的无力面具，认为它们无法揭示世界或人的潜能，因为它们本身就是技术科学“座架”（enframing）的产物。
    Hence, Heidegger dismisses values as “the powerless and threadbare mask of the objectification of beings... No one dies for mere values” (Heidegger, 2002, 77).
  • (Lyotard) 将现代性描述为一个通过更可靠、灵活的规制来追求效率的无情过程。
    It is mediation and thus alienation of the regulated elements—living and non-living alike—that enables the coordination of an ever more complex web of modifications and permutations (Lyotard, 1991, p. 6).
  • (Lyotard) 指出这一复杂化过程没有终极目的，但有一个极限：太阳系的熵终。
    Importantly, although this logic has no telos, its self-contained dynamic has a limit: the entropic end of the solar system (Lyotard, 1991, p. 7).
  • (Lyotard) 认为技术科学的终极目标是使思想脱离其物质条件，以克服死亡的极限。
    For Lyotard, “this and this alone is what’s at stake today in technical and scientific research in every field from dietetics [...] to particle physics [...]” (Lyotard, 1991, p. 12).
  • (Lyotard) 对比机器的递归性和人类的符号系统，指出数字技术将人类的复杂性处理能力提升到新的数量级，导致“笛卡尔式现代主义”，即抛弃物质肉身。
    For Lyotard this process leads inexorably to “Cartesian modernism,” the abandonment of life’s material corporeality, as matter becomes ultimately a hindrance to thought, “its inert mass, stupidity” (Lyotard, 1991, p. 38).
  • (Lyotard) 强调具身思想的开放性，它在模糊环境中运作，并与一个构成、维持和决定它的世界纠缠在一起。
    Embodied thought does not transcribe the perceptible world onto an external recording substrate but rather finds itself enmeshed in a world that composes, sustains and determines it (Lyotard, 1991, p. 17).
  • (Lyotard) 引入两种“非人”概念：第一种是儿童时期与肉身情感和想象力紧密相连的“人性”典范；第二种是进入成人“非人”理性空间的通道，一个抽象的信息空间。
    This constitutes a passage from the exemplary “humanity” of childhood, strongly attached to corporeal affect and thus imagination, into the space of “inhuman” reason... (Lyotard, 1991, pp. 3–4).
  • (Lyotard) 批评当前技术科学所产生的只是“发展中的非人性”（inhumanity "of development"），它旨在瓦解而非超越人类存在的条件。
    At the present juncture of terrestrial collapse, however, this dream of technoscientific disembodiment generates and sustains merely an inhumanity “of development” (Lyotard, 1991, p. 2).
  • (Neyrat) 认为现代技术科学通过将自然外部化并将其作为生产要素来“组织自然”。
    This “organisation of nature” by technoscience means that the latter is not merely operating on nature, but rather that nature is working for technoscience (Neyrat, 2021, pp. 3–4).
  • (Neyrat) 提出一个悖论：世界之所以“非人”（inhuman，此处指不宜居），恰恰因为它“太人性”（human, all too human）。我们缺乏的是拒绝被建构的“非人”元素。
    As such, Neyrat’s diagnosis... is that we suffer not due to a deficit, but an excess of humanity; what is deficient is the “inhuman” element, “that which does not ask to be built...” (Neyrat, 2021, p. 9).
  • (Neyrat) 将“非人”定义为“每个人内心深处拒绝被塑造和重塑的东西”，并将其与利奥塔的思想联系起来。
    For while the “nonhuman” refers to all forms of intelligence and life beside the human, the “inhuman” is found “at the core of every human being as what refuses to be shaped and reshaped” (Neyrat, 2021, p. 8).
  • (Neyrat) 认为“非人”是人类内在的一种无为（inoperativity）能力，这种能力让人可以接触到“神秘”，即抵抗知识和占有欲的东西。
    This capacity affords the human access to “mystery,” not what is extraneous to knowledge, but what resists knowledge and our desire to know and possess (Coccia, 2024, p. 37).
  • (Neyrat) 强调人与非人之间的不对称性：人依赖于不依赖于人的非人。
    Rather, the human exists with and depends on the nonhuman which does not depend on it in turn; there is no symmetry between the human and the nonhuman (Neyrat, 2021, p. 5).

Implications: Inhuman Agroecologies

本章将前述的哲学诊断转化为具体的实践和政治意涵。作者主张发展“非人（inhuman）生态农业”，其核心是抵制建构世界的冲动，为“不可建构之物”（the unconstructible）留出空间。这并非反技术，而是要求技术服务于一种新的目的：帮助人类发现和尊重内外在的“非人”维度。具体的实践可以包括“去增长”（de-growth）、“再野化”（re-wilding），以及福冈正信的“无为农法”。这些实践的共同点是强调人的审慎和退让，让自然拥有话语权，从而在人类内部开辟出一个想象和敬畏的空间。

  • 提出未来生态政治的挑战在于“去殖民化自然”，这可能意味着去增长和减速。
    This may support the decolonisation of nature, which for Neyrat consists in de-growth and deceleration (Neyrat, 2021, p. 6)...
  • 论述“再野化”可以有两种动机：一种是基于人类利益的“生态系统服务”，另一种是服务于“非人”的，即承认野化空间中存在的内在价值，并在人类内心开辟想象空间。
    However, rewilding may also be undertaken in the service of the inhuman. This will entail a recognition of the irreducible significance of the living and non-living beings that compose the rewilded space, as well as of the significance of the rewilded space that opens up within the human through such a process—a space of imagination (cf. Monbiot, 2013).
  • 指出当前大多数技术创新旨在提高效率（如减少工作量），而非有益于环境。
    If, for example, the majority of current technological innovations aim at furthering efficiency gains such as workload reduction, rather than benefiting the environment (Gabriel & Gandorfer, 2023, p. 78), agroecology must labour towards creating meaningful practices that are not merely comfortable, convenient and conducive to the human.
  • 引用福冈正信的《一根稻草的革命》作为一种“非人”农业实践的典范，该实践倡导“不可建构”。
    Masanobu Fukuoka’s One-Straw Revolution—one of the most influential agroecological texts avant la lettre—details what an agricultural practice that foregrounds the unconstructible, looks like (Fukuoka, 1978[1975]).
  • 澄清福冈的“无为”并非懒惰，而是培养一种审慎、克制的农业品性，让自然主导。
    The point is not to facilitate idleness or leisure, but to develop an agricultural disposition marked by reserve and circumspection, allowing nature to have the first and last word.
  • 引用著名生态农业学家Stephen R. Gliessman的观点，强调与自然合作而非对抗是生态农业思想的基础。
    “farming with nature rather than against her is a foundational component of how we as agroecologists think” (Gliessman, 2022, p. 163).

Conclusion

结论部分重申了核心论点：生态农业与农业现代主义在深层次上共享着将世界视为可供人类建构的客体的技术科学框架。作者引用利奥塔关于现代性中“目的的丧失”（foreclosure of ends）的诊断——我们拥有越来越多的知识和力量，却不知道为何如此。面对这种状况，关注“非人”（the inhuman）可能成为一条救赎之路。生态农业的未来，尤其是在接纳数字技术时，不应仅仅是为农民提供更多的“知识和力量”，而应利用这些技术来调整农民对“不可建构之物”的关注，促进农场内外“不可建构之物”的繁荣。

  • 引用利奥塔关于现代技术科学“目的丧失”的精辟总结，强调我们知道如何做，却不知道为何而做。
    When you can simulate in vitro the explosion of the sun or the fertilization or gestation of a living creature, you have to decide what you want. And we just don’t know. This foreclosure of ends […] has been dressed up in all sorts of disguises... Today this foreclosure appears naked. More knowledge and power, yes — but why, no (Lyotard, 1991, pp. 53–54).
