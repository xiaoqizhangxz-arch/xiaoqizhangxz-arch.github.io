Understanding agri-food systems as assemblages: Worlds of rice in Indonesia

Angga Dwiartama, Christopher Rosin and Hugh Campbell

全文概要总结

作为一名长期致力于将后人文主义理论（特别是行动者网络理论与新物质主义）应用于农业食品体系研究的学者，我认为本文是一次极具洞察力的理论实践。作者们成功地将安娜玛丽·莫尔（Annemarie Mol）的“复数性”（multiplicity）概念，应用于剖析印度尼西亚稻米这一复杂的研究对象，从而有力地批判了传统政治经济学分析范式中根深蒂固的人类中心主义和本质主义倾向。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，印度尼西亚的稻米农业食品体系不应被视为一个单一、同质的“系统”，而应被理解为一个由多个共存的“世界”或“现实”构成的“集结体”（assemblage）。这些不同的现实是通过特定实践，在人类与非人类行动者（尤其是稻米本身）的互动中被“生成”（enacted）的。作者挑战那种试图寻找唯一最优解的政策思路，主张稻米自身具有物质能动性（material agency），其不同亚种（如indica与javanica）的特性深刻地塑造了与之相关的经济、政治和文化实践。因此，一项更具适应性和韧性的政策议程，应当从“控制”非人类转向“与之共事”（working with），即承认并培育由稻米复数性所催生的实践多样性。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据建立在对印尼稻米三个不同“世界”的细致描绘上：

  1. 经济商品世界：由高产的indica亚种所主导，与绿色革命技术、国家及国际市场紧密相连。其能动性体现在其生物特性（高分蘖率、易脱粒）如何塑造了现代农业实践，以及其谷物特性的模糊性如何催生了复杂的市场行为（如混合售卖），从而稳定了价格。
  2. 政治商品世界：稻米作为维系社会政治稳定的核心要素，被国家用作政治工具（如Soeharto政权和RASKIN扶贫米项目）。其能动性体现在，即使是目标明确的“扶贫米”，在进入不同的地方集结体（如自给自足的传统社区）后，其意义也会被转化，甚至成为富裕的象征。
  3. 文化手工艺品世界：由传统的javanica亚种所主导，与爪哇地方社区的宇宙观、仪式和生计方式深度纠缠。其能动性体现在其物质特性（茎秆坚硬、不易脱粒）如何生成了特定的文化实践（如使用指刀收割、谷仓储存）和强大的社会韧性（如公共谷仓制度）。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑清晰，层层递进：首先，他们指出传统政治经济学在解释印尼稻米政策长期以来的矛盾与僵局时所遇到的困境，认为其根源在于忽视了非人类的能动性。接着，他们引介了莫尔的“复数性”和集结体理论作为替代性分析框架。随后，文章通过详述稻米作为经济商品、政治商品和文化手工艺品这三种不同的“生成”，来具体展示“复数性”是如何在实践中运作的。在这一过程中，作者巧妙地将稻米不同亚种的生物物质性与其所处的社会技术网络联系起来，证明了物质特性并非被动背景，而是塑造实践的主动力量。最后，文章在结论部分将这一分析提升至政治和政策层面，主张承认和拥抱这种复数性，是通向更具韧性和多样性的农业食品体系的必由之路。

4. 方法 (Method)

本研究采用的是一种理论驱动的案例研究方法（theory-driven case study）。作者并非进行一手民族志田野调查，而是精巧地整合和重构了关于印尼稻米农业的既有文献（包括政治经济学、农业史、植物学和人类学研究）。其核心方法论创新在于，运用后人文主义理论（特别是莫尔的本体论政治学）作为一种“理论透镜”，对现有数据和历史叙事进行重新解读和理论再造。通过这种方式，他们将一个为人熟知的案例（印尼稻米）转化为一个展示其核心理论（复数性与非人类能动性）的绝佳范例。文中的集结体示意图（Figure 6.1）也是一个重要的分析工具，它直观地展现了超越传统产业部门分类的、关系性的本体论视角。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

核心案例是印度尼西亚的稻米世界。这个总案例被精细地划分为三个相互关联但又各自独立的子案例，分别对应稻米被“生成”的三种不同现实：

  • 绿色革命与Indica亚种：展示稻米如何被塑造成现代经济商品。
  • RASKIN（扶贫米）计划：展示稻米作为政治商品时，其意义如何在不同社会情境中发生流变。
  • 爪哇传统社区与Javanica亚种：展示稻米作为文化手工艺品，如何与地方知识、宇宙观和物质实践深度融合，共同生产出一个具有高度韧性的社会-生态系统。

Introduction

本章开篇即批判了主流政治经济学分析范式在理解农业食品体系时的局限性。这些范式往往将能动性（agency）完全归于人类和制度，忽视了人与自然关系的复杂性，最终导致了本质主义和单一化的框架。作者提出，应采纳一种“鲜活唯物主义”（vibrant materialist）的后人类视角，将能动性视为分布于人类与非人类实体间的力量。

  • 总结：作者明确指出，传统视角将功劳或过错完全归于人类和制度，这是一种有缺陷的分析起点。
    These points of view, however, fail to address the complexity of human–nature relationships and often end up with monolithic frameworks and outcomes framed by essentialist categories that obscure as much as they reveal. (p. 82)
  • 总结：为了超越此局限，作者引入了“能动性分布于多元实体”的观点，并明确其理论根基为“鲜活唯物主义”。
    As an alternative, we will elaborate on previous arguments regarding agency as a force distributed across multiple entities, both human and non-human (Dwiartama and Rosin 2014), informed by a vibrant materialist approach (Bennett 2007). (p. 82)
  • 总结：作者借用安娜玛丽·莫尔的“复数性”概念，阐明本文的政治目标是培育由非人类行动者（如作物）的本性所塑造的多样化行动与实践。
    To do this, we engage with Mol’s (2002) concept of multiplicity to suggest that the political objective of a vibrant materialist approach in agri-food studies is to nurture a diversity of actions and practices shaped by the nature of non-human actors, and their alignment with the rest of the actors within an assemblage. (p. 82)
  • 总结：引用著名印尼政治经济学家C. Peter Timmer的观点，强调稻米具有特殊性，其作为主食、作物和商品的三重身份，深刻影响了亚洲的经济与政治。
    In answering this question, we refer initially to C. Peter Timmer (2005: 14), one of the most influential political economists studying Indonesia, who stated that ‘rice is different, and the difference has powerfully influenced economics and politics throughout much of Asia’. (pp. 82-83)
  • 总结：用具体数据阐述稻米作为“食物”的重要性，它提供了印尼人口卡路里摄入的绝大部分。
    As a food, rice supplies 35–67 per cent of the total calorie intake of the population, and its consumption has been increasing over the last four decades (Gerard et al. 2001; BPS 2012). (p. 83)
  • 总结：用数据说明稻米作为“作物”在印尼农业中的核心地位，关乎上亿农民的生计，并占据巨大种植面积。
    As a crop, it is the source of income for more than 100 million farmers, farmworkers and retailers in Indonesia, and accounted for an area in 2009 of 12 million hectares, and a total production of up to 64 million tons (BPS 2012). (p. 83)
  • 总结：指出印尼在稻米“商品”方面扮演的双重角色——既是主要生产国，也是主要进口国，这凸显了其在全球贸易中的复杂地位。
    In commodity terms, Indonesia has become both the third largest producer of rice and, at the same time, one of the largest importers of rice in the world (Hill 2000; Dawe 2002; FAO 2011). (p. 83)
  • 总结：由于稻米对农民和城市贫民的重要性，政府不得不采取价格干预措施，以缓冲世界米价波动的影响。
    Therefore, rice price fluctuations have a serious impact on the livelihood of the majority of farmers and urban poor in Indonesia, causing the government to buffer its citizens from the fluctuating world price (Dawe 2001; Timmer 2005). (p. 83)
  • 总结：作者指出印尼稻米政策的历史充满了争议和政治动荡，显示出稻米是一种“不服管束”的作物，任何单一政策都难以奏效。
    The history of Indonesia’s rice agricultural development has been coloured with controversies and political turmoil surrounding what does and does not need to be done with rice production (Hill 2000). (p. 83)
  • 总结：本文的核心问题是：后人类政治经济学如何能为我们对农业食品体系的实践理解做出贡献？作者将通过莫尔的“复数性”概念来探讨稻米如何被塑造成经济商品、政治工具和文化手工艺品。
    We employ what Mol (2002) terms ‘multiplicity’ in respect of rice: to see rice being enacted not only as an economic commodity but also as a political instrument and cultural artefact,1 which intricately shapes different ‘worlds of rice’. (p. 83)

A political history of rice in Indonesia

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章节追溯了印尼稻米与政治权力相互交织的悠久历史。从古代爪哇王国将其作为税收基础，到荷兰殖民时期被迫与经济作物轮作，再到独立后经历绿色革命、国家高度干预以及新自由主义改革，稻米始终处于政治博弈的中心。作者指出，这段历史充满了政策的反复与矛盾，而传统的政治经济学分析往往将其归因于精英腐败、政治策略等常见范畴，却无法解释为何始终无法形成一套行之有效的治理方案。这为后文引入“复数性”视角埋下了伏笔。

  • 总结：追溯稻米在古代爪哇王国的政治经济角色，它不仅是生计作物，也是商品和税收基础。
    Reid (1999), for instance, documented that, between the ninth and tenth centuries in ancient Java, rice became not only a major subsistence crop for the society but also a market commodity, and the basis of agricultural tax systems in several small kingdoms in the region. (p. 83)
  • 总结：在荷兰殖民时期，农民被迫轮作稻米与出口导向的经济作物，这塑造了稻米耕作方式从自给自足向半集约化和工业化农业的转变。
    During Dutch colonialism, the Dutch East Indies had become one of the centres of production of tropical commodities among others... and peasants were forced to plant those crops in combination, or rotation, with rice (Husken and White 1989). (pp. 83-84)
  • 总结：独立后，印尼通过绿色革命重组了稻米农业，引入高产品种、补贴和价格稳定政策，初期产量增加，但随后迅速下滑。
    ...Indonesia, with the help of the Food and Agriculture Organization, entered the era of the Green Revolution, in which it restructured its rice agriculture through the introduction of high-yielding rice varieties (HYVs), subsidization of agricultural inputs and credit, and stabilization of farm-gate prices (Gerard et al. 2001). (p. 84)
  • 总结：1990年后，由于过度补贴和政府腐败，印尼经济停滞，农业资源枯竭，最终在1997年亚洲金融危机时达到顶点。
    After 1990, Indonesia’s economic growth began to stagnate and its resources were depleted, owing to an over-subsidized agriculture sector and a highly corrupt government (Gerard et al. 2001; Sumarto and Suryahadi 2007)... (p. 84)
  • 总结：IMF的援助要求印尼取消农业补贴和贸易壁垒，但几年后政府又自相矛盾地恢复了部分保护主义措施。
    Indonesia was forced to rescind all tariff and non-tariff barriers... (Gerard et al. 2001), but a few years later increased import tariffs and banned imported rice during harvest periods (Fane and Warr 2009). (p. 84)
  • 总结：政府同时推行相互矛盾的农业政策，如有机/杂交稻米，以及粮食主权/工业化粮食园区。
    The government also experimented with contradictory agricultural policies, such as organic/hybrid rice (Dwiartama 2014) and food sovereignty/industrial food estates (Neilson and Arifin 2012). (p. 84)
  • 总结：作者指出，传统的批判性政治经济学分析会将这种政策不一致归因于精英政治和腐败等因素，并开出重新安排治理结构的药方，但这正是本文试图超越的。
    A critical political economy analysis, such as that set out by Neilson and Arifin (2012), might address the current state of inconsistencies as stemming from many familiar categories... and this kind of critique would almost certainly end with a prescription to rearrange elites, peasants and food system governance... (p. 84)

A look beyond political economy

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章节明确提出了超越传统政治经济学分析的必要性。作者认为，印尼政府之所以在不同农业范式（传统、工业化、替代性）的争论中摇摆不定，是因为它未能认识到印尼稻米农业食品体系存在着“多重现实”（multiple realities）。传统、工业化和有机的稻米生产体系并非相互替代，而是同时并存，各自服务于不同的人群和市场。作者通过一张集结体图示（Figure 6.1），形象地展示了“稻米世界”是如何由各种行动者（社区、病虫害、气候、消费者、政府等）通过复杂的联系和组装共同“制造”出来的。这种本体论视角强调，稻米并非一个被动的物体，而是在与不同行动者的关系网络中被赋予了多重意义，从而需要新的理论框架来理解其复数性。

  • 总结：作者指出，不同农业范式间的政治竞争，反映了政府未能承认印尼稻米农业食品体系的多重现实。
    The ongoing, and sometimes harsh, political contests that drive towards hegemony of an agricultural paradigm (traditionalism, industrialism or an alternative) seem to show that the government has failed to acknowledge the multiple realities of rice agri-food systems in Indonesia. (p. 84)
  • 总结：本文的理解基于稻米通过与其他行动者形成集结体而获得多重意义。图示（Figure 6.1）的目的在于进行一种本体论工作，避免使用常规的产业或经济范畴来描述。
    We understand this from the realization that there are multiple meanings attached to rice, acquired through the assemblages formed with other actors, as shown in Figure 6.1. This figure does a particular kind of ontological work by avoiding the normal categories that are used to describe industries, sectors or economies. (p. 85)
  • 总结：各种非人类行动者，如病虫害、气候、水和土壤，都影响着农民和研究中心对种植何种稻米的选择。
    Pests, diseases, climate, water and soil all influence farmers and research centres when they are determining which rice should be planted in a particular area. (p. 85)
  • 总结：作者明确指出，要理解稻米的复数性，就必须在Timmer关于稻米三副面孔（食物、作物、商品）的论断基础上，探索一套新的理论框架。
    Assembling this alternative narrative, and building on Timmer’s (2005) assertion of the three facets of rice (a food, a crop and a commodity), demands that we interrogate a new set of theoretical frameworks in ways that can take account of the multiplicity of rice revealed in Figure 6.1. (p. 85)

Rice in its multiplicity

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章聚焦于稻米自身的物质性，将其作为能动性的来源进行深入剖析。作者首先强调了稻米惊人的生态适应性，能生长于从沼泽到旱地的广泛环境中。接着，文章详细介绍了稻米的起源和在亚洲的传播，并重点区分了在印尼具有重要意义的两个亚种：javanica（爪哇稻，通常为传统品种）和indica（籼稻，现代高产品种的来源）。通过对比两者的植物学特征——如株高、分蘖性、脱粒性、茎秆硬度、对光照和温度的敏感度等——作者为后文分析这两种稻米如何塑造了截然不同的农业“世界”奠定了物质基础。这部分是连接抽象理论与具体案例的关键桥梁，有力地论证了非人类的物质属性如何主动参与到社会实践的建构中。

  • 总结：许多研究稻米的学者都对其广泛的适应性表示惊叹。
    Many authors who study rice start their introduction with an expression of amazement at the adaptability of rice (for example see Hanks 1972; Bray 1986). (p. 85)
  • 总结：稻米既能生长在沼泽地带，也能适应旱地环境。
    Although it is known mainly as a semi-aquatic plant that thrives in swampy areas, it can also grow in dry areas (Lu and Chang 1980; Bray 1986). (p. 85)
  • 总结：一些水稻品种甚至能从淹水环境中获益，淹水有助于养分释放和固氮。
    Mikkelsen and de Datta (1980) note that precipitation and water enhance nutrient availability, help with nitrogen fixation, and create favourable microclimatic conditions for rice growth. (p. 85)
  • 总结：部分品种能利用淹水深度作为启动结实和成熟的信号。
    Some varieties have also adapted to the extent that the water level flooding the stem of rice acts as an indicator for the initiation and ripening of its fruits (Vergara 1980). (p. 85)
  • 总结：稻米的驯化历史悠久，起源于东印度、东南亚和中国西南部，并逐渐取代了根茎类作物成为亚洲主食。
    Domesticated by the early Asian settlers in about 10000–15000 bc, the annual forms of rice are thought to originate from East India, South-East Asia and South-West China (Lu and Chang 1980; Bray 1986). (p. 85)
  • 总结：在起源地，稻米根据生态地理特征分化为三个亚种群：sinica (japonica), indica, 和 javanica。
    The first was the sinica (japonica) subspecies... The second was indica... The third subspecies had only been identified in 1958 as javanica... (pp. 86-87)
  • 总结：javanica亚种（传统品种）的特征是谷粒宽厚、分蘖少、植株高大、不易脱粒、茎秆坚硬，且对光照温度不敏感，适合旱地种植。
    The javanica subspecies, commonly known as the traditional variety group (TV), has broad, thick-culmed grains, low tillering, tall plant stature, low grainshattering, and hard plant tissue (Bray 1986). It is somewhat less sensitive to sunlight and temperature, and thus has a good reputation for its ability to grow on dry upland areas of swidden agriculture (Asai et al. 2009). (p. 87)
  • 总结：indica亚种（现代高产品种来源）的特征是谷粒较长、分蘖多、植株中低、易脱粒、茎秆较软，对温度更敏感，适合低海拔地区。
    The indica subspecies, from which the modern high-yielding rice varieties (HYVs) are derived, has longer grains, profuse tillering, low to intermediate plant stature, easy grain-shattering, and a soft plant tissue. It is more responsive to temperature and often found in lower altitudes. (p. 87)

Rice as an economic commodity

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章详细阐述了稻米如何被“生成”为一种现代经济商品。这一过程与绿色革命的生产主义逻辑紧密相连，而indica亚种因其高产特性成为了理想的候选者。作者论证，现代农业实践并非简单地将技术应用于被动的自然，而是技术与indica稻米的物质特性（高分蘖、易脱粒等）相互塑造的结果。这一集结体也带来了新的挑战，如病虫害和气候变化。文章进一步揭示了市场本身的复杂性：稻米市场并非由标准化商品构成，而是充满了多样性和变数。例如，高端米（如香米pandanwangi）与普通米共存，零售商通过混合不同品种创造出连续的品质和价格谱系。更重要的是，消费者的偏好并非简单的线性排序，他们会基于口感、质地乃至“饱腹感”等多种标准做出选择，这挑战了只关注供需逻辑的传统经济学框架。

  • 总结：在马尔萨斯困境的驱动下，绿色革命的目标是培育高产、速生的粮食商品，而indica亚种因其特性和广泛分布成为理想选择。
    For rice, the perfect candidate was indica, as the once local varieties had already spread across tropical Asia (including Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Malaysia and Indonesia) (Herdt and Capule 1983). (p. 87)
  • 总结：高产品种（HYVs）将稻米农业导向了与国内外市场直接关联的工业化农业模式。
    Through indica rice, the HYVs introduced a new spirit of agriculture under the Green Revolution and re-oriented rice farming towards a more industrialized agriculture, directly connected to the domestic, and also international, rice markets (Dawe 2001). (pp. 87-88)
  • 总结：气候变化和全球经济危机加剧了工业化农业的风险。
    Climatic change, in combination with global economic crisis, exacerbates crisis conditions (Neilson and Arifin 2012). (p. 88)
  • 总结：在土地有限、土壤贫瘠的地区，商业化种植的农民倾向于选择口感更好、价格更高的优质品种，如香米pandanwangi。
    Because of limited land and low soil quality that results in lower productivity, those who farm commercially often choose to plant premium varieties, which taste better and consumers prefer, such as the aromatic pandanwangi rice. (p. 88)
  • 总结：由于稻米的品质只有在煮熟后才能明确区分，其市场身份存在模糊性，这为零售商混合不同品种创造“新品牌”提供了可能。
    Interestingly, because the quality distinction is evident in rice only once it is cooked, there are no clear boundaries for the identity of rice in the market. ... In the market, retailers often mix multiple varieties of rice grains in countless combinations to formulate, in a way, new brands of rice. (p. 88)
  • 总结：这种混合行为创造了一个品质和价格的连续体，从而起到了稳定市场价格的作用。
    This creates a continuum of rice qualities and prices, and consequently also stabilizes prices. (p. 88)
  • 总结：消费者偏好并非简单的优劣排序。一项调查显示，爪哇消费者更偏爱传统品种，即使其价格远高于“口感更好”的现代品种。
    Based on a survey by Damardjati and Oka (1992), consumers in Java preferred traditional rice varieties over the modern ones, and the price premium for the traditional far exceeded the price of the ‘better-tasting’ modern varieties. (pp. 88-89)
  • 总结：体力劳动者更关注能提供“更强饱腹感”的稻米品种，而非口感。
    Labourers commonly also paid less attention to taste, but more to rice varieties that could give ‘an enhanced feeling of satiation’ (Damardjati and Oka 1992: 60). (p. 89)

Rice as a political commodity

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章探讨了稻米如何被“生成”为一种政治商品。鉴于稻米对印尼社会的极端重要性，维持稻米产业的稳定被视为实现社会和政治稳定的先决条件。作者指出，无论是苏哈托长达32年的威权统治，还是2008年粮食危机期间实现的粮食自给，都被视为重大的政治成就。稻米的物质性和象征性（作为本土作物、主食和农民商品）使其深度嵌入区域的地缘政治。国家通过“扶贫米”（RASKIN）项目，将稻米作为一种有针对性的补贴工具，服务于农民、城市和农村贫困人口等更广泛的群体。然而，本文的洞见在于揭示了这一政治商品的意义并非固定不变。在自给自足的传统社区，购买RASKIN反而成为一种“特权”的象征，因为它意味着拥有现金且无需辛苦劳作。这个例子绝佳地说明了，一个物体的意义和功能是在其与不同行动者（在此是传统社区的精英）的集结过程中被转化和重新定义的。

  • 总结：稻米产业的稳定被认为是印尼社会和政治稳定的基础。
    This fact supports the belief that social and political stability could not have been achieved without stability in the rice agricultural sector (Dawe 2001; Timmer 2005). (p. 89)
  • 总结：稻米被认为是苏哈托威权统治的稳定基石，而2008年实现自给自足也被视为一项政治成功。
    As a political commodity, rice is recognized as having been a key foundation of social and political stability during the 32 years of Soeharto’s authoritarian regime (1966–98). Similarly, the realization of self-sufficiency in Indonesia that coincided with the 2008 World Food Crisis (Neilson and Arifin 2012), as well as some formative regulatory moves (Fane and Warr 2009), might be considered as much a political success as a significant agricultural achievement. (p. 89)
  • 总结：政府通过RASKIN（扶贫米）项目向贫困家庭提供打折大米，以缓解经济冲击。
    This has been achieved through a government project called the Rice for the Poor programme (Beras untuk Keluarga Miskin, RASKIN). ... As rice purchases account for more than 50 per cent of disposable income amongst the urban poor, affordable rice offers significant relief to such families, and helps in alleviating the impact of economic shocks (Irhamni and Nuryakin 2009; Neilson and Arifin 2012). (p. 89)
  • 总结：RASKIN项目也覆盖了农村地区，那里的贫困家庭数量更多，且他们通常是稻米的净消费者。
    The RASKIN programme has expanded... in rural areas, in which the number of poor families is greater and they are known to be net consumers of rice (Irhamni and Nuryakin 2009). (pp. 89-90)
  • 总结：在自给自足的传统社区，RASKIN的意义发生了逆转。购买它意味着无需劳动，因此反而成了社区精英的商品。
    As not many people in the traditional communities have money to purchase the rice, RASKIN rice has instead become a commodity for elites in the community. In a way, consuming RASKIN rice in these traditional communities is seen as a privilege for the affluent, evidence that they can have what the poor cannot. (p. 90)
  • 总结：这个例子展示了，即使是象征贫困的RASKIN米，当与其他行动者（社区精英）联系在一起时，也会被转译为富裕和特权的象征。
    Here, even RASKIN – a rice that through its quality embodies a symbol for the poor – is translated into an object of affluence and privilege as other actors attach themselves to it. (p. 90)

Rice as a cultural artefact

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章转向探讨稻米如何被“生成”为一种文化手工艺品，其焦点从indica转向了javanica亚种。在爪哇的传统社区，javanica稻米与人们的生活、文化和宇宙观深度纠缠在一起。作者以稻米女神Dewi Sri为例，指出她并非外在的神祇，而是稻米本身的化身，这使得农民以一种充满敬畏的方式对待稻米。这种人-稻关系不仅体现在象征层面，更深深植根于稻米的物质性。Javanica稻米坚硬的茎秆和较长的生长期，催生了使用小“指刀”精细收割的传统，这既是尊重作物的体现，也便于农民进行选种。其不易脱粒的特性，则使得将稻穗成捆储存在特制谷仓中成为可能，大大延长了保质期。这种储存方式进一步支撑了社区公共谷仓制度，成为抵御气候冲击、实现风险共享的社会韧性基石。本章有力地证明了文化实践与非人类的物质特性之间存在着深刻的“共同生产”（co-production）关系。

  • 总结：javanica稻米（传统品种）与爪哇人民的生命和实践深度纠缠，影响着他们的食物、仪式、农业实践和哲学。
    The traditional javanica rice has been deeply entangled with the life and practices of Javanese people. For centuries, rice has influenced Javanese culture in many ways – through their foods, ceremonies, agricultural practices and philosophies (Soemarwoto 2007). (p. 90)
  • 总结：爪哇人视稻米女神Dewi Sri为农业活动的核心，她并非外在神力，而是稻米本身的化身。
    The Javanese people recognize Dewi Sri, the rice or prosperity goddess, as of central importance to their agricultural activities (for more details see Wessing 1988). In their culture, Dewi Sri is not an external power... She herself is the rice, or the embodiment of rice. (p. 90)
  • 总结：在一个西爪哇的传统社区，他们保存了超过500个稻米地方品种，并根据神圣性、水土适应性等进行分类。
    In one traditional community in West Java, the community leader informed us that they had more than 500 landraces, classified according to their sacredness (buhun, ancient; biasa, regular), their affinity to water–soil regimes (in wetland or dryland), and the elevation at which they are planted (as also noted in Soemarwoto 2007). (p. 91)
  • 总结：传统javanica稻米的物理特性——坚硬的茎秆和较长的生长期——塑造了使用“指刀”收割的传统农业实践。
    The physical features of traditional javanica rice – hard stem tissue, a long growing period, and low-shattering grains – have shaped traditional agricultural practices. The first two features impact on the way traditional farmers in South-East Asia use small ‘finger knives’ to cut the rice stalks off the straw (Hanks 1972). (p. 91)
  • 总结：传统稻米不易脱粒的特性，使其非常适合将稻穗成捆储存在专门的谷仓中，保质期可达数年。
    Another physical feature of the traditional rice is its low-shattering grain. This characteristic is useful for storage, because farmers store the dried rice in stalk bundles, and an easy-shattering rice variety is impractical for this type of storage method. (p. 91)
  • 总结：这种储存方式支撑了社区公共谷仓制度，在歉收时，社区成员可以从中借米，并在下次丰收后归还等量，从而形成一种风险共担机制。
    In circumstances where rice production is compromised owing to climatic shocks, the community relies on this communal barn, from which anyone can borrow rice at any time, and return it with the same amount he/she has borrowed after the next harvest period. (p. 91)

Conclusions

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

结论部分重申并升华了核心论点。作者强调，承认稻米的多样性和复数性，就必然要求我们去促进多样化的行动。稻米作为经济商品、政治商品和文化手工艺品的每一种意义，都显化在不同的农业食品集结体或“世界”之中。借鉴莫尔的观点，作者指出，复数性不仅仅是承认不同视角，而是承认由特定实践所产生的、相互关联的“多重现实”。这些现实之间可能存在冲突，如何让它们“巧妙地结合在一起”是关键。本文挑战了那些主张印尼应摆脱以稻米为中心、转向出口导向农业的经济和政治分析。相反，作者认为稻米与印尼社会之间复杂而深刻的相互联系，使其成为不可替代的存在。强行将两者分离是不可能的。因此，不应追求适用于所有稻米世界的单一农业范式。承认稻米的复数性，才能避免政策陷入僵局。最终，作者提出，后人类研究议程在政治决策中的作用，是实现从“控制”非人类行动者到“与之共事”的转变。社会科学家承认物质世界的复数性，本身就是一种鼓励更多元、更有意识的政策制定的政治行动。

  • 总结：承认稻米的多样性和复数性，就要求我们促进多样化的行动。
    To conclude, we would like to make the point that acknowledging the diversity and multiplicity of rice also demands facilitating a diversity of actions. (p. 92)
  • 总结：借鉴莫尔的观点，复数性并非简单的视角差异，而是由特定实践产生的、相互关联的多重现实。
    In her account, multiplicity is never simply a matter of recognizing different perspectives. Multiple realities are produced by particular practices that relate to each other and that adhere to distinct assemblages of human and non-human actors. (p. 92)
  • 总结：如何让这些多重现实“巧妙地结合在一起”是关键，以避免实践冲突阻碍政治绩效。
    The manner through which multiplicity enhances the agri-food systems depends on how these realities ‘dovetail together . . . or include one another in complex ways’ (Law 2008: 152). (p. 92)
  • 总结：本文挑战了主张印尼应放弃以稻米为中心、转向出口导向农业的观点。
    The argument in this chapter also challenges many economic and political analyses that favour navigating Indonesia away from a rice-centred to a more export-oriented agricultural policy (McCulloch and Timmer 2008). (p. 92)
  • 总结：由于稻米与印尼社会深度交织，将其从社会中分离出去是不切实际的。
    The fact that, through its multiple meanings and practices, rice is so deeply embodied and intertwined with Indonesian society implies that separating rice from society is practically impossible... (p. 92)
  • 总结：作者最终主张，后人类研究议程在政治决策中的角色，是实现从“控制”到“与之共事”的转变。
    In the end, we suggest that the role of a post-human research agenda in political decision making comes as a shift from ‘controlling’ to ‘working with’ the non-human actors. (p. 92)
