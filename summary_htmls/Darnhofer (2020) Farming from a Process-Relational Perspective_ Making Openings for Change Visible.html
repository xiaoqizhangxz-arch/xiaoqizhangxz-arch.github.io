Farming from a Process-Relational Perspective: Making Openings for Change Visible

Ika Darnhofer

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，传统农村社会学对农场层面的研究，因其根植于人文主义、本质主义和结构决定论的理论预设，而存在显著局限。这些预设将农民塑造为理性、自主的行动者，将农场视为被动的物质客体，并过分强调结构性力量的制约，从而遮蔽了变革的真实可能性。为此，作者主张引入一种“过程-关系”（process-relational）视角，并将其与新唯物主义（new materialism）和后人文主义（post-humanism）的洞见相融合，以构建一个全新的概念框架。该框架主张，农业实践（farming）并非孤立个体的决策结果，而是一个由人类与非人类（如土壤、作物、动物、技术）共同参与、持续生成的动态社会-物质过程（socio-material process）。通过将能动性（agency）扩展至非人类，并强调世界万物的“生成”（becoming）属性，这一视角旨在揭示农业系统中无处不在的、不可预测的变革契机。因此，采纳过程-关系视角不仅是一项理论革新，更是一项政治工程：它旨在激发不同于主流的想象力，使构建更具社会公正性和环境友好性的农业未来成为可能（thinkable）。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要建立在对社会学理论的深度梳理与批判性综合之上，而非依赖一手经验数据。作者通过引证一系列关键理论流派来支撑其核心论点： 1. 对传统农村社会学理论的批判：通过引用大量文献，揭示了传统研究中对“理性农民”形象的依赖，对家庭内部复杂动态的简化，以及对社会结构（如市场力量、政策激励、“好农民”规范）决定性作用的过度强调。 2. 关系社会学理论：系统性地引入了关系社会学（特别是Emirbayer和Dépelteau的“过程-关系”分支）的核心思想，如优先考虑“关系”而非“实体”，强调世界的动态生成性和“扁平本体论”（flat ontology），以此作为反本质主义和反决定论的理论基石。 3. 新唯物主义与后人文主义：整合了来自Barad、Haraway、Bennett等学者的洞见，论证了物质（matter）的能动性（agency）和“活力”（liveliness）。将“情感”（affect）概念引入分析，强调非人类（土壤、动物、机器）拥有“影响与被影响的能力”，是共同塑造农业实践的行动者。 4. 非再现理论：借鉴Thrift等人的工作，批判了传统研究对认知和话语的过度关注，强调了具身经验（embodied experience）、前认知（pre-cognitive）冲动和感官知觉在农业实践中的重要性。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑层次清晰，层层递进： 首先，“破”——批判现有范式。文章开篇即剖析了传统农场研究的理论局限性，将其归结为人文主义、二元论和结构决定论的束缚，指出这些范式难以解释农业实践中持续存在的异质性和动态变化。 其次，“立”——构建理论框架。系统地介绍了“过程-关系社会学”的核心世界观，即世界是一个由互动构成的、不断生成的、复杂的流变过程。在此基础上，通过“注入一剂新唯物主义”，将后人文主义的非人类能动性概念和非再现理论的具身性洞见整合进来，形成一个融贯的、反传统的新分析框架。 再次，“应用”——重新概念化核心对象。将此新框架应用于“农民”与“农场”这两个核心概念。农民不再是孤立的、理性的决策者，而是与环境持续“内-在行动”（intra-acting）的、身心合一的、相互依存的存在。农场也不再是被动资源，而是充满活力的、具有情感影响力的能动实体。 最后，“引申”——揭示新框架的理论与实践意涵。论证了这一新视角如何带来“概念的开放性”：它使分析焦点从静态结构转向动态过程，从制约转向变革的可能，从人类中心主义转向人与自然的协同共生，并对技术的角色提出更复杂的理解。进而，文章探讨了其对研究方法论的深刻挑战（如超越访谈、强调研究者反身性和研究行为的“述行性”），并最终将这一理论转向升华为一种旨在“使其他世界成为可能”的政治实践。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的核心研究方法是理论建构（Theoretical Construction）与概念综合（Conceptual Synthesis）。具体而言，作者通过对农村社会学、关系社会学、后人文主义、新唯物主义等多个领域的文献进行批判性回顾与整合，构建了一个新的理论框架。这并非一项经验研究，而是一篇旨在推动领域内理论创新的元理论（meta-theoretical）文章。其方法论步骤包括：1）识别并解构现有研究范式的本体论与认识论预设；2）从其他理论传统中汲取核心概念（如关系、过程、能动性、情感、内-在行动）；3）将这些概念进行创造性地融合，形成一个具有内在逻辑一致性的新分析框架；4）阐释该框架如何能够为研究具体问题（即农场层面的实践与变革）提供新的视角和洞见。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文为纯理论探讨，未设特定的经验案例研究。然而，为了阐释其抽象的理论概念，作者在文中穿插了一些普遍性或说明性的例子。例如：

  • 农民的具身知识：提及农民通过直觉“感觉”到某只动物“不对劲”，却难以用语言清晰解释，以此说明前认知和具身经验的重要性。
  • 人-机关系：引用Carolan (2008)的研究，说明农民可能将拖拉机体验为其身体的延伸，以此展示人类与技术之间深刻的具身纠缠。
  • 土壤的能动性：指出土壤并非惰性物质，而是充满活力的，其气味和外观能“影响”农民，促使他们采取行动，这体现了非人类的能动性。
这些例子服务于理论阐释，其作用是使抽象的哲学概念（如情感、非人类能动性）在农业这一具体情境中变得更易理解。

Introduction

本章首先概述了传统农村社会学在农场层面研究中的主流倾向，即关注“农民为何如此行为”，通常基于一种人文主义理解，将农民视作主动决策者，而农场为被动客体。随后，作者批判性地指出，尽管传统研究承认了自然、家庭、社会结构等多重因素的干扰与制约，但其核心的人文主义假设已受到社会学“本体论转向”的挑战。这一转向引入了后人文主义、新唯物主义等视角，强调了非人类能动性、过程性与不可预测性。作者认为，这些新理论已在农产品-食物研究中展现出巨大潜力，但尚未充分应用于农场层面的研究。因此，本文旨在提出一个过程-关系视角，将变革的可能性置于分析中心，以捕捉农业实践中无处不在的动态性与多样性。

  • 总结：指出传统研究将农民视为主动的，而农场是被动的。
    While it is hard to do justice to the wide diversity of theoretical approaches to conceptualising the farmer in rural sociology, many build on a humanistic understanding, where the farmer is active, making choices that shape the farm, while the farm itself is seen as passive (e.g., Hendrickson and James 2005; Dessein and Nevens 2007; Marr and Howley 2019; Milone and Ventura 2019).
  • 总结：承认传统研究也考虑了自然的不可控性。
    Certainly, provisions are made for the fact that the farmer cannot make the farm as she would wish, for her plans tend to be unsettled by unruly plants, diseases appearing against her will, machines breaking down despite careful maintenance, or the need to take into account financial implications (e.g., Stock and Forney 2014; Schewe and Stuart 2015; Suess-Reyes and Fuetsch 2016).
  • 总结：提及传统研究承认决策并非个体理性行为，而是家庭协商的结果。
    Provisions are also made for the fact that while it may be labelled ‘farmer decision-making’, choices may be less the result of one individual’s rational reflection, but of deliberations between various family members (e.g., Danes and Lee 2004; Price and Evans 2006; Seuneke and Bock 2015; Contzen and Forney 2017; Chiswell 2018).
  • 总结：阐述传统研究强调社会结构对农民能动性的制约。
    And many studies point out how a broad range of societal structures constrain farmer agency, be it traditions and social norms that define the characteristics of a ‘good farmer’, the power of supermarkets or the food processing industry to impose certain practices, or the incentives offered by agricultural policies (e.g., Duram 2000; Morgan and Murdoch 2000; Burton 2004; Vanclay and Enticott 2011).
  • 总结：引入“本体论转向”对传统人文主义假设的挑战，并列举了在农食研究中已有的相关理论探索。
    The turn has triggered alternate theoretical perspectives in a number of agro-food studies (see Goodman 2009; Carolan 2013; Le Heron et al. 2016; Sarmiento 2017), arguing e.g., for a focus on difference rather than dominance (e.g., Wilson 2013; Beacham 2018), for the usefulness to understand Alternative Food Networks as multiple and emergent, as performative orderings... (e.g., Whatmore and Thorne 1997; Stock et al. 2015; Le Velly 2019)...
  • 总结：强调新理论对“多于人类”（more-than-human）能动性的关注。
    ...for taking into account more-than-human agency (e.g., Dwiartama and Rosin 2014; Phillips 2016; Dwiartama 2017), or for analysing the expansive webs of relations through assemblage theory (e.g., Jones et al. 2019).
  • 总结：指出这些另类理论视角也应被用于激活农场层面的研究。
    These alternate theoretical perspectives have strengthened agro-food studies, and they could also enliven work done at farm-level (see e.g., Carolan 2005, 2016; Higgins 2006; Ferguson et al. 2016; Herman 2016).

The conventional conceptualisations: Farmer and farm in the ontological turn

本章系统地剖析了传统社会学方法在农场研究中的三个核心局限性。第一，对农民的理解局限于“人文主义主体”，即一个理性的、稳定的、认知驱动的个体，这种视角忽略了情感、具身性以及人类行为的矛盾性。第二，对农场的理解受制于社会/自然二元论，将农场视为被动的、可被人类塑造的物质资源，忽略了非人类（自然、技术）的能动性。第三，研究中存在隐含的本质主义和决定论倾向，通过类型学和二元对立（如传统/现代、常规/有机）来寻求确定性，并过分强调结构性力量对变革的压制，从而忽视了世界的流变性、杂糅性与开放性。作者通过一张表格（Table 1）清晰地对比了这些传统视角及其局限性与“本体论转向”后的另类概念化。

  • 总结：批判将农民视为理性经济人的传统经济学视角。
    Firstly, regarding the farmer, studies in rural sociology have amply shown that the image of the utility maximising individual – which usually underlies studies in agricultural economics, and is implicit in recommendations derived from plant production or animal husbandry (see Edwards-Jones 2006; Galt 2013) – tends to be tampered by personal values, attitudes, perceptions, interpretations, as well as social norms.
  • 总结：指出建构主义视角虽有进步，但仍将农民视为理性的、稳定的、连贯的人文主义主体。
    In these conventional approaches in rural sociology, the farmer is conceptualised as a humanistic subject, thus privileging reason and reflexivity, assuming farmers to be knowing, rational, stable, unified, and coherent (see St. Pierre 2004, 2008).
  • 总结：以采纳农业环境措施或机器人挤奶系统为例，说明传统研究假设农民能准确报告其决策原因。
    This allows for methods that build on the assumption that a farmer can provide an accurate account of why he has made specific choices, such as adopting agro-environmental measures (e.g., Burton et al. 2008) or investing in a milking robot (e.g., Butler and Holloway 2015).
  • 总结：批判传统视角对人类矛盾性和情感、具身性作用的忽视。
    Yet, these conventional conceptualisations have been questioned, as humans are known to be ambivalent and contradictory (e.g., Smelser 1998; Berliner et al. 2016), thus focusing on the cognitive dimension of choices may underplay the influence of emotions and embodiment (e.g., Carolan 2008; Pile 2010; Castro 2018).
  • 总结：批判传统社会学因学科边界而忽视农场的物质性。
    Indeed, the farm is mostly seen as a pliable resource for material agricultural production or for its social construction (see Alaimo and Hekman 2008, p. 4). This is partly linked to the boundaries that are foundational for mainstream sociology, i.e., the clear distinction between social/natural, with sociology focusing on the social and cultural aspects, leaving nature and matter to natural sciences (Goodman 1999; Fox and Alldred 2018).
  • 总结：强调Actor Network Theory和新唯物主义已确立了非人类的能动性，而这在农场研究中常被忽略。
    Approaches such as Actor Network Theory and new materialism have established the notion that nonhumans have agency. Yet, although farming is fundamentally situated at the interface between society and nature, between humans and technology, the materiality of the farm is rarely taken into account. To understand farming fully, it might then be important to find ways to combine social construction with an understanding of the agency of the material world (see e.g., Ferguson et al. 2016; Herman 2016; Phillips 2016; Legun and Henry 2017).
  • 总结：指出本质主义倾向体现为通过农民或农场的某些属性来预测行为。
    Traces of essentialism can be identified in studies that look for specific attributes of a farmer (age, gender, education, part-time occupation, attitude) or a farm (size, crops, types of animal rearing) and use these as indicators of some essence, some characteristic that determines how likely this type of farmer is to behave in a certain way (e.g., Andrade 2015; Milone and Ventura 2019).
  • 总结：赞扬那些挑战二元对立、揭示杂糅性与流动性的研究。
    Yet, studies have been keen to expose the limitations of binary oppositions such as old/young farmer, modern/traditional, small/large farms, conventional/organic, pointing out hybridities and fluidity (Whatmore and Thorne 1997; Burton 2006; Rosin and Campbell 2009; Sutherland and Darnhofer 2012; Lamine 2015; Le Velly and Dufeu 2016; Lamine et al. 2019; Lehtimäki 2019).
  • 总结：批判决定论视角，如“常规化”论述，其将市场力量视为系统性地破坏有机农业的另类性。
    For example in the debate around the conventionalisation thesis, market forces were held to systematically undermine the alterity of organic farming practices (Guthman 2004).

A process-relational perspective – a different worldview

本章详细阐述了本文的核心理论基础——过程-关系视角。作者首先界定了关系社会学，并特别聚焦于其“激进关系主义”或“过程-关系”分支。这一视角的核心主张是优先考虑关系而非实体，强调相互依存性，认为所有社会现象（包括个体）都是由关系构成的。它提倡一种“扁平本体论”，即社会现实只存在于互动层面，不存在超越互动的“结构”或“系统”。因此，所有看似稳固的秩序都是通过人类与非人类行动者持续的“工作”来维持的，这使得世界本质上是动态的、复杂的、不可预测的，并且充满了变革的可能。作者强调，这种视角与行动者网络理论有共通之处，即都拒绝“幕后”社会力量的解释，转而关注局域化的、关系性的微观政治。

  • 总结：将关系社会学定义为优先考虑关系而非实体，并强调相互依存性。
    What they have in common is that they prioritise relations over the entities, thus emphasising interdependence (Emirbayer 1997; Dépelteau 2018a).
  • 总结：区分关系社会学的两种视角，并明确本文采纳的是将个体也视为关系构型的激进视角。
    For others, all social phenomena are constituted through relations, thus individuals are not pre-existing subjects, but themselves configurations of relations (Powell 2013; Burkitt 2015; Dépelteau 2018a).
  • 总结：引用Dépelteau对过程-关系社会学的定义，强调其关注动态社会场的生成、转变和消逝。
    Dépelteau (2018b, p. 510) defined process-relational sociology as focusing on ‘the analysis of the emergence, the transformation and the disappearance of multiple smaller and larger dynamic social fields happening through interactions between human and non-human interactants’.
  • 总结：阐述过程-关系视角如何通过“扁平本体论”超越社会决定论。
    It thus recognises only one ‘level’ of social reality: the interaction between interactants, and as such is based on a ‘flat’ ontology (Dépelteau 2018b, p. 516).
  • 总结：强调从名词（静态实体）到动词（动态过程）的转变，如用“relating”代替“relation”。
    While these concepts are all nouns, they should not be mistaken for something that ‘is’, static and unchanging (Elias 1978; Carolan 2013). Rather, a relation, a network, an association, an assemblage requires constant work to be maintained, a work that can be done by humans, animals, plants, or objects (Powell 2013).
  • 总结：指出过程视角将分析焦点转向行动者“做什么”，以及他们如何创造或限制变革空间。
    The focus on work, on processes, gives analytic priority to what human and nonhuman actors do, how they make, maintain, or restrain space for change (see Carolan 2013).
  • 总结：强调世界的复杂性和不可预测性是过程-关系社会学的核心关切。
    Indeed, process-relational sociology takes seriously that the world we live in is complex (Cilliers 2005; Urry 2006; Pyyhtinen 2017).
  • 总结：关系的不稳定性既带来非预期后果，也为新的行动创造了机会。
    Yet, at the same time, this relational instability also creates openings for new doings (see Gibson-Graham 2008). These openings can enlarge what is perceived as ‘possible’ and ‘doable’: new doings, an altered routine can lead down a path where the once un-thought becomes thinkable (Carolan 2013).
  • 总结：指出过程-关系视角与行动者网络理论的相似之处，即都拒绝将“社会结构”作为解释。
    Similar to Actor Network Theory, process-relational sociology rejects any sense of social forces or structures working ‘behind the scenes’, replacing these entirely with localised, short-lived interactions or associations (see Latour 2005, p. 65–6).
  • 总结：明确本文的理论综合：在过程-关系视角基础上，整合新唯物主义、后人文主义、非再现理论和德勒兹哲学。
    Given the important role of nonhumans in farming and to refine the conceptualisation of the agency of nature and of ‘things’ more generally, I integrate insights of new materialism and post-humanism (Latour 2005; Barad 2007; Haraway 2016). To take into account the role of embodied experiences in understanding farming practices, I integrate insights of non-representationalism (Thrift 2000; Anderson and Harrison 2010). And to emphasise that the world is always becoming, an on-going differentiation, not a collection of static relations, I integrate elements of Deleuzian philosophy (Deleuze and Guattari 1987).

Reconceptualising the farmer and the farm

本章将前述的理论框架具体应用于重新定义“农民”与“农场”。对农民的重新概念化包含两个步骤：第一，克服身心二元论，将农民视为一个“思考与感知”的统一体，其行动不仅受认知驱动，更受到身体、感官、情感和前认知冲动的引导。第二，打破个体与环境的界限，将农民视为一个与其社会及物质环境持续相互作用、相互塑造的“相互依存的存在”，其行动是在与众多人类及非人类实体不断变化的复杂关系中共同演化的。对农场的重新概念化则借鉴新唯物主义，赋予其能动性。通过引入“情感”（affect）概念——即一种前认知、跨越身体的“影响与被影响的能力”，农场中的土壤、动物、机器等所有物质都被视为具有不同能动 capacities 的行动者，与农民共同参与到世界的“共-构”之中。

  • 总结：指出农民的行动受“背景”互动影响，许多行动是无反思的。
    Farmers are certainly understood as having conscious aims and intentions, but these processes are influenced by a broad range of ‘background’ interactions, which we do not consciously notice (Anderson and Harrison 2010).
  • 总结：以农民对动物的直觉为例，说明日常生活中存在大量非反思性且难以内省的行为。
    For example, many farmers will carefully observe the plants and animals they care for (see e.g., Singleton and Law 2013). They could not say what they are looking for, yet, they would feel if something is ‘off’ with an animal, finding it difficult to explain why it is ‘off’.
  • 总结：强调农民的行动不仅由心智引导，也由身体、感官、情感和过往经验引导。
    Rather, the farmer’s actions are seen as guided by mind as well as body, senses, emotions, affect; they are influenced by past experiences and revisited memories, by embodied experience tied to various farming practices in a specific place (see Thrift 2000; Carolan 2008; Anderson and Harrison 2010).
  • 总结：将行动理解为关系性现象，即所有行动都是互动。
    As a consequence, action is not understood as a one-way street running from the actor to the acted upon, from mind to matter, but as relational phenomena, i.e., all action is interaction (Anderson and Harrison 2010, p. 7; Dépelteau 2018a, p. 18).
  • 总结：将新唯物主义的能动性概念应用于农场，取代传统的人文主义能动性观。
    Building on insights from new materialism, agency no longer privileges human action (Latour 2005; Higgins 2006; Barad 2007; Bennett 2010), and the farm is understood has having agentic capacities.
  • 总结：引用德勒兹和伽塔里的“情感”（affect）概念，定义为一种改变身体状态的强度体验。
    The humanistic notion of agency is replaced by ‘affect’, where all matter is ‘affective – it possesses a “capacity to affect and be affected”’ (Deleuze and Guattari 1987 in Fox and Alldred 2018, p. 318).
  • 总结：区分情感与情绪，强调情感是前认知、前意识且无法被再现的。
    Pile (2010, p. 8) distinguishes emotions from affect, and points out that affect is ‘beyond cognition and always interpersonal’. Being pre-cognitive and pre-conscious, it is inexpressible, i.e. unable to be brought into representation.
  • 总结：指出农场中的不同物质体（土壤、机器等）具有不同的能动能力和“生成”风格。
    For example the soil, which is part of the relational web that produces the ‘farm’, is not an inert matter, but dynamic and lively (Schneider et al. 2010; Ferguson et al. 2016), it will affect the farmer and be affected by him.

Farming as relational: conceptual openings

本章探讨了将农业实践视为关系性过程所带来的三个核心“概念开放性”。第一，分析焦点从静态结构转向了秩序的偶然性与变革的无处不在。它不再问农民如何被结构所限制，而是问秩序是如何被暂时稳定下来，以及如何被打破和重构的，强调每个“事件”都可能成为变革的开端。第二，它促使我们重新概念化人与自然的关系，从人类操控自然的二元对立，转向一种人类与地方生态系统相互依存、共同塑造的协作视角，并建议从原住民世界观中汲取灵感。第三，它为理解技术提供了新视角，超越了技术决定论或人类中心控制论，将技术（如机器人、标准）视为具有能动性和情感影响力的行动者，它们与人类共同参与实践的塑造，并开启新的可能性。

  • 总结：指出常规（Routines）并非简单的重复，而是需要持续工作的动态过程。
    Routines are processes, i.e., they are not understood as sameness and simple repetition, but as dynamic and requiring on-going work (Feldman et al. 2016).
  • 总结：强调将焦点从“是什么”转向过程，关注秩序如何被维持、改变。
    The assumption is thus no longer that farmers are constrained by some form of overpowering structure (e.g., social norms in agriculture, agricultural policy incentives, technological progress, market pressures, the power of supermarkets), and that to achieve change, we need to identify ways to resist, contest, change these structures.
  • 总结：强调每一个事件都可能带来惊喜，打破现有的社会-物质组合。
    Each event potentially leads to a surprise that breaks the specific configuration of a social-material assemblage. The question is: how to extend the potential that an event opens up, the sense of promise that it may hold? (Anderson and Harrison 2010; Carolan 2016).
  • 总结：质疑农业发展的预设轨迹（如“要么做大要么出局”），并断言农业的多样性和多元路径将持续存在。
    This questions the notion of a pre-set trajectory, of a directed development or any seeming imperative inherent to modernity (e.g., the ‘get big or get out’ dictum in agriculture). Rather, given that the ‘world could always be otherwise’ (Law 2004, p. 152), empirical diversity and the plurality of approaches to farming is here to stay (van der Ploeg 2017; van der Ploeg et al. 2019).
  • 总结：呼吁从原住民世界观中学习，以克服人类中心主义的自然观。
    In this effort, a process-relational approach to farming could learn from the worldviews of indigenous societies, such as Andean cosmology where both humans and nonhumans are ‘earth beings’ engaged in mutual relationships of care (de la Cadena 2010); Aborigine cosmology, where reality is not prior, but interactively remade and enacted (Law 2004); or Inuit knowledge systems and how they approach the complexity of environmental processes (Berkes and Berkes 2009).
  • 总结：指出技术既非决定性的，也非被动受控的，而是能动的行动者。
    It undermines a view of technological determinism as well as one of human control over hapless things (Higgins 2006). Indeed, while humans transform matter, matter is also granted active agency in transforming farming practices and human subjectivities (see Alaimo and Hekman 2008; Coole and Frost 2010; Hultman and Lenz Taguchi 2010).
  • 总结：以度量衡（metrics）为例，说明技术物如何拥有超越人类意图和控制的能动性。
    For example Rosin et al. (2017, p. 90) show how metrics have agency in the agro-food system, in that they ‘initiate change beyond the expectations, intent and control of humans’.

Methodological considerations

本章探讨了过程-关系视角对研究方法论的深刻挑战与创新机遇。作者指出，这一视角要求研究方法超越传统，从关注实体转向关注事件和关系，并去中心化人类的主观性。这首先挑战了以访谈为主导的数据收集方式，因为它基于“再现主义”假设，即语言能透明地反映真实经验，而忽略了具身、情感和非人类的能动性。作者呼吁开发能够捕捉这些非话语维度的新方法，如视觉方法、感官民族志等实验性实践。更进一步，作者强调研究者自身的反身性，主张研究者应承认自己并非客观中立的观察者，而是与研究数据“共同生成”知识的参与者，其身体、情感都参与在分析过程中。最后，本章论述了研究的“述行性”（performativity）：研究方法、分析和写作本身就是在干预和塑造世界。因此，研究者的选择是一个政治行为，其目标应是揭示可能性，而非描述必然性，从而为构建另类世界做出贡献。

  • 总结：指出在研究实践中，新的理论洞见（如非人类能动性）往往未能被贯彻到底。
    While in theory an emphasis on relations and taking into account the agency of nonhumans is well debated and engaged with, Dowling et al. (2017) point out that in research praxis the implications are not always carried through.
  • 总结：批判传统访谈法基于“再现主义”假设，认为声音能呈现经验的真相。
    In conventional methodological practice, work with interview-based data tends to be based on the assumption that voice makes present the truth, and reflects the meaning of an experience (St. Pierre 2008).
  • 总结：指出过程-关系视角要求关注情感表达、具身体验以及身体间的相互影响。
    Thus, more attention is given to emotional expressions as well as to affective experiences stemming from relations, to bodies affecting and being affected by each other (Lenz Taguchi 2012; Buser 2014).
  • 总结：强调要从“多于人类”的视角重新审视数据，寻找并承认物质的构成性力量。
    Rather than focusing on human agency, and on interpersonal interactions, the aim is to look for, identify, and acknowledge the constitutive force of matter (Barad 2003; Law 2004).
  • 总结：呼吁关注文本中的“流动性、偶然性或不稳定性”等词汇，以捕捉过程。
    The emphasis on processes within relational sociology also means paying particular attention to terms that convey ‘lively and energetic imaginaries such as fluidity, contingency or instability’ in the stories told (Buser 2014, p. 234).
  • 总结：引用Whatmore的“实验性命令”，呼吁开发超越语言的、能放大感官、身体和情感维度的方法。
    Whatmore (2006, p. 606) called it the ‘experimental imperative’, i.e., the urgent need to supplement the familiar repertoire of humanistic methods that rely on generating talk, with experimental practices that amplify other sensory, bodily, affective registers.
  • 总结：批判研究中的“上帝把戏”，即研究者假装自己是无所不知、无所不在的客观观察者。
    It has been a while that Donna Haraway (1988, p. 581) has denounced ‘the god trick of seeing everything from nowhere’, where researchers position themselves as the knower of classic positivist empiricism.
  • 总结：提出研究者应与数据进行具身性的接触，关注数据如何“影响”自己，从而与数据“共同生成”知识。
    Instead of asking ‘what does this text mean?’, she asks ‘what does it produce?’ on its own, but also when ‘plugged into’ other texts (Lenz Taguchi 2012, p. 268).
  • 总结：强调研究实践的述行性——研究本身就在 enactment 现实。
    Our choices affect, they make differences, they enact realities, they can help bring into being what they also discover (Law and Urry 2004; Lenz Taguchi 2012).

Conclusion

本章总结了全文的核心论点。作者重申，采纳一个融合了后人文主义和新唯物主义洞见的过程-关系视角，能够超越传统社会学中的二元对立（如主体/客体，人类/自然），将农业实践重新概念化为一个由人类与非人类共同参与的、动态的“内-在行动”（intra-activity）过程。这一反本质主义和反决定论的视角，将世界从静态、可预测的结构，转变为一个动态、不可预测、不断涌现的流变过程。它强调了社会“结构”的脆弱性和变革的无处不在。因此，研究的焦点不应再是农民面临的制约，而应是人类与非人类能动性共同创造出的多样性和可能性。最终，作者强调，这一理论项目本质上也是一个政治项目，旨在激发不同的想象力，使另类世界成为可能，并鼓励研究者意识到自身研究实践的述行性力量，积极地去揭示和创造变革的契机。

  • 总结：强调“内-在行动”（intra-action）概念，即所有对象和能动性在一个不可分割的场域中相互构成。
    ...it invites us to move, ontologically, from identifying bodies as separate entities with distinct borders towards thinking in terms of processes of interdependence, of entanglement of ideas and materialities, of ‘intra-action’, i.e. the mutual constitution of all objects and agencies in an undivided field of existence (Barad 2007; Lenz Taguchi 2012).
  • 总结：指出该视角将世界从静态、结构化转变为动态、涌现的过程。
    It proposes an anti-essentialist and anti-determinist perspective, so that the world shifts from being static, rigid, structured, controllable, predictable, to one that is dynamic, changing, unpredictable, emerging.
  • 总结：强调社会“结构”从未像看起来那样稳固，每个时刻都提供了变革的机会。
    Indeed, it conceptualises the ‘structures’ of the social as never as stable and solid as they appear to us or as we wish them to be (Dépelteau 2018b). Rather, each moment offers an opportunity for change, for doing things differently.
  • 总结：将过程-关系社会学视为一个旨在“使其他世界成为可能”的政治项目。
    Indeed, process-relational sociology is not just a theoretical project, it is also a political project to make other worlds possible (see Gibson-Graham 2006), to spur different imaginations, to enable a shift towards actively envisioning and contributing to alternative world constructions.
  • 总结：呼吁研究者展示世界“未完成的制作过程”，使变革的多种可能性变得可见。
    It is an invitation to show the world in its ‘unfinished making’ (Phyyhtinen 2017, p. 306), to make visible the manifold openings for change, to highlight that it could always be otherwise.
