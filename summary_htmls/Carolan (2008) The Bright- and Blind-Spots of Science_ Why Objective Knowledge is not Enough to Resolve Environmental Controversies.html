The Bright- and Blind-Spots of Science: Why Objective Knowledge is not Enough to Resolve Environmental Controversies

Michael S. Carolan

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，环境争议的根源并不仅仅在于科学被外部力量（如金钱、意识形态）所“政治化”，而更深层次地内在于科学本身的认知结构、方法论多样性及社会实践之中。作者挑战了那种认为“更多、更好的科学”就能解决争议的朴素实证主义观点，主张科学本身因其内在的“盲点”——如学科壁垒、对“证据”和“共识”的社会建构、以及无法摆脱的价值预设——而成为争议的孳生地。因此，解决环境争议的出路不在于追求不可能实现的纯粹客观性，而在于建立更有效的政治协商机制，将科学背后隐藏的价值观暴露出来，进行公开的民主审议。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

为支撑其论点，作者综合运用了来自科学与技术研究（STS）、环境社会学及科学史的多个经典案例。主要论据包括：(1) Sarewitz对转基因玉米争议的分析，揭示了生态学和分子遗传学两种不同学科“视界”的冲突；(2) Oreskes对大陆漂移学说和板块构造理论共识形成过程的研究，说明科学共识是在没有绝对“证明”的情况下达成的社会过程；(3) 对气候变化争论的分析，指出《京都议定书》等政策议程的出现加剧了对科学不确定性的关注和利用；(4) NASA在“挑战者号”和“哥伦比亚号”航天飞机灾难中的决策失误，暴露了过度依赖量化数据的“客观性文化”的危害；(5) Jasanoff关于英美两国风险评估文化差异的研究，证明了“科学事实”的解释和权重受到本土法律和政治传统的深刻影响。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程层层递进。首先，他通过拉图尔的“火车轨道”隐喻，解构了科学的统一性，指出不同学科如同在各自轨道上运行的列车，其视野和方法论存在天然的局限与不兼容性。接着，他批判性地审视了“证明”和“共识”这两个科学话语中的核心概念，论证它们并非纯粹的认知产物，而是在特定社会和政治情境下被协商和界定的，因此极易成为利益相关方制造争议的工具。随后，文章深入剖析了科学活动中不可避免的价值预设，从模型构建中的置信区间设定，到“生态系统健康”、“生物多样性”等术语的规范性内涵，揭示了价值判断如何塑造“事实”的认知。最后，基于以上分析，作者得出结论：既然科学无法与价值分离，解决争议的关键就不应是压制价值、追求纯粹的事实，而应转向建立能够坦诚讨论和协商价值的民主程序，例如“共识会议”，从而让政策决策回归其政治本质。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的是一种批判性的理论综合与案例分析方法。作者并未进行一手经验研究，而是巧妙地编织了来自科学与技术研究（STS）、批判社会学和环境政策分析等领域的既有文献和二手案例。通过对这些案例（如转基因玉米、气候变化、NASA）的重新诠释，他将它们置于一个统一的理论框架下，即科学的内在 epistemic (认知) 局限性是环境争议的核心驱动力之一。这种方法的核心在于概念重构——将一个看似是“科学问题”的现象，重新界定为一个深刻的“社会与政治问题”。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文中穿插了多个具有说服力的案例以阐明其抽象论点。转基因玉米争议被用来说明学科分歧如何导致对同一现象（墨西哥玉米中的转基因DNA）产生截然相反的解读。板块构造理论的接受史则作为一个反例，说明在政策利害关系较小时，科学共识可以相对顺利地在缺乏绝对证据的情况下形成。与之相对，全球气候变化的案例则展示了当科学结论触及巨大的经济和政治利益时，科学内部固有的不确定性是如何被放大和利用，以阻碍政策行动。NASA的灾难案例则具体地揭示了对“客观”量化数据的迷信如何压制了关键的、非量化的、情境性的质性判断，最终导致灾难性后果。

导论 (Introduction)

本章节设定了文章的核心议题。作者首先承认关于科学受到外部力量（如意识形态、金钱）扭曲的现有研究，但指出这类研究隐含了一个前提：即“真实”的科学是脱离政治、确定且价值中立的。作者对此提出异议，认为必须将目光转向科学的内部过程，因为环境争议在很大程度上是科学自身实践的产物。文章旨在探索一条少有人走的路，即揭示科学本身如何孕育冲突，而非仅仅是外部力量的被动受害者。

  • 总结了当前学术界对科学话语受外部力量影响的普遍关注。
    Much has been written of late concerning the distorting forces that shape scientific discourse (Austin and Phoenix, 2005; Freudenburg and Gramling, 2002; Kleinman, 1995; Kraft, 2000; McCright and Dunlap, 2000, 2003).
  • 提出本文的中心论点：主张分析应转向科学内部，因为环境争议部分源于科学自身的内在过程。
    Instead, this article highlights why we would do well to turn our gaze back upon science. In doing this, we find environmental controversies to be, at least in part, the result of processes internal to science. (Carolan, 2008, p. 725)
  • 明确指出本文将避开“科学政治化”的传统路径，转而探讨科学本身如何导致专家之间的立场不兼容。
    Thus, rather than walking down the well-worn path concerning the politicization of science (e.g. Kitcher, 2001; McCright and Dunlap, 2000, 2003; Rayner, 2003), I opt for a different, less traveled route: to highlight that environmental contestation... need not be linked solely to politics or ideology but to science itself. (Carolan, 2008, p. 726)

科学中“看”的多种方式 (The Multiple Ways of ‘Seeing’ in Science)

本章论述科学并非一个统一的整体，而是由众多具有不同方法论和认知框架的学科组成。作者运用拉图尔的“火车轨道”隐喻，说明每个学科都有其特定的“轨道”，无法看到轨道之外的复杂现实。这种学科壁垒导致了对同一环境问题（如转基因玉米）的不同、甚至冲突的解读。作者进一步指出，方法论的选择本身就塑造了何为“证据”和“事实”，而科学家对其方法的偏好，混合了认知与情感因素，这进一步加剧了学科间的认知鸿沟。

  • 引用STS文献，指出界定科学与非科学的“本质”标准难以捉摸，科学的边界是社会性建构的。
    As has been well detailed, ‘essential’ criteria upon which to define science from non-science have proven elusive (Gieryn, 1983, 1995; Jasanoff, 1987a; Kuhn, 1970 [1962]; Mulkay, 1976).
  • 指出科学方法并非科学家所独有，其特殊性在于从业者调查的“审慎”态度。
    There is no methodological mode of inquiry used only by scientists (Haack, 2003).
  • 运用拉图尔的“火车轨道”隐喻，说明科学事实在特定的方法论和认知框架内才有效。
    Latour (1992: 266) provides us with the following suggestive metaphor: ‘Scientific facts are like trains, they do not work off their rails. You can extend the rails and connect them but you cannot drive a locomotive through a field.’
  • 论述学科划分的必要性与局限性，即学科将复杂世界分解为可管理的部分，但也导致了视野的割裂。
    The disciplines serve an important analytic purpose: namely, they break down into epistemologically manageable parts what is in reality an immensely complex world (Bhaskar, 1978; Carolan, 2005a, 2005b; Collier, 1994).
  • 引用Sarewitz对Quist和Chapela转基因玉米研究引发争议的分析，将其归因于生态学和分子遗传学这两个学科之间的根本性世界观冲突。
    Daniel Sarewitz (2004) makes a similar point when speaking of the controversy that erupted following the publication of an article in Nature by Ignacio Chapela... and his graduate student, David Quist. (Quist and Chapela, 2001)... The two sides of the debate represented two contrasting scientific views of nature... In disciplinary terms, these competing views map onto two distinctive intellectual schools in life science – ecology and molecular genetics (2004: 391).
  • 讨论Sarewitz对转基因玉米争议分析时，提及其他学者将争议归因于外部偏见。
    While others sought to locate the controversy that erupted over the publication of this article within various biases attributed to either side of the debate (McAfee, 2003; Scott, 2003), Sarewitz opted for a different route.
  • 援引女性主义科技研究的观点，批判所谓的“上帝视角”，强调所有知识都是有限和“境遇性”的。
    Since it is not possible for us to take the proverbial ‘view from nowhere’ – the so-called ‘God’s eye view’ (e.g. Fox-Keller, 1985; Haraway, 1991; Harding, 1991) – all knowledge (all ‘views’) is limited.
  • 指出世界的复杂性和开放性使得因果关系的确定变得困难，研究行为本身也会增加复杂性。
    In light of such ‘abundance’ (Feyerabend, 2000), cause-and-effect can be particularly difficult to establish. Compared to the closed environment of the laboratory, the world is an open system (Bhaskar, 1978). Thus, even the very act of studying the world can add complexity to it... (Rosenberg, 1994).
  • 论述风险评估在实践中无法穷尽所有变量，研究人员必须做出选择，这本身就引入了主观性。
    When assessing risk, for instance, it is simply not possible for researchers to take into consideration every variable and synergistic interaction (Jasanoff, 1999)... Rather, researchers focus upon a few key, empirically observable variables and relationships... (Cohrssen and Covello, 1989).
  • 强调“事实”的选择和排列方式受到制度规范、价值观和学科限制的影响。
    We choose, in other words, which facts to look at and which to ignore, which is to say we choose what constitutes a ‘fact’ and what does not (Jasanoff and Wynne, 1998; Wynne, 2001).
  • 指出方法论不仅塑造观察结果，还定义了何为“证据”和“事实”。
    And, as others have argued, the methodological outlook taken not only shapes what we observe, but also what counts as ‘evidence’ and thus as ‘fact’ (Carolan, 2004; Law and Urry, 2004; Mol, 2002).
  • 引用Oreskes的观点，解释科学家倾向于信任自己学科所产出的证据类型，这其中既有社会原因，也有智识、审美或伦理上的亲和力。
    When it comes to evaluating conflicting evidence, people tend to trust evidence of the kind which they and their close colleagues have dedicated their lives to obtaining... (Oreskes, 2004a: 375)

科学中的证明与共识 (Proof and Consensus in Science)

这一部分深入探讨了“证明”和“共识”在科学实践中的真实含义。作者认为，科学中不存在绝对的“证明”，共识（或STS术语中的“终结”）是一种社会过程，而非对真理的最终确认。当科学议题与重大政策利益挂钩时（如气候变化与《京都议定书》），这种内在的不确定性就会被放大，对“异常数据”的审查变得异常严格，从而制造出争议。作者还分析了企业界“播种科学”（或“钓鱼”）的策略，即寻找观点相符的专家来制造“科学争议”的假象，并利用媒体的“平衡报道”准则来影响公众舆论。

  • 以大陆漂移和板块构造理论为例，说明科学共识可以在缺乏直接证据或“证明”的情况下形成。
    Naomi Oreskes (1999, 2004a) provides an insightful analysis of how scientific acceptance occurs in science, even in the absence of proof.
  • 强调科学共识的达成是一个社会过程，即STS学者所称的“终结”（closure）。
    Yet, even after the mid-1980s, when plate tectonics was empirically confirmed, the consensus (or what science and technology studies scholars call ‘closure’ (Bijker, 1995; Gieryn, 1983, 1995; Jasanoff, 1987a)) around the veracity of this theory (or mechanism) has not been complete.
  • 指出即使在主流理论中，也存在少数科学家坚持替代性解释，例如“地球膨胀理论”。
    A small number of Earth scientists continue to argue for an alternative explanation of the data, for example, from which has emerged the Expanding Earth Theory (e.g. Scalera and Karl-Heinz, 2003).
  • 提出一个反思性问题：如果大陆漂移理论与公共政策相关，共识是否会如此迅速地形成？
    Oreskes (2004a: 371) asks this very question in the context of continental drift theory: namely, had continental drift theory been relevant to the question of public policy, would robust consensus have coalesced around this theory as quickly as it did?
  • 引用Sarewitz对Parkfield地震预测的案例，说明当政策风险较低时，科学界的接受度更高；若预测针对人口稠密区，审查将大大加强。
    With that, Sarewitz (2004) wonders if such a prediction would have been as strongly accepted by the scientific community had it been predicted for an earthquake in San Francisco.
  • 引用了气候变化研究领域的各类文献，以展示该议题的争议性。
    For another example, take the political and scientific contestation that surrounds the issue of global climate change (see e.g. Demeritt, 2001; McCright and Dunlap, 2000; Taylor and Buttel, 1992; Timmons Roberts, 2001; Yearly, 1996).
  • 重新诠释McCright和Dunlap的研究，认为《京都议定书》的出现是气候变化争议加剧的催化剂，因为它提高了科学“正确性”的政策赌注。
    Specifically, what effects did the looming Kyoto Protocol... have on creating pressures in the mid-1990s to reduce the (inherent) uncertainties surrounding the science of climate change? (McCright and Dunlap, 2003)
  • 引用Freudenburg的“播种科学”概念，描述企业如何资助立场相近的科学家，以制造科学不确定性的表象。
    Turning to a recent piece by William Freudenburg (2005), we find another example of how impending institutional factors can increase ‘the science’ – and thus the uncertainty and controversy – surrounding a particular issue.
  • 引用Gitlin和Freudenburg & Buttel的研究，说明媒体的“平衡报道”规范如何放大了少数派的观点，为政治不作为提供了借口。
    As Gitlin (1980) has argued, controversial news stories follow a ‘pro’ and ‘con’ model... This norm, however, results in the perpetuation of extreme viewpoints... which makes it an attractive mechanism for proponents of the status quo to create a justification for political inaction (Freudenburg and Buttel, 1999).
  • 以《蒙特利尔议定书》为例，指出当替代方案存在且经济利害关系较小时，即便科学证据不完美，采取预防性措施也更为可行。
    For example, DuPont had already invented CFC alternatives, so industry interests were minimally threatened with such a phase out (Rowlands, 1993).
  • 援引Oreskes在《科学》杂志上的文章，强调专家在人为气候变化问题上存在广泛共识，政策应遵循这一共识。
    As Oreskes (2004b) details in a widely discussed article in the journal Science, there is tremendous scientific consensus among the experts as to the existence of anthropogenic climate change.

为什么科学预设了价值陈述 (Why Science Presupposes Value Statements)

本章直接论证科学的价值负载性。作者指出，价值判断隐藏在科学实践的多个层面：从设定模型的置信区间（即决定可接受的风险水平），到使用“生态系统健康”、“污染”等本身就带有规范性色彩的术语。他以“生物多样性”为例，说明这个看似客观的指标，其定义和测量都取决于对“物种”这个概念的价值判断，而“物种问题”本身在生物学哲学中就充满争议。此外，对“不确定性”的界定和对证据的评估方式（如法庭上是孤立审查还是整体看待）也体现了深层的价值取向。因此，许多看似是“事实之争”的环境冲突，其本质是“价值之争”。

  • 指出科学术语（如生态系统健康、完整性、外来物种）暗含了对世界应有状态的偏好，即价值判断。
    Ecosystem health, ecosystem integrity, and alien species; terms such as these imply a preferred state of things. They imply, in other words, how we think the world ought to look (Carolan, 2006a).
  • 以生物多样性为例，说明将高多样性等同于“健康”或“优越”并非纯粹的科学结论，而是文化偏好。
    Yet again, science alone cannot hierarchically rank ecosystem states in terms of their inherent superiority (e.g. high biological diversity) or inferiority (e.g. low biological diversity) (Lackey, 2001; Lancaster, 2000). We make those preferences due to cultural beliefs as to what we think nature should look like (Kapustka and Landis, 1998).
  • 进一步解构“生物多样性”概念，指出其操作化定义取决于如何界定“物种”，而“物种问题”本身充满争议。
    As others have detailed, for example, how one chooses to operationalize and define ‘species’... alters significantly how biodiversity is empirically measured (Isacc et al., 2004).
  • 引用生物物种概念（BSC）的模糊性，说明物种界限的划分涉及主观判断，而非纯粹客观事实。
    As Mayr (who, along with Dobzhansky, 1937, first forwarded this concept), explains... (1970: 12). Yet, most biologists allow for some gene flow between species... (Hey, 2001). An answer to this question cannot be arrived at through objective measures alone; it is a matter of value judgments, not objective fact (Hendry et al., 2000).
  • 论述“不确定性”本身也是被定义的，取决于研究目标和何为重要问题的判断。
    Uncertainty is a product of how ‘objectives’ are defined according to the scientific problem at hand, which leads to different conceptions of what is important... (Sarewitz, 2004; Shackley et al., 1998; Wynne, 2001).
  • 以美国最高法院的判例（General Electric Co. v. Joiner）为例，说明法律体系对证据的评估标准（分开评估而非整体评估）会人为地提高不确定性。
    The US Supreme Court, for instance, ruled in General Electric Co. v. Joiner (1997) that judges should evaluate elements of evidence separately rather than in their totality.
  • 引用Jasanoff的观点，指出法院的这种裁决并非基于客观的证据标准，而是基于对世界应如何组织的默会假设。
    Rather, it involves claims about the way the world is, which are in reality based upon tacit assumptions concerning the way the world ought to be organized (Jasanoff, 2005b).
  • 引用Haack的论证，说明证据的整体性（interlocking）可以产生比单个证据加总更强的证明力，而法院的裁决忽视了这一点。
    As Haack explains: ‘[E]vidence... may interlock... to support the claim... much more strongly than any of these pieces of evidence alone could do.’ (2005: S69-S70).
  • 总结指出，许多表面上的事实争议实际上是潜在的价值冲突。
    That is, while these controversies may appear on the surface to rest on disputed questions of fact, beneath often reside differing positions of value; values that can give shape to differing understandings of what ‘the facts’ are. (Carolan, 2006b)
  • 引用Jasanoff等人的跨国比较研究，证明不同国家的政策环境、法律传统和专家文化会影响对科学证据的解读和权重。
    According to this research, ‘scientific evidence was shown to carry different weight in different policy environments...’ (Jasanoff, 2005a: 17). (See also Gillespie et al., 1979; Jasanoff, 1986, 1987b, 1995; Brickman et al., 1985).

科学：认识其局限性 (Science: Recognizing its Limits)

在结论部分，作者提出了政策建议。他认为，出路不在于追求更多的科学数据以消除不确定性——这既不可能也无意义——而在于建立能够公开讨论科学背后隐藏价值观的政治机制。他以NASA的“客观性文化”为例，批判了对量化数据的迷信如何排斥了重要的质性知识，导致了灾难。作者主张，政治家不能再以“等待更多科学证据”为借口推卸责任，而应坦承其决策背后的价值取向。作为具体方案，他推崇“共识会议”或“公民陪审团”这类审议式民主机制，认为它们能够让普通公民在听取专家意见后，就复杂的科技政策问题做出深思熟虑的判断，从而实现对科学的真正公众参与，避免公众在“被动消费者”和“激进抗议者”之间摇摆。

  • 提出核心政策建议：需要的不是更多科学，而是更好的机制来揭示和讨论科学背后的隐藏价值。
    What is needed is not more and/or better science but more and/or better mechanisms to bring those hidden values... to the forefront of the discussion (Fischer, 2000; Sarewitz, 2004; Wynne, 2001, 2002).
  • 指出单纯依靠科学无法在不同行动方案间做出选择，因为行动本身就包含了对未来的信念。
    Science itself rarely provides sufficient basis for selecting between different courses of action, given that such action inevitably involves beliefs as to what the future should look like (Sarewitz, 1996).
  • 批评科学辩论中对可量化性的偏好，认为这体现了一种对客观性的迷恋。
    In scientific debates, only that which can be quantified matters. As others have argued, calculability implies objectivity, which, in turn, implies that which is scientific (Daston, 1992).
  • 以Feldman对NASA“客观性文化”的分析为例，说明过度依赖量化风险评估，忽视质性问题，是导致航天飞机灾难的原因之一。
    For example, Feldman (2004), in his examination of the ‘culture of objectivity’ at NASA, describes some of the pitfalls that came from placing too much evaluative weight on ‘objective’ knowledge in the context of evaluating risks.
  • 批判某些环境“怀疑论者”将人类关怀简化为可量化的指标（如生命数量），而忽略了生活的质量。
    Many environmental ‘skeptics’ prefer to reduce human concerns to the lowest common denominator, which can be easily quantified... (e.g. Bolch and Lyons, 1993; DeGregori, 2001, 2004; Huber, 1999) As Lomborg (2001: 11) remarks, ‘[my goal is] counting lives lost from different problems.’
  • 倡导建立新的政治机制，让事实和价值能够被一同讨论，迫使政治家为其决策背后的价值判断负责。
    In doing this, both facts and values must be discussed together, so that a better understanding can emerge as to how the latter (values) gives shape to interpretations of the former (facts).
  • 推荐共识会议（或公民陪审团）作为一种有效的公众参与机制，让普通公民能就复杂的科技政策做出理性的政策建议。
    One mechanism successfully employed to achieve such ends is the consensus conference (also known as ‘citizens’ juries’)... these citizens deliberate among themselves to arrive at policy guidelines... (Fuller, 2002).
  • 引用Fuller的观点，认为共识会议提供了介于“被动消费”与“激进抗议”之间的第三条公众参与科学的道路。
    Without something like consensus conferences to focus collective thought and action, people will respond as either passive consumers or militant protesters. Neither constitutes an adequate public engagement with science. (2006: 170)
