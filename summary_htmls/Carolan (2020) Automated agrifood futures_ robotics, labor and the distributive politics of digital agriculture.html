Automated agrifood futures: robotics, labor and the distributive politics of digital agriculture

Michael Carolan

全文概要总结

作为一名长期关注农业技术社会学与政治经济学的学者，我认为Michael Carolan的这篇文章是一项极具洞察力的研究。它超越了对数字农业自动化技术的简单评判，而是深入到了其背后复杂的社会建构过程与政治意涵。作者巧妙地融合了STS（科学技术与社会研究）、政治本体论和未来社会学的前沿理论，对农业自动化这一现象进行了深刻的解构。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：农业自动化远非一种中性的技术解决方案，而是一种由“虚构性预期”（fictional expectations）驱动、并具有深刻分配政治后果的社会技术实践。作者主张，我们不应纠结于“自动化是什么”（what automation is），而应聚焦于“自动化做什么”（what automation does）。通过分析其在特定社会技术装置（dispositif）中的运作方式，我们可以揭示这些平台如何通过塑造对未来的想象（特别是关于劳动力市场、移民政策和农场主年龄的想象）来重构当下的权力关系、劳动模式和知识体系，并最终可能固化一种压制异议、消解替代性未来的“反政治”（anti-political）格局。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

论据主要来源于对三个相互关联群体的深度质性访谈：1）已经采用自动化系统的美国农场主（45位）；2）从事自动化技术工程、制造、安装及维修的公司雇员（36位）；3）农场劳工（包括移民和本地工人）及劳工组织代表（24位）。这些来自不同行动者的一手资料，为作者展示自动化技术在真实世界中的多重意涵和复杂影响提供了坚实的经验基础。例如，农场主将投资自动化视为对未来劳动力短缺的“赌注”；大型农业公司可能因自动化而改变其在移民问题上的政治立场；以及未经认证的技术人员在知识产权法律下被剥夺维修权利的困境。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程展现了高超的理论整合能力。首先，他引入Jens Beckert关于资本主义中“虚构性预期”的理论，指出农民投资自动化并非基于当前成本效益的纯粹理性计算，而是基于对未来劳动力市场（受特朗普移民政策影响）、信贷利率和自身老龄化等因素的预期。这种预期具有“述行性”（performativity），即通过行动将想象中的未来变为现实。其次，他运用福柯的“装置”（dispositif）和拉图尔的分布式本体论视角，将自动化技术从一个孤立的“物”解构为一个异质性的集合体，该集合体包括话语（如“养活世界”）、法律（如知识产权法）、制度（如维修认证体系）和实践。这一分析框架成功地将讨论从宏观的“技术替代劳动”转向了微观政治层面，如维修权力的转移、技能的重新定义以及依赖关系的形成。最后，他借鉴Andrew Barry关于“政治”（politics）与“政争”（the political）的区分，批判性地指出当前农业自动化的某些发展路径具有“反政治”特征，即它们通过技术和制度安排，提前关闭了关于农业未来的争议空间。

4. 方法 (Method)

研究采用了严谨的质性研究方法。通过对美国四个州（加州、伊利诺伊州、爱荷华州、内布拉斯加州）和加拿大的多方利益相关者进行105次半结构化访谈，确保了数据的丰富性和多视角。研究对象的选择——从大型农场主到无证维修工，再到移民劳工——体现了对权力结构中不同位置行动者经验的全面关照。作者采用滚雪球抽样法，并持续进行访谈直至达到“理论饱和”，这保证了研究的深度和信度。所有访谈均在2016年美国总统大选后进行，这一时间点的选择极具现实意义，使得研究能够敏锐地捕捉到特定政治环境下“虚构性预期”的形成过程。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文章以一个生动的“水刀机器人”（water knife bot）收割生菜的场景开篇，直观地展示了自动化技术不仅仅是机器，更是一种嵌入了特定政治和资本主义想象的实体。其他核心案例包括：爱荷华州奶农安装机器人挤奶系统，将其视为对未来劳动力成本上涨的“预付”和改善家庭生活质量的方式；年迈的农场主利用无人机和自动化拖拉机来延长自己的职业生涯，挑战了关于农民年龄和退休的传统观念；以及独立的维修技师因约翰迪尔等公司的知识产权限制而无法合法维修“智能”设备，揭示了技能和知识在数字时代被重新定义的政治过程。

  • 技术的政治性: 作者开篇即指出，观察到的“水刀机器人”不仅仅是一台机器，它还内含政治属性，并体现了资本主义的未来想象，这些想象使得某些生活方式得以实现，而另一些则被抛弃。
    It had politics (Haraway 2003; Winner 1980) and futured (capitalist) imaginaries (Beckert 2016) that helped make real certain forms of life over others (Foucault 2003).

    Agrifood scholars have linked the dynamic structural changes in agriculture over the last century to a wide range of factors, including capitalism’s ability to (eventually) overcome biophysical barriers through appropriation and substitution (e.g. Goodman, Sorj, and Wilkinson 1987; Mann 1990; Mann and Dickinson 1978), competing views of what it means to be a ‘good’ farmer (e.g. Burton 2004; McGuire, Morton, and Cast 2013), globalization (e.g. Koc and Dahlberg 1999; Swinnen 2007), and a treadmill-like logic that reinforces intensiﬁcation and corporate dependencies (Nicholls and Altieri 1997; Obach 2007; Ward 1993).
  • 资本主义与虚构性预期: 作者引入了“虚构性预期”这一核心概念，指出资本主义的再生产依赖于对未来的想象。这种预期在社会行动中无处不在，但长期被社会学家所忽略。
    A handful of scholars (e.g. Beckert 2016; Bourdieu 1979) have argued that capitalist reproduction is possible only because of a speciﬁc theory of ﬁctional expectations... (Boldizzoni 2017, 260–261)

    Anticipatory geographies accomplish this feat by ensuring a continuous ﬂow of investment, consumption, trade, and government spending (Anderson 2010).
  • 信贷的虚构性: 以信贷为例，它允许企业利用基于未来产出、市场价格等想象而获得的尚未赚取的资源进行经济活动。
    Take the ‘ﬁction’ (Polanyi 2001) of credit, which enables ﬁrms to engage in economic activities with yet-to-be-earned resources obtained on the premise of imagined future outputs, market prices, demand, weather, and proﬁts.
  • 资本主义对创新的激励: 资本主义通过激励行动者不断想象和开辟新路径，成功地维持了其体系。
    ‘Despite very consequential moments of crisis,’ Beckert argues (2016, 96), ‘capitalism has succeeded more than any other economic and social system in inspiring actors to believe that they should restlessly imagine and embark on new paths.’
  • 福柯的生命政治: 作者引用福柯来强调，某些未来被实现的同时，另一些未来则被“任其死亡”，这揭示了未来塑造过程中的权力运作。
    ...making the subject of digital agriculture a ﬁtting one for interrogating how ﬁctional expectations are made real, while others are, to evoke Foucault (2003, 241), left to die.
  • 农业的未来导向性: 农业本身就是一种内在的未来导向活动，其行动、现在与过去都充满了预见性。
    As discussed in greater detail below, agriculture itself is inherently futured – anticipatory in a way that is equal parts future, present and past (Anderson 2010).

    Our quest to feed future bodies, which we anticipate will eat a particular way, which for a lot of the world is taken to mean more meat, less millets, sorghum and pulses (Neo 2018), is of consequence.
  • 政治本体论与物的稳定性: 引入政治本体论，指出任何客体的连贯性都只是一种分析效果，物是不断被争议和制造出来的。

    One way to talk about this distributed ontology is through Foucault’s (1980) concept of the dispositif. The term is a reminder to decenter ‘things’ (e.g. a robotic milking parlor)... (Foucault 1980, 94).

    This post-structural move provides tools to engage in a needed unpacking when it comes to talking about the concepts of ‘automation’ and ‘skill’, showing both to be accomplishments of a distributive network of human and nonhuman phenomena (Richardson and Bissell 2017, 2).
  • 政治与政争的区分: 文章结尾将引用Barry的理论，区分作为技术实践的“政治”（politics）和作为异议空间的“政争”（the political），并探讨自动化景观在何种程度上扼杀了后者。

Politics, according to this framework, is ‘a set of technical practices, forms of knowledge and institutions’, while the political is ‘an index of the space of disagreement’ (Barry 2002, 270).

Farming for tomorrow today: setting the conceptual stage

本章深入阐述了文章的核心理论框架。作者首先对比了社会学（追溯过去）与经济学（回溯未来）在解释当下事件上的不同倾向，进而引入了将“未来”视为社会事实的理论传统。通过对布迪厄关于卡比尔人社会变迁的研究、韦伯对西里西亚农民的观察以及对绿色革命的重新解读，作者论证了资本主义如何将一种基于计算、利润和抽象未来的时间观植入农业实践。最后，作者将这一框架应用于分析当前的“机器人革命”，并引入“装置”（dispositif）概念，为理解数字技术的复杂性与多重性提供了理论工具。

  • 社会学与经济学的时序差异: 引用Abbott的观点，社会学家倾向于从过去解释现在，而经济学家则从对未来回报的预期来解释当下的决策。
    Abbott (2005) observed that sociologists and economists approach explaining present events diﬀerently. In his words, ‘While sociologists see present events as a ﬁnal outcome emerging from the past, economists reason backwards from the future: Decisions are explained by the present value of expected future rewards’ (p. 406).
  • 社会理论对历史的重视: 强调社会学理论传统对过去的重视，如马克思的历史唯物主义、福柯的考古学/谱系学方法和涂尔干的历史主义。
    It is hard to argue with the point that sociology places considerable analytic and conceptual weight on the past – e.g. Marxian historical materialism (Marx 1993), Foucault’s (1982) archaeological/genealogical approach to the history of thought, Durkheimian historicism (Durkheim 1997; see also, e.g. Bellah 1959).
  • 制度经济学中的路径依赖: 指出制度经济学通过“路径依赖”概念也强调了过去的重要性。
    The ﬁeld of institutional economics, for example, through its concept of path dependency (see, e.g. North 1995), places signiﬁcant explanatory weight on the past...
  • 将未来视为社会事实的学者: 列举了一批将（想象的）未来视为对当下具有因果效力的社会事实的社会理论家。
    Meanwhile, a handful of social theorists have demonstrated sensitivity to the (imagined) future’s causal eﬃcacy on the present, approaching ‘the future’ as eﬀectively a Durkheimian social fact (see e.g. Beckert 2016; Bourdieu 1979; Giddens 1999; Koselleck 2004; Luhmann 1976).
  • 从传统社会到现代社会的时间观转变: 引用卢曼的观点，指出从传统社会到资产阶级社会的结构变迁，使得时间结构变得能够容纳更高的复杂性。
    It was only the structural change from traditional to bourgeois society in the seventeenth and eighteenth centuries which dissolved this older notion and replaced it by a temporal structure that contains in itself the possibility of higher complexity. (Luhmann 1976, 130–131)
  • 资本主义与开放的未来: 引用Beckert的观点，资本主义制度化了一种经济活动组织方式，其中行动者面向一个开放且不可预见的未来。
    In contrast to traditional economic systems, capitalism institutionalizes an organization of economic activity in which actors orient themselves toward an open and unforeseeable future. (Beckert 2015)
  • 布迪厄对卡比尔人的研究: 详细介绍了布迪厄对阿尔及利亚卡比尔人社会经济转型的民族志研究，揭示了传统想象如何被市场、金钱、计算和未来利润的逻辑所侵蚀。
    Bourdieu (1979; see also 2013) oﬀers one of most nuanced and empirically detailed ethnographic accounts of this transition...
  • 传统储备与资本主义积累的区分: 引用布迪厄，清晰地区分了为未来消费而进行的“储备”和以生产性使用为目的的“资本主义积累”。
    We must then make a clear distinction between putting [aside] reserves... and capitalist accumulation, ‘creative saving’ that leads to setting aside indirect goods with a view to their productive use. (Bourdieu 2013, 55)
  • 布迪厄的“结构化的结构”: 引用布迪厄对“经济习性”（economic dispositions）的论述，指出这些习性是结构化的结构，同时又作为结构化的力量，组织着日常生活中的经济实践。
    ...‘these dispositions are structured structures which function as structuring structures, orienting and organizing the economic practices of daily life...’ (Bourdieu 1979, vii–viii, my emphasis).
  • 经济学的述行性: 借鉴卡隆启发的经济地理学，指出经济学模型不仅是描述世界（描述性），更是干预和改变世界（述行性）。
    This argument also overlaps with the central premise of Michel Callon-inspired economic geography (e.g. Callon 2016; Callon and Muniesa 2005; MacKenzie and Millo 2003)... The discipline of economics, according to this literature, can best be described as performative (enacting worlds) rather than purely constantive (describing worlds) (Austin 1961).
  • 韦伯对西里西亚农民的观察: 引用韦伯的经典案例，说明前资本主义的农民在面对旨在提高生产力的资本主义激励时，其行为逻辑（满足于维持现有生活水平）与资本主义的积累逻辑截然不同。
    Weber ([1927] 2003) describes how Silesian peasants responded to capitalist incentives in the nineteenth century... Yet instead of working more, the landless laborers chose to work less, satisﬁed in the maintenance of their existing standard of living (see also Beckert 2016, 25).
  • 绿色革命的物质性与时间政治: 指出绿色革命不仅改变了食物景观的物质性，更改变了这些空间的时间政治。
    The green revolution has been criticized for its role in changing the materiality of foodscapes (Carolan 2018c; Patel 2013). Less discussed in the literature, if at all, is its role in changing the temporal politics of these spaces.
  • 绿色革命的“实践包”: 描述了绿色革命作为一个包含信贷、现代投入、价格激励等在内的完整“实践包”，如何塑造了一种特定的未来-现在关系。
    ...constituted what was described at the time as a complete ‘package of practices’ (Borlaug 2006, 4; Farmer 1986, 176), ‘including credit, modern inputs, price incentives, marketing facilities, and technical advices’ (Chandra Lal Das 1993, 129).
  • 绿色革命与农民的转变: 通过一份1969年的田野报告，展示了绿色革命如何将印度旁遮普的“农民”转变为预期未来市场价格下跌并不断追求增产的“计算的行动者”。
    A ﬁrsthand account of this temporal shift was captured in a 1969 article based on site visits to two of the principal districts of Punjab, India, where the green revolution was well underway (Ladejinsky 1969).
  • 农业“踏车理论”的前瞻性: 重新诠释了Cochrane的农业踏车理论，指出其不仅是关于路径依赖的回顾性理论，也隐含了一种面向未来的时间取向，即行动者预期商品价格持续下降和产量持续增加。
    The agricultural treadmill famously described by Cochrane (1958), for instance, has been interpreted as a theory of agricultural change that looks back... Less discussed, however, is the theory’s implied forward-looking orientation. (Levins and Cochrane 1996, 550)
  • 生产主义意识形态的时间要素: 指出生产主义的逻辑不仅是追求高产，还预设了未来的农民也将同样被生产主义意识形态所驱动。
    Productivism presumes the following temporal element, namely, it anticipates that future farmers will be animated by productivist ideologies too. (Ward 1993)
  • 机器人的不同政治属性: 强调并非所有“机器人”都具有相同的政治属性；有些是促进异议的，而另一些则通过强化现状来扮演“反政治”的角色。
    Not all ‘bots’, however, have the same politics (Bissell 2018; Carolan 2017a, 2017b, 2018b; Warf 2001), which is to say, some are political while others are more anti-political in their politics (Barry 2002).
  • 遏制激进未来的策略: 引用Braun的观点，指出某些技术路径充当了“对潜在更激进未来的战略性遏制”，它们通过动员生产主义想象来维持现状。
    Those in the latter category seek to maintain the status quo, acting ‘as a strategic containment of potentially more radical futures’ (Braun 2015, 1) by enlisting productivist imaginaries.
  • “装置”与“组装”概念的融合使用: 作者阐明其为何将福柯的“装置”与拉图尔、卡隆等人的“组装”概念并用，旨在综合两者的优点，弥补各自的理论短板（如ANT对权力的忽视，或福柯理论的结构主义倾向）。
    Note, for example, Ash, Kitchin, and Leszczynski’s (2018, 36–37) invitation to ‘critically reﬂect upon the wider dispositif or assemblage of the digital’, while Bellanova’s (2017, p. 329) examination of digital mass surveillance claims to be ‘building on governmentality studies and Actor-Network-Theory […] using the conceptual tool of dispositifs’ (other examples include, e.g., Braun 2014 and Coté 2014). Using the concepts in tandem allows the argument to deﬂect potential critiques that risk weakening either if used in isolation (e.g. ANT struggles to account for power [Elder-Vass 2015]; Foucault’s post structuralism is more structuralism than post [Butler 1989]).

Methods

Findings: robots have politics and time

这一核心章节展示了研究的主要发现，分为两个部分。第一部分“制定劳动力市场和扰乱农民的持续性”探讨了农民对未来劳动力短缺的预期如何驱动其投资自动化，这种行为具有述行性，即它通过减少对劳动力的需求，从而在客观上促成了其所预期的“劳动力短缺”的乡村景象。自动化不仅替代了雇佣劳动，也替代了家庭劳动，有时反而增强了农场的经济韧性，并可能延长年迈农民的职业生涯。第二部分“去中心化的‘机器人’：装置和政治本体论”则深入分析了“自动化”和“技能”等概念的社会建构性。作者指出，自动化不仅是技术偏向的，也是“证书偏向的”，知识产权法限制了未经认证的技术人员的维修权。这要求我们采取一种微观政治的视角，将技能理解为分布在人与非人网络中的一种能力，而非个体固有的属性。最终，这种分析揭示了技术如何塑造了新的依赖关系和权力结构。

  • 劳动力的空间转移: 对未来劳动力短缺的预期和相应的自动化投资，促使劳动力从农业地区转移到其他地区，寻找建筑、清洁和餐饮服务等行业的工作，以满足这些行业对灵活劳动力的需求。
    It helps makes real a rurality lacking an available workforce, as those laborers become displaced to metropolitan areas and rural amenity destinations where underskilled (e.g. immigrant) labor provide needed ﬂexibility to accommodate shifting demands across sectors like construction, cleaning, and food service (Nelson, Nelson, and Trautman 2014).
  • 农业游说团体的政治立场转变: 历史上，严重依赖移民劳动力的果蔬产业是亲移民游说团体的重要力量。然而，随着自动化的发展，这种立场可能会发生逆转。
    These industries are especially dependent upon immigrant labor and as such have been historically among the most powerful voices in the pro-immigrant lobby (see e.g. Bunge, Haddon, and Morath 2017).
  • 大型公司的自动化研发投资: 像Driscoll's这样的大型农业公司正大力投资机器人研发，以应对预期的劳动力短缺问题，并公开表示希望未来能完全自动化其生产过程。
    Driscoll’s controls roughly a third of the entire U.S. berry market, which includes commanding nearing two-thirds of the organic strawberry market (Goodyear 2017). They make no secret of wanting to someday fully automate their strawberry production process (McEwan 2018).
  • 农民老龄化与自动化: 自动化技术被一些年长的农民视为延长其职业生涯的工具，挑战了关于农民年龄增长必然导致退出农业的传统看法。
    Operators 65 years and older are the fastest growing population (Obudzinski 2016). Rather than believing such tends are unsustainable... a number of respondents viewed the average farmer age as having no discernable endpoint thanks to automation.
  • 关于自动化与就业的对立观点: 文献中对自动化与就业的关系存在两种截然相反的观点：一种认为自动化导致失业（工作岗位流失），另一种则强调其创造了新的（通常是更高技能的）就业机会。
    Some reduce these platforms to jobs lost (e.g. Attewell 1987; Braverman 1998 [1974]; Nica 2016). Others, noting that someone has to manufacture, install, and repair these machines, emphasize jobs gained because of automation (e.g. McKinsey Global Institute 2017). Indeed, it is a fairly standard argument in economics that technology creates more jobs than it destroys over time (Allen 2015).
  • 技术变革的技能偏向性: 一个普遍的论点是，技术变革具有技能偏向性，即它倾向于用更高技能、更高薪酬的工作来取代低技能工作。
    It is also commonly argued that technological change is skill-biased, as lower skilled jobs are replaced by higher skilled, higher paying opportunities (Fernandez 2001).
  • 自动化的普遍性: 引用哲学家斯蒂格勒的观点，指出自动化是生命本身的核心，从生物细胞的复制到人类的心理和社会习惯，都存在自动化机制，因此将自动化仅仅与资本主义生产模式挂钩是不全面的。
    As French philosopher Stiegler (2017) reminds us, far from exclusively a concept tied to understanding modes of capitalist production, automation lies at the center of life itself. (Stiegler 2015)
  • 习惯（Habitus）作为一种自动化: 斯蒂格勒的观点与布迪厄的“习惯”概念有相似之处，即通过实践、常规和社会化，人们内化了新的自动化行为模式。
    His argument ought to sound familiar to social theorists, given its parallels with Bourdieu’s (1977) concept of habitus.
  • 知识产权对维修权的限制: 引用了关于约翰迪尔公司的主张，即农民并不真正“拥有”他们购买的设备，而只是获得了操作车辆的“默示许可”，这限制了农民自行或寻求第三方维修的权利。
    A few years back the farm implement company John Deere, in a letter to the U.S. Copyright Oﬃce, argued that farmers receive ‘an implied license for the life of the vehicle to operate the vehicle’ (as quoted in Wiens 2015).
  • 数字技能的微观政治: 引入Richardson和Bissell的观点，主张除了关注工会等宏观制度空间外，还应关注数字技能的微观政治，即将技能理解为一种延伸到“人之外”、分布在人与非人行动者网络中的能动性。
    This decentered approach has been called a micropolitical articulation of digital skill, to compliment the more familiar macropolitical articulation that situates labor politics within ‘discrete institutional spaces, such as trade unions...’ (Richardson and Bissell 2017, 2).
  • 美国历史上的教育投资: 回顾了美国在20世纪初的“高中运动”和二战后的“G.I.法案”，指出政府在教育上的大规模投资是实现社会流动性和“美国梦”的关键。
    It was known as the High School Movement as it raised the rate of high school enrollment from 18 percent in 1910 to 73 percent in 1940 and made U.S. adults among the highest educated in the world (Goldin and Katz 2008). Investment continued with the 1944 G.I. Bill... (Mettler 2005).
  • “好农民”形象的演变: 农民将自己适应新技术的能力视为“与时俱进”，这与传统的“好农民”形象（如坚毅的个人主义）有所不同，但仍然符合一种自我叙述。
    Such a narrative also aligns well with (conventional) farmers’ own accounts of what it means to be a ‘good’ farmer, where images of rugged individualism are hailed (Bell, Hullinger, and Brislen 2015; Carolan 2018d).
  • 现代性的神话: 引用拉图尔的观点，指出那种认为技术知识完全内在于某个个体（如精通科技的孙女）的现代观念是一种虚构；技术能力是通过连接到网络而获得的。
    According to the framework articulated in this paper, Beatrice was tech savvy, just not in the modern sense that Latour (1993) points out to be a total ﬁction. Tech savviness is not a trait held ‘in’ any one body...

Conclusion: politics are political

结论部分重申并深化了核心论点。作者认为，对数字农业的讨论必须超越简单的宏观经济指标（如就业增减），而应关注其微观政治层面，即特定社会技术装置如何赋能或约束行动者，并使某些（未来）路径比其他路径更具现实性。作者再次运用Barry的“政治”与“政争”的区分，指出当前一些数字技术平台（如限制维修权的拖拉机）具有“反政治”特征，它们通过技术和法律手段固化了现状，关闭了关于农业未来的争议空间，使另类的未来变得“不可想象”。文章最后呼吁，我们需要对自身预见性行动的述行性保持批判性敏感，并将关于机器人和数字农业的辩论与知识产权、教育政策、移民政策和社会正义等更广泛的议题联系起来，同时需要进一步研究某些预见性想象是如何获得“延伸性”并成为主导力量的。

  • 超越宏观政治的分析: 政策回应往往采用宏观政治的理解，将辩论简化为就业增减等可量化指标，而忽视了微观政治的复杂性。
    Policy responses to this changing landscape have tended to adopt macropolitical understandings, reducing debates to jobs lost and gained and other variables that can be aggregated but that do not capture the micropolitics experienced on the ground. (Eastwood et al. 2017; The Economist 2016)
  • 拓扑学式的理解: 与其将技能视为包含在离散个体和空间中的地形学现象，不如将其视为拓扑学的褶子（enfoldings）。
    Moreover, conventional accounts tend to be topographical, seeing skills as contained within discrete bodies and spaces, as opposed to approaching these phenomena as topological enfoldings (Deleuze 1993).
  • 关注具体的赋能与约束: 本文旨在为辩论注入一个关注具体情境下的赋能与约束的立场，这些赋能与约束使得某些（未来的）社会技术装置比其他装置更为真实。
    This paper seeks to invest the debate with a position that attends to the in-situ forms of enablement and constraint that make certain (futured) socio-technical dispositifs more real than others (Richardson and Bissell 2017).
  • Barry的理论框架: 再次引用Barry关于“政治”与“政争”的区分，作为分析自动化技术政治后果的核心工具。
    Barry’s (2002) analytic distinction between ‘politics’ and the ‘political’ is good to think with in this respect. Politics... is ‘a set of technical practices, forms of knowledge and institutions’... while the political is ‘an index of the space of disagreement’ (Barry 2002, 270).
  • 数字技术的反政治性: 以约翰迪尔为例，指出其知识产权制度和用户协议这一“装置”，通过限制农民的维修权，牺牲了那些旨在挑战现状的未来可能性。
    As Carolan (2017a, 2017b, 2018b) highlights, as certain digital assemblages make repairs illegal, acts reserved for only those suﬃciently credentialized, they risk causing farmers to forget how to repair their equipment.
  • 数字鸿沟与空间不平等: 强调数字农业对基础设施（如宽带）的依赖，加剧了城乡之间以及乡村内部已经存在的社会经济发展不平衡。
    It has been estimated that the 450 million acres of corn in the U.S. produces 14.4 million gigabytes of data annually... (Weis 2017)... Meanwhile, certain parts of the countryside have only dialup and satellite Internet access (West and Karsten 2016), exacerbating already-existing socio-economic developmental asymmetries.
  • 想象的“延伸性”: 引用Jasanoff的“延伸性”概念，指出未来的研究需要关注某些预见性想象是如何从个体或小群体起源，并最终在更广泛的领域获得支配力的。
    I am thinking of the term as it is used by Jasanoﬀ (2015, 32), which speaks to how ‘views and practices originating with individuals or small groups can acquire governing force across much wider domains than their original locations and circumstances of production’.
