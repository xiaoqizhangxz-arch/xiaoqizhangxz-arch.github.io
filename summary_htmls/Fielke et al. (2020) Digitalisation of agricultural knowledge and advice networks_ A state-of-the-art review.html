Digitalisation of agricultural knowledge and advice networks: A state-of-the-art review

Simon Fielke, Bruce Taylor, Emma Jakku

全文概要总结

作为一名长期致力于农业创新系统与社会技术转型研究的学者，我认为Fielke、Taylor与Jakku的这篇综述文章，精准地捕捉到了当前农业领域一个至关重要的转型议题。作者们超越了将数字化仅仅视为提升生产效率的技术工具集的狭隘视角，而是将其定位为一场深刻的、将重塑整个农业创新系统（Agricultural Innovation System, AIS）的社会技术革命。文章的贡献在于，它系统性地梳理了数字化如何冲击并重构传统上由各类顾问（advisors）构成的知识与咨询网络（knowledge and advice networks），并前瞻性地指出了由此引发的在连通性（connectivity）、透明度（transparency）和治理（governance）三大维度上的核心挑战与机遇。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：农业的数字化转型并非简单的技术采纳过程，而是一个深刻的系统性变革，它正在瓦解并重构传统的农业知识与咨询网络。这一转型过程集中表现为三个相互关联的核心趋势：第一，人、技术与价值链之间的“连通性”将空前增强；第二，这种增强的连通性将驱动并同时被农业实践与信息交互的“透明度”所塑造；第三，在数字化进程中，平衡不同利益相关者（农民、企业、政府、消费者）的优先事项，将对现有的“治理”模式构成严峻挑战。因此，我们必须超越技术乐观主义，从社会技术的视角去理解并主动引导这一转型，以应对潜在的权力失衡与系统风险。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据建立在一种双重文献回顾方法之上。首先，通过对Scopus数据库的结构化检索，他们识别出国际上关于数字农业与治理/制度交叉研究的文献（初步筛选出276篇），量化了该领域研究的增长趋势，并揭示了现有研究的碎片化特征。其次，通过更具深度的传统“滚雪球式”文献回顾，他们聚焦于农业推广与咨询服务的核心文献，系统梳理出五大趋势：信息行为多样化、决策专业化、咨询私有化、数据商品化以及新型服务缺口的出现。这些趋势共同构成了数字化转型的社会经济背景，为三大核心主题（连通性、透明度、治理）的提出提供了坚实的实证与理论基础。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑层次分明，从宏观到微观，再回归宏观综合。首先，文章界定了“数字化”作为一种系统性变革的本质，并将其置于“农业创新系统”理论框架下，强调了技术、社会、经济与制度的共同演化。接着，通过澳大利亚这一高度私有化的农业咨询体系作为案例，具象化地展示了国际性的宏观趋势（如私有化、专业化）如何在具体国情下演变，并与数字化浪潮交汇。最后，基于前述的理论框架和案例分析，作者提炼并升华出“连通性”、“透明度”和“治理”这三个具有普遍意义的分析维度。其论证的核心在于揭示三者间的因果链：增强的“连通性”是技术基础，它不可避免地带来了更高的“透明度”，而这种前所未有的透明度则对既有的权力结构和规则体系构成了冲击，从而催生了新的“治理”需求与困境。这一逻辑链条清晰地阐明了为何对数字化的讨论必须从技术层面上升到治理与制度层面。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用了一种严谨且互补的双轨文献分析方法（a two-fold state-of-the-art review）。第一阶段是“结构化文献综述”，通过在Scopus数据库中运用精确的关键词组合（‘digital* OR smart OR precision’ AND ‘agricultur* OR farm*’ AND ‘institution* OR governance’）进行系统检索，旨在宏观地、客观地描绘出相关研究领域的版图、增长趋势及核心主题的交叉情况。第二阶段是“传统滚雪球式文献综述”，该方法更具灵活性和深度，允许研究者沿着关键文献的引用链进行追溯，以深入挖掘农业推广、咨询服务和创新网络领域的经典理论和最新进展。这种结合使得研究既有广度（结构化综述）又有深度（传统综述），有效地应对了数字化这一新兴且跨学科领域文献分散、术语多样的挑战。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文章以澳大利亚的农业部门作为核心案例研究，这一选择极具代表性。澳大利亚的农业知识与咨询网络具有两个显著特征：一是新自由主义政策影响下高度的私有化和市场化，导致公共推广体系式微，咨询服务日益碎片化；二是在此背景下，政府、产业界和民间社会正积极推动数字化农业，以期实现生产力与可持续性的双重目标。这一案例生动地展示了在一个“去管制化”和“市场驱动”的环境中，数字化转型所带来的复杂互动和紧张关系。例如，私有顾问如何在追求商业利益的同时参与研发推广（RD&E），农民如何看待付费服务，以及数据所有权和隐私问题如何在新兴的数字平台中发酵。通过澳大利亚的例子，文章将抽象的全球趋势具体化，使其分析更具说服力和现实感。

Introduction

本章节将“数字化”定义为一种能够改变系统运作方式的重大全球趋势，并将其应用于农业领域。作者指出，虽然数字化在农业中的讨论多集中于提高效率，但其对整个行业的知识网络、制度规范和治理将产生更深远、甚至颠覆性的影响。现有学术研究对此问题的探讨尚显碎片化，未能整体性地把握数字化带来的挑战与机遇，特别是对作为农业创新系统中介关键角色的农业顾问（advisors）所产生的影响。因此，本文旨在通过综述文献，阐明数字化对农业知识与咨询网络的潜在影响。

The digitalisation of agricultural innovation systems

本章界定了核心概念。首先区分了“数字化”（Digitalisation）和“数字化转换”（Digitisation），强调前者是更广泛的系统性变革。接着，文章将“数字农业”或“智能农业”置于“农业创新系统”（AIS）的理论框架下进行理解，指出创新不仅仅是技术采纳，而是技术、社会、经济和制度的共同演化。知识与咨询网络被视为AIS中传播和情境化知识的关键组成部分，其作用是从原始数据和信息中生成隐性知识和建议。文章最后展望，数字技术可能颠覆性地取代传统知识网络中的人力节点，预示着一种向“数字启蒙”的社会转型。

  • 引用 Billon et al. (2010)，界定数字化为人类与信息通信技术（ICT）互动的增长。
    Digitalisation refers to the growth in human-computer or humaninformation and communication technologies (ICT) interaction (Billon et al., 2010).
  • 引用 Preston and Allmand (2001)，区分“数字化”（Digitalisation）与“数字化转换”（Digitisation），后者更具体地指将模拟信号转为数字信号。
    Digitalisation diﬀers from digitisation, which more specifically means to change a component part from analogue to digital (Preston and Allmand, 2001).
  • 引用 Robertson et al. (2016), Shepherd et al. (2018), 和 Wolfert et al. (2017)，说明“数字农业”和“智能农业”在不同地区（澳新/欧盟）被用来指代农业部门数字技术的开发、采纳和迭代。
    The digitalisation of agriculture involves the development, adoption and iteration of digital technologies in the agricultural sector; what has been referred to as both digital agriculture (preferred in Australia and New Zealand) or smart farming (preferred in the European Union) in diﬀerent spatial contexts (Robertson et al., 2016; Shepherd et al., 2018; Wolfert et al., 2017).
  • 引用 Aiello et al. (2019), Eastwood et al. (2019b), 和 Lindblom et al. (2017)，指出数字化在农业中最常被引用的影响是精准农业技术带来的成本降低、产量增加或可持续性价值提升。
    The most often-cited impacts of digitalisation in agriculture tend to refer to precision agricultural technologies that broadly reduce input costs and/or increase yield or sustainability values (Aiello et al., 2019; Eastwood et al., 2019b; Lindblom et al., 2017).
  • 引用 Bramley and Ouzman (2018) 和 Wolfert et al. (2017)，说明新数字技术的融合（如传感器、大数据、物联网等）预计将给农业系统带来重大变化。
    Significant changes in agricultural systems are anticipated because of the convergence of new digital technologies, including, for example, real-time monitoring through sensors, big data, the Internet of Things, machine learning and cloud computing (Bramley and Ouzman, 2018; Wolfert et al., 2017).
  • 引用 Hueske et al. (2015) 和 Leonard et al. (2017)，指出数字颠覆可能导致主导个体、组织、实践和技术的兴衰更替。

Major trends in agricultural advisory services in Australia

本章聚焦于澳大利亚的具体情境，详细阐述了农业咨询服务领域的五大主要趋势：信息寻求行为和知识生产过程的多样化；复杂咨询网络中决策专长的日益专业化；咨询服务的私有化；农业数据的商品化；以及新的供应和服务缺口的出现。接着，作者将这些趋势与澳大利亚不同类型的咨询服务提供者（私营、非政府组织、农民组织、公共研究与教育、公共权力机构）相结合，分析了它们各自的角色、功能和面临的挑战。文章强调，将用户和咨询服务利益相关者纳入技术设计过程，对于提升决策支持系统等工具的采纳率和价值至关重要。

  • 引用 Rose et al. (2018) 的研究，指出在农业技术发展中纳入更多社会伦理“设计”考量，有可能缓解决策支持系统等科学有效工具的采纳失败问题。
    Overarching inclusion of more socio-ethical ‘design’ considerations regarding technological development in agriculture has the potential to alleviate the adoption failures of experiments such as a variety of scientifically-valid decision support systems (Rose et al., 2018).

Synthesis of implications: connectivity, transparency and governance

本章是全文的核心，综合了前述分析，提出了数字化对农业知识与咨询网络影响的三个核心主题：连通性、透明度和治理。作者论证，这三者是理解未来农业创新系统演变的关键。连通性（Connectivity）：人类与技术在知识网络和价值交换中的连接将以新颖的方式持续增加，从传统的价值链转向价值交换过程，引发数据主权等新问题。透明度（Transparency）：日益增长的连通性将驱动并被农业实践和信息互动的透明度所塑造，信任关系和中介角色将随之改变。治理（Governance）：随着系统数字化，平衡各利益相关方的优先事项将面临挑战，需要新的治理模式来应对数据所有权、隐私和价值分配等问题。

  • 引用 Arts et al. (2011) 和 Darnell et al. (2018)，说明通过分析，可以思考数字化对澳大利亚及更广泛国际农业知识网络和创新系统的影响。
    ...it is possible to consider the implications of digitalisation on Australian – and more generally international – agricultural knowledge and advice networks and agricultural innovation systems (Arts et al., 2011; Darnell et al., 2018).
  • 引用 Fielke et al. (2019)，指出由于数字发展的速度，结构化综述无法完全捕捉国际农业创新系统数字化的细微差别。
    The structured review overview... was unable to fully capture the nuances of the international digitalisation of agricultural innovation systems due to the speed at which digital developments are taking place (Fielke et al., 2019).
  • 连通性：引用 Jakku et al. (2019), Lioutas et al. (2019), Regan (2019), Rotz et al. (2019), 和 Wiseman et al. (2019) 的研究，指出关于数据访问和主权的担忧预示了从价值链到价值交换的转变。
    This is pre-empted by increasingly recognised farmer and advisor concerns and questions surrounding data access and sovereignty as we move into the digital age (Jakku et al., 2019; Lioutas et al., 2019; Regan, 2019; Rotz et al., 2019; Wiseman et al., 2019).
  • 连通性：引用 Lubell et al. (2014)，说明技术中介的社交互动（如农户对客户）可以支持新的在线服务商业模式。
    The result will be increasing technologically mediated (on-line) social interaction - farmer-to-customer, farmer-to-farmer and farmer-to-advisor - that can support new online service provision business models (Lubell et al., 2014).
  • 连通性：引用 EU SCAR (2015), Ministry of Defence (2014), 和 Penzenstadler et al. (2014)，指出机器学习将使技术能更有效地与人类及其他技术设备沟通，从而增强连通性。
    This increasing connectivity – of digital devices and individuals as users – is likely to increase as machine learning allows technologies to more effectively communicate with both humans and other technological devices and actors (EU SCAR, 2015; Ministry of Defence, 2014; Penzenstadler et al., 2014).
  • 连通性：引用 Berthet et al. (2018)，说明顾问的角色可能扩展到技术中介设计，需要不断发展的数字技术技能。
    For example, advisor roles may begin to include technological intermediary design as applications (‘apps’) provide information in more palatable and timely formats – requiring evolving digital technical skills and associated adaption of tasks (Berthet et al., 2018).
  • 连通性：引用 Eastwood et al. (2019a)，指出技术驱动的决策可能成为某些咨询形式的基础，顾问需要适应这一空间。
    Increased technologically-driven decision-making (through real-time sensing, auto-bots and intuitive front ends) may become the basis for certain forms of advice and advisors willing to adapt into such a space (Eastwood et al., 2019a).
  • 连通性：引用 Fleming et al. (2019)，说明社会关系对合作创新和共同设计方法的成功至关重要。
    Importantly, agricultural knowledge and advice networks will still be grounded by social relationships that are critical to the success of co-innovation and co- design approaches to agricultural technology development and therefore usefulness and adoption by the farming community, as explained in the context of carbon farming tools by Fleming et al. (2019).
  • 连通性：引用 King et al. (2019)，强调面对面的关系互动在建立社会资本、创造未来愿景和协商数据共享安排方面具有关键的内在价值。
    The critical intrinsic value of relational social (face-to-face) interactions between and within governance, researcher, advisor, and farmer networks will increase as these relationships embody the different forms of social capital required to create future visions and negotiate data sharing arrangements and privacy outcomes (King et al., 2019).
  • 连通性：引用 Lubell et al. (2014)，认为增加的技术能力和社交资本网络将为农业利益相关者提供新的可利用平台。
    ...increasing technological capacity and networks of social capital should naturally provide new platforms for agricultural stakeholders to exploit (Lubell et al., 2014).
  • 连通性：引用 Darnell et al. (2018)，认为即使是网络连接不佳的“数字剥夺”问题，随着数字农业技术的发展也可能被克服。
    Even digital deprivation, in terms of poor internet connectivity, reliability and speed, has been argued to be surmountable as digital agricultural technologies develop (Darnell et al., 2018).
  • 连通性：引用 Sutherland et al. (2013) 和 Walter et al. (2017)，指出合作数据共享安排正日益挑战农业产业中现有的制度遗产。
    Co-operative data sharing arrangements are also increasingly questioning existing institutional legacies in agricultural industries in Australia and globally (Sutherland et al., 2013; Walter et al., 2017).
  • 连通性：引用 Wolfert et al. (2017)，指出与技术发展相关的制度变迁将如何被引导，将决定不同地区、社区和企业的农业食品优先事项。
    How institutional change associated with technological development is guided in different places will determine the agri-food priorities of perhaps increasingly diverse but virtually connected regions, communities, agri-businesses and value exchange stakeholders (Wolfert et al., 2017).
  • 透明度：引用 Lush (2018)，说明透明度不仅是系统内的交换，还需要考虑更广泛的公众舆论。
    ...but also need to consider broader public opinion (Lush, 2018).
  • 透明度：引用 Sligo and Massey (2007) 和 Kelton et al. (2008)，指出农民之间以及与技术互动的信任价值观将随着数字信息流的普及而改变，从而增加透明度。
    Trust values embedded in relationships between farmers themselves (Sligo and Massey, 2007) and interactions with technologies are likely to change over time as digital information ﬂows become ubiquitous – increasing transparency (Kelton et al., 2008).
  • 透明度：引用 Bessant and Rush (1995) 和 Howells (2006)，强调理解信任在数字化知识网络中的影响，对于确定农业中介（人力或技术）未来在影响创新中的地位至关重要。
    Improved understanding of the implications of trust as agricultural knowledge and advice networks digitalise will be critical in determining the place of agricultural intermediaries (either human or technological) to inﬂuence agricultural innovation into the future (Bessant and Rush, 1995; Howells, 2006).
  • 透明度：引用 Wolfert et al. (2017)，说明随着数字技术的普及，数据、信息和分析的流动，以及在这些知识交换组成部分中的信任或价值水平将发生变化。
    Flows of data, information and analytics, along with the levels of trust or value placed in these components of knowledge exchange (Fig. 3), will shift as the pervasiveness of digital technologies increases (Wolfert et al., 2017).
  • 透明度：引用 Aussie Farms (2019), National Farmers Federation (2019), Busch (2011), 和 Duffy (2018)，列举了社会透明度增加的近期例子，如动物福利问题、品牌抵制和数据隐私担忧。
    Recent examples of increasing societal transparency include animal welfare concerns... (Aussie Farms, 2019; National Farmers Federation, 2019), the boycotting of the consumption of certain brands or products on ethical grounds, the alignment of private interests with ethical initiatives (Busch, 2011), and the exposure of privacy concerns surrounding the third party use of data through Facebook (Duffy, 2018).
  • 透明度：引用 Eastwood et al. (2019a)，预测私有化趋势可能导致顾问身份转变为包括私人治理和认证监督，甚至维护传感器设备。
    The trend toward privatisation of agricultural advice will likely see a shift in advisor identity to include private governance and certiﬁcation surveillance – advisors may also be required to maintain sensor equipment to satisfy quality and practice standards of agri-food value exchange (Eastwood et al., 2019a).
  • 透明度：引用 Wiseman et al. (2018)，强调需要将叙事从“所有权”转向澄清良好数据管理的目标，以实现对澳大利亚农业数据更好的控制、访问和利益共享。
    As Wiseman et al. (2018, p. 76) explain... "There is a need to shift the narrative away from ownership… to clarify the goals of good data management in order to achieve a more open culture of better control, access and beneﬁt-sharing [transparency] of Australian agricultural data."
  • 透明度：引用 Stilgoe (2018) 和 Volwes and Story Carter (2018)，说明机器学习和分析效率的提高将增强公共和私人的监控能力，例如数字设备已在美国法律程序中用作证据。
    The increasing quantity of data collected, along with gains in analytical eﬃciency through machine learning will increase surveillance power in both public and private senses – digital devices in homes and autonomous vehicles have already been used to provide evidence in legal proceedings in the United States of America (Stilgoe, 2018; Volwes and Story Carter, 2018).
  • 透明度：引用 Regan (2019)，预测经济增值将越来越依赖于社会环境管理，对独特生产方式和农产品来源的数字监控将带来溢价。
    Economic value adding will become increasingly dependent on socio-environmental stewardship with premiums on oﬀer for unique production and increased digital surveillance of agricultural practices and produce provenance (Regan, 2019).
  • 透明度：引用 Fleming et al. (2018)，指出大数据分析和改进的设计界面有潜力使技术决策更简单、及时，并与农业隐性知识更好地结合。
    Similarly, big data analytics and improved design interfaces have the potential to make technical decision-making simpler, more timely and better aligned with tacit agricultural knowledge (Fleming et al., 2018).
  • 透明度：引用 Fisher (2013) 和 Lush (2018)，提出农民、顾问和行业利益相关者应思考未来如何将“可信”信息转化为隐性知识/智慧，以及社会通过NGO和社交媒体影响农场生产实践的权利日益增强。
    Farmers, agricultural advisory services and agricultural industry stakeholders should consider the implications for their practices in terms of how they might turn ‘trusted’ information into tacit knowledge/ wisdom in the future (Fisher, 2013) and the increasing right of society – through NGO agricultural advisory services and social media – to inﬂuence on-farm production practices (Lush, 2018).
  • 治理：引用 Carolan (2018) 和 Regan (2019)，指出在数字化农业创新系统中，平衡不同利益相关者的优先事项将需要在公共和私人组织之间进行权衡。
    These digital agricultural system challenges will require tradeoﬀs to be made between public and private organisations and their individual representatives (Carolan, 2018; Regan, 2019).
  • 治理：引用 Wolf and Buttel (1996) 和 Wolf et al. (2001)，说明在开发数字技术以满足农业部门公共和私人目标时，对社会伦理的考量已有数十年的历史。
    Socio-ethical considerations when developing digital technologies to meet public and private goals in the agricultural sector have been explicitly considered for decades (Wolf and Buttel, 1996; Wolf et al., 2001).
  • 治理：引用 Botha et al. (2017), Fielke et al. (2018), Klerkx et al. (2017), 和 Vereijssen et al. (2017)，指出农业决策支持系统开发的过往教训表明，不考虑农业社会系统，仅以电子方式模拟决策是远远不够的。
    Past lessons in agricultural decision support system development indicate it will not be enough to electronically simulate decisions without consideration of the agri-social systems that land managers and agricultural advisory services are a part of (Botha et al., 2017; Fielke et al., 2018; Klerkx et al., 2017; Vereijssen et al., 2017).
  • 治理：引用 Fleming et al. (2018)，强调驱动数字工具发展的技术乐观主义必须被通过适当治理来管理创新效益的社会现实所调和。
    Therefore, it is important that technological optimism driving digital tool development is tempered by the social realities of managing innovation beneﬁts through appropriate governance (Fleming et al., 2018).
  • 治理：引用 Knierim et al. (2017), Nettle et al. (2018), Paschen et al. (2017), 和 Prager et al. (2016)，指出已有研究指向农业咨询服务的日益私有化以及农业研发和推广从公共资助者转移的趋势。
    As mentioned earlier, existing research points to the increasing privatisation of agricultural advisory services and the restructuring of agricultural research and extension away from public good funders (Knierim et al., 2017; Nettle et al., 2018; Paschen et al., 2017; Prager et al., 2016).
  • 治理：引用 Jakku et al. (2019) 和 Newell and Taylor (2017)，说明向数字化农业未来的过渡在数据治理方面有重大影响，经济和政治上强大的一方可能决定数字技术的发展方式。
    The transition to a digital agricultural future has signiﬁcant implications regarding the governance of data ownership, curation, analysis, and the negotiation of values, with the economically and politically powerful likely able to determine the manner in which digital technologies are developed (Jakku et al., 2019; Newell and Taylor, 2017).
  • 治理：引用 Wiseman et al. (2018)，支持监管人类与数字技术的互动，而不是技术本身，这样更有效和相关。
    In support of research examining the role of law in keeping up with technological development, it seems more efficient and relevant to regulate the human interactions with digital technologies, as opposed to the technologies themselves (Wiseman et al., 2018).
  • 治理：引用 Oreszczyn et al. (2010) 和 Taylor and Van Grieken (2015)，举例说明存在由共享社会原则支撑的社区主导多方治理模式。
    There are examples, however, of community-led multi-party governance underpinned by shared social principles (Oreszczyn et al., 2010; Taylor and Van Grieken, 2015).
  • 治理：引用 Rattenbury and Nafus (2018) 和 Eastwood et al. (2019b)，指出数据科学有越来越大的动力以负责任的方式使信息有意义，即预见结果、包容多样利益相关者、对变化做出反应并进行反思性实践。
    There is also increased impetus for data science to make information meaningful in a manner that is responsible (Rattenbury and Nafus, 2018) – anticipating outcomes, including diverse stakeholders, being responsive to change and reﬂexive in practice (Eastwood et al., 2019b).
  • 治理：引用 Leonard et al. (2017) 和 Wiseman (2016)，指出围绕数字农业技术“使用条款”的正式和非正式谈判，为确定游戏规则提供了一些空间。
    For example, negotiation around the ‘terms of use’ of digital agricultural technologies, both formally and informally, provides some space to determine the rules of the game (Leonard et al., 2017; Wiseman, 2016).

Conclusions and limitations

本章总结了研究发现，重申了将农业知识与咨询网络置于更广泛的农业创新系统框架内进行分析的有效性。作者再次强调，数字化的结果将涉及连通性和透明度的增加，以及农业治理的多元化和持续重组。研究的主要局限性在于其作为二手分析，结果受到作者判断和解释的影响，尽管通过结构化和协作的综述过程已尽量减少偏见。此外，社会研究无法完全价值中立。最后，文章提出了未来研究的建议，主张采用“远见”（foresight, 5-10年）和“远瞻”（far sight, 10-30年）的时间尺度，以更明确地关注更广泛的社会转型过程，而不仅仅是当前暂居主导地位的技术发展。

  • 引用 Knierim et al. (2017)，说明通过采用农业创新系统框架，文章得以报告数字化转型背景下农业咨询服务的现状。
    By framing agricultural knowledge and advice networks within the broader agricultural innovation system, we were able to report on the current state-of-the-art in relation to the coming digital transformation of these systems... [and] to better understand the implications of digitalisation on agricultural advisory services moving forward (Knierim et al., 2017).
  • 引用 Darnell et al. (2018), Nettle et al. (2018), 和 Wiseman et al. (2018)，总结研究发现，即数字化可能导致连通性和透明度的增加，以及农业治理的多元化和持续重组。
    It was found the outcomes of digitalisation would likely involve increasing connectivity and transparency (Fig. 3), along with the diversification and ongoing re-structuring of agricultural governance (Darnell et al., 2018; Nettle et al., 2018; Wiseman et al., 2018).
  • 引用 EU SCAR (2015) 的 foresight 研究作为例子，建议未来的分析方法可以采用5-10年的“远见”视角。
    The review process does raise recommendations for future analytical methods, however, with foresight (looking 5–10 years into the future such as that conducted in relation European agricultural innovation systems (EU SCAR, 2015))...
  • 引用 Rifkin (2013) 的观点，暗示了超越具体技术，关注更广泛的社会转型过程（如通过社会学习、治理和文化变革）的重要性，这与能源、交通和通信等行业的融合转型有关。
    This forward-thinking approach would also provide space to imagine convergent transformation across industries, for example in energy... transport... and communication... (Rifkin, 2013).
