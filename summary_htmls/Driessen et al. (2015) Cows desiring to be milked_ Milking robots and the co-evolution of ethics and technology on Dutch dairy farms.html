Cows desiring to be milked? Milking robots and the co-evolution of ethics and technology on Dutch dairy farms

Clemens Driessen • Leonie F. M. Heutinck

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

作为一名长期关注农业技术社会学与人-动物关系研究的学者，我认为本文的核心论点极具洞察力。作者超越了对自动挤奶系统（AMS）进行简单的利弊评估，提出其伦理意涵并非固定不变，而是在一个“技术-伦理共同演化”（techno-moral co-evolution）的动态过程中被不断建构和重塑的。该技术不仅改变了物质实践，也深刻地重塑了何为“好牛”、“好农场主”的规范性观念，甚至牛与农场主自身的主体性（subjectivity）和能动性（agency）。因此，对AMS的伦理评价必须置于一个由技术、人类、动物、话语和规范共同构成的、持续变迁的关系网络中进行理解。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

为支撑其共同演化的核心论点，作者整合了丰富的质性研究证据。这些证据来源于对荷兰奶牛养殖业的多点、多层次的田野调查，包括：对AMS关键研究人员的深度访谈；对“农场主网络”会议的参与式观察；对专业期刊、行业宣传材料和专门的奶农网络论坛的话语分析。具体论据表现为对关键概念——如“自由”（freedom）、“自然”（naturalness）、“懒惰的牛”（lazy cows）——在AMS语境下意义变迁的细致追踪，以及对农场主和奶牛行为、身份认同变化的经验性描述。这些论据共同描绘出一幅生动的画面：技术、伦理与实践是如何在互动中相互渗透、彼此塑造的。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

本文的论证逻辑清晰且富有层次。首先，作者在“概念背景”部分奠定了坚实的理论基础，娴熟地运用了科技与社会研究（STS）中的“共同演化”、“共同生产”以及实用主义伦理学和关系本体论（relational ontology）等核心概念，为后续分析搭建了分析框架。其次，文章通过回顾AMS发展的历史，有力地驳斥了技术决定论的观点，揭示了该技术在早期面临的技术、经济与社会接受度等多重不确定性。论证的核心部分，作者系统地展示了AMS如何引发话语（discourse）、主体（subjectivities）和关系（relations）的重构。例如，它追溯了“奶牛自由”如何从一个营销口号演变为一个被学者批判、被农场主实践并不断重新协商的伦理规范。通过将奶牛和农场主的“变化”并置分析，文章成功地论证了技术创新并非单向地施加影响，而是一个双向乃至多向的调适与生成过程。

4. 方法 (Method)

作者采用的是一种典型的质性、民族志式的研究方法。其研究设计具有多点民族志（multi-sited ethnography）的特征，结合了话语分析与经验性田野调查。具体而言，研究方法包括：1）文献与话语分析：系统梳理了科学文献、农场主杂志、制造商宣传材料和网络论坛，以捕捉关于AMS的话语变迁。2）深度访谈：与三位AMS领域的关键研究人员进行访谈，获取了关于技术发展内部视角的深刻见解。3）参与式观察：通过参加一个为期一周的奶牛养殖培训课程和多次“农场主网络”会议，获得了关于农场主实际操作、顾虑和经验的一手资料。这种方法的优势在于，它能够同时捕捉到宏观的话语建构与微观的实践经验，这对于阐释“共同演化”这一复杂过程至关重要。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文的核心案例是自动挤奶系统（AMS）在荷兰奶牛场的研发、采纳与适应过程。选择荷兰作为研究地点极具代表性，因为荷兰不仅是AMS技术的研发先驱地之一，其奶农对该技术的采纳率和相关讨论的广泛性也为研究提供了丰富素材。文章通过这一具体案例，生动地展示了从传统的挤奶厅（milking parlor）到AMS的转变，远不止是简单的设备更替。它是一个牵动整个农场系统性变革的“行动者”（actant），迫使农场主重新学习管理技能，奶牛学习新的行为模式，并最终催生了关于人-动物关系、劳动价值和动物福利的全新伦理思考。

Introduction

本章节介绍了从传统挤奶厅到自动挤奶系统（AMS）的技术变迁，并指出了AMS不仅仅是简单的自动化，而是对整个奶牛养殖实践的重组。文章设定了核心研究问题：探讨AMS在伦理层面的复杂性和模糊性，并提出这些伦理关切与技术本身是共同演化的，而非技术的单向影响。

  • 描述了自1960年代以来，挤奶机在西方世界取代了手工挤奶的普遍情况，为后续讨论技术演进奠定了历史背景。
    Since the 1960s the milking of cows (in the Western world) has mostly been done using a milking machine rather than by hand and bucket, as had been common since the dawn of dairy agriculture (Van Adrichem Boogaert 1970; Bieleman 2000).
  • 阐述了在传统的挤奶厅中，挤奶行为不仅是体力劳动，也是农场主进行战略思考和与牛互动的核心时刻，融合了体力劳动、管理和企业家精神。
    This means that the act of milking becomes an exemplary moment in which the modern dairy farmer, in one of the few such remaining roles in our society, integrates manual labor with strategic management and entrepreneurship (Driessen 2012).
  • 指出在北美等大规模农场中，挤奶工作呈现出不同的分工模式，即员工轮班制，这与荷兰的家庭农场模式形成对比。
    In very large dairy farms with over a thousand cows, which are common in parts of North America, another division of tasks emerges in which employees work in shifts around the clock to continuously milk batches of cows, often in large carousel systems (e.g., Zellmer 2012).
  • 提及荷兰农场主面临的劳动力短缺问题以及他们不愿成为“人事经理”的倾向，这为AMS的引入提供了社会经济背景。
    And Dutch farmers do not always want to become ‘‘personnel managers’’—or find that they are incapable of taking on this role—and try to avoid hiring external labor (vreemde arbeid) (Booij 2004; Debergh 2007).
  • 介绍了AMS自1990年代商业化以来，在荷兰及其他发达国家的普及情况，说明了其日益增长的重要性。
    In the Netherlands, as well as in other (developed) countries the world over, the number of automatic milking systems (AMS) replacing conventional milking parlors on dairy farms has been increasing since their commercial introduction in the 1990s (e.g., Huiden 2009).
  • 描述了AMS的核心工作原理：奶牛自愿前往，系统通过传感器识别奶牛并检测牛奶异常，通过软件进行数据管理。
    Dedicated software stores operational data, milking reports (i.e. daily average per cow, amount of milk, interval between milkings) and milk quality parameters (de Koning and Rodenburg 2004).
  • 指出管理程序允许农场主控制系统设置，并将有异常或长时间未访问的奶牛列入“关注名单”，体现了从直接互动到间接管理的转变。
    The management program enables the farmer to control the settings of the system (Meijering et al. 2004).

Conceptual background

本章构建了文章的理论框架。核心概念是“共同演化”（co-evolution），作者借鉴科技与社会研究（STS）的观点，认为技术与社会（包括伦理）是相互塑造的。文章进一步将此框架与实用主义伦理学、关系本体论相结合，批判了将伦理视为普适、静态原则的传统观点，并对主流的动物福利评估方法提出了反思，强调了农场主和动物在实践中的能动性和经验。

  • 引用STS领域的经典理论，如“科学与社会秩序的共同生产”，来说明技术与社会之间双向塑造的关系。
    From the philosophy of technology to innovation studies and various (other) branches of science and technology studies (STS), the core assumption is that change is to be conceived as a process of two-way shaping. The terms used to describe this kind of process may differ, and accordingly the domains that are thought to interact: the ‘‘coproduction of science and social order’’ (Jasanoff 2013).
  • 引用“社会与自然的共同建构”概念，强调了这种互动关系的本体论基础。
    ...or the ‘‘co-construction of society and nature’’ (Latour 1992)...
  • 提及“技术与社会的共同塑造”和“社会-技术”等术语，进一步巩固其STS理论根基。
    ...the ‘‘co-shaping of technology and society’’ and ‘‘socio-technical’’ (Bijker and Law 1992)...
  • 引入“技术-伦理变迁”概念，直接点明本文关注的核心：技术与道德规范的互动演化。
    ...or ‘‘techno-moral change’’ (Swierstra et al. 2009).
  • 解释了共同演化不仅是“变异与选择”的盲目过程，还包括横向交换，这削弱了有机体边界的清晰性。
    Evolution increasingly is seen to occur not only by ‘‘variation and selection,’’ as a blind and unidirectional affair, but also by horizontal exchange between organisms, through parasitism and symbiosis (Hird 2010).
  • 强调共同演化过程并非缺乏有意义的沟通和能动性，为后续讨论动物的能动性埋下伏笔。
    While at the same time, perhaps paradoxically, biological processes of co-evolution should not be thought of as lacking meaningful communication and agency (Despret 2013).
  • 指出共同演化是理解农业创新的一个通用概念，涉及政策、立法、市场等多种因素的复杂互动。
    Coevolution moreover is a common notion in understandings of agricultural innovation that consider the adoption of new technologies and the development of knowledge as intertwined with alternative ways of organizing agricultural practice (Klerkx et al. 2012).
  • 以从“栓系栏”到“散养栏”的历史变迁为例，说明了物质变化与伦理理解的共同演化过程，例如对去角操作态度的转变。
    Not only was there a development towards increasing the scale of farms and decreasing the amount of labor, also the working conditions of farmers had become a matter of concern, whereby the hard manual labor required by the tie-stall was increasingly thought of as unacceptable (Van Adrichem Boogaert 1970). The change in housing system was accompanied by a shift in what were considered to be desirable and allowable practices with regard to the animals (Bieleman 2000; Van der Ploeg 2003).
  • 引入实用主义伦理学作为理论基础，认为这种伦理观更适合容纳道德变迁，并与社会技术共同演化的关系本体论相契合。
    Instead, this paper starts from a pragmatist understanding of ethics which, as Keulartz et al. (2004) have highlighted, is more geared towards accommodating moral change and has closer affinity with the relational and fluid ontologies of sociotechnical coevolution.
  • 引用杜威的“经验”概念，将其理解为既主动又被动、既身体又精神的互动过程，为将动物纳入伦理考量提供了理论依据。
    This type of ethics is dynamic, situated, and centralizes ‘‘experience,’’ understood not just as a purely subjective affair that organisms privately undergo, but as both passive and active, as doing and undergoing (Dewey 2005), as bodily and mental, comprising both affective encounters and skillful engagement with the world.
  • 指出踢机器人和踢牛会引发农场主不同的情感反应（尴尬vs.羞愧），以此区分人与物、人与动物之间的关系，并探讨了交流的共同演化。
    When a robot would be kicked, the farmer may experience embarrassment upon believing someone else saw it, or be worried this might affect the warranty, but when kicking a cow a farmer may experience a sense of shame (Harbers 2002; see also James 2009, p. 41).
  • 提出在机器人系统中，农场主与奶牛的关系从“照顾动物”转变为“让动物自己照顾好自己”。
    With a robot, the farmer cow relation changes from caring for the animals, towards allowing the animals to take good care of themselves (Heutinck and Driessen 2007).
  • 借鉴Whatmore和Haraway的理论，主张从一种关系性和流动的视角理解人、动物和技术，强调与“伴侣物种”建立关系的伦理挑战。
    Other approaches to ethics that similarly start from a relational and fluid understanding of humans, animals, and technologies have over the past decades been developed by Whatmore (2002) and Haraway (2008).
  • 提及对技术热情的批判，指出一些学者担心关系本体论会消解“自然”、“自主”等关键伦理概念的批判力量。
    Embracing coevolution and disbanding more formal modes of moral theorizing for many involves the risk of losing ethical ground and giving up critical terms such as ‘‘natural,’’ ‘‘autonomy,’’ ‘‘freedom,’’ ‘‘exploitation,’’ and ‘‘alienation’’ in a flurry of techno-enthusiasm (see Crist 2004; Weisberg 2009).
  • 引用Latour的“行动者网络”理论在动物研究中的延伸，并以此提出问题：牛和机器人之间是否存在伦理差异？
    Extending Latour’s ‘‘actor network’’ orthodoxy—in which humans and the entire variety of nonhumans alike are to be considered ‘‘actants’’—may seem to suggest such a thing (Risan 2005).
  • 批判了将“动物福利”视为集约化生产可接受前提的普遍观点。
    When ‘‘animal welfare’’ (rather than ‘‘animal rights’’) is thought of as the central issue in livestock farming, (intensive) animal production is commonly assumed to be an acceptable or even desirable form of animal use (Holloway et al. 2013).
  • 指出当动物福利被商品化时（如在超市中），其定义会倾向于易于营销的指标，如“户外活动”。
    When ‘‘animal welfare’’ is thought best decided on by consumers in supermarkets, this means animal welfare will be defined in ways that allow for easy commodification, such as outdoor access (Buller and Roe 2012).
  • 讨论了国家监管下的动物福利定义，倾向于使用可客观测量和计数的参数。
    Alternatively, when animal welfare is primarily a matter of state regulation and enforcement, different parameters may become prominent, such as those that can be objectively counted and measured (Veissier et al. 2008; Roe et al. 2011).
  • 引用Fraser对动物福利三种主流观念（自然、无压力、健康）的分类，揭示了“好生活”定义中的隐含价值选择。
    ...implicit decisions on what makes up a good life for a farm animal—one that is natural, stress free, or healthy (Fraser 1995)...
  • 强调动物福利研究本身具有特定的场域性，通常在受限空间内研究动物的主观性。
    ...animal welfare science tends to bring a particular focus and situatedness, if only for studying animal subjectivity in confined spaces (Johnston 2013).
  • 指出即使在标准化实验条件下，福利也被发现是“多样的”，依赖于具体情境、动物个体和人-动物关系。
    Even in the standardized conditions of laboratories, welfare has been found to be ‘‘multiple,’’ not easily defined on the level of species, but dependent on contexts, on qualities and life histories of particular animals, environments, and humans relating to them (Davies 2012).
  • 强调主流动物福利讨论忽视了农场主的经验（情感和专业技能），也忽视了动物作为学习新技能的主动参与者。
    Notions of animal welfare that would fit the experience (both affective registers as well as professional skills) of farmers are easily neglected in public debate and policy making (Driessen 2012). ...animals can be thought of as similarly experiential: not just passive ‘‘recipients’’ of welfare, but learning new skills too—something that is salient with the AMS.
  • 提及牛在“再野化”项目中也表现出主动学习过程，佐证了动物作为学习者的观点。
    This active process learning is also clear in the case of cows that are ‘‘dedomesticated’’ and made to live independently in nature reserves (Lorimer and Driessen 2013).

Empirical approach

本章详细说明了研究的设计与执行过程。研究于2007年至2010年间进行，采用了多种质性研究方法，包括对专业文献、机器人制造商宣传材料和网络论坛的话语分析，对三位AMS关键研究人员的访谈，以及通过参与培训课程和农场主网络会议进行的实地观察。研究的地理焦点是荷兰，因为该国是AMS研发和应用的先驱。

Ambiguous technology assessment

本章追溯了AMS的发展历程，并对其技术评估的复杂性进行了深入探讨。作者挑战了技术决定论的叙事，指出AMS并非一个线性和必然的自动化步骤，而是充满了不确定性、怀疑和实践中的困难。本章分析了早期的技术问题、经济效益的模糊性以及公众和专家对其影响的矛盾看法，最终论证了任何对AMS的评估都必然是动态和情境化的。

  • 引用Akrich的“脚本”理论，说明机器人技术如何预设了人和非人行动者的特定行为，但这并不意味着它在任何地方都产生相同的效果。
    In terms of Akrich (1992) one could say the robot clearly came with a ‘‘script’’ that required certain behaviors of both human and nonhuman actors.
  • 引用农场行为学家Wiepkema的观点，他指出这项创新是“技术推动”而非市场需求，并对技术专家声称的福利改善表示怀疑。
    ‘‘Nobody had ever asked for this innovation,’’ the farm ethologist Wiepkema pointed out in his farewell address (Wiepkema 1993, p. 3–4).
  • 指出早期研究对AMS短期应用前景持谨慎态度，认为其经济合理性尚不明确。
    All too high expectations however for its application in the short run do not seem warranted’’ (Mandersloot and Van Scheppingen 1991, p. 30).
  • 提及了多项关于AMS成本效益和投资回报的研究，这些研究试图量化节省的劳动力时间价值。
    Numerous studies calculated the cost efficiency of this labor-saving device and what its return on investment would be (Arendzen and Van Scheppingen 2000; Van Zessen 2007; Rodenburg n.d.).
  • 揭示了AMS引入初期导致奶牛体细胞数（Somatic Cell Counts）上升的问题，这是一个衡量乳房健康的重要指标。
    Their somatic cell counts, an indicator for hygiene and udder health, were measured in real time by the robots, only to reveal that these increased to alarming levels with the introduction of an AMS (Kruip et al. 2002).
  • 提及了更广泛的牛奶质量定义在细菌学上的历史转变。
    See also Atkins (2010, p. 247) on the broader historical shift in defining milk quality in bacteriological terms.
  • 引用农场主论坛的讨论，反映了体细胞数是许多机器人农场面临的实际问题。
    ‘‘I am fond of a robot, but the cell count is a problem on many robot farms...’’ (Prins 2006).
  • 提出早期的机器人被卖给了“错误的”农场主，即“技术型农场主”而非更关注奶牛行为的“奶牛型农场主”。
    In hindsight it can be (and is often) said that at first the robot was marketed to the wrong farmers (Hoefman 1998). ...with the milking robot, however, especially in its early days, there was a need for ‘‘cow-farmers,’’—who are passionate about cow behavior and welfare... (Van Drie 2005).
  • 运用Van der Ploeg的“耕作风格”框架来解释不同农场主身份及其与机器人实践的关系。
    Several farmers and other commentators (implicitly) use the farming styles framework described by Van der Ploeg (2003) to explain the different farmer identities and their relations to milking robot practices.
  • 描述了机器人失败的故事，如奶牛处理不当和牛奶质量下降的问题。
    Stories surfaced of the failure of robots to adequately treat the cows and of detrimental milk quality (Kingmans 1999).
  • 引用Latour对失败技术项目“Aramis”的分析，将AMS的早期困境类比为一个关系本体论的案例，其成败取决于它如何被嵌入更广泛的社会-技术网络。
    In this sense the milking robot resembles many pioneering technological systems, such as the automated transport system ‘‘Aramis,’’ as described by Bruno Latour (1996).
  • 讨论了1994年荷兰农业部委托进行的一项技术评估，该评估认为AMS将普及但并非没有问题，主要关注点是乳房健康。
    In 1994, after the first AMS arrived on the market, a technology assessment was performed, commissioned by the Dutch ministry of agriculture (De Boer et al. 1994).
  • 指出现代观点认为，持续的乳房健康问题更多地归因于整体卫生和牛群管理，而非机器人本身，这暗示了卫生标准的提高已成为新常态。
    If problems persist, these are now blamed on general hygiene, cow health, and herd management, not on the robot per se (Svennersten-Sjaunja and Pettersson 2008, p. 40; Dohmen et al. 2010).
  • 一项研究发现，荷兰公民普遍认为AMS等高科技方案是积极的，因为它们增加了农民的灵活性和奶牛的自由。
    A study into Dutch citizens’ concerns regarding acceptable farm practices found that high-tech solutions such as milking robots were widely valued as positive because they increase the flexibility of farmers and the freedom of cows (Verhue and Verzijden 2003).
  • 另一项研究则将AMS置于大规模农业和人牛关系缺失的负面框架中，发现受访者显著地拒绝了这种被描绘为“不可接受的发展”的技术。
    More recently, however, Boogaard et al. (2011, p. 270) framed the AMS as part of an increasing scale in farming and an absence of farmer-cow relations...The respondents in their study, in significant numbers, dismissed the technology, framed in this way, as being part of an ‘‘unacceptable development’’ (Boogaard et al. 2011, p. 273).
  • 提及了传统上对自动化导致异化的批判，尤其是在强调人-动物关系的奶牛养殖业中。
    ...increasing the automation of work is considered to induce a process of alienation, especially in the dairy farming sector, which is still seen by many as revolving around human-animal relations (Hansen 2013).
  • 引用研究指出，在高科技环境中，奶牛可能被视为工业机器，其投入产出被严密监控，而非独特的生命个体。
    Cows in this type of high-tech environment can appear alienated as well, having been turned into industrial machines, and figuring merely as a means of production with closely monitored inputs and outputs rather than as unique living beings (Stuart et al. 2013).

Co-evolution of ethics and AMS technology

本章是文章的实证核心，详细阐述了AMS如何引发伦理、话语、奶牛主体性和农场主身份的共同演化。作者分析了“自由”、“自然”等核心伦理概念如何在AMS的实践中被重新定义和争论。接着，文章探讨了奶牛和农场主本身的变化：奶牛如何从被动的群体动物被塑造为需要学习技能的个体，而农场主则从体力劳动者转变为数据驱动的管理者。整个过程揭示了一个复杂的相互适应和重构的网络，而非简单的技术替代。

  • 指出伴随AMS的出现，评估体系的话语也随之改变，为科研人员和农场主带来了新的研究问题和讨论焦点。
    And for the farmers that were involved in pioneering these new devices or discussing their desirability, the AMS was a continual topic of discussion (Koning et al. 2004).
  • 引用Brambell报告，说明“自由”作为动物福利的核心概念，在农场政策中已有历史渊源。
    In farming policies, animal welfare came to be defined in terms of freedoms after the 1965 Brambell report.
  • 提及AMS的批评者质疑其解放主义的宣称，并指出奶牛并非自愿挤奶，这与Wiepkema的早期怀疑相呼应。
    A British ethical assessment of the technology was critical of early promises that surrounded the AMS of more freedom for the cows (Millar 2000). ...the promise of cow autonomy (Wiepkema 1993).
  • 研究表明，强制性的奶牛交通系统虽然能增加机器人访问次数，但并不能提高产奶量，因此许多农场主开始转向更自由的模式。
    Most AMS manufacturers and farmers are now moving away from set ups with forced cow traffic, as research has shown that cows in forced systems may be made to visit the robot more often, but do not produce more milk (Jacobs and Siegford 2012).
  • 运用福柯的理论，Holloway认为AMS带来的新自由是以一种新的纪律和主体化为代价的。
    Using Foucault’s work on discipline and subjectivity, he argues that the newly gained freedom for the cows comes at the price of being disciplined (Holloway 2007).
  • 探讨了“懒惰的牛”这一话语，它将不符合系统要求的行为归因于奶牛的个体性格。
    In farming circles there is talk of ‘‘lazy cows,’’ who do not move to the robot as required, without them showing any signs of lacking health as an excuse for their behavior (Klop and Bos 2004; Van der Knaap 2008).
  • 指出“懒惰”可以作为育种决策的一个变量，探讨其遗传因素。
    It is then a variable that can be taken up in breeding decisions to see whether a hereditary factor is involved (Van der Knaap 2003a).
  • Holloway等人认为，谈论奶牛的“懒惰”是一种主体性归因，可能导致对它们的惩戒或淘汰。
    For Holloway et al. (2014) this kind of talk of the laziness of cows is an example of farmers ascribing subjectivity to cows that will lead to either disciplining them, e.g., by electrical prods, or culling.
  • 引用Segerdahl的观点，指出“自然性”是一个难以应用于奶牛场环境的概念。
    A related, centrally contested concept concerning farm animal welfare is naturalness (Segerdahl 2007).
  • 引用一位农场主的观察，他认为奶牛对特定机器人表现出的偏好，以及因更换机器人而“哞叫”的行为，证明了机器人是其“自然”生活的一部分。
    ‘‘It is funny to see that some cows walk from the field straight to the far most robot, apparently these cows have a preference for a particular robot’’ (Van Raay 2003).
  • 引用机器人制造商的宣传语，将机器人的重复性程序描述为符合奶牛“自然循环”的特性。
    Its repetitive procedures are consistent, just as the cow likes it […] allowing the cows to maintain their natural cycle’’ (DeLaval 2009).
  • 描述了奶牛需要被“温和地强迫”通过机器人以习惯这一过程，这是一种结合了操作性条件反射和习惯化的训练方法。
    This is done by a combination of operant conditioning (the feed offered upon visiting the robot) and habituation: ‘‘gently forcing’’ the cows through the robot to get them used to the experience (Davis and Jago 2002).
  • 提及早期的系统设计强调“强制奶牛交通”。
    After earlier setups that emphasized ‘‘forced cow traffic’’ (Ketelaar-De Lauwere et al. 1999), in more recent years it is increasingly acknowledged that leaving the decision to visit the robot more to the cows generates more visits.
  • 引用农场主网络论坛的帖子，表明无围栏的自由交通能让奶牛，特别是小母牛，更快地学会使用机器人。
    ‘‘Indeed, no fences runs better especially heifers learn it faster without fences and with enough free space around the robot’’ (‘‘Anton’’ 2010).
  • 引用农场主的经验，强调需要给奶牛足够的空间，而不是过多地引导它们。
    Another states: ‘‘You have to give the cows a lot of room and not direct them too much’’ (Hiemstra 2007).
  • 描述了奶牛在适应AMS后，从牧场到机器人的移动会形成几头牛一起的固定小团体，显示其部分群体习性得以保留。
    ...they moved from the pasture to the robot in regular groups of a few fellow cows (Heutinck et al. 2004).
  • 指出奶牛进入机器人需要能看到牛群，这表明其群体性需求仍然是系统设计的重要考量因素。
    Some aspects of their herd mentality were found to be more stable, for example one of the basic requirements of motivating the cows to enter the robot was to be in sight of the rest of the herd (Jacobs and Siegford 2012).
  • 引用早期研究指出，对机器人的使用权会成为牛群中社会等级的体现，地位低的牛只能在不利时间使用。
    ...access to the robot becomes an occasion and a site to exert dominance, which means, in an AMS operating to its full capacity, that subordinate cows are only able to get access to the robot at (apparently) less favorable hours such as during the night (Hopster et al. 2002).
  • 进一步的研究发现，自由交通可能加剧等级效应，导致低等级奶牛长时间等待。
    This means that more freedom of cow traffic around the AMS can increase herd processes in which low-ranking cows are kept from visiting the robot, come to a standstill, and wait for the robot for a prolonged time (Wiktorsson and Sorensen 2004; Hermans et al. 2004).
  • 指出对于奶牛养殖者来说，奶牛长时间闲置等待不符合时间预算优化的原则，因为奶牛应该在进食、挤奶或休息。
    ...in order to maintain their high production level, cows should be ‘‘doing something useful,’’ such as eating, being milked, or resting (Munksgaard and Søndergaard 2004).
  • 引用一位农场主的经历，描述了适应新系统的过渡期如同“地狱”，充满压力。
    ‘‘The first three weeks were hell, and that’s putting it mildly’’ (Crowell 2012).
  • 营销话语将农场主塑造为“管理者”而非工匠或牧人，工作地点也从牛棚转移到电脑前的办公室。
    According to some marketing outlets, the farmer will become a manager (not of employees, but of animals) rather than a craftsman or a stockperson (Van Drie 2005; Van Leeuwen 2012). ‘‘This is a completely different life style, now we primarily need to supervise the cows’’ (Debergh 2005).
  • 引用Borgmann和Thompson对技术与实践关系的批判，指出AMS可能导致焦点对象（挤奶厅）和焦点实践（挤奶）的丧失。
    Borgmann (2000), for instance, has described how different technological devices can fulfill the same technical function, but do this in ways that make for very different practices... (see also Thompson 2000).
  • 提及Holloway等学者将农业自动化置于更广泛的工具化世界观中进行批判，这与海德格尔的技术哲学思想相呼应。
    Then the robot can be seen to actively disclose a fully instrumentalized world in which everything and everyone...emerges as mere resources to be efficiently used (Holloway et al. 2013; see also Driessen and Korthals 2012).
  • 指出AMS产生的大量数据（如牛奶细菌水平）可以用来监控和优化奶牛健康。
    ...offering output on parameters such as levels of bacteria in the milk to control and optimize the condition of the cows (Bieleman 2000).
  • 强调农场主需要适应一种新的视觉控制模式，并学习处理新的管理制度，如创建和跟进“关注名单”。
    This means the farmer will need to adapt to a different mode of visual control of his cows and to learn to deal with the new management regime (Ouweltjes and de Koning 2004).
  • 强调“机器人农场主”必须是“奶牛农场主”，需要经常在牛群中观察，而不仅仅是依赖电脑数据。
    A farmer interviewed in a professional magazine stresses that to manage a farm with an AMS ‘‘one has to be a cow farmer, not a technology farmer’’ (Van Drie 2005). With a robot, one has to be in between the cows a lot and keep a close eye on them (Van Drie 2003).
  • 引用一位农场主的话，表达了对失去与奶牛个人化联系的失落感，但也承认这是一种必须适应的变化。
    ‘‘It is much more hands off. I myself used to try to milk at least once a day. This way it is harder to know your cows more personally. You rely more on computer reports’’...‘‘It is just a change. You just have to adapt to it’’ (Youker 2010).
  • 引用一位农场主在论坛上的发言，描述了“机器人牛”是一种更放松、不同的牛，需要用不同的方式对待。
    ‘‘A robot cow is a relaxed, different cow than a traditional one. A robot cow you walk around, you do not push and shove, should mostly treat on the spot and move around as little as possible’’ (Smink 2006; see also Hopster et al. 2002).
  • 引用一则将农场主描绘成骑摩托车享受生活的广告，说明机器人早期被宣传为节省时间、改善社交生活的工具。
    According to one advertisement that depicted men on motorcycles riding against a setting sun, ‘‘they have fun even after the cows come home’’ (Hogeveen and Meijering 2000, p. 318).
  • 描述了机器人如何促成家庭内部角色和任务的新分工，例如男农场主留在家中照顾孩子，而妻子可以外出工作。
    A new situation emerges in which male farmers stay at home and watch over the kids, while their wives can go out to work elsewhere (Van der Knaap 2003b; Mons 2007).

Conclusion: ethical assessment as part of co-evolutionary dynamics

本章对全文进行了总结与升华。作者重申，对AMS的伦理评估不能孤立地看待技术本身，而必须将其视为一个动态的共同演化过程。在这个过程中，关于“好农场主”、“好牛”以及人-动物关系的规范都在不断地被重新协商和塑造。文章最后指出，这种动态的伦理观不仅有助于理解AMS，也对未来“智能农业”的发展具有重要启示，它提醒我们应更明确地关注技术创新过程中角色、经验和技能的变迁，并将其与现有的动物福利问题（如乳腺炎、跛行等）联系起来进行综合考量。

  • 再次强调“自然性”在奶牛场情境中的应用是一个难以捉摸的概念，尽管营销话语常利用它。
    Naturalness is an elusive concept to apply to a dairy farm context (Segerdahl 2007) and thereby also to evaluate AMS with, even though robot marketers tend to claim to have realized a farming practice that reveals the true, natural way of being for a cow (Holloway 2007; Holloway et al. 2013).
  • 引用育种史研究的例子，说明特定的牛种本身就是特定规范观念的产物，类比于“好机器人”的观念也是道德学习和争论的产物。
    Just as specific cow breeds can be described as the product of a particular normative view of good farming and breeding (Theunissen 2008), so too what is deemed a good robot, or whether a robot is deemed to be good at all, is the product—and the site—of moral learning and contestation.
  • 引用了关于“自由选择牛舍”的实验，说明“奶牛自由”的规范已经被机器人农场主所采纳，并试图以物质形式实现它。
    An example of this type of thinking is the ‘‘free choice stall’’ that is experimented with in combination with milking robots by various dairy farmers, in which it is left to the cows themselves to decide whether to go out and graze or remain indoors (e.g., Noordhoff 2009).
  • 提出伦理是一个主动参与技术-伦理变迁的过程，从业者和公众在新的经验中探索他们的道德承诺。
    As ethics then is a process of actively engaging with techno-moral change (Swierstra et al. 2009), in which practitioners and the general public explore their moral commitments in the light of new experiences...
  • 将AMS的讨论延伸至现有的动物福利问题，如犊牛与其母亲过早分离等。
    ...calves being separated from their mothers (Ventura et al. 2013) and veal calves reared and transported under harmful conditions (for instance von Keyserlingk et al. 2013).
