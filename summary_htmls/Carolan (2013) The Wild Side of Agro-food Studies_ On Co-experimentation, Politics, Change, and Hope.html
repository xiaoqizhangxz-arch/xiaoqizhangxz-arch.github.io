The Wild Side of Agro-food Studies: On Co-experimentation, Politics, Change, and Hope

Michael S. Carolan

全文概要总结

作为一名长期深耕于批判性农业食品研究，特别是关注其与科学技术研究（STS）及过程哲学交集的学者，我认为Michael S. Carolan的这篇文章是一次重要的理论干预。它不仅精准地捕捉到了该领域正在发生的一场深刻的本体论转向，更富有远见地为未来的研究议程指明了方向。

1. 论点 (Argument)

Carolan的核心论点是，当代农业食品研究正在经历一场深刻的“形而上学”层面的趋同，即从传统的、基于静态实体和二元对立的分析框架，转向一种拥抱“关系性（relationality）”、“过程（process）”和“多样性（multiplicity）”的“狂野（wild）”学术实践。这种转向不仅仅是理论上的，更是方法论和政治上的。作者主张，研究行为本身并非中立的观察，而是一种具有生成性（generative）和操演性（performative）的“共同实验（co-experimentation）”，它积极地参与并塑造着它所研究的现实。因此，农业食品学者有能力、也有责任通过他们的研究实践，去激活和创造新的“农业食品想象（agro-food imaginaries）”，从而打破现有权力结构的束缚，为社会变革开辟充满希望的多种可能性。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要建立在对多学科理论资源的精妙整合与重构之上。首先，作者系统性地征引了过程哲学家阿尔弗雷德·诺斯·怀特海（Alfred North Whitehead）的思想，特别是“错置具体感谬误（Fallacy of Misplaced Concreteness）”和“时代时间（epochal time）”等概念，以此作为批判传统科学实证主义形而上学基础的理论武器。其次，他大量借鉴了科学技术研究（STS）领域，尤其是布鲁诺·拉图尔（Bruno Latour）和约翰·劳（John Law）的工作，强调研究的“入网（enrolment）”和“实现（enacting）”本质。再次，他援引了后结构主义政治经济学，如吉布森-格雷厄姆（Gibson-Graham）的理论，来挑战资本主义的单一性和决定论叙事。最后，作者引用了一系列具体的农业食品研究案例来例证其观点，如Campbell与Rosin关于新西兰有机农业审计系统如何被研究本身所塑造的研究，以及Cook与Sacks关于香蕉皮的“连接美学”艺术项目等，这些案例共同构成了其“共同实验”概念的经验支撑。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

Carolan的论证过程循序渐进，逻辑清晰。文章首先通过追溯农业食品研究的各种“转向”，引出其背后共享的“形而上学地基”问题，并构建了一个包含四个维度（抽象/具体、向下/向上、决定论/内因论、绝对时间/时代时间）的分析框架，以此描绘出从二元论到关系主义的理论图景。接着，基于这一新的本体论共识，他提出了核心概念“共同实验”，并将其与参与式研究、情感研究和实现性研究等前沿方法论思潮联系起来，阐明研究者作为世界共同创造者的角色。最后，他将这一生成性能力与政治实践相结合，论述了研究如何通过挑战和重塑“农业食品想象”，来打破线性、悲观的变革叙事（如有机农业的“常规化”论题），从而开启一种充满希望、多元且“狂野”的政治。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的方法本身就是其论点的一次绝佳演示。它不是一篇传统的实证研究或文献综述，而是一次“共同理论化（co-theorising）”的实践。Carolan将自身定位为一个召集者，邀请来自不同学科的“人物”（包括人类与非人类，如怀特海、拉图尔、水䶄等）共同参与一场理论对话。通过将看似无关的哲学、社会学和地理学文献进行创造性的编织，他生成了一种新的理解农业食品研究的方式。这种写作方法拒绝被单一学科的“凹槽（grooves）”所驯化，其本身就是一种“狂野”的、旨在“增殖（multiplication）”而非“减省（subtraction）”的学术实践。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

虽然文章以理论建构为主，但Carolan巧妙地穿插了多个关键案例以支撑其论证。例如，新西兰的ARGOS项目（Campbell & Rosin）被用来说明研究如何出乎意料地参与并重塑了其研究对象（可持续农业的审计系统），完美诠释了研究的“实现性（enactive）”能力。艺术家Shelley Sacks的香蕉交换项目（Cook）则作为“情感（affective）”和“连接性”实践的典范，展示了如何通过非传统方式构建生产者与消费者之间新的关系。对“第三食物体制”争论的分析，被用来阐释一种非决定论的、允许多种现实并存的“内因论（intramistic）”因果观。Julie Guthman对加州有机运动的研究则揭示了既有“想象”如何限制了行动者的变革潜力，反过来凸显了拓展想象力的重要性。

导论部分 (Introduction)

本部分设定了文章的基调和目标。作者首先回顾了农业食品研究领域的悠久历史和多次理论“转向”，并提出所有“转向”都预设了一个形而上学的背景。文章的目标不是进行全面的文献综述，而是通过与一系列理论家“共同理论化”，探讨这一形而上学基础，并揭示学术研究本身所具有的、超越单纯描述的“生产性”或“生成性”力量。作者引入了“狂野（wild）”这一核心概念，将其定义为跨越科学与政治二元对立、不断创造新纠缠的实践，并预示了这种实践所蕴含的希望。

  • 作者追溯农业食品研究的历史，以W.E.B. DuBois对美国南部黑人农民的研究为起点，强调该领域的深厚根基。
    The field we know today generally as agro-food studies has a rich past, dating back at least 100 years to studies like that conducted by famed AfricanAmerican sociologist W.E.B. DuBois (1898, 1901, 1904) where the plight of black Southern rural labourers and farmers were examined.
  • 文章指出该领域经历了多次“转向”，这暗示了存在一个可供“转向”的形而上学基础。
    Since then the field, as noted elsewhere (Buttel et al. 1990; Goodman 2003; Carolan 2012), has undergone numerous ‘turns’, in terms of its methodologies, theoretical frameworks, and analytic orientations.
  • 作者明确指出，研究者作为科学家，通过对世界做出基本假设而成为形而上学者。
    Agro-food scholars, like all scientists, are metaphysicians through the simple fact that in order to study the world certain assumptions have to be made about it.
  • 文章的核心论点是拒绝被怀特海所称的习惯性思维“凹槽”所驯化和限制。
    The productive nature of this article lies in its unwillingness to be disciplined – or you might say tamed – by what Whitehead (1967a, p. 197) called the ‘grooves’ of habit, practice, and thought.
  • 作者引用STS学者的观点，强调社会科学研究本身就在塑造其所研究的现实。
    Others have already noted how social scientists, in their doings as researchers, give shape to the very reality they are charged with understanding (Law 2004a; Law and Urry 2004; Lowe 2010).
  • 作者将“狂野”与梭罗式的个人主义自由区分开，强调其关系性和互助性，类似于利奥波德的土地伦理。
    The concept can evoke a sense of individualism; a bourgeois ethic of freedom, popularised in particular by Thoreau, as opposed to, say, the mutualism and relationalism of Leopold’s land ethic.
  • “狂野”被定义为一种无法被整齐地归入“科学”或“政治”范畴的存在，引用了拉图尔和Hinchliffe等人的观点。
    The wild I am referring to speaks to that which does not fit neatly into either cambers, as Latour (1999) calls them, of Science or Politics: that which is sufficiently adulterated to never be True and sufficiently more-than-human to never be Political (see Hinchliffe et al. 2005).
  • “狂野”的概念被比作拉图尔的“物（Things）”，即那些既真实又充满争议，无法被简化为稳定、客观属性的存在。
    This wild is reminiscent of Latourian Things – ‘much too real to be representations and much too disputed to play the role of stable, obdurate, boring primary qualities, furnishing the universe once and for all’ (Latour 2000, p. 119) – without the baggage that comes from being mistaken as mere ‘things’.

一些形而上学地貌 (Some of the metaphysical terrain)

本节深入剖析了农业食品研究背后的形而上学假设，通过四个连续谱（具体/抽象、向下/向上、决定论/内因论、绝对时间/时代时间）来解构从二元论到非二元论的转变。作者认为，该领域正逐渐摒弃将抽象概念误认为现实、偏好宏大或微观的静态视角、寻求线性因果关系以及将时间视为独立背景的传统做法。取而代之的是一种关系主义的取向，它承认现实的生成性、过程性、复杂性和内在关联性，强调“生成”先于“存在”。

  • 作者引用Murdoch和Goodman的工作，指出社会科学中普遍存在对二元/非二元论的讨论，并以此为起点进行更深入的挖掘。
    It is common in the social sciences to talk about how an orientation is either dualistic or non-dualistic (usually the former is criticised while the latter exulted). ... most of these continuums also figure prominently in the dualistic/non-dualistic distinction alluded to routinely in the literature (see e.g., Murdoch 1997; Goodman 2002).
  • 借鉴怀特海的“错置具体感谬误”，批判西方宇宙观将分析性的抽象概念误认为具体现实的做法。
    Whitehead (1967a) argues that western cosmology is guilty of mistaking the abstract for the concrete, giving rise to what he terms the ‘Fallacy of Misplaced Concreteness’ (Whitehead 1967a, p. 51).
  • 以怀特海对决定论基因概念的批判为例，说明科学抽象如何被误解为现实。
    Whitehead (1934, pp. 32–33), for example, sounding much like a contemporary Science and Technology Studies (STS) scholar, notes that the concept of the deterministic gene is an abstraction that has since been mistaken for what is.
  • 再次引用怀特海，强调科学的抽象价值和局限性，任何科学都只是对部分证据的理论构建。
    As a mathematician, Whitehead knew the value of abstraction... (Whitehead 1967a, p. 34). Yet he also recognised that neither science nor mathematics offer portals to all of reality. ‘Each science’, rather, ‘confines itself to a fragment of the evidence and weaves its theories in terms of notion suggested by the fragment’ (Whitehead 1938, p. 178).
  • 引用Stengers和Feyerabend的观点，说明科学实验的成功来自于将事物从其纠缠的世界中“解开”，这必然导致丰富性的丧失。
    As Isabelle Stengers (2005, p. 38) notes, experimental ‘success’ in science comes by way of disentangling an article from an otherwise highly entangled world (Paul Feyerabend (2001) makes a similar argument when discussing the richness lost when we abstract from the universe’s great ‘abundance’).
  • 怀特海警示说，科学的确定性是一种错觉，如果不加审视，就会接受原本作为替代品的抽象概念为真实。
    For Whitehead (1967b, p. 154), ‘the certainties of Science are a delusion. They are hedged around with unexplored limitations’. ... we risk accepting as real what was originally meant as a proxy. (Whitehead 1967a, p. 59).
  • 作者以农村社会学在1950年代转向社会心理学的采纳-扩散研究传统为例，说明早期研究中固化的本体论状态。
    For example, the literature from the 1950s marks a clear turning point in rural sociological circles toward the social psychological, defined largely by its embracing of the adoption-diffusion research tradition (Carolan 2012).
  • Buttel等人将这一时期描述为农村社会学的“社会心理学-行为主义时代”。
    Buttel et al. (1990, p. 44) describe this period as the ‘social psychological-behaviourist era of rural sociology’; an era that ‘would remain unchallenged in rural sociological studies of agriculture until the early to mid-1970s’ (Buttel et al. 1990, p. 46).
  • 引用Le Heron和Goodman & Sage的观点，说明当代研究将人类理解为与食物和农业“共同旅行”，在这一过程中不断重塑关系和自我。
    Humans, in the words of Richard Le Heron (in press) are, ‘co-journeying’ with food and agriculture across multiple spatial scales and institutional settings. In doing this, we are not only constantly remaking new agricultural and food relations but also ourselves (see e.g., Goodman and Sage 2013).
  • Jarosz指出，即使是另类食物网络等现象，也并非静态物体，而是从政治、文化和历史过程中涌现的。
    As opposed to being ‘static objects or sets of relationships they emerge from political, cultural and historical processes’ (Jarosz 2008, p. 242).
  • 作者指出，在食物安全、种族、正义和性别等领域，仍有进一步探索关系性的空间。
    As others note, there remains room to grow in the spaces currently given in agro-food scholarship to examining the relationality that constitute such phenomena as food security (e.g., Carolan 2013; Minkoff-Zern 2012), ethnicity (e.g., Slocum 2007; Alkon and McCullen 2011), justice (e.g., Allen 2010; Alkon and Agyeman 2011), and gender (e.g., Cairns et al. 2010).
  • 借用Kwa和Law的隐喻“向下”和“向上”，来讨论分析师的不同观察视角，同时批判了这种视角本身的二元对立。
    As previously discussed by Kwa (2002) and Law (2004b), the metaphors of ‘down’ and ‘up’ speak to the orientation of the analyst.
  • 商品链和食物体制分析的兴起标志着农业食品研究开始转向关系性思维，超越了静态的尺度分析。
    Agro-food literature began to turn away from this scale-centric form of theorising in the late 1980s with the rise of the commodity chain analysis and food regime scholarship. In doing this, relations began taking descriptive and explanatory priority over states and things (see e.g., Stringer and Le Heron 2008; Le Heron and Lewis 2009; Raynolds 2012).
  • McMichael强调食物体制的核心是关系，而非食物本身。
    In the words of McMichael (2009, p. 281) the term food regime is a bit of a misnomer, as it ‘is not about food per se, but about the relations within which food is produced, and through which capitalism is produced and reproduced’.
  • Mann-Dickinson理论被Mooney等人批评为线性思维，而商品链和食物体制框架因其关系性焦点而具有更大的灵活性。
    Their focus on relationality affords them tremendous flexibility, versus, say, the rather linear trajectory that was widely thought to be contained in earlier conceptual frameworks like the Mann and Dickinson (1978) thesis (for such a critique see, for example, Mooney (1987).
  • 通过引用Larner, Li, Wilson等人的研究，说明“去中心化新自由主义”的实践如何体现了关系性的尺度观。
    Other examples of where scale is understood relationally come from scholars engaged in the practice of decentring neoliberalism (see e.g., Larner 2003; Larner and Craig 2005; Li 2007; Wilson 2012).
  • Wendy Larner倡导使用新的方法论和概念，将新自由主义和全球化视为非固定的、具有生产性的现象。
    Wendy Larner (2003, 2011), for example, has argued for the creation of new methodologies and concepts that have broken free from assumptions concerning neoliberalism and globalisation as atemporal and monolithic phenomena. ... opened up opportunities and potentialities for new styles knowing and doing (see also Campbell 2011).
  • Tania Murray Li对社区林业管理的研究展示了多元行动者和要素如何共同构成一个“狂野”的过程，而非简单的资本逻辑 subsumption。
    ...she details how a diverse array of ‘things (trees, logs, non-timber forest products, tools, documents), socially situated subjects (villagers, labourers, entrepreneurs, officials, activists, aid donors, scientists), objectives (profit, pay, livelihoods, control, property, efficiency, sustainability, conservation) and an array of knowledges, discourses, institutions, laws and regulatory regimes’ (p. 266) came together to constitute the (wild) process... (Li 2007)
  • 作者提出“内因论（intramistic）”概念，作为决定论和完全不确定论之间的第三条道路，强调解释内在于事件本身的关系构成。
    Here explanation resides solely within the confines of each event, in the relationships that are formed through the various actors enrolled (Latour 2005) and enacting (Law and Urry 2004) that takes place.
  • 1980年代的“土地问题”文献被批评为线性思维，Mooney和Marsden等人的工作反映了这一时期的理论局限。
    There were concerns that the theoretical arguments that dominated much of sociology of agriculture scholarship in the 1980s were – or at least were interpreted as – painting a linear understanding of agrarian change (Mooney 1983, 1986, 1987; Marsden 1989; Carolan 2012).
  • Mooney后期的工作转向，旨在“瓦解资本主义发展必须遵循任何特定方向的观念”。
    For example, Mooney (1987) by the late 1980s, as evidence of a rejection of this linear theorising, began to move away from his initial objective... seeking instead to ‘undermine the notion that capitalist development need flow in any particular direction’ (p. 293).
  • 关于第三食物体制的多种不同解读（Friedmann的“绿色资本主义”、Campbell的“食物来自某处”、McMichael的“企业食物体制”、Burch & Lawrence的“金融化”以及Pechlaner的“新自由主义”）被用来证明非线性理论的价值，即允许多重现实并存。
    Friedmann (2005, p. 229), for instance, has pointed to an emerging ‘green capitalism’ regime... Campbell (2009, p. 309) writes of something similar in his discussion of an emerging ‘food from somewhere’ regime... McMichael (2009, p. 285), conversely, asserts that we have entered a ‘corporate food regime’... David Burch and Geoffrey Lawrence (2009, p. 277) write about a financialised third food regime... More recent still, Gabriela Pechlaner (2012, p. 75) details an emerging third ‘American-led neoliberal food regime...’.
  • 批判牛顿的“绝对时间”观，认为其将时间视为独立于万物的、均速流逝的实体。
    Newton (1966, p. 1) refers to this understanding of time as follows: ‘Absolute, true, and mathematical time, of itself, and from its own nature, flow equably without relation to anything external’.
  • 怀特海批判牛顿宇宙观的内在矛盾，指出速度和动量在“瞬间”是无意义的，揭示了绝对时间的抽象性。
    Again I return to Whitehead (1938, pp. 145, 146): ‘There is a fatal contradiction inherent in the Newtonian cosmology ...’. Rather than being a part of the nature of actualities, time is conceived, under Newton... as some-thing that requires nothing but itself to exist (Wallack 1980, p. 129).
  • 引用Capek的观点，将时间实体化的倾向追溯至西方思想的源头，即试图将“生成”还原为“存在”。
    This objectification of time has been traced back to the origins of western thought, subsumed in the ‘belief that becoming can be reduced to being, process to substance, time to the timeless, events to things’ (Capek 1961, pp. 384–385, emphasis in original).
  • 引入与绝对时间相对的“时代时间（epochal time）”，认为事件在时间上是厚重的但不可分割的，时间是与主观目标相连的“生成”过程。
    In Whitehead’s (1978, p. 69) words: ‘The conclusion is that in every act of becoming there is the becoming of something with temporal extension; but that the act itself is not extensive...’.
  • 引用Felt的观点，时代时间观意味着事件“在某种意义上是一次性完成的”，这引导研究者关注过程、网络和组合。
    An epochal view of time, conversely, where events ‘are in some sense all-at-once’ (Felt 2002, p. 24), directs attention to the process itself, the network, the assemblage.
  • 农业食品研究领域受关系地理学、实现性学术以及操演性/具身理论的影响日益增长，体现了“生成先于存在”的时代时间观。
    This is an orientation of mounting influence within agro-food studies thanks the growing influence of relational geography (see e.g., Le Heron and Lewis 2009; Larner 2011; Heley and Jones 2012), enactive scholarship (Lowe 2010; Campbell and Rosin 2011), and performative and embodied theorising (Busch 2007; Carolan 2008, 2011).

从共同理论化到共同实验 & 参与式、情感性和实现性研究 (From co-theorising to co-experimentation & Participatory, affective, and enactive research)

这一部分将前述的形而上学讨论转化为具体的研究实践。作者正式提出“共同实验（co-experimentation）”这一核心概念，将其定义为一种承认并拥抱研究过程生成性的实践。它是一种“狂野化”与“相互驯化”并存的过程，其目的不是追求更准确的表征，而是改变参与方式，并创造人与物的新组合。作者通过整合参与式、情感性（affective）和实现性（enactive）研究的文献，进一步阐述了“共同实验”的内涵。他强调，知识是具身的、情感驱动的，是通过实践获得的。研究者并非置身事外的观察者，而是深嵌于研究情境中，通过自身的研究行为，与研究对象共同“实现”着新的现实、新的知识和新的主体性。

  • Latour提出科学涉及“学习被影响”，即科学家需要与研究对象建立情感和身体的连接。
    This is what Latour meant when we wrote science involves ‘learning to be affected’ (Latour 2004, pp. 226–227).
  • Deleuze也强调，定义一个存在的关键在于其“影响与被影响的能力”。
    Deleuze (1988, p. 27) makes a similar point: If you define bodies and thoughts as capacities for affecting and being affected, many things change.
  • Harry Collins关于TEA激光器的研究是早期例证，说明科学家必须先获得“默会知识”，才能复制实验。
    Harry Collins (1974) made this point decades ago when writing about the transversely excited atmospheric laser, noting how scientists had to first learn to be affected by the laser...
  • 作者指出，农民、土壤科学家、植物育种家等群体的知识都是他们实践的产物。
    The same holds, for example, for farmers (e.g., Lyon et al. 2011), soil scientists (e.g., Ingram 2008), and plant breeders and geneticists (e.g., Carolan 2010) – what they know is an artifact of what they do.
  • Hinchliffe等人关于“水䶄书写”的研究被用作“实验”概念的来源，展示了研究者如何通过学习被水䶄“影响”来获得新知识。
    Hinchliffe et al. (2005, p. 647), where I draw the idea of ‘experimentation’ from, investigate individuals who are being trained in ‘water vole writing’.
  • Carolan自己的研究阐述了身体如何被食物“调谐”，强调食物知识是完全感官化和具身化的。
    Carolan (2011) engages in a similar form of affective theorising when discussing how bodies become ‘tuned’ to food.
  • 作者认为，要改变人们对食物的看法，必须改变他们的实践，这需要“共同实验”。
    To make bodies think differently you have to have them do differently. And that requires coexperimentation. As Carolan (2011, p. 13) explains, Social change requires bodies... that think social change ought to occur.
  • 传统科学因忽视关系（Latour）和未能将科学家置于具身情境中而存在缺陷。
    Conventional understandings of science not only have a problem of missing mass, by ignoring the multitude of relations the make science possible (Latour 1992). They also suffer from a lack of address...
  • Charles对社区支持农业的研究展示了如何将所有参与者（包括研究者）都视为有自身关切的主体，从而产生更有意义的共同成果。
    Charles (2011), for example, in attempting to account for some of this missing mass and address, alerts us to the ethical complexities that are inevitable in research.
  • 引用Le Heron等人和Cook等人的工作，强调田野调查的生成性能力。
    Elsewhere, we find research that highlights the generative capacities of fieldwork (see e.g., Le Heron et al. 2006; Cook et al. 2007).
  • Cook与艺术家Shelley Sacks的香蕉皮项目被描述为一个具有本体论不确定性的“连接美学”实验。
    Take Cook’s (2000) description of his collaboration with artist Shelley Sacks... Cook (2000, p. 342) is careful to note the risky as well as ontologically unsettled nature of the experiment: This isn’t about connective aesthetics. It is connective aesthetics.
  • “共同实验”的成功标准不是输赢或表征的准确性，而是改变参与方式和创造新的可能性。
    Instead, it is about changing engagements and making new configurations of people and things possible (Hinchliffe et al. 2005).
  • 作者引用了Lowe, Campbell & Rosin, Le Heron & Lewis的工作，讨论研究的“实现性（enactive）”能力。
    This leads the discussion to a small but growing literature in agro-food studies around the enactive capabilities of research (see e.g., Lowe 2010; Campbell and Rosin 2011; Le Heron and Lewis 2011).
  • Campbell和Rosin的研究详细说明了他们的ARGOS项目如何帮助“实现”了他们所研究的新西兰有机农业部门，并影响了审计系统的架构。
    Studying the emergence of commercial forms of organic and other ‘sustainable’ agriculture in New Zealand (see e.g., Campbell and Coombes 1999; Campbell and Liepins 2001; Campbell et al. 2006), Campbell and Rosin (2011) detail how their research helped enact the very emerging organic sector the team was charged with investigating. Campbell and Rosin (2011, p. 359) describe their roles in this co-experimentation...

农业食品想象与政治的狂野化 (Agro-food imaginaries and a wilding of politics)

本节是全文的高潮，将“共同实验”的生成性力量与政治和希望直接联系起来。作者认为，通过共同实验，研究者可以帮助塑造新的“农业食品想象”，即关于什么是可能的、什么是可做的集体认知框架。这种实践挑战了线性的、决定论的叙事（如有机农业的“常规化”论题），代之以多元性、可能性和希望。作者借鉴后结构主义政治经济学和STS中关于操演性的观点，主张学者们应有意识地承担起这一政治角色，通过研究实践来“激活”世界，扩大“可能”与“可行”的领域。这种政治不是传统意义上的宏大政治（Politics），而是一种根植于日常实践、涉及人与非人共同参与的、 messy 的、但充满方向感的“狂野”政治。它通过不断的重新配置，而非固定的程序，来酿造决策和未来。

  • 作者以有机农业的“常规化”论题为例，指出其背后潜藏的线性思维。
    One such space where some of this is found is in the conventionalisation of organic agriculture thesis (for a superb review of this literature see Constance et al. 2008).
  • Campbell和Rosin的研究通过采取更具反思性和关系性的方法，打破了组织有机农业讨论的“宏大二元对立”，代之以可能性和多样性。
    Campbell and Rosin (2011) suggest that this research tradition is not so much wrong as it is incomplete. ... they were able to disrupt assumptions underlying ‘the grand binary that organised both academic and popular discussion of organic agriculture’ (p. 359) and in doing this replace the arrow of linearity... with possibility, multiplicity, and perhaps even a little hope.
  • 后结构主义政治经济学，特别是Gibson-Graham的工作，提醒我们资本主义并非铁板一块，为多样化的经济实践提供了理论空间。
    Another area of (inherently hopeful) scholarship... can be found in the so-called post-structural political economy literature (see e.g., Gibson-Graham 2008; Le Heron 2009; Larner 2011). The path-breaking work performed by such scholars as Gibson-Graham (1996) reminds us that capitalism is not operated by any command centre.
  • 引用Certeau的观点，强调需要根据每个空间的独特性来对待，这与“共同实验”的过程相呼应。
    ...it is important to treat each space according to its unique event-ness (Certeau 2011).
  • STS领域的最新发展趋势是将认识论、本体论、伦理学和美学融合起来，进行历史化和情境化的分析。
    Paralleling recent turns in related fields, such as STS (see e.g., Lynch 2013), agro-food scholarship has slowly moved to merge epistemology, ontology, ethics, and aesthetics.
  • 作者认为农业食品学者在描述世界的同时，也在帮助构建它，无论愿意与否，都在塑造“农业食品想象”。
    The literature coalescing around relationality is telling us that we do not just study the aforementioned event-ness, we help constitute it. Thus, whether we like it or not, we play a role in giving shape to agro-food imaginaries (Carolan, forthcoming).
  • 引用Guthman对加州有机运动的研究，说明既有想象如何限制了利益相关者的行动，因为他们的投入反映了“在当前新自由主义气候下已经形成的关于可能性的观念”。
    Julie Guthman (2008, p. 1242), for example, in her research into the organic movement in California, explains that ‘stakeholders played a constitutive role in producing the neoliberal outcomes of the project, in part because their input reflected already-developed notions of the possible within the current climate of neoliberalism’.
  • Busch评论Guthman的发现，指出某些解决方案（如向国家寻求法律救济）甚至“超出了想象的范围”。
    As Busch (2010, p. 345) later remarks, commenting specifically on this finding: ‘Petitioning the state for legal redress was not merely absent from the discourse; it was beyond the imaginable!’
  • 作者认为，通过写作、教学和“激活”世界的实践，曾经不可想象的事情会变得可以想象，从而推动变革。
    And the more we write about it, discuss it with our students, and practice co-experimentation by how we choose to ‘enliven’ (Carolan 2009) the world the more that becomes imaginable and the more that will continue to change.
  • Stengers引用怀特海的“想象力的飞跃”，强调实验思想所带来的效果的重要性。
    Isabelle Stengers (2011), evoking Whitehead’s (1978, p. 13) ‘leap of the imagination’, reminds us of why it is important ‘to experiment with the effects of that leap...’ (p. 22).
  • 作者将自己的“共同实验”概念与Garfinkel的“破坏性实验”相比较，但主张实验的对象不应仅限于社会规范，而应扩展到构成“常规”关系性的整个过程、实践和习惯。
    I am referring to the experimental techniques for researching everyday life articulated by Harold Garfinkel (1984) and his disciples in the 1960s. ... Why stop there?
  • 作者引用福柯、利科、布迪厄和泰勒等思想家，说明“想象”根植于实践。
    Imaginaries, as others have detailed (though often utilising different terms (e.g., Foucault 1982; Ricoeur 1986; Bourdieu 1997; Taylor 2004)), are rooted in practice.
  • 以Cook的艺术合作、Marres的“以物为中心”的共同实验和Carolan自己的具身政治研究为例，说明了可以采取的多种共同实验形式。
    Earlier I mentioned Cook’s (2000) collaboration with artist Shelley Sacks... Another is found in the work of Noortje Marres (2012). ... Many of the practices described in Carolan’s (2011) book Embodied Food Politics are also co-experimental in nature...
  • 引用Mol的“ontororms”概念，来说明物质习惯所产生的本体论和规范性效应。
    ...Marres writes of ‘object-centered’ co-experimentation, where the focus is on material habits and the ontological and normative effects – or what Mol (2012) calls ‘ontonorms’ – that emerge out of these entanglements.
  • 作者承认研究稳定性和秩序的重要性，但警告不要陷入Gibson-Graham所说的“结构主义偏执”，即在批判中无意中再生产了主导秩序。
    The trick is to remain critical without becoming trapped in ‘a kind of structuralist paranoia, inadvertently reproducing the self-same dominant order that it seeks to critique’ (Gibson-Graham 2008, p. 62).
  • 引用Callon和Mitchell的观点，指出经济学等预测性框架有时会“实现”它们声称要表征的现实，这是一种“预测即我实现”的预言。
    (The closest relationalism gets to prediction is by noting how alleged predictive frameworks, such as those found in economics (see e.g., Callon 2009; Mitchell 2008), have in some cases enacted the very reality they claim to be representing...).
  • Law和Urry的观点被用来警示，对研究的操演性视而不见，本身就会产生后果，即可能会实现我们批判的稳定性，并干扰其他现实的生成。
    After all, choosing to be blind to them is not without consequence, as it risks enacting the very stabilities we pride ourselves in being critical of while interfering with the performance of other realities (Law and Urry 2004, p. 404).
  • 作者将“狂野政治”比作Latour的“世界政治（cosmopolitics）”，它不是自上而下的命令，而是像发酵一样从内部生成方向和决策。
    We can think of this along the same lines as how Latour (1999, p. 247) envisions cosmopolitics, in so far as he likens it to ‘fermentation through which the people brews itself toward a decision...’.
  • 通过引用Marres, Callon & Rabeharisoa, Lezaun, Girard & Stark的研究，说明政治参与可以“去主体化”，各种“参与技术”（如焦点小组、PPT）在其中扮演重要角色。
    Research into device-centred public participation radically de-centres–or more accurately un-subjectivises – what it means to do politics (Marres 2012). Recent research into, for example, face-to-face interviews (Callon and Rabeharisoa 2004), focus groups (Lezaun 2007), and PowerPoint presentations (Girard and Stark 2007) bring into view the power of what Thrift (2008) refers to as ‘technologies of participation’...
  • 引用Callon的“共同阐明政治（politics of co-articulation）”，强调这种政治是关于重新配置，而非简单的增加参与者。
    ...or what Callon (2009) calls a politics of co-articulation. This is not a politics of addition... but one of reconfiguration.
  • 作者将阅读和写作本文本身视为一种共同实验，引用Massumi的观点，认为这些情感性相遇可以预示更广泛的集体政治。
    ...a collection of affective encounters that generates feelings of possibility that prefigure a broader collective politics (Massumi 2002).
  • 文章的结尾，引用Stengers的观点，强调“发现”是一种转变描述者和被描述者的“生成”过程。
    For discovery implies, ‘each time, a becoming that transforms both the person doing the describing and what is described’ (Stengers 2011, p. 249).
