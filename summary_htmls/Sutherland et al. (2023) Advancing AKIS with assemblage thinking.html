Advancing AKIS with assemblage thinking

Lee-Ann Sutherland, Anda Adamsone-Fiskovica, Boelie Elzen, Alexandros Koutsouris, Catherine Laurent, Egil Petter Stræte, Pierre Labarthe

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点在于，当前在欧盟政策层面广泛应用的“农业知识与创新系统”（AKIS）概念存在两大根本性缺陷：其一，分析尺度过于宏大，过度聚焦于国家层面的“基础设施视角”（infrastructural view），忽视了农场层面的动态实践；其二，该概念与当代关于创新过程的学术前沿理论，特别是“关系转向”（relational turn）和“超越人类中心主义”（more-than-human）的思潮严重脱节。为弥补此缺陷，作者提出运用“集置理论”（assemblage theory），特别是德兰达（DeLanda）的新实在论版本，来重构和推进AKIS的分析框架。他们主张，集置理论提供了一套有效的概念工具，能够识别并整合非人类行动者（non-human actants）——如新作物、技术、动物、气候等——在创新过程中的能动性，从而揭示农业知识与转型的共构（co-constructed）本质。通过引入集置理论，能够更好地理解农业实践中根植于物质生产条件的“路径依赖”（path dependency）以及当这些依赖被“断裂”（rupture）时所激发的学习过程。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据建立在对欧洲四个截然不同的农业创新案例的经验研究之上。这些案例包括：希腊引种牛油果这一新作物；挪威主流化应用的机器人挤奶技术；拉脱维亚直销模式的“复古式创新”（retro-innovation）；以及法国日益普遍的（非人化的）农业劳动力外包。每一个案例都作为经验证据，用以说明农民如何主动“集置”（assemble）起一个异质性的知识网络，即作者提出的“微观AKIS”（microAKIS）。这些网络远超传统AKIS框架所关注的官方咨询服务范围，囊括了同行农民、投入品供应商、银行、退休科学家、家庭成员乃至消费者等多元行动者。更重要的是，案例详实地展示了非人类行动者的关键作用：例如，牛油果树对特定微气候的物质需求、挤奶机器人对牛舍空间和牛群行为的重构、公路基础设施对直销成功的决定性影响等，都强有力地支撑了其理论论点。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑层次清晰。首先，作者对AKIS概念进行了谱系学梳理，将其从罗林（Niels Röling）最初提出的、强调动态自组织的“软系统”或“过程视角”，追溯到当前欧盟政策中僵化、聚焦组织结构的“基础设施视角”，从而精准定位了问题的根源。其次，系统性地引介了集置理论的核心概念，包括“领域化/解域化”（territorialisation/deterritorialisation）、“历史性与断裂”（historicity and rupture）以及“物质性”（materialities），并将其与AKIS研究对接，构建起新的分析框架。接着，作者将此理论框架应用于四个案例的比较分析中，论证该框架的解释力。例如，通过“断裂”概念解释了市场崩溃或代际更替如何打破原有路径依赖，为创新打开缺口；通过“领域化”过程，阐释了农民如何将机器人、新市场、外包工人等异质元素整合进农场这一“集置体”（assemblage）中，并在此过程中生产出新的知识。最终，通过综合所有案例的发现，作者归纳出其理论推进对于理解农业创新过程和反思相关政策的深刻意涵，形成了一个从理论批判到理论构建，再到经验验证的完整论证闭环。

4. 方法 (Method)

本研究采用了一种定性的、比较案例研究的方法。研究数据源自欧盟“地平线2020”计划下的AgriLink项目，该项目旨在深入理解咨询服务提供者在农民决策中的作用。作者从该项目覆盖的32个案例中，有目的地选取了四个案例进行二次分析。案例的选择标准极具代表性，既覆盖了欧洲AKIS系统强度各异的地区（从希腊的薄弱、碎片化到法国的强大、一体化），也涵盖了当前农业面临的主要转型挑战（气候适应、数字化、市场重构、劳动力结构变迁）。研究方法的核心在于，将一个为评估咨询服务而收集的既有数据集，通过集置理论这一全新的理论棱镜进行重构性解读。数据收集本身采用了滚雪球抽样，对采纳者、放弃者和未采纳者进行了深入访谈，并对农民提及的知识来源（关键信息提供者）进行了追踪访谈以实现三角互证。作者将这种从农民视角出发、识别其知识网络的调查方式，界定为一种“微观AKIS”的方法论部署。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

以希腊的牛油果引种案例为例，它生动地诠释了文章的核心观点。这里的“断裂”事件是2008年橄榄油和柑橘市场的崩溃，这“解域化”了传统的种植模式，为牛油果的“领域化”创造了机会。然而，创新过程远非线性采纳。牛油果树本身作为一种“物质性”行动者，其对特定土壤、水分和微气候的苛刻要求，极大地塑造了创新路径。由于官方AKIS系统薄弱，农民的“微观AKIS”便自发集置而成：他们依赖的并非官方顾问，而是少数身为种植者的农学家（即“混合行动者”）、退休的早期研究人员、苗木供应商以及最重要的——通过反复试错积累了大量地方性知识的先驱农民。这个案例清晰地表明，创新知识是农民与作物、土地、市场等多种人类与非人类行动者互动的产物，是高度情境化和共构的，而这种复杂动态是国家层面的AKIS宏大规划所无法捕捉的。

2. Assemblage thinking
  • 介绍集置理论的起源，它融合了德勒兹与加塔利的哲学思想与后期关于行动者网络及物质对象作用的思考。
    Assemblage theory brings together ideas from Gilles Deleuze (a French philosopher) and Felix Guattari (a French psychotherapist and philosopher) with more recent thinking about actor networks (e. g. actor-network theory, Latour, 2005) and the role of material objects in shaping action.
  • 聚焦于Manuel DeLanda的理论路径，强调其核心原则：理解一个集置体，关键在于其形成过程，而非其构成要素的固有属性。
    The overarching principle of Delanda’s (2006) approach to assemblage theory is that to understand an assemblage, it is the process of formation that is important, rather than the properties of the actants in the assemblage.
  • 阐释集置的异质性和动态性。集置体的构成要素通常属于多个不同的集置体，使其边界不断演化和交叉。
    DeLanda’s (2006) conceptualisation emphasises that most components or ‘elements’ of assemblages are part of multiple assemblages... Assemblages are thus constantly evolving and intersecting.
  • 定义“领域化”（territorialisation）为影响集置体结构的规则或“编码”（正式或非正式）的形成过程，并以咨询服务资金为例。
    Processes of inclusion or exclusion from the assemblage are termed ‘territorialisation’ – the formation of rules or ‘codes’ (formal or informal) that influence the structure of the assemblage (DeLanda, 2006).
  • 以欧洲咨询服务私有化为例，解释“解域化”（de-territorialisation），即行动者从集置体中被释放出来，导致旧关系瓦解，为新关系的形成创造了空间。
    The widespread privatisation of agricultural advisory services across Europe since the 1980s (see Garforth et al., 2003) could be understood as a change in rules or codes, which led to the process of de-territorialisation of the role of advisors within farm assemblages.
  • 强调集置体的“历史性”（historicity），即过去的实践、制度和期望如何塑造了当代的集置体构成。同时引入“断裂”（ruptures）概念，指代集置体构成发生剧烈改变的突变点。
    However, ruptures can occur – abrupt points of deterritorialization and new territorialisation where the composition of the assemblage substantially alters. These ruptures are typically the starting points for describing assemblage formation... (Müller and Schurr, 2016).
  • 论述集置理论如何包含“超越人类中心”的元素，认为物质元素（如农场位置、土地形态）和表达性能力（如社会规范）都具有塑造集置体的能动性。
    Material elements are considered to have agencies associated with their material characteristics. The location of the farm can act to resist territorialisation of particular innovations into the farm assemblage... (Sutherland and Calo, 2020).
  • 提出本文对集置理论的推进：聚焦知识在将创新“领域化”到农场集置体中的作用，认为知识是形成和维持集置体的关键“粘合剂”。
    We progress assemblage theory by considering the role of knowledge in territorialising innovations into the farm assemblage... Knowledge is also produced in the process of forming or territorisalising an assemblage; farmers’ co-production of knowledge is well recognised in the rural studies literature (e.g. Ingram et al., 2020).

4. Historicity of the case study assemblages
  • 描述希腊牛油果案例的历史性：尽管在20世纪60-70年代已有引种尝试，但直到2008年传统作物（橄榄、柑橘）市场崩溃，才出现了真正的“断裂”，为牛油果种植的扩张提供了契机。
    This situation changed in 2008 with the collapse in olive oil and orange markets, which coincided with an increase in global demand for avocados. ... From an assemblage perspective, there was a clear rupture in 2008, caused by a weakening of market codes (i.e. the profitability of production).
  • 介绍挪威机器人挤奶案例的背景：在奶业核心区，机器人技术自2001年起被引入。其“断裂”并非全行业性的，而是发生在农场层面，通常与农场生命周期（如代际传承）或提高生产效率的需求相关。
    ‘Ruptures’ in existing assemblages were thus not industry wide, instead tending to reflect the life course of the farm (e. g. when a farmer was approaching retirement and/or a successor introduced), or as means of increasing production efficiency.
  • 阐述拉脱维亚直销案例的历史演变：同样没有单一的断裂点。农场集置体的“解域化”是渐进的，由低盈利性、对中间商的不信任、意识形态转变以及农场内部因素（如生命周期变化、出现销售机会）等复杂因素共同驱动。
    Like milking robots, there was no single rupture point. Instead, existing farm assemblages gradually de-territorialised through low profitability; integration into different markets was sought as a business opportunity.
  • 追溯法国劳动力外包的历史：外包某些作业（如收割）是传统做法，但自90年代中期起迅速增加，并出现了“派遣工人”等新形式，这与农场规模扩大和技术进步（如精准农业设备）同步发生。
    Outsourcing to access precision technologies... is now common. This coincided with increased employment of workers through another legal entity (foreign or national contractor, specific associations), to perform specific labour-intensive tasks...
  • 总结四个案例中的“断裂”动因：低盈利性是一个普遍的“解域化”力量，但新技术、农户家庭及其生命周期问题在打破现有集置体的路径依赖中也扮演了重要角色。大多数案例中，断裂点是因农场而异的。
    The cases as a set thus demonstrate the deterritorialising effect of low profitability on farms – weakening the path dependency and pushing farmers to consider other options - but also the important role played by new technologies and the farming household and associated life course issues in deterritorialising existing assemblages.

5. The intersection of materialities and knowledge in territorialisation processes
  • 在希腊案例中，将牛油果树这一新植物“领域化”到农场集置体中，其成功与否深受物质性因素（微气候、土壤、水质）的制约。
    “The new [imported] varieties require careful acclimatization … the seedlings react differently in different environments” (Male advisor/ hybrid actor, Greece)
  • 由于缺乏系统性知识，农民的microAKIS主要由“混合行动者”（既是顾问也是种植者）和通过自身试错积累经验的先驱农民构成。知识生产过程与物质实践紧密交织。
    “[we] relied on 2–3 agronomists [members of the Organic Producers’ Cooperative] who draw much of their knowledge from their experiences as avocado producers”. (Male farmer, Greece)
  • 在挪威案例中，“领域化”一个挤奶机器人通常意味着对整个牛舍的物质重构（如改造为散栏饲养），这是一个涉及多重元素（新建筑、融资、技术）的复杂过程。
    Installation of a milking robot was therefore part of a major reorganisation of the farm assemblage: combined with new buildings and robots for feeding, activity measuring, cleaning, etc.
  • 农民的microAKIS在机器人安装初期高度依赖技术供应商和奶业合作社，随着自身经验知识的积累，对外部支持的依赖逐渐减少。同行农民的经验在技术品牌选择中起决定性作用。
    “Many in the area had DeLaval [supplier of milking robot], and I chose the same type because it is a great advantage to have others in the area who have experience with the same technology and brand.” (Male farmer, Norway)
  • 在拉脱维亚案例中，直销的“领域化”涉及将新市场这一经济实体整合进农场。公路等基础设施的物质性改变（或农场靠近城市的地理位置）是成功的关键。
    “The direct sales expanded with the reconstruction of the nearby highway. The increased flow of cars made us decide that it is worth selling on-farm rather than going somewhere else.” (Male farmer, Latvia)
  • 拉脱维亚农民的microAKIS高度分散且非正式，大量依赖家庭成员的技能（如网络营销）、同行的经验以及与客户的直接互动，而官方咨询服务在此领域作用甚微。
    MicroAKIS were comprised of a wide range of individuals, including other farm actors (beyond the ‘primary farmer’, e. g. grandparents, spouses, successors), extended family and acquaintances, local peers, organisers of sales points...
  • 在法国案例中，将外包劳动力这一新组织形式“领域化”入农场，面临法律和信任上的不确定性，这些问题超出了传统咨询服务的范围。
    “The technicians from the cooperative are salesmen. They don’t give real advice on this [sub contracting and outsourcing]. By talking with neighbours now I found a contractor I can trust.” (male farmer, France)
  • 外包工人在物质上被“非人化”，被视为可互换的“身体”或“物质”，其自身的知识、技能和权利在这一集置过程中被边缘化。
    In some cases, the workers contracted through the companies were essentially considered a substance whose quality was negotiated in relation to the task at hand (evident in statements like “I want Moroccans for pruning” (farmer, male): in French “je veux du marocain [substance]” and not “je veux des marocains [people]”).

6. Discussion
  • 6.1 理解农业创新: 研究结果支持了前期研究观点，即农民会根据不同主题寻求不同知识来源。通过引入集置理论，本研究将分析扩展至物质行动者，提供了经验证据来响应在农业研究中纳入非人类能动性的呼吁。
    Findings support contentions by Cofré-Bravo et al., 2019; Madureira et al., 2022 and Sutherland et al. (2017) that farmers access different sources of knowledge and advice on different topics. ... In utilising assemblage thinking, the analysis in this paper included material actants, presenting empirical evidence in line with recent calls to include non-human agency in agrarian studies...
  • 6.1 理解农业创新: 创新过程必然涉及多个异质元素的整合，这些元素（如新建筑、银行家、同行）往往不属于传统定义的“农业部门”。银行等金融行动者在决定创新能否发生上扮演了关键角色，但他们在AKIS文献中却鲜被关注。
    As such, banks are pivotal actors for supporting the transformative change required for the agricultural sector... but have had very limited connection to the AKIS literature.
  • 6.1 理解农业创新: “断裂”是农场实践变革的关键，农户家庭生命周期与盈利性同等重要。这提示政策干预应关注这些变革更易发生的特定时期。
    Triggers, or ‘ruptures’ as they are described in assemblage theory, suggest an important area for intervention; that is, there are some periods in the lifecourse of a farm household where change is more actively considered...
  • 6.2 政策启示: 案例表明，当代农场的知识需求远超传统农业咨询范畴，而现有咨询服务能力薄弱，甚至缺位。microAKIS的主要构成是同行、投入品供应商等，这些角色常在AKIS评估中被忽略。
    As a set, the cases demonstrate that the knowledge needs of contemporary farms far exceed the traditional territory of agricultural advice. ... The microAKIS identified primarily involved input suppliers and peer farmers, both of which are often omitted from AKIS assessments (Fieldsend et al., 2021).
  • 6.2 政策启示: 挑战了欧洲政策中对“独立”“公正”咨询的强调。技术供应商的深度绑定、独立顾问资源有限以及农民基于信任关系的选择，都说明现实的复杂性远超政策理想。
    Recent European initiatives have emphasised ‘independent’ and ‘impartial’ advice... The case examples demonstrated the problems with this perspective.
  • 6.2 政策启示: 肯定了EIP-AGRI等政策对“点对点学习”的重视，但指出案例中的先驱农民成为专家是自发形成的，而非由顾问促成的“互动式创新”。农民在主动共创新的咨询配置中展现了强大的能动性。
    The cases presented do affirm the emphasis on peer-to-peer learning embedded in EIP Agri approaches. ... However, farmers appeared to have taken up this role themselves, rather than through the facilitation of agricultural advisors (i.e. ‘interactive innovation processes’).
  • 6.3 推进AKIS思考: 重申集置理论作为一种分析工具的优势，它提供了一套概念（历史性、断裂、领域化等）来理解创新的过程性、灵活性和共构性，并将学界和政策的注意力引向农业部门之外。
    A particular advantage of assemblage theory is that it offers a set of conceptual terms for analysing innovation processes, particularly historicity and rupture, elements and spaces of possibility, and territorialisation and deterritorialization.
  • 6.3 推进AKIS思考: 通过将集置理论应用于AKIS，本文也发展了该理论本身，即将“学习”视为一种关键的“领域化”力量，是维系集置体的“粘合剂”。
    Reciprocally, applying assemblage theory to AKIS develops the role of learning as a territorialising force, a ‘glue’ which holds assemblages together.
  • 6.3 推进AKIS思考: 承认集置理论应用的局限性，如变更机制模糊、存在“挑选”分析元素的风险，以及本文以农民为中心的方法论与超越人类中心理论的去中心化倾向之间的张力。
    There are limitations to the application of assemblage theory... the precise mechanisms of change remain vague, attributed by the researchers retrospectively.

7. Conclusion
  • 总结指出，尽管欧洲日益重视通过咨询服务支持农业创新，但这种“过程”导向的政策（如多主体方法）与主要关注狭隘服务设施的“基础设施”导向的资金支持之间存在脱节。
    While valuable, there is a disconnection between these ‘process’ or soft system approaches and the funding for AKIS, which largely oriented towards maintaining a narrow infrastructure of service provision.
  • 强调农民正面临数字化、气候变化、外来劳工等外部冲击，这些冲击要求他们将新元素“领域化”到农场集置体中，而这些动态是宏观视角难以捕捉的。
    Farmers are confronted with exogenous shocks and triggers (e.g. digitalisation, climate change, migrant workers) that impact the conditions of farming and create the need to territorialise new elements into their farm assemblages...
  • 主张在确定政策方向之前，必须对农民的microAKIS的复杂性、细微差异和演变过程进行调查，识别和研究新型混合咨询模式。
    Therefore, we argue that new hybrid models of advice provision require recognition and investigation, and the complexities, nuances and evolution of farmers’ microAKIS need to be investigated, before these determinations can be made.
  • 呼吁对更广泛的行动者（如数字化互动）进行综合研究，以揭示农民在获取知识方面的新差距和不平等机制，同时发现打破路径依赖和整合新行动者的机会。
    Comprehensive studies of these assemblages could reveal new gaps and mechanisms of inequalities between farmers in their access to knowledge, but also highlight some opportunities to break path-dependency and integrate new actors.
