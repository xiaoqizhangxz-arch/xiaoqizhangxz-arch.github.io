The Role of Tacit Knowledge in Developing Networks for Sustainable Agriculture

Nigel Curry* and James Kirwan

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

作为一名长期关注农业知识体系（AKIS）与另类食物网络（AFNs）交叉领域的农村社会学专家，我认为本文的核心论点极具洞察力：在从“生产主义农业”向“可持续农业”的范式转型中，占主导地位的客观主义认识论（objectivist epistemology）已显不足。作者们令人信服地主张，可持续农业所内含的复杂的社会、文化与环境价值，必须通过建构主义认识论（constructivist epistemology）的棱镜才能被充分理解。在此框架下，默会知识（tacit knowledge）——那种无法被完全形式化、需通过经验内化的直觉性知识——是理解和驱动可持续农业网络（如文中的LINSA）成功运作的关键，却往往被研究所忽视。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论证坚实地建立在一个为期两年的深度参与式案例研究之上。研究对象是英国的“布莱顿和霍夫食物伙伴关系”（Brighton and Hove Food Partnership, BHFP），这是一个典型的“可持续农业学习与创新网络”（LINSA）。作者们通过与超过100名网络参与者的互动（包括访谈、焦点小组、工作坊等），收集了丰富的质性数据。这些经验证据随后通过一个精巧的理论框架进行分析，该框架将默会知识操作化为六个可观察的维度（风俗、技艺、民间传说、身份认同、角色和话语），从而系统地揭示了默会知识在网络内部的实际运作方式。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑层次分明，极具说服力。首先，他们通过批判传统农业知识与信息系统（AKIS）的局限性，指出其基于客观主义、自上而下的“专家知识”模式无法满足可持续农业的多元价值需求。其次，文章深入梳理了建构主义对客观主义的批判，援引了波兰尼（Polanyi）、库恩（Kuhn）、福柯（Foucault）等思想家，强调了知识的社会建构性、价值负载性以及信任在知识采纳中的核心地位。一个重要的理论贡献在于，作者清晰地辨析了“默会知识”与“地方知识”（local knowledge）的差异，强调前者是*无法*被言说的，而非仅仅是*尚未*被言说。随后，文章巧妙地借鉴并改造了Gerholm（1990）的框架，将波兰尼较为抽象的默会知识概念转化为六个具体的、可在经验层面被探究的分析范畴。最后，通过将此六维框架应用于BHFP的案例分析，文章生动地展示了默会知识——从对食物跨领域角色的共同理解（风俗），到驾驭复杂非正式网络的直觉能力（技艺），再到关于布莱顿特殊性的地方叙事（民间传说）——是如何具体地塑造网络凝聚力、指导行动并最终促成其成功的。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的方法论与其理论立场高度一致，堪称典范。研究者们采用了参与式的“合作生产”（co-production）方法，这本身就是建构主义认识论在研究实践中的体现。通过世界咖啡馆、工作坊、焦点小组等互动式学习方法，研究者不仅是“外部观察者”，更是与研究对象共同建构理解的“参与者”。这种“阐释主义”（interpretivist）路径致力于从行动者自身的视角去理解其社会世界，并赋予了参与者在研究过程中的话语权。数据的分析则遵循了扎根理论精神的归纳式主题分析法。此方法论的选择不仅增强了研究的效度，也体现了对研究伦理和赋权理念的深刻反思。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文选择英国的布莱顿和霍夫食物伙伴关系（BHFP）作为单一深度案例，这一选择极具代表性。BHFP作为一个在主流AKIS之外运作的多方利益相关者网络，致力于在城市层面推行可持续食物战略，是诠释LINSA理念的绝佳样本。分析表明，BHFP的韧性与成就，并不仅仅源于其明确的战略或成文的规则，而是更深层次地植根于网络成员间共享的、非言说的默契、价值观和实践智慧。这个案例有力地证明了，若要真正理解这类新兴的可持续农业网络，分析其背后的默会知识维度是不可或缺的。

引言 (Introduction)

本章节设定了研究的核心问题：传统农业研究侧重于知识的“内容”与“传递系统”，却忽略了知识的“形式”。作者提出，主导“生产主义”农业的客观主义认识论，无法捕捉“可持续”农业所蕴含的社会、文化与环境价值。因此，本文主张采用建构主义认识论，并聚焦于其中的核心概念——默会知识（tacit knowledge），以期更深刻地理解可持续农业网络的运作。文章预告了将通过一个英国案例，并借助一个六分法的默会知识框架进行实证探讨。

  • 作者指出，现有研究多关注可持续农业的知识内容和学习系统，而较少关注知识形式的差异。
    Much has been written from a social-scientific perspective about the content of the knowledge required for the development of sustainable agriculture (Hendrix et al. 1992; Lichtfouse et al. 2009; Hanayani and Prawito 2011) and about the appropriate systems of learning required for knowledge transfer in such development (Pretty 1994, Röling and Wagemakers, 1998).
  • 引用Morgan和Murdoch的观点，批评传统农业中客观主义知识用“专家”知识取代“地方”知识，并忽视了社会文化价值。
    Morgan and Murdoch (2000) note that the dominance of objectivist knowledge in ‘conventional’ agriculture tends to replace ‘local’ knowledge with ‘expert’ knowledge and underplays social, economic and cultural values.
  • 作者主张默会知识因其能明确包含价值观和信仰体系，故值得深入研究，以揭示可持续农业的内涵。
    Within this constructivist knowledge domain, the article examines the nature of tacit knowledge because it is claimed to offer the potential to embrace both values (Merton 1973) and belief systems (Polanyi 1946) explicitly...
  • 为了进行实证研究，作者借鉴Gerholm的框架，将默会知识操作化为六种形式。
    Using the notions of tacit knowledge asserted particularly by Polanyi (1966) and developed by Gerholm (1990), six forms are postulated: customs; savoir-faire, folklore, identity, roles and discourse.
  • 研究采用了参与式方法论，强调研究者与利益相关者的共同参与和知识的“合作生产”。
    A participatory methodology has been deployed, which advocates the involvement of research stakeholders in the majority, if not the entirety, of the research process (Allen-Collinson et al. 2005). ... Commonly termed research ‘co-production’, such an approach provides experiential expertise that outside ‘experts’ on their own might not perceive (Collins and Evans 2002).
  • 方法论的选择基于“阐释主义”路径，旨在从行动者的视角理解其社会世界，并可能产生更好、更可行的结果。
    As an ‘interpretivist’ approach (Evans et al. 2000) it commits the researchers to understanding the social world from the perspective of those social actors who inhabit it. It also allows participant-stakeholders’ preferences and needs to inform the research process, leading to what Ostrom (1996) considers are potentially better and more achievable outcomes.
  • 作者明确了本文对“知识”的界定，区分了数据、信息和知识，认为知识是经过个人或群体吸收和阐释后的信息。
    Information has to be understood and interpreted to become knowledge (Lee and Yang 2000). Whilst open to debate, using this convention allows objectivist and constructivist knowledges (as opposed to data and information) to be distinguished.

可持续农业的知识背景 (The knowledge context for sustainable agriculture)

本章界定了可持续农业的复杂性，指出其本质上无法被精确“定义”，因为它融合了市场与非市场的多元目标（社会、文化、环境）。这种复杂性要求一种参与式的、基于信任的学习系统。然而，英国现有的农业知识与信息系统（AKIS）在市场化转型后，日益偏重商业和规制导向的“科学”知识，无法满足可持续农业的非市场、价值导向的知识需求。因此，催生了如本文案例所示的、在主流体系之外运作的替代性知识网络（LINSAs）。

  • 引用Pretty的观点，说明可持续农业因其非客观主义的本质而无法被统一定义。
    Sustainable agriculture cannot be defined (Pretty 1994). This is because, Pretty claims, it is not an objectivist construct subject to independent verification by immutable laws.
  • 综合多方定义，指出可持续农业的核心在于平衡经济、社会、环境等多重目标。
    Thus, it should indefinitely meet food needs within acceptable social, economic and environmental costs (Crosson, 1992) and be resilient to environmental and economic change (Conway and Barbier 1990).
  • 作者强调，可持续农业的学习系统必须是参与性的，因为它需要容纳所有利益相关者的价值观，并且知识的建立更多依赖信任而非验证。
    To the extent that knowledge of, and for, these values goes beyond objective knowledge, such values are commonly developed through trust as much as through verification (Palmer et al. 2009).
  • 批评英国的AKIS在市场化后，失去了原有的信任网络，转而专注于商业化的“科学”知识。
    From the late 1980s, it shifted from a state-based system to a market-based one where commercial research into ‘scientific’ knowledge displaced trust-based processes, and learning networks between farmers and state advisers were lost (Hall and Pretty 2008).
  • 指出AKIS提供的非市场建议也多是出于商业考量（如获取补贴、合规），而非真正服务于可持续农业的多元价值。
    Whilst advice about the non-market components of agriculture has had a role in the sector, this non-market advice has developed largely in the context of securing grant aid and for regulatory compliance (Garforth et al. 2003; House of Commons Environment, Food and Rural Affairs Committee, 2011), both of which are commercial considerations.
  • 由于主流AKIS的缺位，关于可持续农业价值观的知识通常在常规体系之外传播，形成了LINSAs这类通过合作学习和非正式机制进行知识转移的网络。
    Knowledge and information systems for sustainable agriculture that embrace beliefs and actions beyond conventional agriculture are not only different in the content of knowledge... but in the means of knowledge transfer. This more commonly takes place through co-learning, networks, mutual support and more informal mechanisms than through ‘expert’ advice (Curry et al. 2012).

农业知识的形式 (Forms of agricultural knowledge)

本章深入剖析了客观主义与建构主义两大认识论。首先，它将客观主义（或实证主义）知识描绘为追求普适性、价值中立的“科学真理”，并以作物育种等技术性农业领域为例，说明其在提高生产力方面的巨大成功。随后，文章系统地阐述了建构主义对客观主义的批判，论点包括：1) 科学知识是社会建构的，受权力关系和文化背景影响；2) 知识的接受依赖于信任和信念，而非纯粹的“事实”；3) 客观主义通过“有限理性”过度简化了复杂现实，忽视了系统间的关联；4) 科学“真理”本身是范式转换的结果，具有不确定性。这一系列批判为下文引入默会知识作为建构主义知识的重要形式铺平了道路。

  • 作者将客观主义知识定义为试图消除社会影响，以确定独立于个体思想的“真实”世界。
    Objectivist (sometimes termed ‘positivist’ in the literature) knowledge approaches in agriculture aspire to eliminate all social influences over knowledge creation and in so doing determine the ‘true’ nature of the world outside of the individual mind (Louth 1990; Wilson 1998).
  • 以植物育种为例，说明客观主义“科学知识”在农业领域的应用，其目标是提高产量，并且被认为是社会中立的。
    Cleveland (2001) notes that plant breeding has exemplified the development of this objectivist ‘scientific knowledge’ in agriculture... Objectivist knowledge is argued to be socially neutral and responds to market signals such as profitability or gross margins (Cleveland 2001), avoiding any consideration of values outside of the market.
  • 引用波兰尼，指出所谓的客观知识实际上依赖于科学家之间的相互认可和共识，而非绝对真理。
    At an interpersonal level, far from being value neutral, objectivist knowledge is simply validated as being true by mutual endorsement rather than any objective truth (Polanyi 1951).
  • 建构主义者认为客观主义忽视了地方情境，例如高产杂交作物的开发可能更多地反映了资本主义追求利润的文化，而非普适的科学进步。
    Whist developing hybrid plants that yield increased outputs per acre is considered good objectivist science, it may well be more to do with the culture of capitalism seeking to maximise profit (Kloppenburg 1988).
  • 强调信任在知识采纳中的重要性，指出人们对科学机构的信任度会影响其对科学策略的接受程度。
    Palmer et al. (2009) for example, report people’s lack of trust in scientific institutions having a strong influence on the uptake of strategies for the control of animal disease.
  • 批评客观主义的还原论倾向，它将复杂问题分解为简单变量，即所谓的“有限理性”，但这可能导致对现实的认知不全。
    This approach has been termed by Simon (1991) ‘bounded rationality’ which, through simplification, can lead to imperfect knowledge, uncertainty and an increased risk of making wrong decisions.
  • 引用Dryzek和Haas的观点，指出建构主义知识能够更好地处理复杂问题的联动性、多重价值体系和政策交叉点。
    Constructivist knowledge is better at addressing the linkages. Haas (2004) emphasises this in the context of sustainable development generally because, he claims, it takes place through interacting elements and intersections of complex problems.
  • 引用库恩的“范式转移”理论，论证科学“真理”并非线性累积，而是依赖于科学共同体的共识，并且会发生根本性转变。
    This new consensus, Kuhn (1962) states, then changes the nature of ‘objective’ science sometimes fundamentally, through paradigm shifts, which often provide competing accounts of scientific knowledge.

农业知识中的客观主义与建构主义 (Objectivism and constructivism in knowledge for sustainable agriculture)

本章将前述理论辩论具体应用于农业领域。作者引用Morgan和Murdoch的经典研究，指出常规农业中编码化的、客观主义知识的扩张，不仅是一种技术转变，更是一场权力转移，它剥夺了农民的地方知识，将食物生产的控制权转移给了产业链上下游的工业巨头。与此相对，可持续农业呼唤对建构主义知识形式的回归，特别是对“地方知识”的重新重视。作者同时探讨了将二者结合的“整体论”或“混合”观点，但引用Sparkes和Smith的观点，认为这种中间立场在哲学上难以成立。本章的最后，作者做出了一个关键的理论区分：将Morgan和Murdoch所说的“地方的、情境特定的、默会的知识”进一步细化，把可言说的“地方知识”与不可言说的“默会知识”分离开来，为后文的分析奠定基础。

  • 作者阐述了Morgan和Murdoch的观点，即常规农业被客观主义、编码化的知识所驱动，这种知识易于标准化和转移。
    Morgan and Murdoch (2000) set these differing epistemologies into an agricultural context, arguing that conventional agriculture (in Europe at least) has been driven by objectivist approaches, using codified knowledge that is standardised, explicit and easily transferable.
  • 这种编码化知识的增加，代表了知识从农场向外的重新分配，以及从历史上占主导地位的建构主义方法的转变。
    This increase in ‘codified’ knowledge has redistributed knowledge away from the farm, but it also represents a shift away from more constructivist approaches that pertained in agriculture historically.
  • 引用Cleveland的观点，强调发展可持续农业需要更清晰地理解生物物理现实和社会现实对科学知识的共同贡献。
    ‘Our ability to develop more sustainable agriculture will depend to an important degree on a clearer understanding of the joint contribution of biophysical and social reality to scientific knowledge, and of the epistemological processes of its production.’ Cleveland (2001, p. 263)
  • 虽然有研究者观察到农民在决策中会“混合”使用客观主义和建构主义知识，但作者引用Sparkes和Smith的观点对此提出质疑，认为世界要么是客观的，要么是相对的，不存在中间地带。
    But Sparkes and Smith (2009) argue that this ‘mingling’ position is untenable. Either there is an objective scientific reality that exists outside of ourselves, or there is not. The world is either absolute, or it is relative (Smith and Hodkinson 2005).
  • 作者指出，可持续农业需要重新学习“地方知识”，摆脱对集约化农业编码知识的路径依赖。
    As well as social and economic context, Morgan and Murdoch (2000) emphasise the importance of relearning local knowledge for sustainable agriculture. Such agriculture requires a departure from path dependent knowledge – a stepping aside from the codified knowledge of intensive agriculture.
  • 在文章的结尾，作者对Morgan和Murdoch的观点进行了修正和发展，明确区分了可言说的“地方知识”和不可言说的“默会知识”。
    A departure from this position is argued here, drawing a distinction between local knowledge which is context dependent but known and articulated, and tacit knowledge, which essentially remains unstated. This distinction is critical in being able to differentiate tacit knowledge in the way set out below.

默会知识 (Tacit knowledge)

本章是文章的理论核心，对“默会知识”进行了深入界定和操作化。作者首先追溯至波兰尼和默顿的经典论述，将默会知识定义为科学知识中非显性的、基于信念和价值观的部分。关键在于采纳Lejeune的观点，强调默会知识是“无法被形式化的”，而非“尚未被形式化的”。它源于经验和直觉，通过互动、观察和社会化等非语言方式被内化。本章最大的贡献在于，将波兰尼较为抽象的哲学概念，通过借鉴人类学家Gerholm的框架，转化为一个包含六个具体、可供实证研究的分析范畴：风俗（customs）、技艺（savoir-faire）、民间传说（folklore）、身份认同（identity）、角色（roles）和话语（discourse）。

  • 引用波兰尼，指出客观知识包含一个非显性的、我们通常无意识的潜在信念系统部分，即“默会”知识。
    Polanyi (1946) claims that objectivist knowledge has an ‘explicit’ part (the findings) but also a ‘non-explicit’ part which is our underlying belief systems.
  • 引用默顿，将默会知识定义为科学中一套具有约束力的、复杂的价值观和规范。
    Merton (1973) suggests that scientific knowledge comprises three components: the methods that verify facts, the facts themselves and then a set of complex values and norms that are held to be binding... Merton terms these values and norms, ‘tacit’ knowledge.
  • 采纳Lejeune的关键区分，强调默会知识的本质是“不能被形式化”，这将其与仅仅是“未被形式化”的知识区别开来。
    In particular, he suggests, this fails to distinguish between that knowledge which has yet to be formalised (which is not tacit knowledge) and that which cannot be formalised (which is).
  • 根据波兰尼的观点，默会知识源于经验和直觉，并通过共享经验获得，因此难以用理性语言表达。
    Tacit knowledge, Polanyi feels, is that which derives from experience and intuition and through shared experiences (Allen-Collinson 2004). As such, it is often difficult to translate into rational language because it derives from a context, or a set of cultural rules, often implicit (Ingram 2008).
  • 作者借鉴Gerholm的框架作为启发性工具，以进行对默会知识的实证检验。
    Gerholm (1990) provides a useful framework for adapting Polanyi’s (1966) four elements of tacit knowledge into an empirically testable frame. ... This is done as a heuristic device in order to allow empirical testing of tacit knowledge (Garfinkel 1967).
  • 基于Gerholm的框架，文章提出了六种默会知识类型，包括“风俗”、“技艺”（savoir-faire，引用布迪厄的“游戏感”）、“民间传说”、“身份认同”、“角色”和“话语”（引用戈夫曼的前后台理论）。
    The second, to use Gerholm’s (1990) term, is savoir-faire. ... It is similar to that form of tacit knowledge that Albino et al. (2001) term ‘the knack’, Giunipero et al. (1999) ‘intuition’ and Bourdieu (2000), ‘a feel for the game’. ... Here, situations have to be read correctly and discourses adjusted as a result.

实证探索默会知识的性质 (Exploring the nature of tacit knowledge empirically)

本章是研究的实证核心，将前述六维框架应用于BHFP案例，通过参与者的引述生动地展示了默会知识在网络中的具体体现。风俗体现为一种普遍共识，即将食物议题置于更广泛的可持续生活（社区发展、健康、经济）的整体框架内。技艺(Savoir-faire)表现为在没有明确规章的复杂网络中，依靠直觉、信任和“感觉”来推动工作的能力。民间传说则围绕着布莱顿这座城市的独特性展开，认为网络的成功与该地特有的“嬉皮”文化、开放性和合作精神密不可分。身份认同在两个层面被感知：一是对城市内部的强烈归属感，二是在与主流商业农业对比中形成的独特身份。角色的默会理解体现在，尽管没有明确的职责划分，参与者对自己及他人的位置和贡献有一种心照不宣的认识。话语的默会知识则表现在成员能根据不同场合（如政策文件、内部通讯、与不同群体的对话）灵活调整沟通的重点和方式。

  • 风俗 (Customs): 参与者普遍默认，BHFP的核心是推动一种更全面的可持续生活方式，而食物只是其中的一个切入点，而非最终目的。
    ‘Our system is looking at the whole system, so ... our role concerns a more holistic approach to sustainable living: community development, healthy lifestyles, mental health – it is about all of these things. ... Agriculture and food production are part of this but we are not taking this as a starting point’.
  • 技艺 (Savoir-faire): 在一个庞大、动态且非规制化的网络中，参与者认为纯粹的“理性”规划是不可行的，必须依靠“直觉”和“本能”来把握方向和保持动力。
    ‘This intuitive grasp is strong and critical. There are so many things going on in developing a sustainable Brighton and Hove that there has to be an intuition that sits underneath it. A purely “rational” basis for development would make the whole process just too big for progress to be made. There has to be a degree of “gut instinct” in developments to keep the momentum up.’
  • 民间传说 (Folklore): 参与者普遍将网络的成功归因于布莱顿独特的城市文化和历史，认为这里的开放性、合作精神和“嬉皮”底蕴是网络得以蓬勃发展的土壤。
    ‘There is a certain “hippyishness” of the, sort of, the people who’ve settled here, that have kind of formed this together, but actually now they’re now looking at it very cleverly, very responsibly ... It’s the nature of Brighton is why it’s worked’.
  • 身份认同 (Identity): 网络成员对主流商业农业持有一种批判性的疏离感，强调社区农业与前者在资金、规模和目标上的巨大差异。
    ‘Yes, there’s little connection with commercial agriculture, yes, ... there is more crossover with small scale organic agriculture but even then ... it remains to be seen.’
  • 角色 (Roles): 尽管网络是松散、非正式的，但核心成员对其运作方式有一种共识，即伙伴关系作为一个“枢纽”，促进沟通和知识共享，但不直接命令各个自主项目。
    ‘Now, the Partnership can act as a hub for all of those and facilitate communication between all of those but, you know, they’re autonomous projects at the same time. The Partnership does not tell them what to do but they are part of the larger movement and they can help share knowledge’.
  • 话语 (Discourse): 参与者认识到，与不同对象沟通时，重要的不是改变语言，而是调整对话的“焦点”，去理解对方的需求和挑战，并寻找协同作用。
    ‘So it’s not so much the language, I suppose, but it’s about understanding what their priorities are’.

实证材料分析

  • 本章节主要基于研究收集的原始数据（访谈、焦点小组等），未引用外部文献来支撑其论点。所有论据均来自案例研究中的直接引语。

结论：默会知识对可持续农业有多重要？(Conclusion: how important is tacit knowledge for sustainable agriculture?)

本章总结了研究发现，强调尽管默会知识因其内隐性而难以研究，但它在可持续农业网络的形成与发展中扮演着至关重要的角色。这种知识是通过经验“内化”而非直接“学习”的，它与“信念”和“事实”紧密相连，因此被客观主义认识论所边缘化。作者指出，默会知识的交流是高度情境化的，需要给予者和接受者之间有共同的价值观，这是传统AKIS所无法提供的。最后，文章拓展了Morgan和Murdoch关于“协商性知识”的观点，认为默会知识是其核心组成部分，并呼吁未来的农业知识体系必须更全面地拥抱这种建构主义的、情境依赖的知识形式。

  • 作者重申，采用Gerholm的分类法可以有效探索默会知识的不同方面，并且观察到它在案例LINSA的发展中扮演了重要角色。
    Nevertheless, the adaptation of Gerholm’s (1990) taxonomy does allow an exploration of some of its different facets. As a subset located within constructivist epistemologies, tacit knowledge described in this way can be observed to be a significant presence in the development of the LINSA in this case-study.
  • 有研究表明，农民（无论是“有机的”还是“常规的”）都会混合使用客观主义和建构主义知识，这说明二者的关系是复杂的。
    Kings and Ilbery (2010), for example, found that there is no necessary relationship between those who believe that they are farming in sustainable ways and their adherence to constructivist knowledge.
  • 默会知识必须通过体验和吸收来获得，而不是简单地传递，它涉及“信念”和“事实”，这使得它在客观主义认识论中被边缘化。
    Certainly, tacit knowledge cannot be overtly learned. It has to be experienced and assimilated rather than simply passed on. It invariably involves ‘beliefs’ as well as ‘facts’, which can marginalise it within the objectivist epistemology.
  • 作者引用Morgan和Murdoch的观点，认为知识是连接LINSAs这类伙伴关系的关键要素，并可分为“规定性”和“协商性”两种。
    Morgan and Murdoch (2000) suggest that knowledge is one of the key elements that binds partnerships such as LINSAs together. In this role it can be prescriptive or negotiative.
  • 作者进一步发展了这一观点，将默会知识视为“协商性知识”的核心，并指出不同类型的知识反映了伙伴关系中不同的权力关系。
    Murdoch (1998) suggests that the prescriptive knowledge has a ‘coercive’ element to it and negotiative, a ‘co-operative’ (or even consensual) element and therefore different types of knowledge describe different kinds of power relations within partnerships.
