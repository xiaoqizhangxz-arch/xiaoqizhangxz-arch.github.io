How do coffee farmers engage with digital technologies? A capabilities perspective

Francisco Hidalgo · Athena Birkenberg · Thomas Daum · Christine Bosch · Xiomara F. Quiñones‑Ruiz

全文概要总结

作为一名长期关注农业技术社会建构与农村发展的学者，我认为 Hidalgo 及其同事的这篇文章，是对当前数字农业话语的一项精辟而及时的批判性干预。它有力地揭示了全球北方主导的、以技术为中心的“解决方案主义”与全球南方小农户复杂的、根植于地方的生活世界之间的深刻断裂。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：数字农业中普遍存在的“现实-设计鸿沟”（reality-design gap）源于主流发展范式未能认识并尊重农民多样化的世界观、发展理念和对“美好生活”的定义。作者运用阿玛蒂亚·森（Amartya Sen）的“能力アプローチ”（Capabilities Approach），主张哥伦比亚咖啡农对数字技术的接触（或不接触）并非简单的“采纳”或“拒绝”，而是一个复杂的、由能动性驱动的社会建构过程。这一过程的核心动力是农民追求他们所珍视的生活（valued life），这种生活以“关系式农业”（relational way of farming）、家庭凝聚和社区福祉为中心，而非单纯的生产效率最大化。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的论据坚实地建立在对哥伦比亚两个咖啡种植社区73个农户的定性访谈数据之上。这些经验证据被巧妙地组织成三个相互关联的主题：首先，“关系式农业”揭示了农民将经验知识和与土地的情感联系置于数字工具之上，视后者为与田间劳动不相容的“外来物”。其次，“（失）连接的机器” ((dis)connected machines) 探讨了农民对机器人、无人机等新兴技术的看法，这些看法深受文化想象（科幻电影）和过往制度性失败（如FNC推广的机械采收试点）的影响，从而产生了普遍的不信任。最后，“被滋养的家庭与社区” (nurtured families and communities) 则展示了技术的选择性挪用——智能手机等通讯工具因其能维系家庭纽带、促进子女教育，而被深度整合进家庭生活，这恰恰与农民的核心“功能”（functionings）相契合。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑清晰且富有层次。他们首先解构了主流数字农业话语中的技术中心主义和生产主义偏见。随后，引入“能力アプローチ”作为核心分析框架，将分析焦点从技术本身转移到农民的“能动性”（agency）和“发展自由”上。通过将这一理论框架应用于田野数据，文章极具说服力地阐释了农民的技术选择何以在其自身的价值体系中是完全理性的。例如，拒绝一台采收机器并非源于“数字素养”的缺乏，而是为了捍卫手工艺品质、保护社区劳动关系，以及维护一种将农场视为有感知实体的“关系本体论”（relational ontology）。相反，采纳智能手机的目的不是为了实现精准农业，而是为了社会再生产（social reproduction）。这种鲜明的对比构成了其论证的核心，深刻揭示了技术的意义是如何在特定社会文化情境中被重新协商和定义的。

4. 方法 (Method)

该研究采用了以人为本的定性研究方法。通过在两个具有代表性（一个有机，一个常规）的哥伦比亚咖啡产区，对73个农户（包括农民本人、其配偶和子女）进行半结构化访谈，研究获得了丰富而深入的一手资料。这种家庭层面的访谈设计，深刻洞察了农场决策作为家庭成员间协商过程的复杂性。数据分析则运用了扎根理论中的归纳编码法（inductive coding），从中提炼出三个核心主题，确保了研究结论是直接从经验数据中生发而来，而非先验理论的强行套用。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

研究案例选取了哥伦比亚两个典型的小农咖啡种植社区。咖啡作为一种全球性商品，其生产者却往往是地方性的小农，这一案例本身就内含了全球与地方、现代与传统之间的张力。通过对这一具体案例的深入剖析，研究不仅为数字农业在小农经济体中的应用提供了宝贵的经验证据，也使其理论洞见具有了更广泛的现实关照意义，超越了咖啡种植这一特定情境。

Introduction

本章节开宗明义，指出了数字农业领域存在的“现实-设计鸿沟”。作者认为，这一鸿沟源于一个主导性的、技术中心主义的发展范式，该范式忽视了地方世界观和对“理想未来”的多元解读。文章将自身定位为对这一主流话语的回应，旨在通过运用森（Sen）的能力アプローチ和关系理论视角，将全球南方的地方性数字化实践故事置于学术讨论的中心。

    A gap between the perspective of developers and proponents of digital technologies (electronic tools that generate, store, process, transmit, display and use data) and the reality of farming communities has been systematically reported in the social literature on digital agriculture and on Information and Communication Technologies for Development (ICT4D) (Prost et al. 2012; Heeks 2002; Dodson et al. 2013; Contreras-Medina et al. 2020; Rose et al. 2018; Eastwood et al. 2019).

    Critical views argue that digital agriculture is dominated by a productivist and technocentric perspective of development (Bronson 2018; Bronson and Knezevic 2016; Kuch et al. 2020; Lajoie-O’Malley et al. 2020; Rotz et al. 2019a; Wolf and Wood 1997).
  • 指出即使是提倡包容性的“负责任创新”研究，也往往聚焦于促进技术“采纳”，而忽视了“采纳”对社区的真实意义以及这是否是他们期望的发展路径。
    Even the research under the scope of responsible innovation (see Bronson 2019; Hellström 2003; Owen et al. 2013; Stilgoe et al. 2013), which advocates for a more inclusive approach in the design of digital technologies, tends to narrow down the possibilities to understand alternative perspectives of development, as it is often mainly concerned with identifying the mechanisms to promote the uptake of digital technologies, without much reflecting on what uptake really means for the communities, or whether it is actually their desired development pathway (Kleine et al. 2012; Zheng 2009).
  • 强调了研究中存在的地理和文化偏见，即现有研究主要集中在全球北方的工业化农业背景下，因此亟需来自全球南方的本土分析。
    For this reason, there is a pressing need to overcome geographical and cultural bias in research about digital presences in rural spaces (Cieslik et al. 2018; Mann 2018; Sulaiman et al. 2012; Ash et al. 2018).
  • 引入阿玛蒂亚·森的能力アプローチ作为核心分析框架，强调研究焦点应是技术如何帮助人们实现他们自己珍视的生活，而非满足教条式的发展观念。
    Under this framework, resources only become assets when they can be used by individuals to accomplish the life they value. This idea opens the possibility to explore multiple development perspectives. Through this conceptualization, we focused our analysis on the opportunity for people to use technologies to achieve their own particular goals, rather than on how technologies fulfill aspirations of dogmatic notions of development (those that compare development with economic growth or modernization). (Sen 1999)
  • 结合关系理论视角，认为农民的能动性是在与知识、价值体系、社会制度以及非人类能动者（土地、技术等）的互动中形成的。
    Following a relational perspective (Higgins 2006; Darnhofer 2020; Forney and Dwiartama 2023), we conceive that farmer’s agency in this process of appropriation is shaped by occurring interactions between knowledge, value systems, social institutions, and the agency of non-humans (land, plants, animals, technologies), in the search for integrating technologies into the life they find valuable.

Capabilities approach, agency, and farmers’ interactions with technologies

本章详细阐述了研究的理论框架。它首先解释了森（Sen）关于“发展即自由”的核心思想，以及“功能”（functionings）和“能力”（capabilities）这两个关键概念。接着，将这一框架应用于技术研究，认为技术只有在能扩展人类能力时才成为“资产”。本章进一步论证，农民是积极的能动者，他们通过选择、拒绝和改造来“社会地建构”技术，而非被动接受。最后，引入“机会结构”（opportunity structure）概念，强调农民的能动性是在与社会结构和包括土地、机器在内的非人类行动者的复杂互动中被共同建构的。

  • 阐述了森（Sen）的发展观，即发展是人们拥有过上他们珍视的生活的自由，这与正统的、将发展等同于经济增长的观念形成对比。
    Sen (1999) defines development as the freedom that people have, to live the lives that they value. In contrast to more orthodox notions that make development a convention comparable to economic growth, this perspective focuses on the expansion of people’s agency to pursue their own goals in life as the basis for human development (Kleine et al. 2012; Zheng et al. 2018; Jiménez and Zheng 2018; Poveda and Roberts 2018).
  • 界定了“功能”（functionings）和“能力”（capabilities）的概念，前者指一个人可能珍视的各种“做”与“是”的状态，后者指一个人实际能够实现的功能。
    Central to Sen’s conceptualization of development are the concepts of functionings and capabilities (Kleine et al. 2012). Functionings refers to the various things a person can value doing or being in life, while capabilities are the functionings that a person can actually achieve (Sen 1999).
  • 提出技术只有在被选择用来实现有意义的生活目标时才能被视为资产，这与技术作为人类已有能力的“促成者”的观点相符。
    Building on this perspective, technologies can be considered assets if they can be used to obtain meaningful goals in life, if chosen to do so, resulting in the expansion of human capabilities. The idea that technologies can be perceived as “enablers to the pre-existing capabilities of human beings” (Banerjee 2013 p. 79) alings with this value of technologies as a tool for the expansion of human-development.
  • 强调技术是被社会建构的，农民在创新中扮演着积极的转化者角色，而非被动的采纳者。
    This understanding links the capabilities approach with the idea that technologies are socially constructed (Bijker 1995; Hughes 1987; Orlikowski 1992).
  • 引用了“机会结构”（opportunity structure）的概念，说明能动性受到社会、政治和经济机会的促成或制约，这些机会由正式和非正式制度决定。
    Capabilities and personal agency, however, are not solely the result of the intrinsic capacities of the individual (Crocker 2008), but rather are entitlements made possible or constrained "by the social, political and economic opportunities available to us" (Sen 1999 p. xi-xii). The set of these opportunities is described by Alsop and Heinsohn (2005) as the 'opportunity structure'.
  • 论述了农民的能动性并非绝对，而是持续地被与技术、生物物理实体和社会情境的物质关系所塑造，例如通过“修补”（tinkering）的过程。
    The agency of farmers is not absolute, as it is constantly shaped by material relationships established with technological and biophysical entities and contexts. It is well known that farmers continuously have to 'negotiate' with and work around uncontrollable weather, unruly plants, diseases, breakable machines, and financial constraints (Darnhofer 2020). The concept of 'tinkering' as it is used by Higgins et al. (2017), is useful to understand the operation of agency in technological appropriation on farms.

Results

本章呈现了研究的核心发现，分为三个主题。“关系式农业”主题揭示了农民的工作和生活实践根植于与土地、植物和动物的互惠关系中，这种关系本体论使得数字技术常常被视为不兼容的外部事物。“（失）连接的机器”主题探讨了农民对机器人等新兴技术的复杂看法，这些看法深受文化叙事和过去失败的技术推广项目的影响，导致了普遍的怀疑。“被滋养的家庭与社区”主题则展示了数字技术在何处找到了切入点：当它们能够服务于维系家庭和社区关系这一核心价值时（如通过智能手机和社交网络），即便面临成本和基础设施的障碍，农民也会积极地将其整合到生活中。

  • (关系式农业) 描述了农民将他们的工作视为与农场各要素（土地、树木）之间的一种互惠对话，而非等级控制关系。
    The land has taught me a lot, when you are in the plot, walking through the trees, talking to the trees, looking at the tree, you begin to merge with it, and then we become friends (F5).
  • (关系式农业) 指出农民认为数字技术属于与农事实践不同的领域，且其物理属性（如脆弱性）不适应艰苦的田间工作环境。
    A smartphone is very good, but is very fragile, and you are very dedicated to the work, smeared with so many things. What is one going to do with it? So, one doesn’t use it, one working cannot use that (F17)
  • ((失)连接的机器) 揭示了农民对机器人的想象受到两方面影响：科幻电影中的威胁形象和FNC（哥伦比亚国家咖啡种植者联合会）失败的机械采收试点项目。
    During the interviews, it became evident that two key elements influenced the interviewees’ conceptions of these technologies. First, elements of science fiction, especially the representations in cinema -where robots are predominantly portrayed as a threat to humanity-. Second, a pilot project for the mechanization of coffee harvest in Colombia, carried out by the FNC as part of their broader innovation program. (Noticias Caracol 2018; FNC 2020)
  • ((失)连接的机器) 论证了农民认为机器人缺乏生产优质咖啡所需的“诀窍”（know-how），如细致、敏感度和热情，这是一种手工技艺。
    Coffee is so artisanal and it should be so artisanal. Obviously, you must create conditions for the work not to be so difficult, but I think that so much technology could really affect the coffee process, because if you know about coffee, you know that in such dedicated selection, in such dedicated washing, there is a good cup of coffee. (C7).
  • (被滋养的家庭与社区) 强调了家庭在农民生活中的核心地位，维持家庭联系是他们的主要目标，而通讯技术为此提供了重要工具。
    Well, to me, they are very good (digital technologies), it also depends on knowing how to use them, but it is very good, because if one realizes it, you are not with your family, but the way you communicate, it is as if you were with them (P4).
  • (被滋养的家庭与社区) 指出为子女提供教育以寻求更好的（通常是城市的）未来，是农民家庭的核心追求，这促使他们投资于电脑等教育工具，尽管存在连接性和成本障碍。
    I want my daughters to progress, allow them to study, so that they can defend themselves. That’s the best inheritance. Each one has a computer; I bought it for them. (Farmer 42)
  • (被滋养的家庭与社区) 展示了农民对机器人可能取代人力、破坏当地就业和社会结构的深切担忧，这超越了潜在的经济效益考量。
    Well no, robots no. For me it shouldn't be, since coffee support too many families, and if there were that, totally, there would be more poor people, then for me it would not be a good idea, we would not use it on the farm (F38).

Discussion

本章将研究发现与理论框架和现有文献进行对话，从而深化了分析。作者指出，研究中观察到的地方性、日常的数字化路径与主流的、宏大叙事的“聚光灯式数字化”（spotlight digitalization）形成鲜明对比。这种差异根植于两种对立的本体论：农民的“关系式”本体论（强调自主、关怀、互赖）与主流数字农业背后的“理性-资本主义”逻辑。文章进一步分析了农民如何在他们具体的“机会结构”中能动地航行，当技术与其核心“功能”（如维系社会关系）一致时，他们会积极地克服各种限制。FNC的失败项目被作为一个典型的案例，说明了自上而下、忽视地方情境的技术中心主义方法的弊端。

  • 区分了“日常数字化”（everyday digitalization）与“聚光灯数字化”（spotlight digitalization），前者是混乱、情境化的，后者则呈现为看似稳定的主流社会技术项目。
    It is the “everyday digitalization,” which differs greatly from the big narratives or “spotlight digitalization” (Forney and Dwiartama 2023). We contend the co-occurrence of an 'everyday non-digitalization' resulting from the tension between these contrasting visions.
  • 对比了农民所体现的非经济价值（如自主、关爱土地、家庭凝聚）与主流数字化想象中常见的理性-资本主义逻辑。
    For the communities in this study, capital and production are valued components of their functionings (it is not necessary to romanticize them). However, non-economic values such as autonomy, care for the land, and cohesive families and communities, largely contribute to a valuable life. (Bronson 2019; Hütten 2019; Rotz et al. 2019b; Birch et al. 2020)
  • 批判了农业科技公司将农场想象为“无人化景观”的愿景，这与受访者珍视与土地的联系、恐惧被取代的感受形成鲜明对照。
    Therefore, while influential agri-tech corporations tend to imagine peopleless landscapes populated by smart machines and artifacts that liberate farmers from the 'burden' of agricultural labor (Bronson 2018; Baur and Iles 223), the respondents in this study are precisely afraid of this scenario.

Conclusion

文章在结论部分总结了其两大核心贡献：首先，通过应用能力アプローチ，揭示了地方性的发展理念如何在农民生活中定位或排除技术，从而批判了基于普适性幻觉的技术推广模式。其次，通过哥伦比亚咖啡农的案例，为全球南方地方性数字化进程的关系研究领域增添了新的经验证据。结论重申，技术挪用是一个在追求有价值生活的过程中共同建构能动性的过程。其结果是一种复杂的、兼具数字化（在社会交往领域）与非数字化（在农事实践领域）的混合形态。最终，作者呼吁用对多元性的承认取代对普适性的追求，以期推动更具包容性和情境化的数字创新，从而弥合“现实-设计鸿沟”。

  • 重申了研究的首要贡献：应用能力アプローチ来强调地方性发展理念在技术定位中的关键作用，并批判了对技术扩散的盲目追求。
    Firstly, it applies a capabilities approach to highlight the relevant role of situated ideas of development in positioning technologies on or off the farms, as well as broader digitalization agendas, on or off farmer’s life projects. Amartya Sen’s capabilities approach presents a critique of the unquestioning pursuit of diffusing technologies across contexts based on a false sense of universality.
  • 明确了研究的第二项贡献：为全球南方地方性数字化进程的关系研究提供了来自哥伦比亚咖啡农的案例。
    Secondly, this study contributes to the emerging field of relational studies on local processes of digitalization in the Global South, by presenting a case of two communities of coffee growers in Colombia.
  • 概括了研究的核心发现：技术挪用是在追求有价值生活的过程中，一个共同建构能动性的过程。其结果是一种混合了数字化与非数字化的新兴形态。
    The local outcome of this process, articulated through the themes of a relational way of farming, (dis)connected machines, and nurtured families and communities, is an emerging form of digitalization and non-digitalization.
  • 提出最终呼吁：应以对多元发展理念的承认来取代不变性、普适性和统一性的观念，从而使数字创新更具包容性。
    Drawing upon a relational perspective, we present evidence of the need to replace notions of immutability, universality and uniformity, with the recognition of multiple ideas of development and the role of technologies in personal and collective development.
