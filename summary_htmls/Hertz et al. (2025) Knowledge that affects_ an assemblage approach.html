Knowledge that affects: an assemblage approach

Tilman Hertz and Francois Bousquet

全文概要总结

作为一名长期致力于可持续性科学中知识共创（co-production）的认识论与本体论研究的学者，我认为Hertz与Bousquet的这篇文章是一次极具理论雄心且恰逢其时的重要贡献。它精准地切入了当前知识共创领域的核心困境：即便是程序上设计精良的共创过程，其产出的知识为何常常无法转化为有效的在地行动，即所谓的“知识-行动鸿沟”。作者的诊断是深刻的：问题出在对知识本质的理解上，尤其是在于忽略了知识的“情动”（affective）维度。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，为了让知识共创过程产生的知识真正具有行动力（actionable），它必须能够“影响”（affect）参与者，即触发一种“体验强度”（experiential intensity）。作者批判了当前共创实践中，以科学话语为主导的、抽象的、表征性（representational）知识的过度中心化地位。他们主张，知识应被理解为一个动态的“集聚”（assemblage），而有效的知识共创是一个“对齐”（alignment）的过程——即将科学的、话语性的知识与参与者具体生活世界中的情动体验对齐。这种对齐通过多模态、多感官的方法（如参与式戏剧）得以实现，从而超越单纯的事实传递，触及“被感受到的事物的生命重要性”。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据主要建立在对现有学术文献的批判性整合与理论重构之上。首先，他们引用可持续性科学领域关于知识共创策略（如“双眼同看”、“知识编织”）的文献，肯定其进步性，但同时指出这些策略仍未充分解决科学表征知识的主导地位问题。其次，他们深入哲学理论，特别是后结构主义和新唯物主义思想，来构建其核心框架。这包括：(1) 德勒兹与加塔利的“集聚理论”（Assemblage Theory），用以描述知识作为异质元素的动态组合；(2) 斯宾诺莎及德勒兹对“情动”（affect）的定义，即身体行动能力的变化；(3) 凯伦·巴拉德（Karen Barad）的“物质-话语实践”（material-discursive practices）概念，用以解释“视角”（perspective）如何建构现实。最后，他们引用认知科学、关系性研究方法（如关系性访谈）和艺术为本的方法（如参与式戏剧）的文献，为如何实践性地促进“情动”与“表征”的对齐提供证据。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程是一个连贯的理论递进。首先，他们从“知识-行动鸿沟”这一经验问题出发，将其归因于知识的“抽象性”。接着，他们引入“表征”（representation）与“情动”（affect）这对核心概念，并回顾了认知科学中关于二者关系的不同理论，最终导向一种具身、情境化、分布式的认知观。在此基础上，他们正式提出“集聚”理论作为分析框架。论证的关键一步在于将知识重新概念化为一个依赖于“视角”的集聚，而“情动”是此集聚的涌现属性。这巧妙地解释了为何不同知识体系（甚至相互矛盾的知识）可以共存。随后，他们论述了集聚的动态性（“生成”/becoming）以及“情动盈余”（affective surplus）如何可能突破既有表征的编码，甚至改变“视角”本身。最后，他们将这一复杂的理论框架聚焦于一个可操作的概念——“对齐”（alignment），并提出通过多模态、艺术化的实践方法来促进这一过程，从而将抽象的哲学思辨引向具体的共创实践指导。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文的方法论本质上是概念性的和理论建构性的（conceptual and theoretical）。作者通过对哲学（特别是德勒兹、斯宾诺莎、巴拉德）、可持续性科学、认知科学和社会学等多个领域的文献进行综合与再创造，发展出一个新的理论框架——一种基于集聚理论的知识共创方法。为了阐释该框架的实践潜力，作者引入了一个“进行中的经验展示”（an empirical demonstration in the making）。这部分描述了一个应用该框架的研究项目，其具体方法包括“关系性访谈”（Relational Interviewing），并结合了“行走访谈”（Walking Interviews）和“回放剧场”（Playback Theater）来捕捉个体的“情动”体验，并计划使用“论坛剧场”（Forum Theater）来进行集体的知识共创，旨在将气候科学家的表征性知识与在地行动者的具体体验进行“对齐”。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文章以一个正在法国南部进行的、关于一个多功能湖泊景观适应气候变化的研究项目作为案例。该案例旨在展示作者理论框架的实际应用。在这个项目中，研究者并非简单地收集数据，而是通过关系性访谈和参与式戏剧等方法，深入探究不同行动者（如农民、旅游从业者、官员）对“脆弱性”的情动体验。例如，一个湖泊对于不同的人可能意味着灌溉水源、运动场所或身份认同的源泉。这些不同的体验构成了异质性的知识“集聚”。研究团队通过回放剧场等方式放大和回馈这些情动体验，建立信任，并计划在下一步的论坛剧场中，让气候科学家与在地行动者共同面对这些具体、充满情感的场景，从而迫使科学知识不再是抽象的输入，而必须在与具体情动体验的互动中寻找其意义和位置，最终生成能够驱动行动的、被“对齐”了的知识。

本章首先点出核心问题：尽管关于全球环境的评估报告日益严峻，但并未引发相应规模的行动，即存在“知识-行动鸿沟”。作者认为，部分原因在于这些知识通常以抽象、非个人化的语言呈现，无法触发行动者内在的“情动”（affect）。为此，现有知识共创（co-production）策略虽有进步，但仍受以科学为中心的表征性知识主导。本文提出，知识共创的核心目标应是生成能“影响人”的知识，并为此引入“集聚”（assemblage）理论作为核心框架，旨在将表征性知识与参与者的具体情动体验“对齐”（align）。

  • 总结：作者指出传统单向的知识传递模式容易导致知识与行动的脱节。
    That the resulting knowledge is experienced as abstract is often said to be characteristic of a traditional model of knowledge production according to which there is a unidirectional flow of information from researchers to knowledge users. This model risks leading to a knowledge/action gap (Leichenko and O’Brien 2019).
  • 总结：为了应对知识-行动鸿沟，学界已发展出多种知识共创策略，并引用了一系列概念来描述这些方法。
    To address this gap, scholars have developed a variety of strategies, many of which center on approaches to the co-production of knowledge (see Orlove et al. 2022 for an extensive overview). Among such strategies figure, for example, the Mi’kmaw concept of “two-eyed seeing” (Bartlett et al. 2012), “braided knowledge” (Bartlett et al. 2012), “weaving” (Sidik 2022), “boundary work” (Gieryn 1983), or “trading zone” (Galison 1997), to name just a few.
  • 总结：学者们进一步提出了确保共创有效性的原则和政策工具，以服务于共创的多元目标。
    To ensure that such strategies are operationalized in the most effective way and serve the various purposes of co-production (Chambers et al. 2021), researchers have proposed principles for knowledge co-production (e.g., Clark et al. 2016, Norström et al. 2020, Orlove et al. 2023) and articulated corresponding policy instruments (e.g., Orlove et al. 2023).
  • 总结：遵循这些原则的共创过程能创造条件，以多元和整合的方式进行学习、实验和能力建设。
    Embarking on knowledge co-production processes along those lines can thus provide the conditions that allow “embracing a learning attitude as well as experimenting with knowledge, action and capacity building processes in pluralistic and integrated ways” (Caniglia et al. 2020:98) in view of producing usable or actionable knowledge.
  • 总结：作者采纳了West等人对知识“可用性”的定义，强调其取决于知识在特定生活世界中不断展开的关系，因此可用性是涌现、多维且不可预测的。
    We conceive of the usability of knowledge alongside West et al. (2019:549) in terms of its utility within a particular life world and consider it being “determined by the unfolding relationships within which it is invoked.” That these relationships are continuously unfolding means that usability is emergent to the very process of unfolding and therefore is “inherently multi-dimensional and unpredictable” (West et al. 2019:549).
  • 总结：作者认为，知识不仅关乎内容，更源于受到程序、情感、结构和情境影响的关系过程。
    Put differently, relational knowledge is not only about content but also about how this content “originates as a result of relational processes that are shaped by procedural, emotional, structural, and context-dependent characteristics” (Brugnach and Ingram 2017:35).
  • 总结：知识生产过程本身是情境特定的，是复杂社会互动的结果。
    Accordingly, the process of knowledge production is also situation-specific, resulting from complex and synergetic social interactions (Brugnach and Ingram 2012, Brugnach 2017).
  • 总结：尽管承认知识的关系性，许多共创过程仍被西方式、技术科学性的表征知识所主导。
    However, despite these strategies and despite the recognition of knowledge as relational, many processes of co-production continue to be dominated by a representational kind of knowledge of technical/scientific nature (Turnhout et al. 2013, Goldman et al. 2018, Turnhout et al. 2020) that is often based in Western worldviews and not adequate for all contexts (Yua et al. 2022).
  • 总结：作者指出，这种主导地位的原因是多方面的，包括权力动态和现实制约。
    The reasons for this are without doubt manifold, ranging from persisting power dynamics that give scientific knowledge particular authority vis-avis other knowledge systems to issues around time and availability of different participants (Wyborn et al. 2019, Turnhout et al. 2020), and research is currently ongoing to rigorously explore how the outcomes of co-production processes connect to specific designs, activities, and situations (see, e.g., Seiferth et al. 2024).
  • 总结：本文的核心假设是，知识要变得可用，就必须能“影响”行动者，触发体验强度。

    This is in line with recent calls by scholars, such as Nightingale et al., who call for “affective knowing”: “Rather than relying exclusively on ‛hard’ scientific data as means to ‛tame’ uncertainty and unknowability, we need affective knowing to transform current inaction and promote effective [...] action” (our emphasis; Nightingale et al. 2022:7).
  • 总结：为了实现这一目标，作者转向德勒兹和加塔利的“集聚理论”来重新概念化知识。
    For this we turn to assemblage theory as developed by Deleuze and Guattari (1987) and propose to conceptualize knowledge as an assemblage.
  • 总结：将知识概念化为“集聚”的第一个好处是，它能在不质疑各种知识系统真实性主张的情况下，平等地呈现它们，避免相对主义。
    First, conceptualizing knowledge as an assemblage allows the rendering of different knowledge systems without putting into question their ability to make truthful claims (Hacking 1999) thus safeguarding against charges of relativism. It thus allows giving equal consideration to diverse knowledge systems that is a point so central to the work of key actors and institutions in the field, such as IPBES (Díaz et al. 2015).
  • 总结：作者区分了表征性知识和具身知识，并指出两者原则上都可以通过集聚方法来理解。
    There is, of course, representational knowledge... But there is also embodied knowledge (Merleau-Ponty 2010, Shapiro 2019) that is situated in the body as well as expressed via the body and is often tacit and pre-discursive. Both kinds can in principle be rendered with an assemblage approach...
  • 总结：作者认为，促进共创成功的关键在于促使参与者挑战其固有的思维方式，而非简单的知识传递。
    What matters is a negotiation (Ortiz-Przychodzka et al. 2023) where participants to the process have their habitual ways of thinking challenged (Fazey et al. 2005).
  • 总结：这一过程可以动员不同类型的知识。
    This can be done in many different ways, mobilizing different kinds of knowledge (Tengö et al. 2014), and we here specifically highlight the role and importance of affect.

  • 总结：基于艺术的方法因其多样的“情动”能力而特别受到推崇。

    We argue that such an open, dynamic, and collaborative process of knowledge production can enable the kind of self-reflective stance required to ensure that processes of knowledge production across different knowledge systems “matter” to those involved (Paschen and Ison 2014).

本章界定了“表征”（representation）与“情动”（affect）两个核心概念。表征被定义为通过概念对现实进行编码的心理实体，传统上以其与现实的“对应”（correspondence）或与其他表征的“融贯”（coherence）来评判。作者指出这两种观点都限制了表征内容的灵活性。相对地，“情动”被引入，它源自斯宾诺莎和德勒兹与加塔利，被定义为一个身体行动能力增减的“前个人化的强度”（pre-personal intensity）。情动发生在身体相遇的“事件”中，是体验的质感，它先于并支撑着所有表征性的编码。因此，同一事物可以有多种表征，这取决于不同行动者在相遇中的情动体验，从而指向一种动态、开放且多元的知识观。

  • 总结：作者引用Karen Barad对两种传统表征观点的批判，指出它们都将我们与物质世界的接触置于中介之下，区别仅在于指涉对象是自然还是文化。
    Where they differ, philosopher Karen Barad notes, “is on the question of referent, whether scientific knowledge represents things in the world as they really are (i.e., nature) or objects that are the product of social activities (i.e., culture)” (Barad 2007:48), a point she attributes to philosopher Joseph Rouse (Rouse 1996).
  • 总结：作者采纳德勒兹与加塔利对“情动”的定义，即身体从一种体验状态到另一种体验状态的转变，伴随着行动能力的增强或减弱。
    Deleuze and Guattari build on Spinoza’s definition of affect as an ability to affect and be affected: It is “a pre-personal intensity corresponding to the passage from one experiential state of the body to another and implying an augmentation or diminution in that body’s capacity to act” (Deleuze and Guattari 1987:xvi).
  • 总结：引用O'Grady和Shaw的观点，情动是身体理解、表达并塑造时空情境的方式，源于我们与其他身体及物质环境的相互关系。
    In this quality, as O’Grady and Shaw mention, “affects function as ways in which bodies make sense of, express themselves within and performatively shape different spatially and temporally bound situations.” They emanate “from the reciprocal relationships we hold with other bodies and the material circumstances with which we interact” (O’Grady and Shaw 2023:518).
  • 总结：作者指出，情动的“前个人化”特质意味着它先于所有表征性的编码。
    The “pre-personal” part of affects that Deleuze and Guattari identify refers to the idea that affects ultimately underlie all coding, that is, all fixing of reality into determinate bodies via representations.
  • 总结：作者将情动与柏格森的“生命冲力”（elan vital）进行类比，强调其作为生命原动力的潜能。
    ...some scholars have identified similarities with Bergson’s “elan vital” that refers to “the vital impetus or force that propels life forward from its beginnings and through all its varieties and forms” (Robinson 2009:224).
  • 总结：一个事物的表征取决于具体相遇中身体被体验的方式，这意味着知识具有情境性。
    Therefore, how a particular body is represented at any particular moment is the result of how the body is experienced within encounters (Cabello 2024). For instance, the same place can be represented in radically different ways, depending on how encounters by different actors have shaped it (Ingold 1993, Schama 1996).
  • 总结：相应地，关于一个地方的知识将反映问题框架、优先级设定和尺度考虑的独特性。
    Accordingly, corresponding knowledge about it will reflect the unique ways in which problems are framed, priorities set, scales considered, etc. (Brugnach 2017).
  • 总结：情动具有超越表征的潜力，因此表征无法完全捕捉情动在事件中的所有可能性。
    Therefore, a representation cannot always exhaustively capture how an affect will play out in events, what Uhlmann refers to as “fluid identity” (Uhlmann 2022:167).

本章是全文的理论核心，系统阐述了作者基于“视角主义”（Perspectivism）的“集聚”（Assemblage）方法。作者整合了德勒兹与加塔利的“集聚”概念和凯伦·巴拉德的“物质-话语实践”理论，提出一个本体-认识论（onto-epistemological）框架。集聚被定义为异质性要素（人、非人、观念、工具等）的集合，它们组合在一起产生涌现的能动性（即“做什么”）。知识本身就是一个集聚。视角则是由“物质-话语实践”构成的，它划定了一个可理解性的空间，决定了现实如何被体验和建构。作者的核心创新在于提出“集聚是视角依赖的”，这意味着一个集聚的“情动”能力取决于它所在的视角。最后，文章探讨了集聚的动态性（“生成”/becoming），指出在身体相遇的事件中可能产生超越现有表征编码的“情动盈余”（affective surplus），这种盈余不仅能改变集聚，甚至有潜力改变视角本身，从而解释了知识的开放性和变革潜力。
