The resilience of family farms: Towards a relational approach

Ika Darnhofer, Claire Lamine, Agnes Strauss, Mireille Navarrete

全文概要总结

作为一名长期致力于社会生态系统韧性与农村社会学交叉研究的学者，我认为本文是一篇极具理论企图心的重要文献。它精准地切入了当前韧性研究领域的核心困境——即如何超越结构与能动性、生态与社会之间的二元对立——并提出了一种富有洞察力的“关系性”路径作为解决方案。这不仅是对家庭农场韧性研究的深化，更是对社会生态韧性理论本身的一次后结构主义式的精致重构。

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是：当前对家庭农场韧性的研究主要分为两种视角——强调物质结构的“结构视角”和强调能动性的“社会行动者视角”，但这两种视角均无法完全整合生态与社会动态，也难以充分解释根本性的转型变革。作者们主张，引入第三种“关系性视角” (a relational perspective) 能够克服这些二元对立。该视角将“关系”本身视为本体论上的首要单位，而非预先存在的实体（如农场、农民）。因此，韧性不再被视为一种静态属性或资产，而是一个在物质、社会、文化关系不断生成与重构的过程中浮现的动态过程，即一种持续的“生成”(becoming)。这种方法论转向，使得分析能够更好地捕捉变化的普遍性、路径的创造性以及人与非人行动者之间的对称互动，从而为理解农场的适应与转型提供了更具整合性的理论框架。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要由两部分构成：一是深厚的理论援引，二是来自实证研究的例证式说明。在理论层面，作者娴熟地穿梭于三大知识体系：其一，社会生态韧性理论的核心概念（如适应性循环、缓冲-适应-转型）；其二，对前两种视角的批判性文献，涵盖了从系统动力学到政治生态学的广泛文献；其三，为构建“关系性”视角而引用的后结构主义和社会理论，特别是德勒兹与加塔利的“生成”哲学、拉图尔的行动者网络理论（ANT）以及关系社会学的思想（如Emirbayer）。在经验层面，作者并未呈现一项完整的实证研究，而是策略性地从五个位于法国和奥地利的、各自独立的定性研究（涉及有机果蔬种植者和奶农）中抽取简短的案例片段。例如，奥地利奶农因个人认同而非结构性因素拒绝多元化经营，法国农民将失败的实验转化为创新的“修补”（bricolage）过程等。这些案例的功能并非验证，而是精巧地“例证” (illustrate) 现有视角的局限性和关系性视角强大的解释潜力。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证结构呈现出一种清晰的“正-反-合”辩证逻辑。首先，文章设定了“正题”——“结构视角”，剖析了其优点（如识别关键结构变量）与根本缺陷（如技术化约论、决定论倾向）。其次，文章转向“反题”——“社会行动者视角”，肯定了其对能动性、价值观和权力关系的关注，但同时指出了其不足（如对生态动态的理论化不足、难以解释激进转型）。最后，文章提出了“合题”——“关系性视角”，将其定位为超越前两者二元对立的综合性框架。通过将本体论的基石从“实体”转向“关系”，作者论证了这一新视角如何能够对称地处理人与非人、物质与意义，从而将变化的动态过程置于分析的中心。这种论证方式不仅结构严谨，而且极具说服力，因为它并非全盘否定已有研究，而是在承认其贡献的基础上进行了一次深刻的理论超越与整合。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的核心研究方法是理论建构（theory-building）与概念综合（conceptual synthesis）。它本质上是一篇理论性论文（theoretical paper），其主要贡献在于提出一个新的分析框架。作者通过对社会生态韧性、农村社会学和后结构主义社会理论等多个领域的文献进行批判性梳理与创造性整合，构建了“关系性韧性”这一核心概念。经验材料（来自法国和奥地利的五个案例研究）的使用是辅助性和说明性的，服务于理论论证，而非系统性的数据分析。这种方法非常适合于推动一个领域的理论前沿，因为它允许研究者跳出具体经验数据的束缚，进行更高层次的抽象思考和理论创新。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文中所引用的案例虽然简短，但非常具有代表性。主要案例包括：1) 法国中等规模果农为应对产业危机而尝试多元化经营（如果蔬种植），但这一决策受到劳动力和技能等关系的严重制约，揭示了结构性策略的局限性。2) 奥地利奶农尽管具备多元化经营（如农场旅游）的物质条件，但因认为自己“不是那种人”而放弃，凸显了价值观和主体认同在 mediating 结构影响中的关键作用。3) 奥地利萨尔茨堡地区有机农业的兴起，被诠释为一个不断演变的“关系网络”的结果，涉及农场内外的物质流（养分循环）、价值链、知识共享和政策支持，展示了韧性作为关系集合体的动态性。4) 一位奥地利农民在制作青贮饲料的实验失败后，偶然转向堆肥并最终将其发展为农场核心实践。这个案例被用来阐释农民实验的“修补”（bricolage）本质——它不是线性的、目标驱动的解决问题，而是一个开放的、在关系中探索可能性的“生成”过程。

Introduction

本章节设定了研究背景：在一个全球互联、变化加速且日益不可预测的时代，欧洲的家庭农场面临着经济、政策、生态和社会的复合型压力。作者指出，家庭农场的持续减少不仅是个体农民的挑战，也关乎农村地区和整个社会的福祉。在此背景下，“韧性”（resilience）概念应运而生，成为理解和应对这些挑战的关键分析工具。

  • 总结了全球变化的加速和不可预测性，这些变化超越了其直接发生的背景，对各领域产生深远影响。
    There is increasing consensus that change is accelerating and becoming less predictable, as global interconnections lead to events having consequences beyond their immediate context (Freibauer et al., 2011; Rosa, 2013; Sardar, 2015; Steffen et al., 2015).
  • 指出2007年美国银行业危机如何通过欧洲主权债务危机传导，并最终通过紧缩政策加剧了新自由主义农业政策对农民的冲击。
    For example, the banking crisis in the US in late 2007 has been tied to the sovereign debt crisis in Europe, which diminished public finances and spread austerity measures (Kitson et al., 2011).
  • 描述了农业面临的生物物理矛盾，即在应对气候变化、减少温室气体排放的同时，又要满足日益增长的全球粮食需求。
    These contradictory societal demands are embedded in the broader need to reduce dependence on fossil energy, in the face of peak oil and of climate change (Weis, 2010).
  • 提及集约化生产方式对生态系统造成的负面影响，如生物多样性下降、土壤退化和水资源污染。
    Indeed, biodiversity is declining, soils are losing their organic matter, fresh water resources are being polluted (EEA, 2015).
  • 引用数据说明欧盟农场数量的显著下降，凸显了家庭农场面临的生存危机。

    Indeed, farms play an important role in maintaining social cohesion, producing food, providing energy from renewable resources, offering recreational and health care services, and maintaining the cultural landscape (Renting et al., 2008; Seuneke and Bock, 2015).

Resilience thinking

本章节重点阐述了本文所采用的“社会生态韧性”（social-ecological resilience）理论框架。作者强调，这一概念超越了简单“恢复原状”的工程韧性，而是关注系统在面对冲击和变化时，通过适应和转型来维持其长期存续的能力。核心工具是“适应性循环”（adaptive cycle）模型，它描述了系统经历的四个阶段：开发（exploitation）、保持（conservation）、释放（release）和重组（reorganisation）。成功的韧性意味着能够驾驭整个循环，包括平稳的增量改进和剧烈的转型重组。

  • 定义了本文所采用的韧性概念——社会生态韧性，强调其关注社会与生态动态的相互依存，以及适应与变革的重要性，而非简单地缓冲冲击和恢复常态。
    We build specifically on the concept of social-ecological resilience (see Holling, 2001) as it emphasizes the interdependency of social and ecological dynamics... and emphasizes the need to adapt and change, rather than the ability to buffer shocks and return to ‘normal’.
  • 引用霍林（Holling）的经典论述，指出基于韧性的管理方法应强调保持选择的多样性、异质性，并承认知识的局限性和未来的不可预测性。
    “A management approach based on resilience (…) would emphasize the need to keep options open (…) and the need to emphasize heterogeneity. Flowing from this would be not the presumption of sufficient knowledge, but the recognition of our ignorance: not the assumption that future events are expected, but that they will be unexpected.” (Holling, 1973: 21)
  • 介绍了作为启发式模型的“适应性循环”，及其四个阶段：开发、保持、释放和重组，用于捕捉社会生态系统的非线性动态。
    The ‘adaptive cycle’ is a heuristic model used to capture the non-linear dynamics of social-ecological systems, and to illustrate qualitatively different types of change (Gunderson and Holling, 2002; Burkhard et al., 2011).
  • 指出韧性系统需要能够驾驭两种性质不同的变化：稳定的增长和增量变化，以及动荡的重组和根本性转型。
    It needs to navigate relatively long phases characterised by steady growth and incremental change, where current strengths are exploited, but also turbulent phases of reorganisation and radical transformation, where new opportunities are explored (Scheffer and Westley, 2007).
  • 将韧性分解为三种能力：缓冲冲击（buffer shocks）、适应（adapt）和转型（transform）。
    To successfully navigate the adaptive cycle, a farm needs to be able to buffer shocks, to adapt and to transform (Darnhofer, 2014).
  • 以从常规农业到有机农业的转变为例，说明了“转型”不仅是生产方法的改变，更涉及认知、价值观、目标以及社会网络和市场关系的根本性转变。
    For example, the conversion from conventional farming to organic farming can be such a transformation, if it is not limited to new production methods, but linked to shifts in perceptions, values and goals of the family members, as well as shifts in the configuration of social networks and market relations (Lamine, 2011).
  • 强调韧性思维的贡献在于，它将“持久”与“变化”联系起来，提供了“路径依赖”和“路径创造”的分析视角。
    This offers alternative analytical insights by emphasizing the need to explore both path dependencies and path creation.
  • 指出韧性思维可用于评估公共政策在面对不可预测事件时，是增强还是削弱了农场和农村的韧性。
    It also points to the need of assessing farm strategies and public policies regarding the extent to which they enhance or erode farm and rural resilience in the face of unpredictable events (Scott, 2013).
  • 批评了过度依赖经济指标来评估农场健康状况的做法，因为这可能掩盖了正在发生的、损害长期韧性的脆弱化过程。
    Thus, while economic indicators may look solid, these might build on unseen processes of fragilization, which undermine the resilience of the farm. (Knaus, 2009; Sundrum, 2015; EEA, 2015).

3.1. Focusing on structures

这一部分剖析了第一种主流韧性研究视角：“结构视角”。该视角假定韧性与系统的结构（如农场规模、类型、生物物理要素、市场和政策等）直接相关。研究的核心任务是寻找可量化的韧性“替代指标”（surrogates），例如多样性、连通性、避免“命令与控制”等，并建立模型来预测系统行为。作者认为，这种方法的优势在于可以检验因果假设和揭示长期生态效应，但其局限性在于倾向于技术化约论和决定论，且常常隐含“理性经济人”的假设，忽视了复杂的知识、价值观和意义网络。

  • 指出开发一个可预测的韧性理论仍然是一个挑战，因为韧性的重要方面可能无法直接观察，因此需要使用“替代指标”。
    Indeed, early on Carpenter et al. (2001) noted that important aspects of resilience in social-ecological systems may not be directly observable, but must be inferred indirectly. They thus proposed to avoid the term ‘indicators’ of resilience and proposed ‘surrogates’ instead.
  • 罗列了一系列识别韧性替代指标或原则的学术努力，显示出学术界在这一方向上的持续探索。
    For example Folke et al. (2003) identified four ‘critical factors’ ... Anderies et al. (2006) proposed ten ‘heuristics’ ... Walker et al. (2006) advanced 14 ‘propositions’ ... Cabell and Oelofse (2012) suggested 13 ‘behaviour-based indicators’ ... Biggs et al. (2012) put forward seven ‘generic policy-relevant principles’.
  • 总结了识别普适性韧性原则的困难，指出具体原则的应用依赖于情境和适应性循环的阶段。
    ...whatever principles or surrogates are identified, they are unlikely to be universal, and therefore they require a nuanced understanding of how, when, and where they apply, as well as how they interact with or depend on other principles (Biggs et al., 2012).
  • 以多样性为例，探讨了其作为增强农场适应能力和韧性的关键结构性因素，涵盖活动、作物、品种、销售渠道和收入来源等多个维度。
    Flexibility is often seen as being enabled by diversity, in its various dimensions (see Stirling, 2007)...At the farm level the impact of a diversity of activities, of crops, of seed varieties, of marketing channels, of on- and off-farm income sources have received attention (see e.g. Dedieu et al., 2008; Darnhofer, 2010; Astigarraga and Ingrand, 2011).
  • 通过法国和奥地利的案例研究说明，多元化策略的适用性受到劳动力等结构性因素的限制。
    Thus, while diversification is a broad strategy to strengthen resilience, its relevance and applicability depends on structural factors such as farm size, marketing channels and labour availability (Lamine and Navarrete, 2015).
  • 引用“命令与控制”病态的观点，强调过度控制系统以追求效率会降低其对外部扰动的韧性。
    Another resilience principle is to avoid the pathology of ‘command and control’, i.e. a one-sided focus on controlling the system to ensure efficiency (e.g. expressed in high and stable yields), as such systems become isolated from their context and inflexible (Holling and Meffe, 1996).
  • 解释了结构视角下经济模型的隐含假设，即行动者是理性的决策者，这可能导致将复杂的社会现实简化为一个技术-还原主义框架。
    By shifting from a descriptive to a normative approach, and by drawing upon unchallenged assumptions about the social world, studies within this perspective can effectively impose a technical-reductionist framework upon more complex webs of knowledge, values and meaning (Cote and Nightingale, 2012; Weichselgartner and Kelman, 2015).

3.2. Focusing on social actors

本节转向第二种研究视角：“社会行动者视角”。该视角将人的能动性（agency）置于分析中心，强调农民的价值观、主观认知、经验学习和社会网络在塑造农场韧性中的决定性作用。结构被视为约束而非决定因素。这种视角揭示了相似结构下农场实践的异质性，并引入了对权力关系、社会公正等关键社会议题的批判性反思。作者指出，这一视角有力地批判了韧性话语可能被新自由主义收编、强化现状的风险。但其局限在于，往往对生态动态的理论化处理不足，且在解释维持主导地位的权力结构时，可能更侧重于解释稳定性而非转型变革。

  • 阐述了社会行动者视角的核心观点：农民并非被动接受变革，而是主动的行动者，其能动性受到结构约束但不受其决定。
    In expressing his agency, the farmer is constrained — but not determined — by the structure of the farm or its context. (Emirbayer and Mische, 1998)
  • 通过奥地利奶农拒绝多元化经营的案例，说明农民的个人认同和价值观如何影响其决策，从而导致了“耕作风格”（style of farming）的差异。
    Thus, even in a very similar structural context (topography, policies, markets, farm size), farms can differ significantly in their activities and how they implement these activities, i.e. their style of farming (van der Ploeg, 1994).
  • 引用社会学习能力的观点，强调社会生态系统的韧性取决于其社会学习能力。
    As Davoudi et al. (2013) have suggested, social-ecological systems can “become more or less resilient depending on their social learning capacity” (Davoudi et al., 2013: 311, emphasis in original).
  • 强调农民作为实验者的角色，他们通过尝试新品种、新技术和新市场来主动应对问题。
    Indeed, farmers are well known to experiment (see Raedeke and Rikoon, 1997; Leitgeb et al., 2012; Meynard et al., 2012).
  • 指出农民网络和其中的“战略性对话”对于理解制度变迁、探测威胁、评估机会至关重要。
    By cultivating networks and the ‘strategic conversations’ taking place in them, various aspects of observed changes in (in)formal institutions can be better understood, which can play a role in the extent to which experiments and new enterprises are successful (Balducci et al., 2011).
  • 引入批判社会科学的视角，指出韧性概念可能被用于推行自上而下的技术-理性方法，并可能因其非政治化立场而强化既有的不平等权力结构。
    In the context of farm resilience, three related issues raised in this body of literature are particularly relevant: that resilience is often linked to a top-down technical-rational approach; that an apolitical stance privileges established social structures; and that the resilience rhetoric is easily co-opted by neoliberalism. (e.g., Leach, 2008; Davidson, 2010; Cote and Nightingale, 2012; Reghezza-Zitt et al., 2012; Hatt, 2013; Scott, 2013; Fabinyi et al., 2014)
  • 批判了将韧性概念从描述性转为规范性的做法，这种做法往往忽视了农场异质性和社会多元性，偏爱技术解决方案。
    As MacKinnon and Derickson (2012) point out, while at the outset resilience was a descriptive concept, state agencies and expert knowledge often normatively define how resilience is to be achieved.
  • 批评主流韧性研究的非政治立场，认为这忽视了权力关系在定义问题和选择解决方案中的作用。
    This stance ignores the impact of currently dominant power relations in shaping how societal problems are defined, which problems are seen as salient, and what approaches are preferred to address them (Smith and Stirling, 2010).
  • 指出韧性思维与新自由主义的契合之处，即通过强调个人责任来淡化结构性因素（市场、政策）的影响。
    Finally, by highlighting how much individual farms can do to strengthen their resilience, resilience thinking aligns well with neoliberalism (Joseph, 2013; Zebrowski, 2013).

3.3. Focusing on relations

本节是全文的理论核心，提出了作者倡导的第三种视角：“关系性视角”。这一视角拒绝将世界视为由预先存在的、独立的实体构成，而是主张关系本身具有本体论上的优先性。因此，农场和农民不是孤立的分析单位，而是嵌入在时空关系网络中不断被塑造和重塑的过程。韧性不再是一种静态属性，而是从这些不断变化的关系中浮现出来的结果，是一种持续的“生成”（becoming）。这种方法论强调变化的普遍性，将农民的实验理解为一种探索新关系可能性的“修补”（bricolage），并通过对称地处理人类与非人类“行动者”（actants），实现了社会与生态的深度整合。

  • 阐述了关系理论的核心主张：拒绝以预先给定的、离散的单元作为分析起点，认为世界由动态、展开的关系构成。
    Indeed, relational theorists reject the notion that there are discrete, pre-given units that can be used as starting point of analysis (Emirbayer, 1997).
  • 将农场重新概念化为一个在持续“生成”过程中的暂时稳定时刻。
    As such a farm can only be understood in terms of the relations in which it is entangled: the farm as it is now, is but a stabilized moment in a process of continual becoming (see Chia, 1999).
  • 借鉴德勒兹的“生成”（becoming）概念，将韧性理解为在永不稳定的、需要不断展演的关系中浮现的特性。
    Building on these approach, resilience is not a character or attribute of the farm... but in relations that are never stable, that must be enacted, performed every day. (Deleuze and Guattari, 1987; Deleuze and Parnet, 1996)
  • 提出关系性思维对韧性的重新表述：韧性不是可以被拥有或测量的“物”，而是由物质、社会、文化等不断变化的关系模式所产生的涌现结果。
    This leads to a reformulation of farming resilience in terms of relational thinking (see Davoudi, 2012). It reemphasizes that resilience is not a ‘thing’ that can be seized, held or measured... Rather, resilience is the emergent result of ever changing patterns of relations, relations that are material, social, cultural.
  • 将农民的实验重新诠释为“修补”（bricolage），这是一个持续的过程，通过组合异质元素来创造在特定时刻“可行”的事物，并在此过程中探索新的情境框架和行动可能性。
    Indeed, Farmers’ experiments is more akin to ‘bricolage’ (see Innes and Booher, 1999; Cleaver, 2002).
  • 指出农民的实验不是科学的实验室实验，而是一种推测性的认知方法，一种与不确定性共处的方式。
    Farmers' experiments are thus not so much a scientific laboratory experiment, than they are a speculative method of knowing, of working with uncertainty (see Balducci et al., 2011; Hillier, 2011).
  • 强调关系视角下变化的持续性，计划在实践中总会被修改和调整，行动者总是在即兴发挥和微调。
    Change always has ramifications and implications beyond those initially imagined; plans, when they are translated into action, are always modified and adapted; actors improvise, fine-tune and adjust (Tsoukas and Chia, 2002).
  • 引入行动者网络理论（ANT），通过将能动性扩展到人与非人“行动者”的异质性组合，来对称地处理社会与生态关系，从而克服自然与文化的二元对立。
    Similarly, in Actor-Network-Theory, agency is extended to include heterogeneous associations of human and nonhuman ‘actants’ (Latour, 2005).

Conclusion: integrating insights on farming resilience

结论部分对全文论点进行了精炼的总结与升华。作者重申，“结构视角”和“社会行动者视角”各有洞见但整合不足，且都倾向于对韧性进行静态的、基于状态的评估。作者强调，他们提出的“关系性视角”通过将关系作为本体论基础，成功地整合了社会与生态维度。更重要的是，这一视角将分析重心从静态的“是什么”（what a farm is）转向动态的“做什么”和“如何生成”（the unfolding of relations that constitute farming）。它将韧性理解为一个需要通过培育多样化、异质性关系来不断展演和重塑的过程。最终，这种方法论不仅能更好地解释“路径依赖”（bounce back），更能揭示“路径创造”（bounce forward）的可能性，为理解农场如何实现根本性转型提供了强大的理论工具。

  • 总结了本文的核心论证：关系性方法通过将关系（而非实体）视为基础，能够更好地整合社会和生态方面，从而加强或削弱家庭农场的韧性。
    We argue that a relational approach, which conceptualizes the relations (rather than entities) as foundational, allows a stronger integration of the social and ecological aspects that strengthen or weaken the resilience of family farms.
  • 指出关系性方法将注意力从对（看似）稳定状态的分析，转向了增量和转型变革得以实现的过程。
    ...a relational approach highlights that patterns of relations are always changing, shifting the attention away from the analysis of (seemingly) stable states towards the process through which incremental and transformational changes are enacted.
  • 强调关系性视角将韧性的理解从一种属性转变为一个需要通过培育多样和异质关系来不断重新实现和执行的过程。
    This focus on relations shifts the understanding of resilience from being an attribute, to being a process that needs to be constantly re-enacted and performed through nurturing diverse and heterogeneous relations.
  • 提出关系性视角有助于实现韧性思维的平衡，既涵盖农场如何缓冲冲击和“反弹”（bounce back），也包括如何转型和“前跃”（bounce forward），即同时覆盖路径依赖和路径创造。
    A relational perspective also contributes towards a more balanced approach to resilience thinking, covering how farming can buffer shocks and ‘bounce back’ as well as how farming can be transformed and ‘bounce forward’, i.e. covering both path dependency and path creation.
