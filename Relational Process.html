Farming from a Process-Relational Perspective: Making Openings for Change Visible

Ika Darnhofer

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，传统的农村社会学在研究农场层面时，普遍受困于人类中心主义、本质主义和结构决定论的局限，将农民视为理性的、自主的决策主体，而将农场视为被动的物质客体。为了突破这些局限，作者主张引入一种“过程-关系”视角 (process-relational perspective)。该视角融合了关系社会学、新物质主义和后人类主义思想，旨在将世界视为一个由动态、不可预测的互动过程构成的网络。通过赋予非人类（自然、技术）能动性 (agency)，该框架不仅重新概念化了“农民”与“农场”，更重要的是，它将“务农” (farming) 理解为一个持续“生成” (becoming) 的过程。这不仅是一个理论项目，更是一个政治项目：它旨在揭示变革无处不在的可能性，使研究者能够看到并放大那些通往更具社会公正性和环境友好型农业实践的路径，而不是仅仅关注结构性束缚。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要建立在对现有理论文献的批判性整合与再创造之上。作者系统地梳理了传统农村社会学研究中的三大理论预设：将农民视为独立的“人文主义主体”；将农场视为被动的物质资源；以及强调结构性束缚和连续性而非变革与可能性。针对这些预设，作者引入了一系列“后人文转向”的理论资源作为反驳和替代方案的证据，包括：关系社会学（Emirbayer, Dépelteau），用以强调万物皆由关系构成；行动者网络理论与新物质主义（Latour, Barad, Bennett），用以论证非人类的能动性与“活力”；非再现理论（Thrift, Anderson），用以强调身体、情感和实践在行动中的重要性；以及德勒兹哲学，用以支撑“生成”与差异化的世界观。这些理论共同构成了支持其核心论点的坚实理论基础。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程遵循一条清晰的“破-立”逻辑。首先，文章通过“漫画式”的描绘，集中批判了传统研究范式的本体论和认识论缺陷（如本质主义、人类中心主义、二元论、决定论等），指出这些范式难以捕捉农业实践的动态性和多样性。接着，文章系统阐述了“过程-关系”视角的核心内涵，特别是其“扁平本体论” (flat ontology)，即社会现实只存在于互动层面，不存在超越其上的“结构”。然后，文章将这一视角应用于农业研究，分别对“农民”和“农场”进行了颠覆性的再概念化：“农民”不再是孤立的理性决策者，而是与环境持续相互塑造、具身化的存在；“农场”不再是被动资源，而是充满“活力” (lively) 和“感染力” (affective) 的能动集合体。基于此，作者进一步推导出“务农” (farming) 作为一种关系性过程的核心洞见，并探讨了这一新视角在三个方面带来的观念突破：1. 强调变革的无处不在；2. 重新理解人与自然的关系；3. 对技术角色的新认识。最后，文章探讨了这一理论转向对研究方法论的挑战与启示，强调研究实践本身的“操演性” (performativity)，并最终回归其政治目标——即通过研究让另类的世界成为可能。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的是理论思辨与概念建构的研究方法。它不依赖于一手经验数据的收集与分析，而是通过对现有学术文献的深度梳理、批判性评估和创造性综合，来构建一个新的理论分析框架。具体而言，作者首先界定并批判了一个“传统”的研究范式，然后引入一个对立的、更前沿的理论集群（过程-关系社会学、后人类主义、新物质主义等），最后将这些理论工具系统地应用于“农场层面研究”这一具体领域，并推演其在本体论、认识论、方法论和政治意涵上的全新可能性。这是一种典型的理论创新型研究路径。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文并未提供一个贯穿全文的详细经验案例。相反，作者在阐述理论观点时，会运用一些高度概括化、启发性的农业场景作为例证。例如，在讨论传统研究的局限时，提到了对农民采纳环保措施或投资挤奶机器人的研究。在阐述农民的具身体验时，引用了农民通过观察动植物的细微变化来感知其是否“不对劲”的例子，以及农民感觉拖拉机成为其身体延伸的例子。在说明非人类能动性时，提及了土壤自身并非惰性物质，而是充满活力的。这些案例的功能并非为了实证检验，而是为了帮助读者更好地理解抽象的理论概念。

本章节通过“漫画式”的概括，批判了传统社会学研究在农场层面上的三个核心预设。第一，对“农民”的理解：传统研究将农民视为一个理性的、统一的、连贯的“人文主义主体”，即使考虑到价值观和社会规范的影响，也过度强调了认知和反思，忽视了情感、身体和矛盾性。第二，对“农场”的理解：传统研究将农场视为被动的物质资源，受社会力量塑造，从而忽视了物质（自然、技术）本身的能动性。第三，对“变化”的理解：传统研究倾向于本质主义和结构决定论，强调连续性、可预测性和结构性束缚，难以解释现实中农业的杂糅性、流动性和多样性。

  • 总结：传统研究将农民视为能够理性反思的、统一和连贯的主体，即便承认价值观的影响，也过分强调其认知能力。
    In these conventional approaches in rural sociology, the farmer is conceptualised as a humanistic subject, thus privileging reason and reflexivity, assuming farmers to be knowing, rational, stable, unified, and coherent (see St. Pierre 2004, 2008).
  • 总结：这类研究通常假设农民能够准确地解释自己的选择，例如为何采纳农业环保措施或投资自动化设备。
    This allows for methods that build on the assumption that a farmer can provide an accurate account of why he has made specific choices, such as adopting agro-environmental measures (e.g., Burton et al. 2008) or investing in a milking robot (e.g., Butler and Holloway 2015).
  • 总结：然而，这种认知主义的视角被批评为忽视了人类行为中的矛盾性、情感和身体体验。
    Yet, these conventional conceptualisations have been questioned, as humans are known to be ambivalent and contradictory (e.g., Smelser 1998; Berliner et al. 2016), thus focusing on the cognitive dimension of choices may underplay the influence of emotions and embodiment (e.g., Carolan 2008; Pile 2010; Castro 2018).
  • 总结：传统社会学往往聚焦于社会文化因素，而忽视了农场的物质性，将其视为被动的资源。
    This tends to neglect the materiality of the farm (see Escobar 2010, p. 97). Indeed, the farm is mostly seen as a pliable resource for material agricultural production or for its social construction (see Alaimo and Hekman 2008, p. 4).
  • 总结：这种偏向源于社会学与自然科学的学科划分，导致对非人类力量的忽视。
    As a result, many conventional sociological perspectives take humans, human meaning-making, and social structures as the sole constitutive force, neglecting the nonhuman forces at play in our world (Hultman and Lenz Taguchi 2010, p. 539).
  • 总结：尽管像行动者网络理论（ANT）和新物质主义已提出非人类具有能动性，但在农场研究中，物质性仍未得到充分考虑。
    To understand farming fully, it might then be important to find ways to combine social construction with an understanding of the agency of the material world (see e.g., Ferguson et al. 2016; Herman 2016; Phillips 2016; Legun and Henry 2017).
  • 总结：这需要一种能整合物理互动、生物过程、社会交往、反思、情感和身体感受的综合视角。
    This would allow to link farming to a wide variety of forces, including physical interactions, biological processes, social encounters, reflective thoughts, revisited memories, emotional desires, and bodily feelings (see Alaimo and Hekman 2008; Anderson and Harrison 2010; Fox and Alldred 2018).
  • 总结：许多研究带有本质主义倾向，试图通过农民或农场的某些固定属性（如年龄、规模、类型）来预测其行为，并建立分类学。
    Traces of essentialism can be identified in studies that look for specific attributes of a farmer (age, gender, education, part-time occupation, attitude) or a farm (size, crops, types of animal rearing) and use these as indicators of some essence, some characteristic that determines how likely this type of farmer is to behave in a certain way (e.g., Andrade 2015; Milone and Ventura 2019).
  • 总结：然而，大量研究揭示了二元对立（如传统/现代，常规/有机）的局限性，指出现实中充满了杂糅和流动性。
    Yet, studies have been keen to expose the limitations of binary oppositions such as old/young farmer, modern/traditional, small/large farms, conventional/organic, pointing out hybridities and fluidity (Whatmore and Thorne 1997; Burton 2006; Rosin and Campbell 2009; Sutherland and Darnhofer 2012; Lamine 2015; Le Velly and Dufeu 2016; Lamine et al. 2019; Lehtimäki 2019).
  • 总结：强调结构性束缚的视角（如“常规化”论述）过度关注连续性和稳定性，而忽视了变化和可能性。
    For example in the debate around the conventionalisation thesis, market forces were held to systematically undermine the alterity of organic farming practices (Guthman 2004). It is stabilised – and can be destabilised – through the creative, collective practice of the intentionalities of many and diverse inter-dependent actors (Whatmore and Thorne 1997).

本章节介绍了作为替代方案的“过程-关系”视角。作者首先区分了两种关系社会学：一种关注预先存在的实体间的关系，另一种则认为所有实体本身就是由关系构成的。本文采纳后者，即“激进关系主义”或“过程-关系”视角。该视角的核心主张包括：1. 关系优先于实体，强调相互依赖性；2. 具有“扁平本体论”，认为社会现实只存在于人与非人互动者之间的互动层面，没有超越其上的“结构”或“系统”；3. 世界是持续“生成”的，充满了不确定性和复杂性，任何秩序都是暂时的、需要不断维持的；4. 强调“行动” (doing)，分析的焦点应放在人与非人行动者如何创造、维持或破坏关系，从而为变革开辟空间。最后，作者申明将融合新物质主义、后人类主义、非再现理论和德勒兹哲学来进一步完善这一框架。

  • 总结：关系社会学是一个宽泛的术语，其共同点是优先考虑关系而非实体，强调相互依赖性。
    What they have in common is that they prioritise relations over the entities, thus emphasising interdependence (Emirbayer 1997; Dépelteau 2018a).
  • 总结：作者采纳的是一种更激进的关系主义，认为个体并非预先存在，而是关系的配置。
    For others, all social phenomena are constituted through relations, thus individuals are not pre-existing subjects, but themselves configurations of relations (Powell 2013; Burkitt 2015; Dépelteau 2018a).
  • 总结：过程-关系社会学的核心是分析由人类和非人类互动者之间的互动所产生的、动态变化的社会场域。
    Dépelteau (2018b, p. 510) defined process-relational sociology as focusing on ‘the analysis of the emergence, the transformation and the disappearance of multiple smaller and larger dynamic social fields happening through interactions between human and non-human interactants’.
  • 总结：该视角基于“扁平本体论”，认为社会现实只存在于互动这一个层面，否定了“结构”的因果力量。
    It thus recognises only one ‘level’ of social reality: the interaction between interactants, and as such is based on a ‘flat’ ontology (Dépelteau 2018b, p. 516).
  • 总结：分析的焦点从名词（事物、结构）转向动词（关联、组合、互动、实践），强调维持关系需要持续的“工作”。
    It would thus be more accurate to use verbs: relating, associating, assembling, networking, interacting, communicating, transforming, practicing, farming (Elias 1978; Carolan 2013; Powell 2013).
  • 总结：世界充满了复杂的、不可预测的互动，这导致了无处不在的动态和意外后果。
    This leads to ubiquitous dynamics, marked by complexity, i.e., the unpredictable effects of interactions. Indeed, process-relational sociology takes seriously that the world we live in is complex (Cilliers 2005; Urry 2006; Pyyhtinen 2017).
  • 总结：但这种关系的不稳定性也为新的行动创造了机会，使曾经不可思议的事情变得可能。
    Yet, at the same time, this relational instability also creates openings for new doings (see Gibson-Graham 2008). These openings can enlarge what is perceived as ‘possible’ and ‘doable’: new doings, an altered routine can lead down a path where the once un-thought becomes thinkable (Carolan 2013).
  • 总结：该视角否定了任何背后起作用的“结构”，认为秩序是局部的、短暂的互动结果，分析应聚焦于事件和活动的内在微观政治。
    Similar to Actor Network Theory, process-relational sociology rejects any sense of social forces or structures working ‘behind the scenes’, replacing these entirely with localised, short-lived interactions or associations (see Latour 2005, p. 65–6; Dépelteau 2008; Fox and Alldred 2018, p. 320–21).
  • 总结：作者明确提出，将整合新物质主义、后人类主义（以理解非人类能动性）、非再现理论（以关注具身体验）和德勒兹哲学（以强调“生成”过程）来构建其分析框架。
    ...I integrate insights of new materialism and post-humanism (Latour 2005; Barad 2007; Haraway 2016). To take into account the role of embodied experiences...I integrate insights of non-representationalism (Thrift 2000; Anderson and Harrison 2010). And to emphasise that the world is always becoming...I integrate elements of Deleuzian philosophy (Deleuze and Guattari 1987).

本章节具体阐述了如何运用过程-关系视角来重新概念化“农民”和“农场”。对“农民”的再概念化分两步：第一，克服身心二元论，将农民理解为同时思考和感知的“具身主体”，其行动不仅受认知驱动，也受情感、感官、身体技能和记忆的影响。第二，打破个体与环境的边界，将农民视为与其社会-物质环境持续相互作用、相互依存的存在，其行动是在一个充满不确定性的关系网络中共同演化的结果。对“农场”的再概念化则借鉴新物质主义，认为农场及其组成部分（工具、动物、土壤、作物等）并非被动的客体，而是具有能动性的“活力”存在。这种能动性被定义为“affect”（感染力），即影响和被影响的能力。因此，农场被视为一个由多元、异质的能动者共同构成的、持续生成和变化的动态过程。

  • 总结：第一步是克服身心二元论，承认农民的行动不仅受有意识的意图影响，也受无意识的“背景”互动影响。
    The first move is to overcome the body/mind duality. Thus, the cognitive is not understood as primary, as emotions and the materiality of the body are taken seriously, i.e. the farmer is understood as a being who thinks and senses. ...these processes are influenced by a broad range of ‘background’ interactions, which we do not consciously notice (Anderson and Harrison 2010).
  • 总结：农民许多日常行动是无反思的，例如通过直觉感知动物的异样，这挑战了传统决策理论。
    For example, many farmers will carefully observe the plants and animals they care for (see e.g., Singleton and Law 2013). They could not say what they are looking for, yet, they would feel if something is ‘off’ with an animal, finding it difficult to explain why it is ‘off’.
  • 总结：借鉴非再现理论，行动的根源不应仅归于认知过程，而应更多关注身体、感官、情感、技能和冲动。
    Building on the insights from nonrepresentational theory...the farmer’s actions are seen as guided by mind as well as body, senses, emotions, affect; they are influenced by past experiences and revisited memories, by embodied experience tied to various farming practices in a specific place (see Thrift 2000; Carolan 2008; Anderson and Harrison 2010). In short, farmers are not just deliberative minds, but have bodies, thus much more attention needs to be given to the influence of embodied movements, precognitive triggers, practical skills, affective intensities, enduring urges, sensuous dispositions (see Lorimer 2005; Maclaren 2019).
  • 总结：第二步是将农民视为与其环境相互作用、相互依存的存在，而非单向影响环境的孤立主体。
    As a consequence, action is not understood as a one-way street running from the actor to the acted upon, from mind to matter, but as relational phenomena, i.e., all action is interaction (Anderson and Harrison 2010, p. 7; Dépelteau 2018a, p. 18).
  • 总结：农民是在一个不稳定的、由过去和现在构成的关系网络中才能被理解的相互依存的存在。
    Rather, the farmer, an interdependent being, is only comprehensible in this unstable tapestry of fluid relations, past and present (Mitchell 1988 in Dépelteau 2018a).
  • 总结：对农场的再概念化借鉴新物质主义，赋予非人类能动性，将其视为具有能动能力的实体。
    Building on insights from new materialism, agency no longer privileges human action (Latour 2005; Higgins 2006; Barad 2007; Bennett 2010), and the farm is understood has having agentic capacities.
  • 总结：能动性被定义为“affect”（感染力），即一种改变身体状态的强度体验，它连接、循环于不同身体（人和非人）之间。
    The humanistic notion of agency is replaced by ‘affect’, where all matter is ‘affective – it possesses a “capacity to affect and be affected”’ (Deleuze and Guattari 1987 in Fox and Alldred 2018, p. 318). Thoburn (2007, p. 84) defines affect as ‘an experience of intensity...that changes the state of a body...’. Pile (2010, p. 8) distinguishes emotions from affect, and points out that affect is ‘beyond cognition and always interpersonal’.
  • 总结：农场的物质实体（工具、动物、土壤）都具有不同的能动能力，它们并非惰性物质，而是动态和有活力的。
    For example the soil...is not an inert matter, but dynamic and lively (Schneider et al. 2010; Ferguson et al. 2016), it will affect the farmer and be affected by him.
  • 总结：农场本身是一个由多元、异质事件构成的动态过程，其稳定性是暂时的，可以从历史角度研究其生成条件。
    Understanding the farm as dynamic allows us to investigate conditions of possibility, de-essentialising phenomena, treating them as contingencies to be explained, as expression of human and nonhuman agency, as relations that can be investigated historically (Powell 2013).

本章节探讨了将“务农” (farming) 视为一种混合关系过程所带来的三个核心观念突破。第一，强调变革的无处不在。由于任何秩序都是不稳定的、需要不断维持的，分析的焦点从宏大的、看似坚固的“结构”转向了局部的、充满偶然性的“事件”和微观政治。研究问题也从“如何抵抗结构”转变为“如何抓住事件开启的可能性”。第二，重构人与自然的关系。该视角超越了人操控自然的二元论，主张将务农视为人类与地方生态系统之间相互塑造的协作过程，并呼吁向原住民的世界观学习。第三，对技术的新理解。该视角反对技术决定论或人类完全控制论，认为技术物质（如机器人、标准）和人类一样是能动的行动者，它们会反过来塑造农业实践和人的主体性。

  • 总结：将务农视为一个持续生成、不断被重新组合的混合关系过程，其构成包括生物、物质、社会、文化等多种关系。
    Farming is understood as situated action, as a series of events that take place in particular contexts and unfold in time (see Whatmore and Thorne 1997; Alaimo and Hekman 2008, p. 7; Vanderberghe 2018). ... Farming is thus a fluid, vibrant, and evolving process, a precarious effect of relations, emerging from and transformed by manifold interactions, contingent and ephemeral (see Barad 2007; Coole and Frost 2010).
  • 总结：第一大突破是关注变革的无处不在。分析焦点从业已形成的秩序转向秩序如何被维持、破坏和创造的过程。
    The assumption is thus no longer that farmers are constrained by some form of overpowering structure...the emphasis is on how relations are being stabilised and on opportunities for change. (Anderson and Harrison 2010).
  • 总结：分析重心从宏大结构转向局部事件的微观政治，每个事件都可能带来惊喜，打破既有组合。
    The question is: how to extend the potential that an event opens up, the sense of promise that it may hold? (Anderson and Harrison 2010; Carolan 2016). How to create turning points in the here and now? How do interdependent human and nonhuman actors attempt to shape various and fluid social processes (Dépelteau 2015)? How are differences made (in)visible and how are alternative imaginings of the possible strengthened or foreclosed (Legun and Henry 2017)?
  • 总结：这种视角质疑了农业发展的预设轨迹（如“要么做大要么出局”），并肯定了农业多样性将持续存在。
    Rather, given that the ‘world could always be otherwise’ (Law 2004, p. 152), empirical diversity and the plurality of approaches to farming is here to stay (van der Ploeg 2017; van der Ploeg et al. 2019).
  • 总结：这有助于关注农民的灵活性、适应性实验以及对技术的“修补”创新。
    This approach highlights the flexibility of farmers (Cheshire et al. 2013), the range of experiments farmers engage in to adapt their farming to address their own needs (Brédart and Stassart 2017), and how they ‘tinker’ with technology to respond to societal demands (Higgins et al. 2017).
  • 总结：第二大突破是重构人与自然的关系，将务农视为人类与地方生态系统的协作与相互塑造，强调万物相互依存。
    It would follow Escobar’s (2018) call to overcome the duality of a dynamic culture that manipulates an inert nature, by tuning into the radical interdependence of all life...or as Haraway (2008) put it, the ‘lively knottings’ between humans and nonhumans.
  • 总结：该视角倡导借鉴原住民的世界观，例如安第斯宇宙观中的相互关怀关系或因纽特人处理环境复杂性的知识体系。
    In this effort, a process-relational approach to farming could learn from the worldviews of indigenous societies, such as Andean cosmology where both humans and nonhumans are ‘earth beings’ engaged in mutual relationships of care (de la Cadena 2010); Aborigine cosmology, where reality is not prior, but interactively remade and enacted (Law 2004); or Inuit knowledge systems and how they approach the complexity of environmental processes (Berkes and Berkes 2009).
  • 总结：第三大突破是对技术角色的新见解，反对技术决定论，认为技术物质和人类一样是能动的行动者。
    It undermines a view of technological determinism as well as one of human control over hapless things (Higgins 2006). Indeed, while humans transform matter, matter is also granted active agency in transforming farming practices and human subjectivities (see Alaimo and Hekman 2008; Coole and Frost 2010; Hultman and Lenz Taguchi 2010).
  • 总结：例如，度量衡标准这类“社会技术”本身就具有超越人类意图的能动性。
    For example Rosin et al. (2017, p. 90) show how metrics have agency in the agro-food system, in that they ‘initiate change beyond the expectations, intent and control of humans’.
  • 总结：研究应关注物体（如机器、法规）如何设定行动条件、如何与人互动，以及如何开启新的可能性。
    It draws attention to questions such as how objects under analysis establish conditions of action, how humans incorporate and improvise with objects, what imaginaries objects rely on and establish, how objects may enable to escape normalising discourses and habituated acts, opening new conditions of possibility (see Lupton 2018).

本章节探讨了过程-关系视角在方法论上的挑战与机遇。首先，它要求研究方法超越人类中心主义，关注事件、非人类的affect以及关系如何建立和改变。其次，对于访谈等传统方法，分析时应超越表面的“再现”，关注情感、身体体验、矛盾和模糊之处，并从“超越人类”的视角解读数据，看到人与非人如何共同构成行动。再次，它鼓励方法创新，采用能够捕捉非言语、感觉和情感的实验性方法，如视觉方法（漫画）或感官方法。最后，该视角强调研究的“操演性”（performativity）：研究者并非中立的观察者，而是世界的共同建构者。研究方法、分析和写作本身就是一种干预，能够影响现实。因此，研究者需要反思自己的身体-心智在研究过程中的卷入，并有意识地选择能够揭示可能性、使另类世界变得可思议的叙事策略。

  • 总结：过程-关系视角要求在研究实践中真正贯彻其理论主张，需要社会学想象力的创新，不仅在理论上，也在方法上。
    ...in research praxis the implications are not always carried through (Dowling et al. 2017). We need new sociological imaginations, not only to develop new perspectives, but also to re-imagine our methods (Law and Urry 2004; Pyhhtinen 2016; St. Pierre et al. 2016).
  • 总结：传统访谈分析假设受访者能准确再现其经历的“真相”和意义。
    In conventional methodological practice, work with interview-based data tends to be based on the assumption that voice makes present the truth, and reflects the meaning of an experience (St. Pierre 2008).
  • 总结：新的分析方法则需关注情感、身体体验，并从超越人类的视角来重新想象数据，看到非人类的构成性力量。
    Thus, more attention is given to emotional expressions as well as to affective experiences stemming from relations... (Lenz Taguchi 2012; Buser 2014). ... By highlighting the agency of animals, plants, machines, and rules, we can pay attention to entanglements, and raise questions about human control (Barad 2003; Law 2004).
  • 总结：分析应关注那些传达“流动性、偶然性或不稳定性”的词语，并重视模糊和矛盾之处，将其视为多重现实秩序的体现。
    The emphasis on processes...means paying particular attention to terms that convey ‘lively and energetic imaginaries such as fluidity, contingency or instability’ in the stories told (Buser 2014, p. 234). More attention should be paid to ambiguities and contradictions... (see Berliner et al. 2016).
  • 总结：需要开发新方法来捕捉难以言表的体验，这需要研究者敢于实验和创新，超越公式化的方法论。
    It is an invitation to move away from the formulaic, to risk, to experiment and be creative... Whatmore (2006, p. 606) called it the ‘experimental imperative’, i.e., the urgent need to supplement the familiar repertoire of humanistic methods that rely on generating talk, with experimental practices that amplify other sensory, bodily, affective registers.
  • 总结：这些实验性方法可能包括使用使差异可见的方法、漫画、食谱书和照片等视觉和感官工具。
    For example Stirling (2011) has been advocating the use of methods that make differences visible. Darnhofer (2018) explored the use of comics... Van Oudehoven and Haider (2015) have experimented with using a recipe book and photographs...
  • 总结：该视角也要求研究者反思自身的介入，承认知识是在研究者与研究对象（包括非人）的互动和斗争中产生的。
    It extends to the researchers themselves, inviting us to make explicit our own implication in the contents of scientific accounts of the world (Powell 2013). ... STS scholars and Actor-Network theorists such as Bruno Latour, Michel Callon, John Law, Annemarie Mol have amply shown how scientific accounts emerge through interactions, negotiations, struggles between scientists and their nonhuman objects of study.
  • 总结：研究者需要进行身体-心智层面的反思，承认数据可以“affect”研究者，研究过程是与数据“共同生成”的过程。
    ...data analysis is a process in which the body-mind of the researcher engages and interferes with the data (Alaimo 2008). ... Instead of asking ‘what does this text mean?’, she asks ‘what does it produce?’... (Lenz Taguchi 2012, p. 268).
  • 总结：在结果呈现上，需要超越客观主义的写作范式，并探索如何再现关系、情感和物质性，例如使用照片散文或为非人类发声的写作方式。
    The practice is still dominant in many farm-level studies...to present their results as if they are ‘objective knowledge’ (see Lowe 2010). ... This may be explored by using other media than words, e.g., in the form of a photo essay such as Swanton (2012); or by writing in ways that ‘decenter human authority’ (Dowling et al. 2017, p. 827).
  • 总结：研究的目的不是揭示唯一的“真相”，而是创造不同的视角，这是一个政治行为。
    The purpose of research is then not to bring forth the story as may be intended by the interviewee, but a story that helps bring about a different viewpoint (Lenz Taguchi 2012; Byrne 2017).
  • 总结：最终，研究实践本身是“操演性”的，它参与并塑造世界。研究者的选择可以帮助揭示可能性，使变革成为可能。
    Indeed, our choice of methods...are all performative elements, through which we engage, participate, intervene in the world (see e.g., Campbell and Rosin 2011; Daniel 2011; Law and Singleton 2014; Popa et al. 2015). Our choices affect, they make differences, they enact realities, they can help bring into being what they also discover (Law and Urry 2004; Lenz Taguchi 2012).

本章节总结了全文的核心观点。作者重申，采用一种融合了后人类主义和新物质主义思想的“过程-关系”视角，能够将“务农”重新概念化为一个由人类和非人类共同参与、持续“生成”的关系过程。这种视角是反本质主义和反决定论的，它将一个看似稳定、可预测的世界转变为一个动态、不可预测、持续涌现的世界。这不仅能更真实地反映世界的复杂性和混乱性，更重要的是，它揭示了变革无处不在的可能性。因此，该视角不仅是一个理论项目，更是一个政治项目，旨在通过研究来激发不同的想象，使研究者和实践者都能看到并创造通往另类未来的多种路径。最后，作者再次呼吁研究者认识到自身研究实践的操演性，有意识地去展示世界“未完成”的状态，从而为变革开辟空间。

  • 总结：该视角将农民和农场从二元对立中解放出来，将务农理解为人与非人行动者相互构成的“内部-行动”（intra-action）。
    By injecting insights from post-humanist and new materialist understandings, it invites us to move, ontologically, from identifying bodies as separate entities with distinct borders towards thinking in terms of processes of interdependence, of entanglement of ideas and materialities, of ‘intra-action’, i.e. the mutual constitution of all objects and agencies in an undivided field of existence (Barad 2007; Lenz Taguchi 2012).
  • 总结：这个反决定论的视角强调世界的动态和不可预测性，认为所谓的“结构”远不如看起来那般稳固。
    It conceptualises the ‘structures’ of the social as never as stable and solid as they appear to us or as we wish them to be (Dépelteau 2018b). Rather, each moment offers an opportunity for change, for doing things differently.
  • 总结：它指出了许多既有配置既是约束也是机遇，为创新实践提供了不确定的可能性。
    Many configurations do not just enable or constrain, they also provide undetermined opportunities for action, for innovative practices (Powell 2013).
  • 总结：因此，这不仅是一个理论项目，也是一个旨在使另类世界成为可能的政治项目。
    Indeed, process-relational sociology is not just a theoretical project, it is also a political project to make other worlds possible (see Gibson-Graham 2006), to spur different imaginations, to enable a shift towards actively envisioning and contributing to alternative world constructions.
  • 总结：最终，这是一种邀请，旨在通过研究实践，展示世界“未完成的创造过程”，并使变革的多种可能性变得可见。
    It is an invitation to show the world in its ‘unfinished making’ (Phyyhtinen 2017, p. 306), to make visible the manifold openings for change, to highlight that it could always be otherwise.
