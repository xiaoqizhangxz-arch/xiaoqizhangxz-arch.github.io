Participation and co-theorising: How stakeholder interests and scientific outputs clash in the Horizon 2020 multi-actor approach

Daniel van der Velden, Lies Debruyne, Laurens Klerkx and Joost Dessein

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点在于揭示了欧盟“地平线2020”多主体（multi-actor）研究项目中的一个根本性困境：研究人员在实践中面临着三种相互冲突的逻辑——来自利益相关者的“实践相关性”（practical relevance）需求、来自资助方的项目框架要求、以及自身的学术产出（尤其是理论构建）压力。为了维系利益相关者的持续参与，避免因讨论抽象理论而导致其疏远，研究人员会有意地采取一种“分离策略”（separation strategy）。他们将优先保障参与活动的实践价值，而把理论探讨和项目任务与核心互动过程剥离。这一策略虽然是应对现实压力的务实之举，但最终却以牺牲更深层次的“共同理论化”（co-theorising）为代价，从而限制了参与式研究的知识生产潜力，尽管研究者们普遍认同“共同理论化”的规范性价值。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的论据主要建立在对一个大型H2020项目（DESIRA）中7个“生活实验室”（Living Labs）的质性研究之上。作者通过对负责引导这些“生活实验室”的研究人员进行17次半结构化访谈，获得了第一手经验数据。这些访谈资料深刻地揭示了研究人员对于参与式研究的真实看法、在操作层面遇到的困境、为维持项目运行所采取的妥协策略，以及由此产生的挫败感。数据直接呈现了研究人员普遍存在的“害怕利益相关者退出”的焦虑，以及这种焦虑如何最终导致他们将“共同理论化”视为一种高风险、低回报的活动，从而在实践中予以规避。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑层次清晰。首先，通过文献梳理，将“共同理论化”定位为参与式研究的理想模式和前沿方向。随后，通过呈现访谈数据，将这一理想与研究实践中无法达成的现实进行对照，暴露出其中的巨大鸿沟。文章论证的核心在于，将这一失败归因于上述提及的“三重张力”。特别是，大型泛欧项目预设的、具有普适性的研究框架（如本案例中的“数字化影响”）往往与特定地域情境下利益相关者真正关心的核心问题（如社群构建、市场议价能力）相脱节。为了留住参与者，研究人员不得不刻意减少“学术性”和“项目任务性”的讨论，从而将“共同理论化”边缘化。最终，文章的论证指向一个更深层次的结论：H2020项目本身的结构性缺陷——如在项目申请阶段就已固化理论框架、缺乏过程中的灵活性——是阻碍更深层次、更具合作性的知识生产模式形成的主要制度性障碍。

4. 方法 (Method)

本研究采用了一种质性的案例研究方法，并带有行动研究（action research）的色彩。核心的研究方法是与项目研究人员进行三轮半结构化访谈。数据分析过程结合了初期的归纳式编码和后期基于理论框架的演绎式编码。一个值得注意的方法论特点是，文章的前两位作者本身就是比利时“生活实验室”的领导者，他们将自身的实践经验和反思也纳入了分析之中，这体现了研究的高度自反性（self-reflexivity）。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文的分析案例是欧盟H2020框架下的DESIRA项目，具体聚焦于其分布在欧洲不同国家的20个“生活实验室”中的7个。这些“生活实验室”旨在理解和评估“数字化”（digitalisation）对农业、林业和农村地区所带来的社会与经济影响。这一案例为研究提供了一个具体的、多情境的分析对象，以考察在大型、多层次、跨国参与式研究项目中，理论与实践是如何发生冲突的。

1. Introduction

本章引出研究的核心议题：在欧盟科研项目中，以“知识共创”为核心的参与式研究日益成为主流，尤其是在应对农业和农村地区的复杂挑战方面。然而，作者敏锐地指出，关于参与的真实深度和具体形式，特别是利益相关者在理论构建（即“共同理论化”）中的角色，仍然存在大量争议和研究空白。本章通过梳理现有文献，明确了文章的核心研究问题：在H2020这类大型多主体项目中，研究人员是如何处理和解决项目需求、利益相关者需求以及“共同理论化”这三者之间的内在张力的？

  • 总结作者引用 Hakkarainen et al. (2022) 的观点，指出知识共创被视为解决复杂“棘手问题”（wicked problems）的关键策略。
    The co-creation of knowledge and innovations is often described as a key aspect of solving complex, or “wicked” problems (Hakkarainen et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 EIP-AGRI (2017) 的官方文件，证明在欧盟层面，知识共创已从理念转变为农业创新伙伴关系（EIP-AGRI）的指导原则。
    “Knowledge is co-created between practice, scientists, advisers, enterprises, NGOs, etc” (EIP-AGRI, 2017, p. 3).
  • 总结作者引用 Caniglia et al. (2020) 的观点，将知识共创置于一个更宏大的学术背景下，即整合多元知识以应对时代的“重大挑战”。
    Such knowledge co-creation is part of a multifaceted yet cohesive effort to integrate diverse knowledges and integrated approaches in knowledge development, aimed at addressing the “grand challenges” of our time (Caniglia et al., 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Jahn et al. (2012) 和 Nowotny (2003) 的经典论述，将这一趋势描述为知识生产模式从传统的线性、等级制向社会分布式、跨学科和应用导向的根本性转变。
    It is commonly described as a move away from disciplinary, linear and hierarchical modes of knowledge production to a socially distributed, transdisciplinary and application-oriented mode of knowledge production (Jahn et al., 2012; Nowotny, 2003).
  • 总结作者引用 Ingram et al. (2020) 的观点，指出这一转变与更广泛的“科学民主化”呼声相呼应，创新被视为多方共享的实践。
    This resonates with a broader call for democratising science, where innovation becomes a shared practice between multiple actors and stakeholders, involving their concerns, knowledge, experiences and practices (Ingram et al., 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Chambers (1989) 和 Neef & Neubert (2011) 的观点，追溯了农业科学领域对多元知识包容性的学术渊源，指出这一思潮自80年代末已开始兴起。
    This development has seen increased popularity in agricultural science since at least the late 80s with Chambers (1989) frequently cited as the starting point of a broader involvement of diverse knowledges in agricultural research (Neef & Neubert, 2011).
  • 总结作者引用 Basco-Carrera et al. (2017) 和 Doudaki & Carpentier (2021) 的观点，指出尽管参与式研究日益流行，但关于其潜力和所需参与程度，学术界仍存在不同看法和争议。
    However, there are varying views on the potential of participatory research and the required extent of participation (Basco-Carrera et al., 2017; Doudaki & Carpentier, 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Mobjörk (2010) 的观点，强调关于应达到的参与水平，存在反复的批判和辩论。
    There are also recurring critiques and debates over what level of participation should be reached (Mobjörk, 2010).
  • 总结作者引用 Arnstein (1969) 的经典“参与阶梯”理论，作为分析和识别不同参与层次的理论工具。
    The levels or intensity of citizen and stakeholder participation desired, or required, have been recognized in the participation ladder (Arnstein, 1969).
  • 总结作者引用 Arnstein (1969) 和 Galende Sánchez & Sorman (2021) 的观点，具体阐述了“参与阶梯”理论涵盖了从非参与、象征性参与到完全公民控制的广泛范围。
    This highlights the different forms that participation can take, from non-participation and manipulation, through consultation, to full citizen control and partnership (Arnstein, 1969; Galende Sánchez & Sorman, 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Slavova et al. (2023) 的观点，将H2020的多主体方法置于欧洲研究资助的整体政策背景下，即项目被鼓励甚至被要求吸纳多元行动者。
    Multi-actor approaches fit within a broader European research funding context, where research projects are encouraged, if not required, to include diverse actors in the process of co-creating demand-focused knowledge (Slavova et al., 2023).
  • 总结作者引用 Feo et al. (2022) 的研究，揭示了当前H2020项目中参与的现实形态：通常是围绕特定活动组织的、短暂的参与时刻。
    Considering the intensity and depth of participation, Feo et al. (2022) describe participation in these projects as centred around events, with short moments of participation.
  • 总结作者引用 Doudaki & Carpentier (2021), Dunlap et al. (2021) 和 Koskinen (2014) 的观点，指出关于参与的形式和期望结果，存在更根本性的持续辩论。
    On a more fundamental level, there are running debates about the forms and desired outcomes of participation. ... there are different views on what this diversity means for the participatory process (Doudaki & Carpentier, 2021; Dunlap et al., 2021; Koskinen, 2014).
  • 总结作者引用 Adamsone-Fiskovica & Grivins (2022) 的观点，描述了多主体项目中一种常见但较为工具性的观点，即认为多元知识的价值在于加强科学知识、使其合法化并提升应用性。
    The common approach in multi-actor research projects is that a diversity of knowledges is valuable in that it can strengthen scientific knowledge and legitimise the outputs of the research process, and increase its applicability in practice (Adamsone-Fiskovica & Grivins, 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 Felt et al. (2012) 和 Hakkarainen et al. (2022) 的批判性观点，指出上述工具性观点将参与局限于方法层面，并未从根本上挑战项目背后的核心概念和理论。
    This has however been critiqued as limiting participation to involvement in research methods and analytical tools, as it does not fundamentally question concepts and theories underpinning the projects (Felt et al., 2012; Hakkarainen et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 Arribas Lozano (2018) 和 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 的观点，提出作为对上述局限性的回应，学者们呼吁让利益相关者更深入地参与到科学知识的生产过程中，包括理论发展。
    In response, other authors have called for the involvement of stakeholders in the production of scientific knowledge and outputs such as scientific articles and the development or advancement of scientific theory (Arribas Lozano, 2018; Caretta & Pérez, 2019).
  • 总结作者引用 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 和 Feo et al. (2022) 的观点，强调这种深度参与超越了H2020项目中常见的、仅限于数据收集和成果推广的参与形式。
    This goes beyond the forms of participation common to Horizon 2020 research projects, where participation usually does not extend beyond participation in data gathering and implementation of project results in practice (Caretta & Pérez, 2019; Feo et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，明确本研究的核心关切是研究人员如何处理和应对参与过程中的各种张力。
    These tensions between what participation is desired, what contributions participants can make and how researchers deal with tensions between desired participation and other needs of the research project are the principal interests of this study (Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Felt et al. (2016), Schikowitz (2020) 和 Slavova et al. (2023) 的观点，指出关于研究人员如何处理参与式研究（尤其是在理论发展层面）中的张力的研究相对匮乏，从而凸显了本研究的学术贡献和新颖性。
    ...there are relatively few studies that analyse how researchers deal with the tensions that participatory research brings, especially concerning the development of theoretical concepts and broader theories through integrating diverse knowledges (Felt et al., 2016; Schikowitz, 2020; Slavova et al., 2023), and this is the gap the study addresses and where the study makes a novel contribution.

2. Theoretical Framework

本章为后续的实证分析构建了坚实的理论框架。作者首先对“参与”这一核心概念进行了辨析，界定了不同层次的参与，并强调参与是一个连续的光谱而非简单的二元对立。其次，该章节深入探讨了参与式研究中固有的、难以回避的张力，特别是科学知识生产（追求普适性、理论性）与社会相关知识生产（强调情境性、实用性）之间的核心矛盾。最后，本章聚焦于“共同理论化”这一前沿概念，系统地讨论了让利益相关者直接参与科学产出（如理论构建与论文撰写）的巨大潜力、方法论挑战以及其对于实现真正知识民主化的必要性。

  • 总结作者引用 Fleming et al. (2021) 的观点，强调由于“参与式研究”概念的广泛性，有必要清晰界定本文所研究的参与形式。
    Considering the participation ladder, and the diversity of approaches that are classified as forms of participatory research (Fleming et al., 2021), there is a need to clarify and specify the forms of participation studied in this article.
  • 总结作者引用 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 的观点，指出参与常常局限于数据收集阶段，而更理想的“有意义的参与”应赋予利益相关者在决策中的发言权。
    Participation is often limited to the collection of data, but would ideally form more meaningful participation where stakeholders have a say in decision-making (Caretta & Pérez, 2019).
  • 总结作者引用 Basco-Carrera et al. (2017), Jahn et al. (2012), 和 Renn (2021) 的观点，承认本案例中的参与形式存在局限，因为它未能让利益相关者在研究过程的早期阶段（如议题设定）就介入。
    To some authors this is already an imperfect participatory process, as the stakeholders cannot influence the research process beforehand and are also not involved in broader decision-making (Basco-Carrera et al., 2017; Jahn et al., 2012; Renn, 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Arnstein (1969) 和 Basco-Carrera et al. (2017) 的观点，来说明参与可以有多种形式，最高可达至完全的公民控制和共同生产。
    Participation can take different forms, moving towards full citizen control in the case of collaborative or co-productive processes (Arnstein, 1969; Basco-Carrera et al., 2017).
  • 总结作者引用 Neef & Neubert (2011) 的观点，反驳了只有高层级参与才有价值的刻板印象，认为即使在参与阶梯的较低层次，也能实现对参与者有意义的互动。
    Several authors indicate that meaningful participation can occur even in processes that at first sight seem to be low on the participation ladder (Neef & Neubert, 2011).
  • 总结作者引用 Ingram et al. (2018) 的观点，将参与式研究置于一个从传统的科学驱动知识生产到更具互动性的跨学科研究的光谱之中。
    For research, this means that participation is somewhere on a spectrum, between linear, science-driven knowledge production and more inter- and trans-disciplinary research, which is oriented towards application (Ingram et al., 2018).
  • 总结作者引用 Ingram et al. (2018) 和 Neef & Neubert (2011) 的观点，指出将研究项目简单地归为光谱的两端是过于简化的，现实中常是两种模式的混合体。
    To place research at either end of this spectrum is too general and simplistic, as research projects often contain elements of both models, where it is more accurate to speak of a blend between these archetypes (Ingram et al., 2018; Neef & Neubert, 2011).
  • 总结作者引用 Nesheim et al. (2021) 的观点，将“有意义的参与”定义为能为利益相关者提供被倾听的权利，并有机会影响研究目标和结果。
    Rather, participation needs to be meaningful to stakeholders, through providing them a right to be heard and a possibility to contribute to setting research objectives and steering research outcomes (Nesheim et al., 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Schikowitz (2020), Feo et al. (2022) 和 Fieldsend et al. (2021) 的观点，点出了“有意义的参与”与项目的其他目标（如科学知识生产、资助方要求）之间可能存在的紧张关系。
    Increased stakeholder say can be in tension with the production of scientific knowledge, and funder requirements do not always allow stakeholders to be involved in setting out the project goals and aims (Feo et al., 2022; Fieldsend et al., 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Brandt et al. (2013), Felt et al. (2016) 和 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，明确指出了参与式研究中一个核心的内在张力：即科学知识的生产与具有社会相关性的知识生产之间的矛盾。
    There is an inherent tension between the production of scientific knowledge and the socially relevant knowledge that is expected to be built in the process of participatory research (Brandt et al., 2013; Felt et al., 2016; Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Felt et al. (2016) 和 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，解释了在科学研究中，来自参与者的经验、知识和反思需要被“翻译”成符合科学规范和标准的学术形式。
    Experiences, knowledge and reflections are translated into a scientific form that is valued following scientific standards (Felt et al., 2016; Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Felt et al. (2016) 的研究，具体列举了参与式研究面临的多重张力，例如：既要承诺对科学进行根本性变革，又要维持在传统科学界内的合法性；在追求普适性结论与尊重地方特殊性之间摇摆。
    Felt et al. (2016) for example describe several tensions: the promise of radical change to science while retaining legitimacy within science, tensions between generalisation and local relevance, and between developing broad knowledge and expertise while retaining distinctions between science and society.
  • 总结作者引用 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，指出当面临这些张力时，许多参与式项目往往会退守到传统的科学产出上，导致科学相关性压倒社会相关性，从而限制了参与的真正潜力。
    Scientific relevance prevails over societal relevance, and limits the potential of participatory research. In response, several authors take the view that scientific outputs might need to become a secondary aim in participatory research... (Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者引用 Gergen & Gergen (2008) 和 Djenontin & Meadow (2018) 的观点，指出了一个普遍现象：在某些参与式研究领域，参与者对抽象的科学理论表现出矛盾心理甚至冷漠，认为其缺乏实际用途。
    Scientific theories are sometimes described as having no real use to participants in the process, lacking utility... (Gergen & Gergen, 2008). Other authors share the view that stakeholders might not find meaning in the scientific process of developing theories (Djenontin & Meadow, 2018).
  • 总结作者引用 Schlüter et al. (2022) 的观点，讨论了完全放弃理论的风险，即可能丧失对从多元行动者互动中涌现出的新知识进行整合与升华的机会。
    ...foregoing theories completely risks losing out on a consolidation of knowledge that emerges from the involvement of broader actors in research processes (Schlüter et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 Friedman & Rogers (2009) 的观点，提出需要构建一种能同时为实践者、学者和其他参与者提供可理解和有用工具的“好理论”。
    In response there is the view that we need to craft different scientific theories that do find relevance with stakeholders. Friedman and Rogers (2009) set out that “good theory” provides accessible and useful tools for practitioners, academics, and other actors/participants alike.
  • 总结作者引用 Genat (2009), Friedman & Rogers (2009), 和 Málovics et al. (2021) 的观点，阐述了在参与式研究中，“好理论”的目标是赋能参与者，让他们能够以新的方式解读世界、理解自身社会现实，并让地方知识参与到理论建构中。
    To these authors, the aims of scientific theory in participation are to enable research participants to interpret the world in new ways, to understand their social reality and to have local knowledge participate in theory-building (Friedman & Rogers, 2009; Málovics et al., 2021).
  • 总结作者引用 Nowotny (2003) 的“社会稳健性知识”（socially robust knowledge）概念，将上述观点与通过利益相关者深度参与来发展与社会需求紧密结合的科学知识的理念联系起来。
    This connects to the idea of socially robust knowledge that we set out, where socially relevant scientific knowledge is developed through the participation of stakeholders (Nowotny, 2003).
  • 总结作者引用 Schlüter et al. (2022) 的观点，指出当吸纳更多行动者时，理论构建过程本身也转变为一个更具协作性、审议性的集体学习过程。
    Schlüter et al. (2022) mention that by involving more actors, such as in a multi-actor process, theorising becomes a more collaborative and deliberative learning process that involves a wider diversity of actors.
  • 总结作者引用 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 和 Huebner et al. (2017) 的观点，引入了“共同理论化”（co-theorising）这一核心概念，并强调其关键在于对参与过程中的不同行动者承担一种“认知责任”（epistemic accountability）。
    Interesting in this light is the notion of co-theorising (which includes a level of epistemic accountability to the different actors involved in the participatory process) (Caretta & Pérez, 2019; Huebner et al., 2017).
  • 总结作者引用 Schlüter et al. (2022) 的观点，区分了研究者在理论构建中的两种角色：传统的“外部观察者”和在参与式研究中更为常见的“嵌入系统者”。
    The role of the researcher in theorising can be distinguished between outside observers or as embedded in the system they seek to observe, with the latter being more common for participatory research (Schlüter et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者引用 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 的观点，进一步阐述了“共同理论化”可以采取多种形式，从简单的研究结果验证，到让被研究社群深度参与分析和科学产出的撰写。
    Depending on the community involved in the participatory process, this might involve simply verifying the results but can also include a deeper involvement of the researched community in the analysis and production of scientific outputs (Caretta & Pérez, 2019).
  • 总结作者引用 Arribas Lozano (2018) 的观点，强调将参与扩展到科学产出的生产过程本身，能够极大地丰富知识生产，因为它促进了反思，实现了研究者角色的“去中心化”，并给予了利益相关者知识更大的权重。
    It allows for increased reflection, allows for a de-centring of the role of the researcher and allows for a greater role of stakeholder knowledges in the production of scientific knowledge (Arribas Lozano, 2018).

3. Methods

本章详细介绍了本研究严谨的研究设计与方法。作者首先阐明了其案例研究方法，以宏大的H2020 DESIRA项目为背景，并聚焦于其中7个“生活实验室”（Living Labs）进行深度分析。接着，清晰地界定了“生活实验室”这一核心场域及其在本研究中的具体运作方式（主要通过工作坊和焦点小组）。然后，详细描述了本研究的数据收集过程，即通过对领导这些“生活实验室”的研究人员进行三轮、共计17次半结构化访谈。最后，系统说明了数据分析方法，包括访谈的转录和使用Nvivo软件进行的两轮编码（先归纳后演绎），并特别强调了作者作为研究参与者的自反性（self-reflexivity）实践，这增加了研究的厚度与可信度。

  • 总结作者引用 Gamache et al. (2020) 的观点，来定义“生活实验室”（Living Labs）是一种以用户为中心、强调共同创新的开放式创新过程。
    Living labs are used to describe user-centred, open innovation processes with significant co-innovation (Gamache et al., 2020).

4. Findings & 5. Discussion and conclusion

这两章构成了文章的实证核心与理论升华。Findings部分系统地呈现了访谈数据，揭示了三个关键发现：1）研究人员在理念上普遍认同扩展参与和共同理论化的价值，但在实践中却困难重重，实际参与度远低于预期；2）研究揭示了参与过程中的核心张力来源——项目宏大、统一的目标（如“数字化”）与地方利益相关者具体、紧迫的需求（如社群建设、产品议价）之间的显著脱节；3）为应对此张力，研究人员普遍采取了一种“分离策略”，即把满足项目和科学需求的活动，与满足利益相关者实践需求的活动进行切割，前者被尽可能地压缩以保证后者的“真实”参与。

Discussion and conclusion部分则对这些发现进行了深刻的理论化提炼和引申。首先，文章分析了研究人员“害怕利益相关者退出参与”这一普遍焦虑的深层原因，将其归结为项目预设议题与地方现实的结构性错位。其次，将研究人员采取的“分离策略”概念化，指出这与文献中常见的“科学相关性优先”不同，反而是一种“实践相关性优先”的妥协，并通过灵活地“升降参与阶梯”来应对不同任务。最后，文章得出极具洞见的结论：H2020这类大型项目的结构性问题（如在项目早期阶段就固化议题和理论框架，缺乏过程灵活性）是阻碍实现真正“共同理论化”的主要制度障碍。文章据此建议，未来的参与式项目设计应更加开放和归纳，允许理论从真实的参与过程中涌现，而非被预先设定。

  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，说明研究人员为了迎合利益相关者的兴趣而主动调整参与活动，这与现有文献的发现是一致的。
    ...with researchers being careful of stakeholder interests, where they seek to shape activities around stakeholder interests (following Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Klerkx et al. (2017) 和 Berthet et al. (2016) 的观点，呼应了文献中早已指出的，在大型项目中“普适性”研究成果与“地方性”知识需求之间的持久张力。
    ...these results might not be universally true across living labs, situated in different countries and contexts (echoing Klerkx et al., 2017). This is in agreement with the tensions described by Felt et al. (2016), where generalisable results are demanded despite the realisation that locally specific knowledge is needed (echoing Berthet et al., 2016).
  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Boogaard (2021) 和 Latulippe & Klenk (2020) 的观点，批判性地指出，许多所谓的“参与”过程，实际上仍将多元知识视为待收集的“数据”，而非将其持有者视为平等的知识共创伙伴。
    Participation is often a name put on processes where diverse knowledges are still seen as data to be collected, to be used in the production of scientific knowledge (Boogaard, 2021; Latulippe & Klenk, 2020).
  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Felt et al. (2016) 和 Schikowitz (2020) 的观点，指出本研究的一个有趣发现是，研究人员并未像一些文献所描述的那样，在面临张力时退回到“科学相关性优先”的立场，反而优先考虑了利益相关者的需求。
    Interestingly, our findings indicate that researchers in this research did not necessarily fall back on scientific relevance over other forms of relevance in response to these tensions, which is contrary to some of the findings of other authors (Felt et al., 2016; Schikowitz, 2020).
  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Slavova et al. (2023) 的研究，进一步证实了在多主体项目中，项目需求与利益相关者需求之间的冲突是常态，而研究人员需要不断地做出妥协以维持项目的运作。
    This fits with the analysis of Slavova et al. (2023) that compromises are constantly made to keep the multi-actor project functioning.
  • 总结作者在讨论中引用 Arnstein (1969) 和 Galende Sánchez & Sorman (2021) 经典的“参与阶梯”理论，来形象地描述研究人员根据不同活动目标，灵活地调整参与程度的动态策略。
    Researchers thus “ascend and descend the participation ladder” during the project; there is no specific level of participation that is achieved throughout the project, but rather varying levels of participation depending on the activity and the aims of the researcher (Arnstein, 1969; Galende Sánchez & Sorman, 2021).
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Feo et al. (2022) 的观点，承认本研究的发现部分归因于所研究项目的结构性限制，即项目时间有限，且参与活动多为一次性的事件。
    This, to an extent, is specific to the structure of the investigated research project, which is limited in time and scale to four years with participation being limited to events (Feo et al., 2022).
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Potters et al. (2022) 和 Toffolini et al. (2021) 的观点，对本研究中的“生活实验室”是否真正达到了其理论上所倡导的“共同实验空间”的理想状态提出了质疑，认为它们在实践中更接近于传统的、以咨询为目的的参与空间。
    It can also be questioned whether the living labs in this research were used as co-experimental spaces, which would set them apart from earlier participatory exercises (Potters et al., 2022; Toffolini et al., 2021). In our research the living lab seemed to align more with classical participatory spaces based on stakeholder consultation events...
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Numans et al. (2019) 的观点，提出了一个重要的假设：如果“生活实验室”的主题能更紧密地与利益相关者的内在兴趣相结合（例如由他们共同设定），那么“共同理论化”的成功可能性将会更高。
    There is the possibility that co-theorising would be of more interest to stakeholders when the living lab topic is more closely aligned with their interests (as would be the case when the stakeholders are directly involved in setting this topic) (echoing Numans et al., 2019).
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Shortall and Meredith (2025) 的观点，指出这种由项目结构带来的局限性并不仅仅存在于H2020，在后续的“地平线欧洲”（Horizon Europe）计划中同样存在。
    This is a limitation of the project structure of the Horizon 2020 projects in question as well as its successor Horizon Europe (see also Shortall and Meredith, 2025).
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Caretta & Pérez (2019) 的观点，提出了一个关键的政策建议：与其在项目后期努力推动“共同理论化”，不如在项目最初的设计阶段就深化利益相关者的参与，这可能更为有效。
    Before deepening participation in the data gathering or in co-theorising, it makes more sense to include stakeholders in the project design (following Caretta & Pérez, 2019).
  • 总结作者在结论中引用 Cronin et al. (2022), Klerkx et al. (2017), 和 Shortall and Meredith (2025) 的观点，重申了当前大型国际合作项目的普遍结构性问题（时间限制、伙伴众多、早期参与不足）是实现深度共同理论构建的主要障碍。
    ...as these projects are restricted in time, deal with a large number of partners and actors, and their structure does not easily allow participation at early stages of the project (as also observed by Cronin et al., 2022; Klerkx et al., 2017; Shortall and Meredith, 2025).
