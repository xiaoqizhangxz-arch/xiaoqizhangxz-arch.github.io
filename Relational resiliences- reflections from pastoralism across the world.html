Relational resiliences: reflections from pastoralism across the world

Greta Semplici, L. Jamila Haider, Ryan Unks, Tahira S. Mohamed, Giulia Simula, Palden Tsering (Huadancairang), Natasha Maru, Linda Pappagallo & Masresha Taye

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，主流的、以实体为中心的（object-oriented）韧性（resilience）概念框架，不足以深刻理解和有效支持游牧系统（pastoral systems）。作者们主张采用一种“过程-关系”（process-relational）的认识论，将韧性不视为一种静态的能力或属性，而是视为在不断变化的社会-生态关系网络中动态浮现的过程，即“生成韧性”（becoming resilient）。作者们认为，这种关系性视角能够更好地回应学界对韧性理论的既有批判（如其权力盲点、新自由主义倾向），并为支持游牧生计提供一种更公正、更符合在地实践的理论路径。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要建立在对六个全球不同地区的游牧案例研究进行的质性、比较分析之上，这些案例地跨非洲、亚洲和欧洲（具体为意大利撒丁岛、肯尼亚伊西奥洛、埃塞俄比亚博拉纳、印度古吉拉特邦、中国安多藏区和突尼斯南部）。证据并非直接的一手民族志材料，而是通过与这些案例的资深研究者（“case study leads”）举办两次专题研讨会，系统性地综合了他们深厚的民族志知识和在地理解。第一次研讨会通过问卷收集了研究者们对韧性的在地化定义和理解，第二次则通过“事件分析法”深入探讨了韧性在具体情境中的生成过程。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑分为两个层面。首先，在理论层面，文章系统梳理并批判了主流韧性理论的局限性，特别是其忽视权力不对等、易被新自由主义治理术收编、以及固守主客二元论的本体论根基。在此基础上，作者将游牧主义定位为发展替代性理论的理想场域，因为游牧民本身就是变异性（variability）和不确定性（uncertainty）的专家管理者，其生计从根本上是由复杂的、流动的关系所构成的。其次，在经验层面，通过对六个案例的比较分析，特别是在“事件分析”环节，文章展示了韧性是如何在日常实践、社会组织、文化过程以及人与非人（more-than-human）的纠缠中被不断地塑造与再造的。这一过程将分析的焦点从“韧性是什么”（what resilience *is*）巧妙地转移到了“韧性是如何生成的”（how resilience *is becoming*），从而完成了从实体思维向过程-关系思维的范式转换。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用了一种质性的、归纳的、比较综合的研究方法。其核心数据源于与六位民族志研究者进行的两次深度研讨会。第一阶段，研究者们通过开放式问卷，收集并视觉化编码（visual coding）了各个案例中关于韧性的多元理解，并将其绘制在不同的分析谱系图上。第二阶段，为了更深入地捕捉动态过程，研究采用了“事件分析”（event analysis）的方法，要求每位研究者描述一个能反映日常生活与压力情境下社会-生态关系的具体民族志片段（vignette）。这种方法借鉴了过程哲学的思想，将“事件”视为流变过程中暂时的稳定态，从而能够更细致地揭示构成韧性的背后那些复杂、交织的关系与过程，实现了对韧性的“过程-关系性”的深度诠释。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文章开篇引用的印度拉巴里（Rabari）游牧民的案例，是贯穿全文的典型例证。他们的营地并非一个静态实体，而是一个动态过程，在与天气、地景、农民、牲畜和劳动分工的持续互动中被不断地“制造-重制”。一场突如其来的冰雹灾害，恰恰揭示了这个“生成中”的营地是如何通过一系列即时的、关系性的行动（如挖沟排水、众人合力撑起油布）来适应冲击的。这生动地诠释了文章的核心观点：韧性并非预先存在的属性，而是从这些持续的、动态的相互依存关系中涌现出来的。其他案例，如肯尼亚的迪巴（Diba）在重病期间依靠亲属和社区的照料网络维持生计，或埃塞俄比亚的博卡约（Bokayo）通过复杂的家庭、市场和亲属关系来管理牧群，都同样有力地证明了韧性是一种在关系网络中不断生成和维系的社会-生态过程。

1. Introduction: relational resilience, gaps and contributions

本章节通过一个生动的民族志案例（印度Rabari游牧民）引入核心议题，即韧性是在日常变化的关系中浮现的过程。接着，章节回顾了韧性理论的演变及其在发展领域应用时引发的诸多批判，如忽视权力、沦为新自由主义治理工具等。最后，文章明确提出本文的贡献：通过借鉴人类学、社会学和游牧主义研究中的“关系转向”，探索一种以过程和关系为核心的方法论，以期更好地理解游牧景观中的韧性，并反过来思考游牧主义能为关系性韧性理论提供何种洞见。

  • 对韧性理论的演变进行概述： 作者指出韧性理论已从早期的系统功能维持定义，发展到近期关注实体在关系网络中“浮现”和被重塑的阶段。
    Resilience theory has evolved substantially from early definitions as ‘the capacity of a system to absorb disturbance and reorganize while undergoing change so as to still retain essentially the same function, structure, identity, and feedbacks’ (Walker et al. 2004, p. 2).
  • 韧性理论的新发展——关系视角： 作者提到，近期一些韧性理论学者开始尝试通过关系网络来理解变化，认为实体在其中“涌现”并被不断塑造。
    Recently, some scholars working with resilience theory have sought to understand change through the relational networks within which entities ‘emerge’ and are constantly shaped and re-shaped (Hertz et al. 2020; West et al. 2020; Folke et al. 2021).
  • 关系视角的优越性： 这种新的理解被认为更符合社会正义、包容性可持续发展和气候适应的目标。
    It is argued that these understandings are more aligned with the goals of social justice, inclusive sustainability, and climate adaptation (Walsh et al. 2021).
  • 韧性理论与实践的脱节： 理论的进步与在可持续发展实践中的应用之间存在显著差异。
    Hence, resilience thinking remains quite varied, with important discrepancies between theoretical advances and the application of theory in practice in sustainable development (Reyers et al. 2022).
  • 对韧性在发展领域应用的批判 (机会主义)： 众多批判指出，权力不对等使其被机会主义地应用。
    Numerous critiques of the application of resilience in development have pointed to how power asymmetries have allowed it to be applied in opportunistic ways (Grove 2018), ...
  • 对韧性在发展领域应用的批判 (维护权力结构)： 韧性概念可能被用来维护现有的权力结构。
    ...how it can maintain structures of power (Brown 2015; Boonstra 2016; Coaffee and Chandler, 2017), ...
  • 对韧性在发展领域应用的批判 (新自由主义)： 韧性概念可能助长新自由主义议程，将应对风险的责任从国家和机构转移给社区和个人。
    ...and can expand the influence of neoliberal agendas in the face of rising uncertainty and compound stressors, often times responsibilising communities and individuals rather than states and institutions (Joseph 2013; Rigg and Oven 2015; Chandler and Reid 2016; Grove 2018).
  • 对韧性学术框架的批判 (忽视权力)： 韧性理论本身也因其对系统性权力关系的忽视而受到社会科学家的警惕。
    These include theories of human behaviour and social, political, and economic systems that have led to a tendency of resilience theory, which some argue is inherent to the way it conceptualises systems, to overlook systemic power relations (Côte and Nightingale 2012; Hatt 2013; Welsh 2014; Olsson et al. 2015).
  • 对韧性的再概念化尝试 (融入权力分析)： 存在一些替代性的学术框架，试图对韧性进行再概念化，以挑战主流政策并融入权力分析。
    Alternative academic framings have recommended reconceptualisations to challenge dominant policy-making and open new opportunities (Macrae 2012; Leach et al. 2018), with some, attempting to incorporate power analysis (Boonstra 2016).
  • 韧性的多元性与争议性： 韧性最好被理解为是多元且充满争议的，其理论、分析、应用和规范目标之间存在关键差异。
    Thus, resilience is perhaps best understood as multiple and contested, with key contrasts between different theoretical, analytical, applied, and normative goals (Grove 2018).
  • “韧性多元体”概念： 引入“resilience multiple”概念，强调韧性没有统一定义，不同应用背后有不同的本体论基础和规范性含义，需要批判性地审视。
    Relatedly, the notion of ‘resilience multiple’ as put forth by Simon and Randalls (2016), holds that there is no universal definition of resilience, and that different applications of the term have both different ontological underpinnings and also include normative implications, and so should be critically examined at their points of articulation (see also Konaka 2023).
  • 韧性话语的批判性潜力： 韧性话语不仅是去政治化的控制叙事，也可能开启新的批判性对话，因为它本身在概念上是可塑的，具有“别样生成”（becoming ‘otherwise’）的潜力。
    Grove (2018) suggests that resilience discourse, rather than merely serving as an apoliticizing narrative of control, also creates possibilities to open up new critical dialogues, as itself is conceptually malleable, and has potential for becoming ‘otherwise’ (see also Côte 2019, cf; Chandler 2014).
  • 将韧性与批判理论结合的尝试： 一些学者已经开始将他们的工作与这种批判性潜力相结合。
    While some have aligned their work (Wakefield 2020; Garcia et al. 2022; Gonda et al. 2023), others remain wary that resilience remains inherently wedded to neoliberal, managerial, functionalist roots (Watts 2019).

2. Conceptual framework: relational resiliences in pastoral contexts

本章节构建了论文的理论框架。它首先回顾了游牧主义研究中的范式转变：从视游牧为“落后”、“非理性”的“均衡”生态学视角，转变为认可其作为适应“非均衡”动态环境的、管理“变异性”的专家系统的视角。然而，作者指出，这种学术认知上的进步并未有效转化为主流发展政策，后者仍倾向于将变异性视为需要控制的障碍。接着，文章将这一困境与可持续性科学中的“关系转向”相连接，主张一种关注过程和关系的认识论，能够超越主客二元对立，重视在地知识，并与政治本体论（political ontology）相结合，从而抵制对韧性的普遍化、客观化理解。最后，文章明确其认识论立场：借鉴人类学、社会学和新物质主义中的关系性思维，将韧性视为在充满权力差异和非人能动性的纠缠关系中“生成”（becoming）的过程。

  • 游牧主义研究中的范式转变： 描述了自1980年代以来，对游牧生计的看法发生了巨大变化，从负面的“落后”形象转变为正面的“适应性”和“机会主义”形象。
    Since the 1980s there has been a large change of views of pastoral livelihoods, from being seen as ‘backward’, ‘irrational’, and environmentally destructive (Brown 1971; Lamprey 1983; Le Houérou 1989), to adaptive, opportunistic, and contributing a number of benefits to social-ecological systems (Chatty 1972; Fratkin 1997; Westoby et al. 1989; for a review of these changes and how they link with resilience studies, see: Konaka 2023).
  • 生态学理论的平行转变： 这一观念转变与当时生态学理论的变化有关，特别是从均衡的牧场生态模型转向强调资源不可预测性的动态生态模型。
    This shift of views was linked with changing ecological theory at the time, including broader shifts in understanding ecosystem change through the lens of dynamical systems resilience (Holling 1973). More specifically equilibrium rangeland ecological models moved to more dynamic ecological models that emphasised unpredictable and short-lived concentrations of resources (Vetter 2005; Briske et al. 2020), ...
  • 变异性（Variability）作为核心概念： 这一转变使游牧发展学者强调“变异性”是理解游牧生计的关键。
    ...leading scholars of pastoral development to emphasise ‘variability’ as key to understanding pastoralist livelihoods (Krätli et al. 2013; Krätli 2015).
  • 政策与实践的脱节： 尽管学术上认可游牧民的贡献和知识，但政府和国际组织往往未能认识到这一点，并且在“韧性建设”项目中，常将变异性视为障碍而非资源。
    This is seen prominently in the contradictions between understandings of variability in pastoralism as a resource, and ‘resilience building’ programmes in pastoral settings, that often see variability as an obstacle to stable production and attempt to control or minimise it (Semplici 2020; Semplici and Campbell 2023).
  • 韧性概念在干旱区的误用： 发展部门在干旱区应用韧性时，常与“荒漠化”等叙事挂钩，强化了对 inherently 变化的干旱环境进行控制的企图。
    For example, climate change narratives focused on desertification, typical of how resilience is applied in programmes in drylands by the development sector, can reinforce historical attempts to create stability in dryland environments that are inherently variable and dynamic (Ellis 1995; Hiernaux et al. 2016; Behnke and Mortimore 2016).
  • 主流韧性政策的局限性： 政策和发展领域的韧性讨论，有沦为纯粹工具性叙事的风险，为自上而下的、市场驱动的农业发展政策辩护，忽视了游牧系统自身应对干旱和其他冲击的能力。
    Discussions about resilience in policy and development domains, in other words, risk limiting resilience to a purely instrumental narrative that justifies prescription of ‘top-down’ state and market-driven agricultural and rural development policies that favour alternative livelihoods, overlooking the ability of pastoral systems to respond to drought and other shocks (Cervigni and Morris 2016; Catley 2017).
  • 引入“关系转向”： 引用Reyers等人研究，指出从“实体导向”转向“关系建构”的社会-生态系统观，是韧性科学支持可持续发展的关键但最具挑战性的转变之一。
    One of the shifts Reyers et al. (2022) identify as the most challenging is to move from object-oriented thinking to viewing social-ecological systems (SES) as relationally constituted.
  • “关系转向”的核心思想： “关系转向”主张将过程和关系视为现实的主要构成要素，而非本质或实体，从而超越人与自然的二元对立。
    Such a shift would add to discussions of a ‘relational turn’ in sustainability science (West et al. 2020), which proposes to advance social-ecological systems research by focusing on ways of knowing that are attuned to ‘processes and relations as the main constituents of reality instead of fundamental substances or essences’ (Mancilla Garcia et al. 2019, p. 221; Hertz et al. 2020).
  • 致敬原住民思想： 承认在“西方”关系思想之前，早已存在丰富的原住民学术和哲学，这些思想与韧性思维早有交集。
    Important to note is a rich indigenous scholarship and philosophy that long precedes ‘western’ relational thought (particularly in the realm of nature-society relationships), which already has a history of convergence with resilience (see for example Trosper 2002; Todd 2018).
  • 与“关怀”伦理的结合： 关系性思维可以与“关怀”（care）为中心的、拒绝控制导向的、更具适应性的应对方式产生协同效应。
    An approach to resilience that is informed by relational thinking could also create synergies with alternatives to dominant operationalisations in development practice through centering variability and rejecting control-oriented, technocratic approaches in favour of more tentative, adaptive, responses rooted in care (Scoones 2019; Scoones and Stirling 2021).
  • 与政治本体论的共鸣： 本文的分析方法与“政治本体论”视角相呼应，主张朝向一种多元的、在权力关系中共构的本体-认识论形态。
    Our analysis therefore also resonates with ‘political ontology’ perspectives (Blaser 2009, 2013) and thus encourages movement of knowing and conceptualizing pastoral resilience toward ‘a vision of multiple onto-epistemic formations, ineluctably co-constituted within power relations’ (Escobar 2017, p. 66).
  • 借鉴人类学与社会学的关系思想： 明确借鉴人类学、社会学、科技研究中关注关系性、纠缠和过程的思想，以超越方法论个人主义和结构-能动性的二元对立。
    We draw on a long history of thinking in anthropology, sociology, and science and technology studies that has focused on relational concepts of personhood, entanglement, and process to move beyond methodological individualism as well as the dichotomy of structure and agency (e.g. Emirbayer 1997; Latour 2005; Haraway 2008; Govindrajan 2018).
  • 批判性地运用“超越人类”方法： 强调采用的人类学方法避免了对“超越人类”（more-than-human）解释的浪漫化，并展示了等级、排斥和暴力等系统是如何构成“超越人类”过程的一部分。
    Such approaches show how systems of hierarchy, exclusion, neglect and violence are constitutive of more-than-human processes that are constitutive of identity (Govindrajan 2018).
  • 与新物质主义的结合： 借鉴新物质主义和超越人类方法（如Barad, Haraway），可以使韧性学者更好地关注常被忽视的社会、政治和经济维度。
    Drawing on relational approaches from new materialism and more-than-human approaches (Barad 2007; Haraway 2008) could enable resilience scholars to gain an enhanced ability to centre social, political, and economic dimensions of resilience that are often overlooked, ...

3. Methods

本章详细阐述了研究方法。核心作者团队本身是具有不同韧性观点的游牧主义研究者，他们共同设计并执行了一个基于PASTRES项目下六个案例地的比较研究。研究数据主要通过两次研讨会收集。第一次线上研讨会，通过分发包含四个开放性问题的问卷，收集了六位案例研究负责人（case study leads）对其研究情境中韧性的理解，并对答复进行了视觉化编码和光谱式分析。第二次线下研讨会，为了更深入地捕捉过程性，采用了“事件分析法”。研究者们被要求描述其案例中，在日常和压力情境下的具体“事件”。这些“事件”被定义为流变过程中暂时的稳定态，旨在揭示复杂的社会-生态关系。分析过程由核心作者团队主导，采用归纳法和视觉编码，通过迭代和协商，最终形成了呈现结果的图表。

  • 研究团队的背景和共同点： 描述了核心作者团队都长期从事游牧社区研究，对韧性持有不同观点，但共同着迷于游牧民与不确定性共存的能力。
    What ties our experiences as independent researchers together is our fascination with pastoralists’ ability to live ‘with’ uncertainty (Scoones 1995) and ‘off’ uncertainty (Krätli and Schareika 2010) with vitality and liveliness, and the lack of power this response capacity had/has in development discourse (studies and policy).
  • 第二次研讨会的方法论深化： 为了更明确地识别“过程”，第二次研讨会聚焦于分析具体的“事件”，以获得更扎根于民族志材料的共同反思。
    During this second workshop, we aimed to explore methodological approaches intended to identify processes more explicitly, as we identified this as limited in the first workshop, and therefore focused on examples of events from the various cases to strengthen our joint reflections by means of more grounded and ethnographic material.
  • “事件”的理论定义： 借鉴Hertz等人和Debaise的观点，将“事件”定义为不断演变的相互关联过程中的暂时稳定状态。
    Following Hertz et al. (2020), we defined events (Debaise 2017) as temporary stabilizations in ever evolving interconnected processes.
  • 对“过程-关系性”的分析： 引入并采纳了Darnhofer的“过程-关系性”方法，该方法关注异质元素间不断被制造和重塑的关系，为分析提供了更深的诠释维度。
    In seeking an answer to these questions, a fourth dimension of analysis emerged on process-relationality, derived from Darnhofer’s approach: ‘A process- relational approach focuses on the relations between heterogenous elements, the relations that are constantly made and remade, that could always be made differently, not least through different beliefs, values, perceptions, and expectations’ (Darnhofer 2021, p. 2).

4. Results: becoming resilient

本章呈现了研究的核心发现。第一部分（4.1）展示了第一次研讨会的结果，通过四组视觉化图谱，分别探讨了：1) 韧性的定义（从应对日常微小变化的即兴创造到应对剧烈冲击的生存能力）；2) 构成韧性的要素（从日常实践如市场互动、身份切换，到更高层次的社会组织和治理如联盟、网络）；3) 促成韧性的关键关系（从家庭、宗族到更广阔的身份认同和宇宙观）；4) 影响韧性的宏观变化（市场动态、技术、土地利用变化和冲突等）。第二部分（4.2）深入分析了第二次研讨会的“事件”叙事，通过印度、西藏、肯尼亚和埃塞俄比亚的案例，生动地展示了韧性是如何作为一个“生成过程”（becoming）展开的。例如，印度的营地是在与环境、他者的持续互动中不断被“制造-重制”的；西藏的生计是在人、牲畜、土地、菌类和游客等多元行动者的关系重构中展开的。这些分析揭示了韧性并非静态属性，而是由多元、异质的关系在特定时空下共同塑造的、持续展开的过程。

  • 对“超越人类”关系的认识深化： 作者指出，在第一次工作坊中，“超越人类”的关系并未被明确提出，这凸显了若无明确提示，思维易局限于人类领域。而第二次工作坊的“事件叙事”则成功激发了对这些关系的深入反思。
    More-than-human relationships were however elicited by event narratives during the second workshop (see Section 4.2), which allowed case study leads to enrich and deepen their joint reflections. The lack of reference to more-than-human relations here highlights the constraints of thinking beyond the human realm without the explicit prompt to think in more relational terms (as with an event narrative).
  • 宇宙观和身份认同在韧性中的作用： 强调了群体认同感（如“作为博拉纳人”）和宇宙观在强化关系、促成韧性中的重要性，这些维度常被发展部门的韧性项目所忽视。
    At the bottom of this spectrum are responses that specify dimensions of identity and cosmology, normally overlooked in resilience programming by the development sector (see also Semplici 2021).
  • 关系性价值观的重要性： 将身份认同和宇宙观的作用与“关系性价值观”联系起来，认为这种价值观强调万物互联，在管理、关怀和正义中扮演重要角色。
    The role of identity and cosmology in shaping people’s relationships and corresponding practices can be understood as relational values that emphasise interrelatedness of all beings and play an important role in stewardship, care and justice (Himes et al. 2024).

5. Discussion: learning relational resilience from pastoralism

本章对研究发现进行了深入的理论讨论和总结。首先，作者重申了可持续性科学中“关系转向”的必要性，并指出本文通过经验性地探索“关系性韧性”，为这一转向贡献了具体的方法论。研究发现，只有将韧性置于具体情境中，理解为日常实践、社会组织和文化过程等多元维度相互作用的复杂集合（assemblages），其讨论才有意义。这种情境化、关系化的视角，避免了普遍化定义的政治危险。其次，通过“事件分析”，研究团队得以将分析焦点从静态的“关系”和“过程”转向动态的“过程-关系性”（process-relationality），将研究对象（如营地、家庭）理解为不断“生成”的过程，从而更深刻地把握了韧性的复杂性和流动性。最后，文章指出，这种关系性认识论有助于融合系统思维与批判社会科学，能够更流畅地将结构性因素（如权力、政治经济背景）与日常实践联系起来，从而更清晰地揭示脆弱性的社会分异性和根源。文章呼吁未来的研究将关系性方法与政治本体论下的“关怀”（care）伦理相结合，以保护和促进多元的生存方式。

  • “关系转向”的理论与实践挑战： 指出尽管“关系转向”在概念上已被广泛接受，但开发能够解释主客体如何动态共构的经验性方法仍然是一个挑战。
    While some conceptual progress has been made, the development of empirical approaches that apply a relational epistemology to account for how subjects and objects are dynamically co-constituted remains a challenge (as exemplified in the debate between West et al. 2020; Raymond et al. 2021).
  • 情境化韧性的必要性： 强调只有在特定情境中讨论，韧性的操作化才能获得相关性并具有实际意义。
    In addition, during the first workshop we concluded that operationalizations of resilience only gain relevance or can be used to specify meaningful implications when discussed as situated within each specific context (see also Konaka 2023).
  • 韧性作为复杂集合（assemblages）： 韧性应被理解为多个维度（日常实践、社会文化、治理等）在复杂的集合中持续相互作用的产物。
    Rather, the emergent understanding that we reached emphasized that resilience could only be thought of in a meaningful way as occurring in relation to the constant interplay of all of these multiple dimensions that are all implicated as part of complex assemblages (Tozzi 2021).
  • 普遍化应用的政治危险： 再次强调，过度概括化的韧性应用在政治上是危险的，因为它没有指明应用的情境。
    Further, this also supports conclusions that overly generalized applications of resilience are politically dangerous when their context of application is not specified (Simon and Randalls 2016).
  • 关系性方法与脆弱性研究的结合： 指出关系性认识论可以更好地解释脆弱性（vulnerability）的社会分异性，因为它能将冲击和压力流畅地置于特定的社会、政治和经济情境中。
    For example, in conceptualizing vulnerability, which is usually considered to be closely related to resilience (Adger 2006), socially differentiated shocks and stressors could be more fluidly considered as situated in specific social, political, and economic contexts with a relational epistemology (Ribot 2017).
  • 关系性方法在游牧脆弱性研究中的应用： 引用已有研究证明，关系性方法可以阐明游牧民的脆弱性是如何在特定政治经济背景下被社会性地制造出来的，并涉及多重复合的社会、政治和经济压力。
    Applying a relational epistemology, as others have shown in studies of pastoral vulnerability, can clarify how vulnerabilities are socially differentiated, produced within specific political economic contexts, and often involve multiple compound social, political, and economic stressors that pastoralists face (Goldman and Riosmena 2013; Turner 2016; Unks et al. 2019), and help to clarify the ultimate causes of vulnerabilities.
  • 未来研究方向： 呼吁将关系性方法与政治本体论中的“关怀”（care）框架联系起来，并与关系性价值观的研究对齐，以中心化地保护多元的生存方式。
    The next step we ought to see in future research, in contrast to a responsibilizing resilience, is to link relational approaches to a political ontology framing of ‘care’ (Simon and Randalls 2016), aligning with recent work on relational values (Himes et al. 2024), enabling a centering of the preservation of relations that are important to plural ways of being (Escobar 2017) and resonates with similar framings of ‘care’ in pastoral development (Scoones 2023).
