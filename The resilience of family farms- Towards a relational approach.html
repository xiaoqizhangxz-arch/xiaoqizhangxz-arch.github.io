The resilience of family farms: Towards a relational approach

Ika Darnhofer, Claire Lamine, Agnes Strauss, Mireille Navarrete

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，当前理解家庭农场韧性的主流方法——无论是侧重于生物物理结构的“结构主义”视角，还是侧重于能动性的“社会行动者”视角——都因固守“结构/能动性”和“生态/社会”的二元对立而存在根本性局限。作者们主张，必须转向第三种视角，即“关系式方法”（relational approach）。该方法将“关系”本身视为本体论上的首要单位，而非预先存在的实体（农场、农民）。据此，韧性不再被视为农场或农民的静态属性，而是一个动态的、不断生成的“过程”（a process of becoming）。它是在由人类与非人类行动者共同构成的、异质性的关系网络中，通过持续的实践（enactment）而涌现出来的。这种视角能够更好地整合生态与社会动态，并真正将“变革”（尤其是转型性变革）置于分析的核心。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要建立在对现有社会生态系统（SES）韧性理论文献的批判性综合之上，并辅以来自奥地利和法国五个不同经验研究的说明性案例。这些案例涉及对有机和常规农场（包括奶牛、水果和蔬菜种植者）的访谈，它们并非用于严格的假说检验，而是作为具体例证，用以阐明三种不同理论视角（结构、行动者、关系）在解释农场实践时的洞察力与局限性。例如，案例展示了结构因素（如农场类型）如何影响变革的时间尺度，行动者的价值观如何超越结构限制，以及农民的“胡搞”（bricolage）式实验如何体现关系生成的动态过程。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证遵循一种清晰的“正-反-合”逻辑结构。首先，作者们构建并评述了第一个主流视角——“结构视角”，该视角关注可量化的系统属性（如多样性），并倾向于一种技术决定论的解释框架。接着，作者们引入了对立的第二个视角——“社会行动者视角”，该视角强调农民的能动性、价值观、学习网络以及对韧性话语中权力关系的批判。在指出这两个视角各自的优点和无法克服的二元论缺陷后，作者们提出了作为综合方案的第三个视角——“关系视角”。通过借鉴后结构主义（如德勒兹）和行动者网络理论（如拉图尔）的哲学资源，该视角将韧性重新概念化为在持续变化的关系流中不断被重新创造和维持的能力，从而超越了前两种视角的对立。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文的方法论本质上是理论建构与概念综合。作者通过对农业韧性研究文献的系统性梳理，识别并划分出两种主导性的分析范式。在此基础上，他们引入关系社会学和后结构主义哲学，提出了一种新的、更具整合性的理论框架。这种理论工作通过五个作者先前参与的、非直接相关的定性研究案例（主要使用深度访谈法）进行经验“锚定”和例证，使得抽象的理论概念（如“关系本体论”、“生成”）与具体的农场实践（如作物轮作、直接销售、实验性堆肥）联系起来。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

文中的案例研究组合来自法国东南部（阿代什省、德龙省）和奥地利（萨尔茨堡）的五个独立研究项目。具体案例包括：法国果农为应对行业危机而引入新作物或发展直销；奥地利奶农因个人价值观而拒绝看似理性的多元化经营（如农场度假）；法国菜农通过小规模实验逐步调整病虫害管理方式；以及奥地利高山牧场的韧性被阐释为生态（牧草、牛）、社会（传统、旅游）和物质（牛奶、奶酪、细菌）之间不断演变的关系网络。这些案例共同服务于一个目的：说明任何单一视角都无法完全捕捉农场韧性的复杂性，而关系视角则能更好地解释这些动态的、情境化的实践过程。

2. Resilience thinking

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章介绍了社会-生态韧性思想的核心概念。它首先澄清了韧性的多样化定义，并明确本文采纳C.S. Holling及其学派的定义。核心是“适应性循环”（adaptive cycle）模型，它描述了系统经历的四个阶段（开发、保育、释放、重组），并强调了变革的普遍性和非线性。韧性被定义为成功驾驭整个循环的能力，这不仅包括缓冲冲击和渐进适应，更关键的是包括经历崩溃和实现转型性变革的能力。最后，本章总结了韧性思维的两个主要贡献：挑战了以均衡和效率为核心的传统农业现代化范式，并强调了生态与社会过程之间不可分割的相互依赖性。

  • 指出韧性概念日益流行，但也因此涵盖了广泛的定义和内涵。
    “Resilience is a term that is increasingly popular, both in policy contexts and in scientific debates (Davidson, 2010; Walker and Cooper, 2011).”
  • 明确本文仅使用由C.S. Holling和Resilience Alliance发展的社会-生态韧性概念。
    “In this paper we only refer to the concept of social-ecological resilience, as defined by C.S. Holling and further developed within the Resilience Alliance. (see Brand and Jax, 2007; Walker and Cooper, 2011; Alexander, 2013)”
  • 引用Holling的早期论述，强调韧性管理方法承认无知和意外，并注重保持选择的多样性。
    “‘A management approach based on resilience (…) would emphasize the need to keep options open (…) and the need to emphasize heterogeneity. (…) not the assumption that future events are expected, but that they will be unexpected.’ (Holling, 1973: 21)”
  • 阐述了“适应性循环”模型及其四个阶段，作为捕捉非线性动态的启发式工具。
    “The ‘adaptive cycle’ is a heuristic model used to capture the non-linear dynamics of social-ecological systems, and to illustrate qualitatively different types of change (Gunderson and Holling, 2002; Burkhard et al., 2011).”
  • 指出系统需要驾驭两种不同性质的变化：长期稳定的渐进式增长和短期的重组式转型。
    “It needs to navigate relatively long phases characterised by steady growth and incremental change, where current strengths are exploited, but also turbulent phases of reorganisation and radical transformation, where new opportunities are explored (Scheffer and Westley, 2007; Fath et al., 2015).”
  • 将韧性分解为三种能力：缓冲（buffer）、适应（adapt）和转型（transform）。
    “To successfully navigate the adaptive cycle, a farm needs to be able to buffer shocks, to adapt and to transform (Darnhofer, 2014).”
  • 以从常规农业到有机农业的转变为例，说明转型性变革涉及价值观、社会网络和市场关系的根本性转变。
    “[...] for example, the conversion from conventional farming to organic farming can be such a transformation [...] linked to shifts in perceptions, values and goals of the family members, as well as shifts in the configuration of social networks and market relations (Lamine, 2011).”
  • 论述韧性思维挑战了以均衡、稳定为核心的农业现代化模式。
    “[...] thereby challenging approaches building on equilibrium, stability, predictability and efficiency, which are at the heart of the modernisation of agriculture (Weis, 2010).”
  • 强调韧性思维要求评估政策和策略是否会侵蚀农场面对不可预测事件的能力。
    “It also points to the need of assessing farm strategies and public policies regarding the extent to which they enhance or erode farm and rural resilience in the face of unpredictable events (Scott, 2013).”
  • 举例说明了过度追求经济效益可能以牺牲生态健康为代价，从而侵蚀农场的长期韧性。
    “[...] while economic indicators may look solid, these might build on unseen processes of fragilization, which undermine the resilience of the farm. (Knaus, 2009; Sundrum, 2015; EEA, 2015)”

3.1 Focusing on structures

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本节描述了第一种韧性研究视角：结构主义方法。该方法将韧性视为系统结构的内在属性，致力于寻找可测量的指标或“替代物”（surrogates）来评估韧性。在农业研究中，这通常表现为关注农场层面的多样性（如作物、收入来源）、灵活性和避免“命令-控制”式的管理模式。然而，该视角也存在局限性，其模型往往基于“理性经济人”的假设，对复杂的社会动态采取了技术化、去政治化的简化处理，并隐含着一种决定论倾向，即认为结构特征决定了农场的行为和韧性水平。

  • 指出韧性研究中的一个重要挑战是，许多关键方面无法直接观察，必须间接推断。
    “Indeed, early on Carpenter et al. (2001) noted that important aspects of resilience in social-ecological systems may not be directly observable, but must be inferred indirectly. They thus proposed to avoid the term ‘indicators’ of resilience and proposed ‘surrogates’ instead.”
  • 列举了一系列旨在从案例研究中提炼韧性替代指标的综合性研究。
    “For example Folke et al. (2003) identified four ‘critical factors’ ... Anderies et al. (2006) proposed ten ‘heuristics’ ... Walker et al. (2006) advanced 14 ‘propositions’ ... Cabell and Oelofse (2012) suggested 13 ‘behaviour-based indicators’ ... Biggs et al. (2012) put forward seven ‘generic policy-relevant principles’ ...”
  • 强调韧性原则（如多样性）并非普适，其有效性取决于具体情境和适应性循环的阶段。
    “Indeed, individual principles might point in opposing directions, depending e.g. on the context or the phase of the adaptive cycle (Holling, 2001; Cabell and Oelofse, 2012).”
  • 指出韧性与其替代指标之间的关系是多维且随时间变化的，因此普适性原则难以建立。
    “In other words, whatever principles or surrogates are identified, they are unlikely to be universal, and therefore they require a nuanced understanding of how, when, and where they apply... (Biggs et al., 2012; Carpenter et al., 2005).”
  • 列举了在区域尺度上应用结构主义视角研究农业韧性的多个案例。
    “...e.g. the impact of irrigated agriculture on the water table in Australia (Walker et al., 2009); the impact of river management strategies ... in Hungary (Sendzimir et al., 2008); the impact of management choices on ... soil organic matter ... in the Netherlands (van Apeldoorn et al., 2011); the exploration of alternative regimes ... in coffee production (Vandermeer and Perfecto, 2012); or the extent to which rural livelihood strategies exhibit dynamics observed in ecological systems ... (Tittonell, 2014).”
  • 指出农场层面的研究多关注灵活性，并视其为适应能力的关键。
    “At farm level, empirical studies have focused mostly on the structures that enable flexibility, which is seen as key to the ability of farms to adapt over time (Darnhofer et al., 2010).”
  • 论述了多样性作为增强灵活性的关键策略，并引用了相关研究。
    “Flexibility is often seen as being enabled by diversity, in its various dimensions (see Stirling, 2007). At the farm level the impact of a diversity of activities ... have received attention (see e.g. Dedieu et al., 2008; Darnhofer, 2010; Astigarraga and Ingrand, 2011).”
  • 引用法国的研究案例说明，多元化经营虽然能增强韧性，但受到劳动力等结构性因素的制约。
    “However, surveys within the French case studies showed that the decision to diversify is highly dependent on the available labour force... (Navarrete et al., 2015; Dupre et al., 2015; Lamine, 2012).”
  • 引用奥地利的研究案例进一步指出多元化带来了协调工作的额外劳动负荷。
    “The challenges linked to diversification were also highlighted in the Austrian study, where interviewed farmers pointed out that the need to coordinate between the diverse activities increases the labour load even further (Darnhofer and Strauss, 2014).”
  • 总结道，多元化策略的适用性依赖于农场规模、销售渠道和劳动力等结构因素。
    “Thus, while diversification is a broad strategy to strengthen resilience, its relevance and applicability depends on structural factors such as farm size, marketing channels and labour availability (Lamine and Navarrete, 2015).”
  • 引用“命令-控制”病理学的概念，指出过度追求效率和控制会降低系统韧性。
    “Another resilience principle is to avoid the pathology of ‘command and control’, i.e. a one-sided focus on controlling the system to ensure efficiency ..., as such systems become isolated from their context and inflexible (Holling and Meffe, 1996).”
  • 以法国菜农为例，说明通过促进天敌、构建内部养分循环等方式可以利用自然过程，而非仅依赖化学投入品。
    “Indeed, natural regulations and balances ... may take several years to establish and will shift over time. (Coquil et al., 2014)”
  • 指出模型在告知参与式过程或展示个体行动与系统动态的相互影响方面具有价值。
    “Moreover, these models can be valuable to inform participatory processes (see e.g. Etienne, 2011). Furthermore, models that integrate both ecological and social variables can show how individual actions contribute to the dynamics of the system, and how the system influences farmers' actions (see e.g. Feola and Binder, 2010).”
  • 批判该视角下的经济模型隐含了理性决策者的规范性假设。
    “Farmers and regional actors are seen as having fixed preferences and clearly defined goals, and as choosing means suitable for realizing them.”
  • 指出经验研究表明农民行为并非总是“理性”的。
    “While empirical studies show that this is not necessarily the case (e.g. Walker et al., 2009; Sendzimir et al., 2008), it is usually seen as a regrettable deviation from what ‘should’ be done...”
  • 批评这种技术-还原论框架可能忽视了更复杂的知识、价值和意义网络。
    “By shifting from a descriptive to a normative approach, and by drawing upon unchallenged assumptions about the social world, studies within this perspective can effectively impose a technical-reductionist framework upon more complex webs of knowledge, values and meaning (Cote and Nightingale, 2012; Weichselgartner and Kelman, 2015).”

3.2 Focusing on social actors

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本节阐述了第二种韧性研究视角：社会行动者方法。该视角是对结构决定论的回应，将分析中心从生物物理结构转向社会行动者的能动性（agency）。它强调农民并非被动地对外部冲击做出反应，而是主动的变革推动者。农民的价值观、主观认知和“耕作风格”会调节结构性因素的影响，导致在相似的结构条件下出现多样化的实践和发展轨迹。此外，该视角关注农民通过实验和网络进行的体验式学习，并引入了批判性社会科学的视角，探讨了韧性话语中的权力不对等、社会公正问题，以及韧性概念被新自由主义收编的风险。其局限在于，它在强调社会建构的同时，往往对生态动态的理论化不足，且对解释根本性的转型变革能力有限。

  • 引用文献对“能动性”概念的不同理解进行了说明。
    “In expressing his agency, the farmer is constrained—but not determined—by the structure of the farm or its context. (Emirbayer and Mische, 1998)”
  • 以奥地利奶农为例，说明个人价值观和自我认同会超越结构性的经济机会，影响其经营决策。
    “They felt that they are not ‘the right type of person’ for direct marketing, or do not want to have guests around the farm the whole day (Strauss, 2015).”
  • 引用“耕作风格”概念，解释为何相似结构下的农场会产生显著差异。
    “[...] farms can differ significantly in their activities and how they implement these activities, i.e. their style of farming (van der Ploeg, 1994).”
  • 通过法国的研究案例指出，即使是同质性很高的一组农民，其长期发展轨迹也可能因经营理念不同而分化。
    “Other farmers, with similar structural conditions at the outset, entered a process of relative specialization to make it more “liveable”. (Lamine, 2012)”
  • 指出社会学习能力是影响社会-生态系统韧性的关键因素。
    “As Davoudi et al. (2013) have suggested, social-ecological systems can ‘become more or less resilient depending on their social learning capacity’ (Davoudi et al., 2013: 311, emphasis in original).”
  • 引用文献说明农民作为实验者的角色，他们通过实验来识别和解决问题。
    “Indeed, farmers are well known to experiment (see Raedeke and Rikoon, 1997; Leitgeb et al., 2012; Meynard et al., 2012).”
  • 通过奥地利和法国的案例，具体展示了农民在牲畜医药、食品加工和病虫害管理等方面的个人实验。
    “In the Austrian study, farmers not only experimented with alternative methods to medicate livestock (e.g. homeopathy, herbal medicine), but also tested new possible activities such as food processing or traditional handicrafts (Darnhofer and Strauss, 2015). (Lamine and Navarrete, 2015)”
  • 强调了农民网络在共享设备、协同工作、信息交流和解读外部环境变化中的重要作用。
    “By cultivating networks and the ‘strategic conversations’ taking place in them, various aspects of observed changes in (in)formal institutions can be better understood... (Balducci et al., 2011; Berkes and Berkes, 2009; Lamine, 2012).”
  • 列举了一系列从批判社会科学视角审视韧性概念的文献。
    “Indeed, there is a burgeoning engagement by critical social scientists with the implications of various understandings of resilience (e.g., Leach, 2008; Davidson, 2010; Cote and Nightingale, 2012; Reghezza-Zitt et al., 2012; Hatt, 2013; Scott, 2013; Fabinyi et al., 2014).”
  • 批评韧性概念常被用于一种自上而下、技术理性的政策路径。
    “As MacKinnon and Derickson (2012) point out, while at the outset resilience was a descriptive concept, state agencies and expert knowledge often normatively define how resilience is to be achieved.”
  • 指出主流政策和研究结构偏好技术解决方案（如精准农业、生物技术），而忽视了基于农民知识和农业生态学的方法。
    “This techno-rationality implies consensus rather than recognize the value of diversity...and it leaves little room for participatory approaches, for farmer knowledge, or for approaches building on agroecological practices (Vanloqueren and Baret, 2009; Leach et al., 2012; Levidow, 2015).”
  • 批判韧性话语的“去政治化”立场，认为这会强化现存的不平等权力结构。
    “Moreover, the apolitical stance in much of the resilience literature implicitly reinforces the status quo by privileging established social structures, which are often shaped by unequal power relations, injustice and marginalization (Leach, 2008; Cote and Nightingale, 2012).”
  • 指出有影响力的精英和企业集团倾向于推动旨在维持现状的渐进式变革，而非根本性的转型。
    “Indeed, influential elites and powerful corporations have little interest in transforming established systems from which they benefit. (Smith and Stirling, 2010; Beilin et al., 2012; Davoudi et al., 2013; Brown, 2014)”
  • 论述了韧性思维如何与新自由主义逻辑相契合，即将应对风险的责任个体化。
    “Finally, by highlighting how much individual farms can do to strengthen their resilience, resilience thinking aligns well with neoliberalism (Joseph, 2013; Zebrowski, 2013).”
  • 引用澳大利亚的案例说明，政策可能通过削弱农村支持体系等方式，实际侵蚀了农场的韧性。
    “As examples from Australia show, policies might well erode the resilience of farms by eroding rural support structures ... and by reinforcing maladaptive responses (Beilin et al., 2012; Sysak, 2013; Sinclair, 2014).”
  • 批判行动者视角未能充分理论化生态动态，往往视之为静态背景。
    “Yet, ecologists have pointed out that, especially close to a threshold, ecological change can be both rapid and radical (Scheffer and Carpenter, 2003).”

3.3 Focusing on relations

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本节详细阐述了作者提出的第三种视角：关系式方法。该方法源于社会学和地理学的“关系转向”，其核心是本体论的转变：不再将世界视为由预先存在的、独立的实体构成，而是由动态、展开的关系构成。在此视角下，“耕作”（farming）本身被理解为一个德勒兹式的“生成”（becoming）过程，一个在不断变化的关系中被持续塑造的稳定化瞬间。因此，韧性不是一种可被持有的属性，而是从不断被实践、被重构的物质-社会关系模式中涌现的结果。该视角特别强调农民实验作为一种“胡搞”（bricolage）的重要性，它并非为了解决明确问题，而是探索关系可能性的开放式过程。最后，通过引入行动者网络理论（ANT）和能动实在论（agential realism）等思想，该视角实现了对社会与生态、人类与非人类行动者的对称性处理，从而真正整合了社会-生态系统研究的两个维度。

  • 指出关系式方法可以克服行动者/活动、结构/能动性、社会/生态等二元对立。
    “A relational perspective on the resilience of farms can contribute to overcoming the conceptual distinction between the actor and his activity, between structure and agency, between the social and the ecological...”
  • 阐明关系理论的核心，即拒绝将离散的、预先给定的单元作为分析起点。
    “Indeed, relational theorists reject the notion that there are discrete, pre-given units that can be used as starting point of analysis (Emirbayer, 1997).”
  • 引用Chia的观点，说明农场只是持续生成过程中的一个稳定化瞬间。
    “As such a farm can only be understood in terms of the relations in which it is entangled: the farm as it is now, is but a stabilized moment in a process of continual becoming (see Chia, 1999).”
  • 建议使用动词“farming”（耕作）代替名词“farm”（农场），以强调关系和动态。
    “To focus on relations, to convey the interdependency of farm, farmer and context, it seems helpful to refer to ‘farming’. By using a verb rather than a noun, the emphasis is on relations and dynamics, rather than on separateness and stability (see Elias, 1978).”
  • 借用德勒兹和瓜塔里的“生成”（becoming）概念，来说明主体是通过与环境的互动来定义的。
    “Becoming—as opposed to being—is used to convey the idea that more than by stable qualities, human beings are characterized by their interactions with the multiples objects and beings in their environment (see e.g. Deleuze and Guattari, 1987; Deleuze and Parnet, 1996).”
  • 强调关系总是在生成之中，充满了“别样生成”（become otherwise）的可能性。
    “This perspective emphasizes change and transformability, through highlighting the pervasive potential of relations to become otherwise (Balducci et al., 2011; Allen, 2012).”
  • 再次引用Chia的观点，论述“耕作”总是充满可能性，可以被赋予新的意义和应用。
    “Depending on the relations of which it is part, farming is ‘always loaded with possibilities and can be continually enriched with newer and novel meanings, understandings and applications’ (Chia, 1999:220).”
  • 将韧性重新表述为关系思维的产物，它不是一个“事物”，而是关系模式的涌现结果。
    “This leads to a reformulation of farming resilience in terms of relational thinking (see Davoudi, 2012). It reemphasizes that resilience is not a ‘thing’ that can be seized, held or measured, it is not an attribute or property of a farm or a farmer.”
  • 以打牌游戏为喻，说明韧性如同牌局的成功，取决于多种动态关系的互动，而非单个行动或预设策略。
    “Building on Elias (1978), farming resilience can be seen as akin to playing a game of cards (Fig. 4).”
  • 将农民实验与追求明确目标的理性推理和工程学方法区分开。
    “This highlights that farmers’ approach to experimenting is different from both engineering and from rational reasoning which seeks the most effective way to achieve a clearly defined goal (Duymedjian and Rüling, 2010; Johnson, 2012).”
  • 将农民的实验比作“bricolage”（胡搞/修补术），一个开放的、组合异质元素以创造可行方案的持续过程。
    “Indeed, Farmers’ experiments is more akin to ‘bricolage’ (see Innes and Booher, 1999; Cleaver, 2002).”
  • 将农民实验视为一种探测未来的思辨性认知方法。
    “Experiments that farmers engage in—individually or collectively—are designed to probe the future, to test new potential combinations... (see Balducci et al., 22011; Hillier, 2011).”
  • 指出实验不仅是物质操作，也推动了对旧有见解和新兴趋势的重新框架化。
    “The experiments feed the imagination of different ways to farm, as much as they are fed by these imaginations. They introduce new ways of framing old insights, as much as new ways of making sense of emerging trends (see Kaplan and Orlikowski, 2013).”
  • 强调关系视角下，变化是持续发生的，即便是看似微小的调整也在不断重塑关系。
    “Change always has ramifications and implications beyond those initially imagined; plans, when they are translated into action, are always modified and adapted; actors improvise, fine-tune and adjust (Tsoukas and Chia, 2002).”
  • 引用Barad的“能动实在论”和Carolan & Stuart的“关系实在论”作为克服自然/文化二元论的理论资源。
    “In the context of social-ecological resilience, relational approaches, building on e.g. ‘agential realism’ by Barad (2007) or ‘relational realism’ by Carolan and Stuart (2016) can contribute to overcoming the divisions between nature and culture.”
  • 引入行动者网络理论(ANT)，指出其将能动性扩展到人与非人的异质性组合中。
    “Similarly, in Actor-Network-Theory, agency is extended to include heterogeneous associations of human and nonhuman ‘actants’ (Latour, 2005).”
  • 指出承认非人类的能动性为探索农业适应能力开辟了新途径。
    “Recognizing that nonhumans (e.g., crops, technology, climate) are not mere passive objects ... opens new ways to explore how ‘vibrant’ matter can shape the manner in which the adaptive capacity of farming is expressed (Dwiartama and Rosin, 2014).”

4. Conclusion: integrating insights on farming resilience

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本结论部分系统地回顾了全文的论证。它重申了结构视角和行动者视角的贡献与局限，指出二者虽互补，但难以真正整合，且都倾向于对韧性进行静态评估，未能充分解释转型性变革。作者们最后强调，他们提出的关系式方法通过将关系置于本体论的核心，成功地整合了社会与生态维度，并将分析焦点从静态的“是什么”转移到动态的“如何生成”上。这种方法不仅关注特定农场内部的关系，也关注更广泛背景下（如“生产主义”）的深层“关系性”（relationalities）。最终，关系视角将韧性理解为一个需要通过培育多样化关系来不断实践和重演的过程，从而为理解和促进农业系统的适应性与转型能力提供了更强大、更均衡的理论工具。

  • 总结了结构主义和行动者视角的贡献，但指出其整合困难，且对韧性的理解偏于静态。
    “The insights on resilience generated by the two perspectives are complementary, but their integration has remained difficult, despite the explicit aim of social-ecological resilience to understand the interdependency of social and ecological dynamics. Moreover, both the structural and the actor perspective focus on what a farm is or what a farmer does, to explain what makes a farm resilient (or not). Through focusing on assessing states, the perspectives promote a somewhat static understanding of resilience.”
  • 强调关系式方法将分析焦点从实体转向关系，从而实现了对社会和生态维度的更强整合。
    “We argue that a relational approach, which conceptualizes the relations (rather than entities) as foundational, allows a stronger integration of the social and ecological aspects that strengthen or weaken the resilience of family farms.”
  • 指出关系式方法将注意力从（看似）稳定的状态转向变革被实践的过程，从而更好地解释动态变化。
    “Indeed, a relational approach highlights that patterns of relations are always changing, shifting the attention away from the analysis of (seemingly) stable states towards the process through which incremental and transformational changes are enacted.”
  • 将韧性最终定义为一个需要通过培育多样异质关系来不断重演和实践的过程。
    “This focus on relations shifts the understanding of resilience from being an attribute, to being a process that needs to be constantly re-enacted and performed through nurturing diverse and heterogeneous relations.”
  • 结论认为，关系式方法为韧性思维提供了一个更均衡的视角，同时覆盖了路径依赖（缓冲冲击）和路径创造（转型变革）。
    “A relational perspective also contributes towards a more balanced approach to resilience thinking, covering how farming can buffer shocks and ‘bounce back’ as well as how farming can be transformed and ‘bounce forward’, i.e. covering both path dependency and path creation.”
