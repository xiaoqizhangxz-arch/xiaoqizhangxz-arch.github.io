How do coffee farmers engage with digital technologies? A capabilities perspective

Francisco Hidalgo, Athena Birkenberg, Thomas Daum, Christine Bosch & Xiomara F. Quiñones‑Ruiz

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，哥伦比亚咖啡小农与数字技术的互动并非一个简单的“采纳”或“拒绝”过程，而是一个深刻嵌入其“关系性”世界观的复杂社会协商过程。这种本土本体论（local ontology）强调具身经验、与土地的直接联系、以及家庭和社区的凝聚力，它与主流数字农业所倡导的技术中心、生产主义的想象（techno-centric, productivist imaginary）存在根本性冲突。作者运用阿马蒂亚·森的“能力アプローチ”（Capabilities Approach），主张技术只有在能够扩展农民珍视的“可行能力”（functionings）——即他们有理由珍视的存在和行动方式（如维系家庭纽带、保持农场自主性）——时，才会被有意义地整合进其“机会结构”（opportunity structure），而不是去被动迎合外部强加的、普遍主义的发展模式。因此，研究揭示了一种独特的、自下而上的“日常数字化”（everyday digitalization）与“日常非数字化”（everyday non-digitalization）并存的现实。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据主要来源于对哥伦比亚两个咖啡种植社区的73个农户进行的定性、半结构化访谈。作者通过丰富的叙事数据，提炼出三个核心主题来支撑其论点：1）关系性的耕作方式（a relational way of farming），揭示了农民如何将基于直接经验和与土地“对话”的具身知识置于数字媒介之上；2）（失）连接的机器（(dis)connected machines），展现了农民基于过去制度性失败（如FNC推广的采摘机）和对咖啡生产技艺的理解，对机器人等自动化技术抱持的普遍怀疑；3）被滋养的家庭与社区（nurtured families and communities），则有力地证明了当智能手机和WhatsApp等技术服务于维系社会关系这一核心价值时，它们是如何被积极地选择、变通和整合进日常生活之中的。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑首先从批判数字农业研究中普遍存在的“现实-设计鸿沟”（reality-design gap）入手，指出其根源在于技术中心主义的霸权。随后，引入“能力アプローチ”作为核心理论框架，旨在超越传统的“技术采纳”分析范式，转向以人为中心、关注农民能动性的视角。接着，文章通过三个实证主题，系统地展示了农民的“可行能力”如何塑造其技术选择：对某些技术的排斥是因为它们威胁到“成为一个好农民”的核心身份认同和实践，而对另一些技术的接纳则是因为它们显著扩展了与家庭、社区相关的社会交往能力。通过对比自下而上的“日常数字化”（如WhatsApp社群）与自上而下的“聚光灯式”项目（如FNC的失败案例）的迥异命运，文章有力地论证了技术的意义和功用是在与地方社会、生态及制度环境的互动中被共同建构的，而非技术本身所固有的。

4. 方法 (Method)

本研究采用以人为中心的定性研究方法。核心数据收集手段是在哥伦比亚两个咖啡产区，以“家庭”为单位，对73个农户进行了半结构化访谈。这种方法论选择，意在捕捉小农经济中决策制定的复杂家庭动态。数据分析则运用了扎根理论中的归纳编码（inductive coding）以及显性和隐性内容分析，从访谈文本中提炼出贯穿全文的三个核心主题。这一方法确保了研究结论是紧密植根于农民自身的生活经验和话语体系之中的。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

核心案例是哥伦比亚的两个小农咖啡社区（位于Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta和中西部安第斯地区）。贯穿全文的一个关键反面案例是哥伦比亚国家咖啡种植者联合会（FNC）推广机械咖啡采摘机的失败项目。这个案例精准地诠释了“现实-设计鸿沟”：一个自上而下的技术方案，因完全忽视了当地陡峭的地形（生物物理环境）和高品质咖啡采摘所需的精细技艺（农民的具身知识），最终被农民拒绝，并深刻影响了他们对未来自动化技术的负面认知。与之相对，农民自发利用WhatsApp建立社群网络以协调劳动、分享信息，则成为一个展示技术如何成功融入地方社会结构、扩展集体能力的正面案例。

第二部分：Capabilities approach, agency, and farmers’ interactions with technologies 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章是论文的理论核心，详细阐述了阿马蒂亚·森的能力アプローチ（Capabilities Approach）如何为理解农民与技术的互动提供一个以人为中心的框架。作者首先定义了森的核心概念：“可行能力（functionings）”（人们珍视的存在或行动状态）和“能力（capabilities）”（一个人实际能实现的可行能力的集合）。进而，技术被重新定义为一种“资产”，其价值不在于其本身，而在于它能否扩展人们的能力。这一视角将分析焦点从技术本身转向农民的能动性。然而，这种能动性并非无限的，它受到“机会结构”（opportunity structure）——即社会、政治、经济和物质环境——的制约和塑造。最后，文章引入关系性社会学视角，强调农民的能动性是在与人类（同伴、开发者）和非人类（土地、作物、机器）能动者的持续“协商”与“修补”（tinkering）中共同建构的。

  • 阐述森的发展定义，即人们过上他们所珍视的生活的自由，这与正统的经济增长观念形成对比。
    Sen (1999) defines development as the freedom that people have, to live the lives that they value. In contrast to more orthodox notions that make development a convention comparable to economic growth, this perspective focuses on the expansion of people’s agency to pursue their own goals in life as the basis for human development (Kleine et al. 2012; Zheng et al. 2018; Jiménez and Zheng 2018; Poveda and Roberts 2018).
  • 解释“可行能力”（functionings）和“能力”（capabilities）是森理论的核心。
    Central to Sen’s conceptualization of development are the concepts of functionings and capabilities (Kleine et al. 2012). Functionings refers to the various things a person can value doing or being in life, while capabilities are the functionings that a person can actually achieve (Sen 1999).
  • 引用Carolan的观点，资源本身没有内在价值，其价值在于能被用来产生有价值的成果。
    Following the work of Sen, Carolan (2018) expressed that goods, services, and rights have no intrinsic value; it is the possibility of using these resources to produce valued outcomes that transforms them into assets.
  • 将技术视为扩展人类能力的工具，这促使技术研究从技术中心转向以人为中心。
    The idea that technologies can be perceived as “enablers to the pre-existing capabilities of human beings” (Banerjee 2013 p. 79) alings with this value of technologies as a tool for the expansion of human-development. A consequence of this idea is the transition from a technological to a human-centered perspective in technology studies (Madon 2004).
  • 强调技术挪用是一个主动的选择、拒绝和适应过程，与社会建构论的技术观相呼应。
    This understanding links the capabilities approach with the idea that technologies are socially constructed (Bijker 1995; Hughes 1987; Orlikowski 1992).
  • 引用Cash等人的比喻，说明新技术不是“成衣”，而是被“缝合”到用户的社会结构和既有实践中。
    Cash et al. (2006) argue that “new technologies are not adopted as if they were ready-to-wear fashion but rather sewn, in bits and pieces, into the fabric of the users’ social setting and existing practices” (p. 474).
  • 引入“机会结构”概念，说明个人能力受制于社会、政治和经济机会。
    Capabilities and personal agency, however, are not solely the result of the intrinsic capacities of the individual (Crocker 2008), but rather are entitlements made possible or constrained "by the social, political and economic opportunities available to us" (Sen 1999 p. xi-xii). The set of these opportunities is described by Alsop and Heinsohn (2005) as the 'opportunity structure'...
  • 指出社会结构（政策、市场、科研）在驱动社会-技术路径方面发挥着关键作用。
    Social structures, including policy-making, industry, markets, and scientific production, the assemblages of these structures, along with the institutions that they generate, play a crucial role in driving socio-technical pathways... (Klerkx and Rose 2020; Pigford et al. 2018).
  • 强调农民的能动性并非绝对，它是在与不可控的生物物理环境（天气、作物、机器）的持续“协商”中形成的。
    The agency of farmers is not absolute, as it is constantly shaped by material relationships established with technological and biophysical entities and contexts. It is well known that farmers continuously have to 'negotiate' with and work around uncontrollable weather, unruly plants, diseases, breakable machines, and financial constraints (Darnhofer 2020).
  • 运用“修补”（tinkering）概念来理解农民在技术挪用中的能动性操作，这既是对技术和生物物理约束的回应，也是一种关怀农场的实践。
    The concept of 'tinkering' as it is used by Higgins et al. (2017), is useful to understand the operation of agency in technological appropriation on farms.
  • 讨论了“数字鸿沟”如何通过不平等的连接、能源和设备等途径，塑造农民的能动性并导致排斥。
    In the context of digitalization, asymmetrical access to connectivity, energy sources, equipment and devices, and information, which has strong rural dimensions (Pfeizer et al. 2020), favor processes of hierarchization and exclusion, commonly referred to as the 'digital gap'.
  • 引入关系性农村社会学，将农场本身视为具有能动性和情感的存在，影响着农民与技术的互动。
    Relational approaches in rural sociology call for understanding farms as entities with agency and affectivity (Darnhofer 2020). These attributes influence the way farmers engage with technologies responding to the agency of the farm.
  • 指出数字化过程正在重塑农村研究中的能动性概念，算法和智能设备成为影响人类和非人类行动者的新力量。
    Moreover, the process of digitalization is reframing notions of agency in rural studies. Algorithms, smart devices, and automated machines, act in material and regulative ways in the cyber, physical, and social spheres, affecting the agency of human and other non-human agents (Lioutas et al. 2019; Rijswijk et al. 2021).

第三部分：Results 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章呈现了研究的核心发现，通过三个相互关联的主题来阐述哥伦比亚咖啡农户如何与数字技术进行互动。第一个主题“关系性的耕作方式”揭示了农民的农业实践深深植根于与土地、作物和动物的互惠、非等级关系中。这种基于直接经验和具身知识的本体论，使得智能手机等数字设备在田间被视为不兼容、脆弱和具有干扰性的外来物。第二个主题“（失）连接的机器”探讨了农民对机器人等新兴技术的看法。这种看法很大程度上受到制度性失败（FNC推广采摘机的案例）和现有关系性世界观的共同塑造，导致他们普遍怀疑机器能否胜任咖啡种植中精细、需“用心”的技艺，并认为机器会破坏农场的自然和谐。第三个主题“被滋养的家庭与社区”则展示了硬币的另一面：当数字技术能够服务于维系家庭和社区纽带这一核心价值时，它们就被积极地整合到日常生活中。农民会努力克服经济和基础设施障碍，使用手机和社交网络来加强社会联系和支持子女教育，同时，他们也对自动化可能破坏社区劳动力关系保持警惕。

  • 描述了农民将劳动视为与农场各元素（土壤、水、植物、动物）之间互惠、非等级的关系。这种关系性是他们通过直接对话和“协商”向土地学习并实现目标的基础。
    Respondents’ descriptions of work and life on the farms largely speak about the practice of a relational way of farming. Labor is described as a process of reciprocal, non-hierarchical, relationships with the different elements of the farm, such as soil, water, plants, and animals.
  • 指出数字技术（手机、电脑等）被认为不属于农业实践领域，与田间工作不兼容，因此不被视为其农业机会结构中的重要组成部分。
    Technologies with which they are familiar, such as cellphones, smartphones, computers, and tablets, are perceived to belong to a different sphere than farming practice, incompatible and non-applicable in the fieldwork.
  • 强调了智能手机等设备的物理属性（脆弱）与田间恶劣、不可控的工作条件之间的矛盾。
    A smartphone is very good, but is very fragile, and you are very dedicated to the work, smeared with so many things. What is one going to do with it? So, one doesn’t use it, one working cannot use that (F17)
  • 提出一种有趣的对立关系：农民因其粗糙的双手和体力劳动而被“塑造”得不适合操作“脆弱”的数字设备。
    What happens is that I am very manual at work. I keep my hands very stiff due to rustic and manual work, so the digitization is not so fast for me, I am trained for something else, I have very heavy hands (F6).
  • 指出农民对机器人和无人机等新兴技术的想象受到科幻电影和FNC失败的咖啡收获机械化试点项目的影响。
    Second, a pilot project for the mechanization of coffee harvest in Colombia, carried out by the FNC as part of their broader innovation program. These elements intertwined with the ontology of the relational way of farming, have resulted in particular perceptions and attitudes towards these technologies. (Citing Noticias Caracol 2018 and FNC 2020)
  • 揭示了农民对机器人技术的不信任源于其认为高质量咖啡生产所需的特殊“诀窍”、细致和情感是机器无法复制的。
    For most interviewees, producing coffee requires a particular 'know-how.' That is, particular skills, meticulousness, and sensitivity, even “passion” or “love” as some respondents expressed...these abilities cannot be developed by robots because they are associated with experiential knowledge...
  • 指出农民认为机器人不适合在咖啡种植区的山区地形工作。
    Interviewees stated it clearly: robots are not suitable for working on the mountainous terrain that characterizes the coffee plots.
  • 强调在关系性耕作的本体论下，农场的生物和非生物元素被赋予了感知能力，机器的噪音和存在被认为会“压迫”植物，损害其福祉。
    For example, machines produce noise, I believe that vegetable matter is incompatible with noise, the wild system is better. To add a strange element to live with them, such as mechanization, I don't think it’s favorable. (F34)
  • 揭示了家庭是农民生活中的核心，他们的主要目标是维系家庭团聚、合作与进步。
    Exploring the elements that farmers find valuable in life revealed the centrality of family. The main goals are to ensure that the family remains together, promote collaboration between its members, progress and thrive...
  • 指出手机和智能手机已成为维持家庭成员联系的重要工具，并深度融入日常生活策略。
    Technologies such as mobile phones and smartphones have become important tools for communication and for maintaining connections between family members. These technologies are deeply integrated into people’s routines and life strategies.
  • 强调为子女提供教育是农民“进步”观念背后的一个基本要素，即便这意味着子女将离开农村前往城市。
    A fundamental element behind the ideas of progress and thriving is facilitating children’s access to education. Paradoxically, although the respondents recognize the values of rural life and the practice of agriculture, they simultaneously act in a permanent search to provide their children with education that will help them migrate and find work in the cities.
  • 引用案例说明，当技术与人们珍视的生活目标（如子女教育）相符时，它会获得特殊意义，从而促进技术挪用，农民会为此购买电脑并努力克服网络信号不佳等困难。
    “I want my daughters to progress, allow them to study, so that they can defend themselves. That’s the best inheritance. Each one has a computer; I bought it for them.” (F42)
  • 描述了家庭之外，维系社区联系是另一个基本目标，通信技术被视为增强社会凝聚力和合作的重要工具。
    Beyond the family sphere, households are immersed in the community, and maintaining this connection is another fundamental goal for the interviewees...communication technologies are considered relevant tools for social cohesion and cooperation at the community level (collective functionings).
  • 突出农民对机器人取代劳动力的社会风险的担忧，认为这会威胁到许多依赖农业为生的家庭，因此即使对个人有利也不愿采用。
    I would not like to have machines here replacing the workers because there are many people who depend on the work...For me it’s not viable (F27).

第四部分：Discussion & Conclusion 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

在讨论部分，作者将实证发现与更广泛的学术文献进行对话，深化了其核心论点。文章首先对比了农民自下而上的“日常数字化”（everyday digitalization）与主流话语中自上而下的“聚光灯式数字化”（spotlight digitalization），指出前者是混乱、情境化且不断协商的，而后者则呈现出一种虚假的稳定性和普遍性。接着，作者论证了研究中发现的关系性本体论如何挑战了主流数字农业背后隐含的理性-资本主义逻辑，后者往往忽视了如自主、关怀和社区凝聚力等非经济价值。这种关系性本体论也解释了为何农民对自动化技术持怀疑态度，因为它威胁到了“好农民”的具身技艺和身份认同。最后，文章强调了农民在结构性制约（如数字鸿沟）下，为实现其核心“可行能力”（如维系社会关系）而展现出的能动性和“修补”策略。在结论部分，作者重申了本文的两大贡献：一是在批判性数字农业研究中应用能力アプローチ，以挑战技术普及的普遍主义谬误；二是为全球南方的数字化进程提供了宝贵的经验案例。文章最终呼吁，未来的数字农业创新应摒弃单一、僵化的范式，转而拥抱地方发展路径和价值观的多样性，从而真正弥合“现实-设计鸿沟”。

  • 提出农业实践是一个关系性过程，农民为了过上他们认为有价值的生活，不断在制度、技术和生物物理环境中航行。
    Farming, as described in the literature (Carolan 2016; Gosnell et al. 2019; Higgins et al. 2017; Layman and Civita 2022; Darnhofer 2020) and evidenced in this study, is a relational process.
  • 引用拉图尔的观点，认为地方性的技术互动是人类与非人类行动者之间局部、短暂的联合，这与主流社会技术项目的稳定性形成对比。
    ...local interactions with technologies result from what Latour (2005) described as localized and short-lived associations of human and non-human actors.
  • 区分了“日常数字化”与“聚光灯式数字化”，并认为前者与主流叙事截然不同，且存在一种“日常非数字化”的张力。
    It is the “everyday digitalization,” which differs greatly from the big narratives or “spotlight digitalization” (Forney and Dwiartama 2023). We contend the co-occurrence of an 'everyday non-digitalization' resulting from the tension between these contrasting visions.
  • 指出研究中描述的“可行能力”挑战了主流数字化想象背后的理性-资本主义逻辑，后者强调个体主义和竞争，而农民更看重集体目标和相互依赖。
    The functionings described by the communities in this study account for components that, at times complement, and in some cases challenge, rational-capitalist logics commonly associated with mainstream digitalization imaginaries and projects (Bronson 2019; Hütten 2019; Rotz et al. 2019b; Birch et al. 2020).
  • 对比了农业科技公司将农场想象为“无人化景观”与本研究中农民害怕这种场景的鲜明反差。
    Therefore, while influential agri-tech corporations tend to imagine peopleless landscapes populated by smart machines and artifacts that liberate farmers from the 'burden' of agricultural labor (Bronson 2018; Baur and Iles 2023), the respondents in this study are precisely afraid of this scenario.
  • 引用文献证明农民普遍怀疑机器人能否像一个好农民那样去诠释农场元素、提供情境化信息或恰当地对待动植物。
    It has been reported that farmers are skeptical about robots having the capacity to interpret the elements of the farm as a good farmer can... (Kaler and Ruston 2019; Rose et al. 2018). Layman and Civita (2022), for example, reported feelings of guilt and empathy experienced by relational farmers...
  • 指出关系性农业的核心是与同伴和更广泛社区建立互惠关系，这需要信任和关怀他人。
    As has been expressed by Layman and Civita (2022), core to relational farming is the establishment of reciprocal relations with peers and the broader community. For Trivette (2017), relational farming has a lot to do with trust and caring for others.
  • 强调数字化作为加剧社会不平等的空间，贫困农民因成本问题更难接入网络和设备，体现了普遍的数字鸿沟。
    This condition echoes propositions of digitalization as a space for broadening social inequities (Abdulai 2022; Fraser 2019; Pfeizer et al. 2020; Seyedsayamdost and Vanderwal 2020).
  • 总结FNC的失败案例，揭示了农民对其技术适用性的批判性反思能力，以及制度性机构对地方情境的误读。
    This represents the critical view that farmers have regarding the applicability of technologies in their functionings... a process described as the reflexive capacity of individuals and collectives to critically assess their engagement with technologies and their proponents (Forney and Dwiartama 2023).
  • 引用文献描述创新是一个动态、波折的路径，日常的、混乱的人与非人类互动催生了特定的数字化形式。
    Owen et al. (2013) described innovation as a dynamic, undulating path, sometimes with dead ends. Meanwhile, messy entanglements of day-to-day human and non-human interactions engender particular forms of digitalization (Forney and Dwiartama 2023).
  • 在结论中重申，本文通过能力アプローチ对基于普遍性假设的技术推广进行了批判。
    Amartya Sen’s capabilities approach presents a critique of the unquestioning pursuit of diffusing technologies across contexts based on a false sense of universality.
  • 最后呼吁，应以承认发展和创新的多样性来取代不变性、普遍性和统一性的观念，从而减少现实-设计鸿沟。
    Drawing upon a relational perspective, we present evidence of the need to replace notions of immutability, universality and uniformity, with the recognition of multiple ideas of development and the role of technologies in personal and collective development.
