A review of social science on digital agriculture, smart farming and agriculture 4.0: New contributions and a future research agenda

Laurens Klerkxa, Emma Jakkub, Pierre Labarthec

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，尽管关于数字农业的社会科学研究正迅速增长，但其文献呈现出分散和碎片化的状态。为了系统性地梳理这一新兴领域，作者提出可以将现有文献归纳为五个核心主题集群（Thematic Clusters）。在此基础上，文章进一步论证，通过对本期特刊收录的17篇论文进行分析，可以揭示这些主题集群内部的前沿进展，并识别出新的研究方向。最终，文章主张建立一个更全面的未来研究议程，该议程不仅深化现有主题，还应开拓四个目前研究尚显薄弱的潜在新集群，从而推动对数字农业进行更具批判性和跨学科的社会科学探究。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的论据主要建立在对超过100篇社会科学文献的探索性回顾之上，这些文献构成了识别和定义五个主题集群的基础。这五个集群分别是：1) 技术的采纳、使用与适应；2) 对农民身份、技能与工作的影响；3) 权力、所有权、隐私与伦理；4) 农业知识与创新系统（AKIS）；5) 经济与管理。此外，本期特刊中的17篇实证和概念性论文构成了第二层核心论据，它们被用以证明这些集群内部的新进展和新见解，例如数字农业与农场多样性的关系、新的商业与制度安排，以及新兴的伦理治理方式等。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程分为三个逻辑步骤。首先，通过对现有社会科学文献的广泛梳理，进行归纳和分类，构建出五个主题集群的分析框架，为该领域建立了一个知识地图。其次，将特刊中的17篇文章“映射”（map against）到这个框架中，检验并展示了这些最新研究如何填充、扩展甚至挑战了现有集群的边界。最后，在综合前两步的基础上，作者识别出现有研究中的空白和新兴趋势，并以此为基础，前瞻性地提出了一个包含深化现有集群和开拓四个新集群的未来研究议程，从而完成了一个从“回顾-整合-展望”的完整学术论证闭环。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的主要研究方法是探索性文献回顾（exploratory literature review）与主题式综合分析（thematic synthesis）。作者明确指出，这并非一次系统的文献回顾，其目的不在于穷尽所有文献，而在于识别和构建一个能够反映该领域研究现状的宏观主题框架。通过在Scopus等数据库中检索关键词，并运用滚雪球抽样法，作者识别出关键文献并进行归类。对于特刊文章的分析，则采用了一种元分析（meta-analysis）的方法，即提炼和整合一组独立研究的核心贡献，以形成更高层次的洞见。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文的核心案例是其所介绍的整个特刊（special issue），其中包含的17篇学术文章。这些文章本身构成了多样的“微观案例”，涵盖了不同的地理背景（如加拿大、澳大利亚、新西兰、欧洲多国）和研究对象（如谷物产业、奶牛养殖、农技推广服务等）。例如，Janc等人（2019）对波兰农民互联网使用的研究，以及Vik等人（2019）对挪威自动挤奶系统影响的分析，都作为具体的例证，被用来阐释和充实“技术采纳”或“农民身份与工作”等宏观主题集群的内涵。

第1章：导言 (Introduction)

本章首先确立了“数字化”作为一股遍及社会并深刻改变农业生产系统、价值链和食品体系的变革性力量的地位。作者列举了数字化的多种技术形态，并梳理了学术界用以描述农业数字化的各种术语（如智能农业、精准农业、农业4.0等）。随后，文章概述了数字化在农业中被寄予的厚望——从优化生产效率到解决食品溯源、动物福利和环境影响等社会关切。作者指出，虽然关于数字农业的技术性文献已相当丰富，但社会科学领域的文献虽在近年快速增长，却显得零散。因此，本文和整个特刊旨在系统梳理社会科学在这一领域的贡献，为该领域构建一个知识框架，并提出未来的研究议程，以促进社会科学与自然、技术科学之间更深度的跨学科对话。

  • 作者引用一系列文献来定义“数字化”这一宏观社会技术过程，并将其定位为一种无处不在的趋势，涵盖大数据、物联网、人工智能等多种技术。
    Digitalization comprises phenomena and technologies such as big data, internet of things (IoT), augmented reality, robotics, sensors, 3D printing, system integration, ubiquitous connectivity, artificial intelligence, machine learning, digital twins, and blockchain among others (Alm et al., 2016; Smith, 2018; Tilson et al., 2010).
  • 作者指出，数字化预计将从根本上改变日常生活，并引用文献说明其在农业及相关供应链中的变革潜力已经初步显现。
    Digitalization is expected to radically transform everyday life (Yoo, 2010) and productive processes in agriculture and associated food, fibre and bioenergy supply chains and systems (Poppe et al., 2013; Smith, 2018) and initial signs of transformation are already visible (Di Silvestre et al., 2018; Leviäkangas, 2016; Rotz et al., 2019a).
  • 作者追溯了农业数字化的早期历史渊源，指出其并非全新事物，而是决策支持系统和精准农业等早期工作的延续。
    At the same time, it should be recognised that digitalization in agriculture has been going for decades already, expressed for example through work on decision support systems (Leeuwis, 1993, 1999. McCown, 2002) and also precision agriculture has a long trajectory already (Wolf and Buttel, 1996; Wolf and Wood, 1997).
  • 作者列举了描述农业数字化现象的多个术语，展现了该领域概念的多样性，并说明了这些术语的来源文献。
    In the agricultural sector, several concepts have emerged to express different forms of digitalization in agricultural production systems, value chains and more broadly food systems. These include Smart Farming (Blok and Gremmen, 2018; Wolfert et al., 2017), Precision Agriculture or Precision Farming (Wolf and Buttel, 1996; Eastwood et al., 2017b), Decision Agriculture (Leonard et al., 2017), Digital Agriculture (Keogh and Henry, 2016; Shepherd et al., 2018), Agriculture 4.0 (Rose and Chilvers, 2018) or what is referred to in French as Agriculture Numérique (i.e. Numerical Agriculture - Bellon-Maurel and Huyghe, 2016).
  • 阐述数字化农业的核心操作逻辑：即通过各类传感器收集数据，并利用这些数据进行分析、预测和决策。
    The data obtained is used to interpret the past and predict the future, to make more timely or accurate decisions, through constant monitoring or specific big data science enquiries (Eastwood et al., 2017a; Janssen et al., 2017; Wolfert et al., 2017).
  • 总结了数字化农业在解决社会关切方面的潜力，如食品溯源、动物福利和环境保护。
    Furthermore, it has been argued that it may help address societal concerns around farming, including provenance and traceability of food (Dawkins, 2017), animal welfare in livestock industries (Yeates, 2017) and the environmental impact of different farming practices (Balafoutis et al., 2017; Busse et al., 2015).
  • 指出数字化在促进知识交流、学习以及危机监控方面的潜在作用。
    Digitalization is also expected to enhance knowledge exchange and learning, using ubiquitous data (Baumüller, 2017; Daum et al., 2018; Eichler Inwood and Dale, 2019) and improve monitoring of crises and controversies in agricultural chains and sectors (Stevens et al., 2016).
  • 概述了数字技术在不同农业部门的应用现状，指出在种植业中应用较为普遍，而在畜牧业中则相对较少。
    The uptake internationally of digital technologies in the past two decades has been most prevalent in agricultural sectors such as cropping and viticulture through precision farming technologies (Bramley, 2009), and to a lesser extent in animal-based farming (Borchers and Bewley, 2015; Eastwood et al., 2017a)...
  • 强调了现有关于数字农业的科学文献主要集中在技术层面，而非社会经济层面。
    The scientific literature on digital agriculture has primarily focused on the technical aspects of applying these technologies for improving agricultural practices and productivity (Rutten et al., 2013; Wathes et al., 2008)...
  • 作者通过列举大量综述性文章，证明了自然科学和设计科学在数字农业研究中的主导地位。
    This is evidenced by an increasing number of review articles on topics such as precision farming, big data analysis, drones, artificial intelligence and robotics, 3D printing, artificial intelligence, IoT, and the transformative potential of these digital technologies... (de Amorim et al., 2019; Dick et al., 2019;...Zhao et al., 2019).
  • 追溯了社会科学对数字农业研究的早期历史，点明了Wolf等人的开创性工作。
    Whereas some of the early publications date from more than two decades ago (Wolf et al., 2001; Wolf and Buttel, 1996; Wolf and Wood, 1997), given the technological advances and the pervasiveness of digital technologies in all realms of society (Scholz et al., 2018), the last 5 years have seen an accelerated growth in social science publications on digital agriculture...
  • 阐述了政策界对数字农业及其社会经济维度的日益关注，并列举了欧盟、世界银行、粮农组织和经合组织等机构发布的相关报告。
    For instance, the Standing Committee on Agricultural Research from the EU, through its Agricultural Knowledge and Innovation Systems Strategic Workgroup, has a specific focus on Smart Farming/Digital Agriculture (Poppe et al., 2013; EU SCAR AKIS, 2019) and reviews commissioned by the EU have emerged on the topic (Soma et al., 2019). The World Bank has published a sourcebook and a future outlook (World Bank, 2019; World Bank, 2017), and the FAO has recently published a status report (Trendov et al., 2019)...the OECD also organized a conference in 2018 on the topic and published a policy-oriented publication (Jouanjean, 2019).
  • 指出现有的社会科学综述文章多聚焦于特定议题，如政治经济学或伦理学，缺乏对整个领域的宏观梳理。
    Despite the existence of some review articles on particular issues, such as the political economy of digital agriculture (Rotz et al., 2019a) and perspective articles on ethics (Carbonell, 2016), most review articles focus on technical issues... (Aker, 2011; ...Zhao et al., 2019).

第2章：社会科学文献中的主要现有主题集群 (Major current thematic clusters in the social science literature)

本章是全文的核心，作者通过探索性文献回顾，将分散的社会科学研究系统地归纳为五个相互关联的主题集群。这五个集群为理解数字农业的社会维度提供了一个全面的分析框架。它们分别是：1) 采纳、使用与适应：关注农民个体采纳技术的决定因素、技术在农场中的实际应用以及后续的调整过程。2) 对农民身份、技能与工作的影响：探讨数字化如何重塑“何为农民”的文化认知、需要何种新技能，以及对劳动过程和农民自主性的影响。3) 权力、所有权、隐私与伦理：采用批判性视角，审视数据政治、企业权力、数字鸿沟以及人与动物的伦理议题。4) 农业知识与创新系统（AKIS）：分析数字化如何改变知识的产生、传播和应用方式，以及创新系统中各行动者（如推广服务、高科技公司）角色的演变。5) 经济与管理：研究数字化的成本效益、投资决策、对市场的影响以及由此产生的新兴商业模式。

  • 在第一个集群“采纳、使用与适应”中，作者指出研究重点包括个体采纳的决定因素以及促进采纳的推广干预措施。
    This literature concentrates on individual adoption determinants (Barnes et al., 2019; Hansen, 2015; Jensen et al., 2012; Kernecker et al., 2019; Leonard et al., 2017; Tey and Brindal, 2012), as well as extension and communicative interventions to stimulate adoption (Kutter et al., 2011).
  • 进一步地，该集群也关注技术采纳后的适应过程，引入了如“修补”（tinkering）和“集结”（assemblages）等社会技术概念。
    Another line of enquiry examines precision agriculture use on farm and how it affects farming practices (Fountas et al., 2005; Hansen, 2015; Hay and Pearce, 2014) and post-adoption processes of adaptation (Higgins et al., 2017; Schewe and Stuart, 2015), through concepts such as ‘tinkering’ and ‘assemblages’.
  • 在第二个集群“对农民身份、技能与工作的影响”中，作者论述了数字化可能带来的农民“去技能化”或劳动力替代问题，及其对边缘群体的潜在负面影响。
    Furthermore, technology aimed at automating tasks and increasing efficiency may deskill or displace farmers and farm workers and exclude or discriminate against those not digitally literate. This may have negative effects on demand for rural labour and hence affect marginalized groups such as migrants, in a context of growing separation between labour and capital in agriculture (Carolan, 2019; Rotz et al., 2019b; Smith, 2018).
  • 同时，该集群也探讨了数字技术与现有实践结合的可能性，可能催生出新的“负责任的专业精神”。
    However, other authors argue that digital technologies may also be merged into existing practices to create combinations of ‘digital’ and ‘analogue’ skills (Burton and Riley, 2018), or give rise to a new sort of ‘responsible professionalism’ (Blok, 2018).
  • 在第三个集群“权力、所有权、隐私与伦理”中，作者强调了对数据所有权、隐私和权力失衡等伦理问题的批判性研究。
    This literature examines how digitalization changes or reproduces the rules, institutions and balances of power governing these systems, how that affects different actors and what responses or resistances emerge and how ethical issues such as those around privacy and data ownership emerge and are addressed (Sykuta, 2016; Bronson, 2018; Carbonell, 2016;...Miles, 2019).
  • 该集群还包括对动物伦理的探讨，特别是在自动化畜牧系统（如机器人挤奶）中，技术如何影响动物的自主性和人与动物的关系。
    This has given rise to philosophical and ethical perspectives, in which ethical challenges affecting animal autonomy and human-animal relationships of farms have been analysed (Bear and Holloway, 2019; Driessen and Heutinck, 2015; Bos and Munnichs, 2016).
  • 在第四个集群“农业知识与创新系统”中，作者从宏观层面分析创新系统如何因数字化而演变，并探讨了“负责任的研究与创新”（RRI）原则的应用。
    Given the ethical concerns raised in cluster 3 (section 2.3), there is an emerging literature that explores how innovation systems can apply principles of Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) (Owen et al., 2012) to the digitalization of agricultural production systems, value chains and food systems (Bronson, 2018; Eastwood et al., 2017a; Jirotka et al., 2017; Rose and Chilvers, 2018).
  • 在微观层面，该集群的研究关注决策支持系统的用户中心设计，以及农技顾问如何在新旧知识体系间进行调解。
    ...other research looks at the continuous processes of how digital decision support systems are better attuned to users (O’Donoghue et al., 2016;...Lindblom et al., 2017) and how advisors interact with farmers to connect ‘digital knowledge systems’ to ‘farmers knowledge systems’ (Tsouvalis et al., 2000; Lundström and Lindblom, 2018; Bechtet, 2019).
  • 在第五个集群“经济与管理”中，作者指出关于数字农业商业模式的研究相对有限，但存在对精准农业成本效益的分析。
    While there is some generic...literature looking at economic and business model aspects of digital technologies and big data..., in agriculture the body of work with this focus seems to be more modest. There is some research reflecting on costs and benefits of unmanned aircraft systems, for example (Hunt and Daughtry, 2018), or of other precision farming technologies (Schimmelpfennig and Ebel, 2016).
  • 该集群也涵盖了对发展中国家市场信息系统的经济影响评估，以及对“数字套餐”商业模式的批判性分析，这些模式可能加剧跨国公司的市场主导地位。
    In such models, multinational corporations offer large ‘digital package deals’ to farmers (Bronson, 2018; Bronson and Knezevic, 2016; Carolan, 2017b, 2018b). These package deals tend to maintain balances of power to the benefit of models of agriculture based on the intensive use of chemical input, as hypothesised by Wolf and Buttel (1996) already in the 1990s.

第3章 & 第4章 & 第5章：特刊贡献、未来展望与结论 (Contributions, Future Outlook & Conclusion)

第3章详细分析了特刊中17篇文章如何对前述五个主题集群做出具体贡献。例如，在“采纳”集群，文章揭示了波兰的数字鸿沟和欧洲农民对技术环境影响的保留态度。在“权力与伦理”集群，多篇文章探讨了数据透明度、所有权和信任问题，并共同呼吁采用负责任的创新方法。在“AKIS”集群，文章提出了“数字农业创新系统”（DAIS）等新概念，并深入剖析了农技顾问在数字化转型中的角色演变。第4章是本文的前瞻性核心，作者在系统梳理的基础上提出了一个全面的未来研究议程。议程分为两部分：首先，针对五个现有集群，提出了深化研究的具体问题，如数字化如何影响农场规模、新农民身份的形成、数据治理模式等；其次，也是最具创建性的部分，作者提出了四个尚待开发的全新研究集群：1) 数字农业的社会系统概念化（如社会-网络-物理-生态系统）；2) 数字农业的政策制定过程；3) 数字化驱动的农业转型路径；4) 数字农业发展的全球地理学（特别是对全球南方的关注）。第5章作为结论，重申了本文的贡献——即为数字农业的社会科学研究提供了一个结构化的概览和前瞻性的议程。作者最后发出呼吁，倡导社会科学与自然、技术科学之间进行更深入的跨学科和超学科合作，以共同引导数字农业向着更有益、更公平的方向发展。

  • 在论述特刊对“采纳”集群的贡献时，引用Janc等人的研究指出，波兰农业的社会结构可能阻碍数字化进程。
    Underlying reasons that Janc et al. (2019) found include the way in which the social fabric of Polish agriculture may have a disabling role, whereby digital technology is seen as individualistic and seen to erode the previous feelings and institutions associated with common knowledge acquisition...
  • 在讨论特刊对“权力与伦理”集群的贡献时，引用van der Burg等人的观点，指出关于数字农场的社会目的存在未言明的、多样的想象。
    However, despite increasing attention in research, policy and practice for ethics of smart farming/digital agriculture, they conclude that there seems to be different yet implicit ideas regarding the purpose and function of digital farms in society, which are not made explicit in research and societal debates. (van der Burg et al., 2019)
  • 在“AKIS”集群下，引用Fielke等人提出的“数字农业创新系统”（DAIS）概念，以促进对数字化影响的反思。
    At a macro level, Fielke et al. (2019) propose the concept of Digital Agricultural Innovation Systems (DAIS) to explicitly consider the element of digital innovation within innovation systems. They discuss how this may alter the way innovation is supported by offering new affordances for organizing innovation... (Nambisan et al., 2019).
  • 在“经济与管理”集群下，引用Phillips等人的研究，他们发现加拿大西部农业中自下而上的商业模式（如初创公司）比自上而下的企业模式更为普遍。
    They find that the first top-down, corporate model is not widespread, despite being adopted by multinationals such as John Deere, with vertical coordination and an integrated business model. (Phillips et al., 2019)
  • 在提出未来研究议程时，作者建议未来研究应更深入地探讨不同农场类型和耕作风格对技术采纳的影响。
    The special issue articles in the thematic cluster of adoption, uses and adaptation of digital technologies (Janc et al., 2019; Knierim et al., 2019) urge us to look deeper into the role of the diversity of farm types, farming styles, and producer characteristics, in terms of adoption and adaptation of digital agriculture technologies.
  • 针对“农民身份”集群，未来议程提出了研究社交媒体如何塑造农民身份和影响其自主性的问题。
    What is the influence of social media on farmer identity? How do farmers perform identity work through social media? How does social media affect their autonomy, in view of public scrutiny and policy and supply chain surveillance?
  • 在“权力与伦理”集群的未来议程中，作者引用Carolan的研究，提出要关注“数据合作社”等新兴的草根组织形式。
    Furthering earlier work by Carolan (2018c), how do novel grassroots and corporate organisational responses emerge around Big Data and the Internet of Things, such as Right to Repair, FarmHack and Data Cooperatives, and how do they function?
  • 作者提出了第一个新的研究集群：“数字农业社会系统概念化”，并引用了Lioutas等人“社会-网络-物理系统”的提法，并建议将其扩展至生态维度。
    Whereas some authors reflect on the need for inclusion of social systems in conceptualizations of digital agriculture, such as cyber physical systems (Wolfert et al., 2017), or the ‘socio-cyber-physical systems’ that Lioutas et al. (2019) refer to in their article for the special issue, this seems an underdeveloped area.
  • 针对第四个新集群“全球地理学”，作者特别强调了对“全球南方”国家进行批判性社会科学研究的必要性，并引用了多位学者的观点。
    It has therefore been suggested that a critical approach towards the pervasive application of digital technologies in developing and emerging country agriculture is much needed (Cieslik et al., 2018; Mann, 2018; Sulaiman V. et al., 2012).
  • 在结论部分，作者呼吁从“模拟社会科学”转向“数字社会科学”，以更好地理解数字化社会。
    ...as Roth et al (2019) argue quite strongly, computer illiterate social scientists produce analogue theories of digital societies.
