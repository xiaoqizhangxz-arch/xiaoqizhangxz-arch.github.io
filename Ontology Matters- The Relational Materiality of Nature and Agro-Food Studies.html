Ontology Matters: The Relational Materiality of Nature and Agro-Food Studies

David Goodman

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，农业食品研究（agro-food studies）因其在理论上未曾反思地继承了源自古典马克思主义的现代主义本体论而备受束缚。这种本体论严格划分了社会与自然，将自然视为一个被动的、可被社会力量（尤其是资本）作用和改造的客体。作者认为，以“劳动过程”（labour process）为核心的传统分析框架，已不足以应对由农业生物技术（如转基因生物）引发的新型“生物政治”（bio-politics）挑战，因为这些技术创造了复杂的“社会-自然”混合体（socionatural assemblages）。因此，为了实现领域的理论革新并有效介入当代复杂的环境与伦理议题，农业食品研究必须明确地进行“本体论选择”，转向一种关系性的、非二元论的本体论，如“行动者网络理论”（ANT）所提供的那样，它能够更好地捕捉和分析自然的能动性及其与社会的共构关系。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者通过多层次的理论梳理来支撑其论点。首先，他追溯了农业食品研究的知识谱系，指出其深受将农业与工业等同的列宁主义式“土地问题”辩论的影响，从而确立了以生产为中心、社会关系为优先的“劳动过程”概念的主导地位。其次，他批判性地回顾了包括他本人早期作品在内的“第一代”生物技术研究，揭示这些研究如何将技术变革统一框架在资本对自然的“占有”（appropriationism）和“商品化”（commodification）叙事中，从而忽视了自然的内在活力和能动性。再次，他探讨了马克思主义内部的“生态化重构”尝试（如Ted Benton的理论），承认其试图重新将自然的物质性带回分析，但暗示这仍是在传统二元框架内的修补。最后，他详细介绍了行动者网络理论（ANT）作为一个激进的替代方案，通过“异质性工程”、“广义对称性”等概念，彻底消解了自然/社会的二分法，为理解和分析社会-自然混合体提供了更有力的理论工具。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

本文的论证逻辑遵循一种“批判-诊断-替代”的路径。首先，作者通过知识考古学的方式，批判性地揭示了农业食品研究领域一个被视为理所当然的核心概念（劳动过程）背后隐藏的本体论预设（现代主义二元论）。接着，他以农业生物技术引发的社会争议为“诊断”案例，指出这一传统理论工具在面对新的、无法被纯粹社会或纯粹自然范畴所解释的现象时，显得力不从心。最后，他系统地比较了两种可能的理论更新路径：在马克思主义内部进行生态化重构，或是从外部引入后结构主义/后人类主义的行动者网络理论。通过对二者在解释力、政治意涵和伦理关怀上的比较，他最终论证了ANT的“关系唯物主义”（relational materialism）为农业食品研究提供了一个更具前瞻性和解释力的本体论基础，更能回应时代的挑战。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的是理论批判与思想史梳理的研究方法。它不涉及一手经验数据的收集与分析，而是对一个学术领域的理论基础进行深度“内省”和“元分析”。其方法论本质上是概念辨析和知识谱系学的，通过追溯“劳动过程”等核心概念的源流、演变及其在不同研究议题（如全球化、生物技术）中的应用，来揭示其内在的局限性。通过引入和评述生态马克思主义与行动者网络理论，作者进行了一场理论对话与综合，旨在推动学科的理论范式转型。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

贯穿全文的核心例证是**农业生物技术（agricultural biotechnologies）**及其引发的广泛的**生物政治行动主义（bio-political activism）**。转基因生物（GMOs）的出现和扩散，以及公众对其引发的伦理、环境和食品安全问题的强烈反应（如破坏试验田、反WTO示威），被作者用来说明传统分析框架的失效。传统框架倾向于将这些问题归结为资本主义生产关系（如合同农业的扩张、家庭农场的命运），而作者认为，这些争议的真正核心在于对物种边界的人为跨越、对基因流不可逆性的担忧以及对新型“生命形式”的疑虑——这些都直接指向了自然的“活性”（liveliness）和“能动性”（agency）。因此，这个案例有力地证明了，我们需要一种能够超越主客二分的本体论，才能理解这些由人与非人（如基因、作物、微生物）共同构成的复杂网络及其政治后果。

The labour process and agro-food studies

本节追溯了“劳动过程”作为农业食品研究中卓越的“中层概念”其核心地位的形成。作者指出，“新农村社会学”和“农业新政治经济学”的基础是古典马克思主义的演绎认识论和以生产为中心的分析方法。这一谱系，特别是通过列宁和考茨基的辩论以及第三世界农民研究的理论注入，确立了“土地问题”——即农业社会结构的资本主义转型——为核心议题。因此，“劳动过程”成为分析家庭农场存续、商品化、农业例外论以及后来的全球商品链等所有关键议题的理论基石，即使在后来的研究声称超越结构主义之后，这一概念仍被不加反思地持续使用。

  • 作者指出，直到1980年代末，古典马克思主义的演绎认识论在“新农村社会学”中都未受严重质疑。
    This centrality reflects the twin foundations of the ‘new rural sociology’ and ‘new political economy of agriculture’ in the deductive epistemology of classical Marxism, which was not seriously questioned until the later 1980s (Long et al. 1986; Vandergeest 1988; Marsden 1988; Buttel and Goodman 1989; Buttel and McMichael 1990)...
  • 作者认为，该领域采纳的“劳动过程”概念源于列宁与民粹派的辩论和考茨基的论战，其特点是将农业与工业等同看待。
    ...the labour process concept widely adopted in the ‘new’ rural sociology, and which posits the mimetic equivalence of agriculture and industry, derives from the politically charged context of Lenin’s debates with the Narodniks and Kautsky’s exchanges with the German ‘reformists’ (Hussein and Tribe 1981).
  • 尽管考茨基的分析更为精妙，但列宁主义的解读最终定义了“经典”辩论的条款。
    Although the subtleties of Kautsky’s analysis more recently have been reclaimed from the Leninist orthodoxy (Watts 1996; Goodman and Watts 1997), “the Russian, and specifically Leninist, contribution . . . (came) . . . to define the terms of the ‘classical’ debate..." (Goodman et al 1987, p. 145).
  • 新马克思主义发展文献中关于第三世界农民的辩论，进一步强化了这一理论和宏观结构的关注点。
    This lineage of theoretical and macrostructural concerns was further strengthened by re-interpretations of the classical tradition, and notably the ‘agrarian question,’ in the neo-Marxist development literature on the fate of the Third World peasantries... (Newby 1985).
  • 经典马克思主义的核心问题，即农业社会结构的资本主义转型，主导了新兴的农业食品研究领域。
    As already suggested, the recovery of the deductivist legacy of classical Marxism also extended to its central problematic: the capitalist transformation of agrarian social structures (Goodman and Redclift 1981; Buttel and McMichael 1990).
  • 即使在试图通过行动者导向视角来摆脱结构决定论的批判性尝试中，这一理论框架的烙印也清晰可见。
    Its imprint as foil can clearly be seen in critical moves to formulate actor-oriented perspectives of agrarian change (Long et al. 1986).
  • 在分析“农业例外论”（即农业在资本主义中的特殊性）时，生产层面的社会和技术关系同样被置于优先地位。
    The immediate farm-level social and technical relations of production similarly are privileged in analyses of agrarian exceptionalism, that is, agriculture as a special case or anomaly within capitalism, arising from the singularities of its natural production process (Mann and Dickenson 1978; Goodman et al. 1987; Mann 1990).
  • 作者指出，一些学者明确提出，早期农村社会学忽视了劳动过程这一概念，而另一些学者则认为商品化理论对其处理不当。
    Thus Friedland et al. (1981) declaim the previous neglect of this concept in rural sociology (ibid, p. 29), while van der Ploeg (1986, p. 27) identifies its “inadequate treatment” as a major weakness of commoditization theory.
  • “劳动过程”概念在各种分析框架中被持续使用，从福特主义/后福特主义生产，到“耕作风格”和食物政体文献。
    Recourse to the conceptual frame of the labour process can be tracked across analyses of capitalist restructuring, the characterization of Fordist/post-Fordist production in agriculture, ‘farming styles’ actor-oriented perspectives, and the food regimes literature (Goodman and Watts 1994).
  • 关于全球农业食品复合体的论点，在很大程度上建立在对商品链视角的重新阐述之上，用以概念化全球采购下的农业劳动地域分工。
    Arguments for the ‘new’ internationalization of agriculture and global agro-food complexes build heavily on reformulations of commodity chain perspectives to conceptualize regional divisions of agricultural labour integrated in spatially extended production systems involving global sourcing (Sanderson 1986; Raynolds et al. 1993; Bonnano et al. 1994; Goodman 1997).

    Buttel (1996) suggests that global agricultural commodity systems, chains and complexes are concepts that “represent the emerging scholarly tradition, or traditions, of ‘global agro-food restructuring’” (p. 17).

  • 消费“转向”的趋势正在复兴早期的商品链研究传统，并与“供给体系”的概念相结合，尤其是在地理学领域。
    Interestingly, the earlier tradition of agro-food commodity chain research, now complemented by the notion of ‘systems of provision’ (Fine and Leopold 1993), is being renewed by the consumption ‘turn’ in social science, notably in geography (Cook and Crang 1996; Leslie and Reimer 1999; Hartwick 1998; Dixon 1999).
  • 作者批评，尽管声称已经超越了结构主义政治经济学，但农业食品研究并未尝试将“劳动过程”这一概念从其原始理论框架中剥离或审视其本体论意涵。
    To the best of my knowledge, agro-food studies has made no attempt to disembed this concept from its original theoretical framework nor to examine the ontological implications of these roots. (Arce and Marsden 1993; van der Ploeg 1993).

The labour process and agricultural biotechnologies.

本节具体分析了“劳动过程”框架如何被应用于理解农业生物技术。作者将这些早期研究（包括自己的工作）归为“第一代”分析，其共同特点是将生物技术变革置于资本主义渗透和农业劳动过程商品化的经典马克思主义叙事中。无论是Kloppenburg的“种子商品化”，还是Goodman等人的“占有主义”（appropriationism），都将自然视为一个被动的、有待被资本通过科技手段征服和改造的领域。这些分析虽然深刻揭示了资本的社会动因，但其单向度的、纯粹人类中心的能动观，使其无法理论化在技术实践中涌现出的新型“活体”（lively entities）和非人类的能动性。

  • 第一代生物技术分析将农业与工业之间的界限问题化，但仍将技术科学的问题框架在资本主义渗透和劳动过程商品化的经典术语中。
    ...problematizing the boundaries between ‘agriculture’ and ‘industry’ (Kloppenburg, Jr. 1988; Goodman, Sorj and Wilkinson 1987). This analytic move provided the basis for wider, more systemic and historically grounded studies of agro-industrial development...
  • Kloppenburg的研究将种子的商品化过程概念化为一种原始积累，其分析深受Braverman关于科学作为资本主义财产的论述影响。
    The work of Kloppenburg, Jr. (1988), for example, is first situated generally on the classical Marxist terrain of agriculture as a ‘recalcitrant sector,’ before taking up the specific theme of the vectors of capitalist penetration of plant science and its gradual reconfiguration as “capitalist property” (Braverman 1976, p. 156).
  • Kloppenburg旨在解释种子如何被商品化并成为一种资本主义的生产力。
    Kloppenburg’s (1988) purpose is to explain how the seed, “. . .which is perhaps the element of agricultural means of production most central to the entire farm production process . . .” (p. 39), is commodified and becomes a capitalist force of production.
  • Goodman, Sorj and Wilkinson提出了“占有主义”（appropriationism）概念，指工业资本逐步瓦解农业生产过程的自然元素，将其转化为工业活动，再作为投入品重新整合回农业。
    The concept of ‘appropriationism’ is used to designate the historically discontinuous, piecemeal “. . . but persistent undermining of discrete elements of the agricultural production process, their transformation into industrial activities, and their re-incorporation into agriculture as inputs . . .”(Goodman et al 1987, p. 2).
  • 这些作者与Kloppenburg一样，认识到杂交技术在为植物改良开辟专有途径以及推动综合作物管理系统中的关键作用。
    In common with Kloppenburg, Jr. (1988), these authors recognize the pivotal importance of hybridization in opening up opportunities for proprietary plant improvement...
  • 在这个框架下，农业生物技术被视为“工业资本操纵自然能力的一次普遍性进步”。
    ...in agriculture, where industrial capitals confront a natural production process, agricultural biotechnologies constitute “a generalised advance in the capacity of industrial capitals to manipulate nature” (ibid, p. 98).
  • Boyd等人扩展了这些第一代分析，借用马克思的价值理论，提出了自然的“形式 subsumption”和“真实 subsumption”之间的区别。
    In a recent critique and extension of these ‘first generation’ analyses, and including Susan Mann’s work (Mann and Dickenson 1978; Mann 1990), Boyd et al. (2000) borrow from Marx’s value theory to propose a distinction between the formal and real subsumption of nature.
  • Yoxen的开创性工作将分子生物学的历史视为一个由政治力量塑造的研究项目和技术工程，其分析与《激进科学杂志》的“科学即劳动过程”思潮紧密相关。
    His seminal, though neglected, contribution is closely linked to the now equally neglected work of the Radical Science Journal Collective, and its commitment to critical praxis and the agitational potential of de-mystifying science and technology (rsj 1981).
  • 这一“科学即劳动过程”思潮借鉴了英国社会主义经济学家会议的辩论，后者又受到大陆马克思主义理论和Braverman著作的影响。
    This “science as labour process” current in early science and technology studies drew on debates in the Conference of Socialist Economists in the u.k. which, in turn, were influenced by Continental Marxist theory and praxis and Braverman’s Labour and Monopoly Capital (rsj 1978,1981; Young 1979).
  • Yoxen的分析得出的结论是，在资本控制下，生物技术成为“一种对活生生的自然的特定占有模式——字面上就是将生命资本化”。
    Under the control of capital, biotechnology “is a specific mode of the appropriation of living nature – literally capitalising life,” which “. . . becomes a productive force, playing a growing role in reconstituting the social relations of a new capitalist order” (Yoxen 1981, p. 112).
  • 作者总结道，这些基于劳动过程框架的分析，其能动性是单向的，完全与人类意图和实践等同，它们将人类行动者视为作用于一个外在、客体化的自然，而不是与自然建立社会伙伴关系。
    It is equally clear, however, that agency in these approaches is unidirectional and uniquely identified with human intentionality and praxis. Human agents act ‘upon’ an external, objectified, but now less ‘recalcitrant,’ nature, rather than in social partnership with nature.

The materiality of nature: reconstructing the labour process

本节探讨了重构“劳动过程”概念以纳入自然物质性的可能性，主要通过审视生态马克思主义的内部改革路径。作者认为，传统劳动过程理论优先考虑生产中的社会关系，将理论入口设在了“资本循环”的特定位置，这限制了其分析自然-社会互动的能力。随后，他引介了生态马克思主义的努力，特别是Ted Benton的贡献，即将劳动过程重构为具有双重性的过程：既是社会剥削关系，也是社会对自然的代谢性占有。作者肯定了这一重构的潜力，即联合社会正义与环境正义的议程。然而，他也指出，农业食品研究领域对这些重要的内部辩论几乎完全忽视。最后，他观察到该领域出现了一种“反思性转向”，开始借用ANT、惯例理论等外部理论资源来处理自然的能动性，但这恰恰凸显了其自身核心理论框架的深刻本体论缺陷，这种缺陷是根深蒂固的，难以轻易剔除。

  • 作者引用Hall的观点，指出劳动过程视角将理论入口设在生产的关口，这决定了观察者的位置和分析的焦点。
    Loosely paraphrasing Hall (1986), this means that theoretical entry is gained through the gateway of production, which situates the observer at a particular location in the circuits of capital.
  • 尽管农业食品研究声称已与演绎式理论“算清了账”，但这种转变更多是关于问题意识（如“土地问题”），而非对其本体论基础和核心概念的根本性质疑。
    We have suggested that agro-food studies by the early 1990s conveyed the impression... that it had ‘settled accounts’ with deductivist theoretical perspectives... (Buttel and McMichael 1990; Arce and Marsden 1993). However, this shift seems to have been more about problematics... than a root-and-branch interrogation of its ontological foundations...
  • 该领域对平行学科中关于将自然“带回”社会理论的理论发酵视而不见，这些辩论旨在质疑社会科学的现代主义本体论。
    This paradox is all the stronger in view of the theoretical ferment in parallel literatures, including debates on the reconciliation of Red/Green perspectives... (FitzSimmons and Goodman 1998). By problematizing the modern ontology... with its dualistic separation of nature and society, these initiatives interrogate the ability of inherited conceptual structures to address the materiality of nature–society interactions (Benton 1991, 1995).
  • 自Alfred Schmidt的经典研究以来，马克思主义内部对缺乏系统的自然理论展开了丰富的论争，许多理论家试图复兴马克思早期著作中的自然主义前提，以构建生态马克思主义。
    This challenge, initiated by Alfred Schmidt’s classic study, The Concept of Nature in Marx (1971) has encouraged theorists to resuscitate the naturalistic, materialist premises of Marx’s early writings as the foundations of an ecological Marxism.
  • 这些关于生态化马克思主义的辩论，核心议题包括马克思的普罗米修斯主义、对“自然限制”的拒斥，以及资本主义的“第二重矛盾”，即对生产条件的破坏。
    ...points of discussion include the Prometheanism of Marx’s conceptualization, the related rejection of ‘natural limits’ arguments, and under-theorization of ‘the ecological crisis-tendencies of capitalism’... (Benton 1989, 1992; Grundmann 1991; Watts 1995; O’Connor 1988; Burkett 1998a,b).
  • Castree在马克思主义地理学中扩展了“自然的生产”论题，并高度评价Benton“绿化”马克思的尝试，尤其是后者对劳动过程概念的重构。
    Here, Castree (1995) welcomes recent efforts to “re-discover nature’s materiality in Marx” (p. 21) and finds that Benton’s “attempt to ‘green’ Marx is especially lucid” (p. 22), including his proposed reconstruction of the labour process... (Smith 1984; Redclift 1987; FitzSimmons 1989 a,b).
  • Benton的目标是恢复劳动过程复杂的二元性，即在关注剥削和价值增殖的社会关系维度的同时，给予其作为社会占有自然形式的第二个维度同等重要的地位。
    That is, to complement the social-relational aspects of exploitation and valorization by restoring to equal prominence the second aspect of labour processes as forms of social appropriation of nature (Benton 1989). (Braverman 1974)
  • 这种新视角的前景在于，它能够将社会正义和环境正义问题联合起来进行理论化和政治实践，为“红绿”和解开辟道路，并将地方性的环境斗争与全球政治项目联系起来。
    In this respect, Castree (1995) envisions ecological Marxism as the analytical thread for an ecological socialist politics... anticipating Harvey’s call to find ways to connect ‘militant particularism’ and global ambition (Harvey 1996).
  • 作者指出，近期农业食品研究中出现了一种反思性转向，开始关注消费、食品质量，并探索ANT、惯例理论等新视角，以赋予自然分析上的显着性。
    It is particularly encouraging, therefore, to see the growing engagement in agro-food studies with theories which urge that ‘the status of nature,’ as Marsden et al. (2000) put it, be reconsidered. This reflexive ‘turn’... includes work on consumption and food quality (Lockie and Kitto 2000), and parallel explorations of ant (Busch and Juska 1997; Murdoch 1997a,b; Whatmore 1997; Goodman 1999), convention theory (Wilkinson 1997; Murdoch and Miele 1999) and, most recently, of complementarities between these two perspectives (Marsden et al. 2000).
  • Benton认为，社会学学科的建制性概念结构本身就排除了关于社会与其“自然”或“物质”基础之间关系的问题，这种二元思维模式是深刻的组织性范畴，无法轻易移除。
    As Benton (1994) observes... “...this weakness of sociology is more than a contingent fact... Rather, the conceptual structure or ‘disciplinary matrix’ by which sociology came to define itself . . . effectively excluded... questions about relations between society and its ‘natural’ or ‘material’ substrate” (p. 29).

Actor-Network Theory and relational materialism

本节将行动者网络理论（ANT）作为一种激进的本体论替代方案进行介绍和评估。与生态马克思主义从内部重构不同，ANT从根本上拒绝了任何先验的范畴划分（如自然/社会、能动者/结构）。作者阐述了ANT的核心理念：世界是由人类与非人类行动者通过“异质性工程”共同构建的“网络”或“混合集体”；分析者必须遵循“广义对称性”原则，平等地对待所有行动者。ANT的“关系唯物主义”强调能动性是网络的集体效应，而非人类的特权。随后，作者讨论了对ANT的主要批评——即其对称性原则导致丧失规范性立场和批判能力。然而，作者反驳了这种观点，认为ANT本身内含一种“关系伦理”，通过揭示我们与非人类共同构建世界的相互纠缠，它开启了一种后人类主义的批判模式，这种模式不再将人类作为万物的尺度，而是聚焦于特定“人-非人”集合体的构成、后果及其权力关系，从而为介入生物政治提供了更有力的批判工具。

  • 作者指出，农业食品研究领域内的反思性转向，选择性地回避了生态马克思主义的内部重构路径。
    What might be termed the internal path of reconstruction, as taken by Benton (1987, 1992, 1993), O’Connor (1998) and others in ecological Marxism, so far has been spurned by the incipient reflexive ‘turn’...
  • ANT提出，诸如自然/社会或结构/能动性等本体论范畴不能先验地定义，而是异质网络建构过程的事后“效应”。
    ant elaborates a set of heuristic concepts... to propose that ontological categories, such as nature/society or structure/agency, cannot be theorized and defined a priori. ...such ‘purified’ categories... are outcomes of network-building processes. (Law 1994, p. 12)
  • Latour关注社会与物质交换属性的“盲点”，旨在揭示被动员到异质网络中的实体的社会-物质性。
    In rejecting the purified or fetishized categories of ‘nature’ and ‘society,’ Latour (1994) focuses on “the blind spot where society and matter exchange properties” (p. 41).
  • 在网络形成过程中，人与非人属性的不断交换创造了“自然与文化的混合体”。
    The constant interchange of human and non-human properties in network formation has created “. . . mixtures between two entirely new types of being, hybrids of nature and culture” (Latour 1993, p. 10).
  • ANT超越了仅仅关注人类行动的物质基础，其前提是能动性总是一种集体的、网络化的结果，由非二元的社会-物质组合来执行。
    ant goes beyond attentiveness to the material foundation of human action. Its premise is that agency is always a collective, networked outcome, performed by nondualist sociomaterial associations. (Callon and Law 1995, p. 502)
  • Law强调ANT的独特性在于其“关系唯物主义”，若未能迈出这一步，分析将退回到不对称的社会建构论，最终回到普罗米修斯式的劳动过程领域。
    As Law (1992) argues, materialism and social relations interact in other sociologies but what distinguishes ant is its relational materialism. ... The analysis thus reverts to the terrain of instrumental transformative labour processes and the Promethean contours explored by Benton (1989). (Murdoch 1997a,b)
  • Haraway批判了那种将自然视为基质或产品，而将人视为唯一能动者的“中介理论”，呼吁开启一场人类不再是万物尺度的对话。
    Haraway (1992) raises similar issues in her critique of Young (1985) for adopting “a theory of mediations between man and nature,” in which “nature could only be a matrix or product, while man had to be the sole agent” (p. 83).
  • ANT因其坚持本体论对称性而受到批评，被指责丧失了规范性立场和批判能力，是一种“扁平化世界”的分析策略。
    Shapiro (1997), for example, objects that ant’s insistence on ontological symmetry involves the loss of normative positioning. ... McClellan (1996), echoing an earlier commentary by Collins and Yearly (1992), singles out Latour... to criticize his use of the principle of symmetry to “flatten the world”... (Lee and Brown 1994; Koch 1995)
  • 一些学者在ANT对人与非人之间“可渗透边界”的强调中，看到了一种伦理立场和关系道德哲学。
    ...they discern an ethical situatedness, a relational moral philosophy, in ant’s emphasis on the construction of worlds through the interchange of properties... (Pels 1996, p. 297; Whatmore 1997; Kovach 2000)
  • Murdoch等人认为ANT的真正价值在于尝试将一套新的伦理关怀融入社会科学方法中，它虽非传统意义上的“批判理论”，却执行了一项关键的批判任务。
    ...Murdoch (1997b) follows Koch (1995) in concluding that “the true value” of ant lies in the attempt “to enshrine a new set of ethical concerns in social scientific approaches to science and nature.” ...ant does not qualify as a ‘critical theory’ but we should recognize that it performs a critical task . . .” (Murdoch 1997b, p. 753).
  • Pickering认为，后人类主义将我们的注意力引向人与非人的多重交织，批判可以指向特定的人/非人集合体，而不必局限于传统的人文主义轴线。
    From a posthumanist perspective, however, as Pickering (1997) argues, “it is no longer clear that critique and reflection have to move along this axis. Posthumanism directs our attention to the multiple intertwinings of the human and nonhuman, and critique might, for example, direct itself towards specific human/nonhuman assemblages . . .” (p. 191).
  • FitzSimmons指出，将自然外在化和抽象化，为一套支配性话语提供了权威来源，这既是对自然的支配，也是自然对人类现实的支配。
    Challenging radical geographies... FitzSimmons urges that “We must recognize that externalised, abstracted, Nature-made-primordial provides a source of authority to a whole language of domination. This is the domination of nature, but also the domination of human reality by Nature” (ibid, p. 109, original emphasis). (FitzSimmons 1989a)
  • 通过对强大网络如何组装和维持的“厚描”，ANT提供了理解如何解构这些“转译政体”并重构权力的概念工具。
    ...ant provides conceptual tools to understand how these “translation regimes” (Callon 1991) might be dismantled – identifying the “anti-programme” (Latour 1992) – and power reconfigured. (Haraway 1997)
