Towards a Process-Relational Approach to Farming: Conceptual Openings from the Ontological Turn

Ika Darnhofer

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，传统农村社会学，尽管在批判农业现代化方面取得了成就，但其理论根基仍深陷于启蒙运动的二元论（如社会/自然、主体/客体、心智/物质）之中，这限制了其对农业复杂性的理解。作者主张进行一次深刻的“本体论转向”，构建一种“过程-关系”的理论框架来重新概念化农业。此框架整合了后人文主义、行动者网络理论（ANT）、生成理论（assemblage thinking）和新唯物主义，旨在将农业理解为一个由人类与非人类行动者（包括农民、动物、土壤、技术、情感等）共同构成的、不断生成、动态演化且充满不确定性的异质性集合体（assemblage）。作者认为，这种本体论的重构不仅是一项学术任务，更是一种政治实践，其目的是揭示被主流科学话语所遮蔽的、多元且更具可持续性的农业未来的可能性。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据完全建立在理论思辨和文献综合之上，而非一手经验数据。作者通过梳理庞大的哲学与社会理论文献来支撑其论点。主要论据包括：第一，追溯笛卡尔和牛顿的哲学遗产，揭示现代科学世界观的本体论预设及其对农业研究的深远影响。第二，系统性地评述了社会科学中的各种“转向”（如语言转向、文化转向、实践转向），并重点阐述了“本体论转向”如何从根本上挑战现代性的基础。第三，深入剖析了后现代主义、后结构主义、批判实在论和后人文主义等思潮对主客二分、人类中心主义的批判。第四，详细引介并综合了四个核心理论工具——行动者网络理论（ANT）、生成理论、新唯物主义和关系社会学，并援引它们在农业食品研究中的应用案例，以证明这些理论在分析社会-物质混合现象时的有效性。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证过程循序渐进，逻辑清晰。首先，作者界定问题，指出传统农村社会学在面对生态挑战时，因固守“社会”与“自然”的边界而显得力不从心。接着，作者通过历史溯源，深入剖析了现代性科学观的本体论根基，为后续的批判和重构奠定基础。然后，文章系统地梳理了以“本体论转向”为核心的各种“后”思潮，解构了现代性的核心二元对立，并引出了“能动性的物质”（agentic matter）和“过程性生成”（processual becoming）等关键概念。在此基础上，作者精选并阐释了ANT、生成理论等四个理论，将其作为构建新框架的基石。最后，作者明确提出了“过程-关系”方法论的三个核心主张（后人类主体与能动性物质、作为展开过程的农业、作为开放生成的涌现），并将这一宏大的理论建构与自己已发表的三篇具体研究论文相连接，展示了该框架的分析潜力。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的核心研究方法是批判性理论综合（critical theoretical synthesis）。作者并未收集和分析新的经验材料，而是通过对现有理论和哲学文献的深度阅读、批判性分析和创造性综合，构建了一个全新的、用于理解农业的理论框架。此方法涉及对不同理论流派（从启蒙哲学到当代后人文主义）的核心概念进行辨析、比较和整合，最终形成一个具有内在一致性和解释力的理论体系。这是一种概念驱动而非数据驱动的研究方法。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

尽管全文以理论建构为主，但“农业”本身就是贯穿全文的核心案例。为了使抽象的理论具体化，作者在论证过程中穿插了多个农业领域的实例。例如，在讨论农村社会学时，提到了对“好农民”概念的研究、替代性食物网络以及家庭农场动态。在介绍ANT和生成理论时，引用了关于英国口蹄疫危机、新西兰奇异果产业、苹果园技术以及瑞士奶酪生产的研究。这些案例的作用在于阐释“过程-关系”视角相比传统方法，如何能更深刻地揭示农业实践中人与物交织的复杂动态、不确定性和多重可能性。

family farming is much more than an economic activity that uses technology and modern agronomic sciences to harness natural processes in order to produce food and fibre. They focus on family farms as a social system, highlighting that household dynamics, the values and perceptions of family members, as well as wider social norms need to be taken into account (Oedl-Wieser & Schmitt, 2017; Schallberger, 1999; Vogel & Wiesinger, 2003). They have studied a number of aspects related to the persistence of family farms, including what enables succession (Suess- Reyes & Fuetsch, 2016); the persistence of gender inequities (Contzen & Forney, 2017); how farmers’ habitus predisposes them to act in particular ways (Shucksmith, 1993); whether social norms around the ‘good farmer’ (Burton, 2004) may constrain options which are perceived as feasible; or the role of autonomy in ensuring the attractiveness of farming as a vocation (Stock & Forney, 2014)

Many studies build on the implicit assumption that policies have predictable effects, and thus that the world can be gently and gradually nudged in the ‘right’ direction, e.g. one that is more environmentally friendly and socially equitable. This contrasts with the view of the world as volatile, uncertain, complex and ambiguous (a ‘VUCA world’). This characterisation of a world that changes quickly and often unpredictably is also found in Bauman's (2000) ‘liquid modernity’; or in Rosa (2017) who characterised our society as one which systematically requires growth, innovation and acceleration.

the ASSUMPTION of a stable world: The approaches are thus built on an understanding of the world, whichv Law (2004, p. 145) characterised as: assuming a reality that is independent and prior to an observer; that is definite in shape and form; that is singular (there is only one reality); that is constant (there are general and invariant laws and processes and nothing changes unless it is caused to change); where objects are passive (they stay the same unless caused to change); and that is universal (causal links are generally the same in all possible locations). These (often implicit) assumptions have shaped the conceptualisation and operationalisation of farms: it sees fixities and seeks observable variables that are linked to mechanisms that ‘cause’ change.

the ASSUMPTION of resilience and complexity thinking: It not only emphasizes that change is ever present, it also emphasizes that change is often discontinuous, unpredictable, that surprises are inevitable. These surprises “occur when causes turn out to be sharply different than was conceived, when behaviours are profoundly unexpected, and when action produces a result opposite to that intended – in short, when perceived reality departs qualitatively from expectation” (Holling, 1986, p. 71, italics in original). Thus while the dominant notion of stability “emphasizes equilibrium, low variability, and resistance to and absorption of change”, resilience promotes a different understanding, one that emphasizes “high variability, and adaptation to change” (Holling, 1986, p. 76). Resilience is thus about “cultivating the capacity to sustain development in the face of expected and surprising change and diverse pathways of development and potential thresholds between them”, as such it includes persistence, adaptability and transformability (Folke, 2016, p. 1).

change is ASSUMED to be reactive: in rural sociology, change on farms is often understood as driven by the pressures of modernisation, globalisation, rationalisation, which are countered by various forms of farmers’ resistance, e.g. through their engagement in organic farming or alternative food networks.

本章首先将农村社会学定位为一个与现实世界紧密相连的“介入性科学”，其发展历程深受农业现代化进程的影响。随后，文章指出农村社会学逐渐认识到传统经济理性主义无法完全解释农民行为，并开始关注农业的多功能性和乡村空间的物质维度。最终，本章强调，理解农业作为一种社会与自然实体混合的“杂合体”（hybrid），需要超越主客二分的传统理论，采用能够同时严肃对待社会与自然的、关注异质性实体关系的全新视角。

第二章：本体论至关重要 (Ontology matters)

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章深入探讨了本文的核心主题：本体论。首先，文章追溯了自笛卡尔和牛顿以来，西方现代科学世界观的本体论遗产，即心物二元、物质惰性、机械因果等深刻影响我们思维方式的预设。接着，文章评述了农村社会学内部的各种“转向”（如文化转向、实践转向），认为它们虽有贡献，但大多未触及根本的本体论问题。随后，文章聚焦于以各种“后”思潮（后现代、后结构、后人文主义等）为标志的“本体论转向”，阐述其如何通过瓦解二元对立、强调物质的能动性（agency）和世界的“关系性生成”（relational becoming）来颠覆现代性。最后，本章讨论了这种本体论转向对研究实践的深刻意涵，即从“再现”（representation）转向“展演”（performativity），强调研究本身就是一种介入和创造世界的活动，从而提出了一种“后定性研究”的取向。

  • 对Overton观点的总结：作者指出，我们使用的许多概念源于启蒙运动，并深受17世纪哲学家笛卡尔的影响，他将世界划分为心/物、主/客等二元对立的独立元素。
    The dominant worldview in modernist western sciences builds on the 17th century philosopher René Descartes, who split the world into dichotomous independent elements (Overton, 2015, p. 16).
  • 对Robinson观点的总结：作者在脚注中补充，心物二元论的历史可追溯至古希腊，尤其是柏拉图，但其现代版本主要源于笛卡尔的《沉思录》。
    The history of dualism between mind and body goes back to the ancient Greeks, not least of which Plato. (Robinson, 2020)

    In modern western culture, matter is still seen as essentially passive stuff, which can be set in motion by human agents; in other words, matter is inherently void of agency and meaning (Coole & Frost, 2010).

  • 对Braidotti和Ferrando观点的总结：后人文主义旨在克服植根于西方思想中人类优于非人类的等级观念，通过挑战只有人类才具有能动性的基本假设，从而质疑了社会科学以人类行动为中心的焦点。

  • 对DeLanda和Deleuze & Guattari观点的总结：文章引入了“能动性”（agency）和“情感”（affect）的概念，指出所有物质都具有“影响与被影响的能力”，这种能力并非意愿性的，而是物质本身固有的。
    Rather, all matter is seen as ‘affective’, i.e. it possesses a ‘capacity to affect and be affected’ (DeLanda, 2006a, p. 4; Deleuze & Guattari, 1987, pp. 127–128).
  • 对Barad观点的总结：作者引用Karen Barad的观点来总结这种本体论转向对研究实践的影响，即“知晓的实践与存在的实践不可分割；我们之所以能知晓，是因为我们属于这个世界”，这构成了“本体-认识论”（Onto-epistem-ology）的基础。
    “Practices of knowing and being are not isolable; they are mutually implicated. We don’t obtain knowledge by standing outside the world; we know because we are of the world. We are part of the world in its differential becoming.” (Barad, 2007, p. 185, italics in original)

本章详细介绍了构建“过程-关系”框架的四个核心理论支柱：行动者网络理论（ANT）、生成理论（assemblage thinking）、新唯物主义和关系社会学。对于每一种理论，文章都阐述了其核心概念、本体论和认识论承诺，并结合农业食品领域的研究案例来说明其分析效用。ANT和生成理论被用来强调社会-物质的交织性、非人类的能动性以及网络的持续建构过程。新唯物主义则进一步深化了对物质能动性、活力和情感（affect）的理解，挑战了人类中心主义。关系社会学，特别是其过程-关系分支，则为理解社会现象如何由持续变化的关系而非静态实体构成提供了理论基础。本章旨在展示这些理论如何共同为一种超越传统社会学范式的、关注动态生成过程的农业研究铺平道路。

  • 对Latour观点的总结：作者阐释了行动者网络理论（ANT）的核心思想，即“社会”并非一个独立的实在领域，社会学的任务是解释异质性元素（物理、生物、经济等）如何被组装成一个网络。
    As a result, ‘the social’ is not seen as a distinct domain of reality (Latour, 2005, p. 4), and with it comes a dissolution of the concepts of ‘social structure’ and ‘underlying mechanisms’.
  • 对Noe & Alrøe观点的总结：在农业背景下，ANT的应用让我们可以将农场视为一个由土壤、机械、动物、人、知识、法规等多种实体构成的异质性系统。
    When Noe and Alrøe (2006) propose to understand a farm as an actor-network, it allows them to highlight that a farm is a heterogeneous system composed of many different kinds of entities...
  • 对Singleton & Law观点的总结：作者引用此研究说明，事物的稳定性（如“一头小牛就是一头小牛”）是通过重复性的实践来维持的；没有这些实践，事物就不会保持稳定。
    if things “are stable this is because the practices themselves are repetitive. Without such repetitions things don’t hold steady” (Singleton & Law, 2013, p. 262).
  • 对DeLanda观点的总结：作者介绍了生成理论的一个核心概念——关系的外部性，即组件可以从一个生成体中分离出来并插入另一个，这意味着生成体并非无缝的整体，其组件具有一定的自主性。
    For DeLanda (2006b, p. 253) the key idea in Deleuze’s theory is the exteriority of relations...
  • 对Baker & McGuirk观点的总结：生成理论强调了将异质元素聚集、排列并建立联系的持续性“劳动”，关注的是组合过程中的能动性、涌现性、多样性和不确定性。
    it emphasizes temporality, i.e. how past associations inform the present; (2) as an assemblage connotes groups or collectives, agency is seen as distributed... (see Anderson & MacFarlane, 2011; Baker & McGuirk, 2017; McFarlane, 2009)
  • 对Bennett观点的总结：作者引用Bennett对新唯物主义的阐述，指出物质具有“活力”，即事物（如食物、风暴、金属）不仅能阻碍人类的意图，还能作为拥有自身轨迹和倾向的准行动者。
    New materialism conceives of matter as “possessing its own modes of self-transformation, self-organization, and directedness, and thus no longer as simply passive and inert” (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 10). Materials are seen as having vitality, by which Bennett (2010, p. viii) means “the capacity of things... to act as quasi agents or forces with trajectories, propensities, or tendencies of their own.”
  • 对Alaimo & Hekman观点的总结：新唯物主义认为，自然是一股能动的力量，它与其他元素（文化、历史、话语、技术）相互作用，其行动对人类和非人类世界都产生后果。
    Nature is seen as an agentic force that interacts with the other elements (culture, history, discourse, technology). Nature acts and these actions have consequences for both the human and nonhuman world (Alaimo & Heikman, 2008, p. 5).
  • 对Donati观点的总结：作者介绍了关系社会学中的一种“关系实在论”观点，即社会并非“包含”关系，而是其本身就是关系构成的，关系是介于能动性与社会结构之间的连接器。
    Donati has developed a ‘relational realist’ theory of society, where the basic idea is that society is not a space ‘containing’ relations, rather relations are the very stuff of what we call ‘the social’... (Donati, 2015, p. 87, italics in original).
  • 对Dépelteau观点的总结：文章对比介绍了另一种更彻底的过程-关系社会学，它建立在Emirbayer的思想之上，认为实体本身就是由关系构成的，不存在离散的、预先给定的分析起点。
    Dépelteau, building on Emirbayer (1997), proposes a relational sociology which fully adopts a process-relational philosophy. Here, the entities themselves are constituted by relations.
  • 对Lenco, Deleuze & Guattari观点的总结：通过对比国际象棋和围棋的例子，阐明了过程-关系社会学的核心思想：实体的特性并非内在固有的（如象棋棋子），而是完全由其在关系网络中的位置决定的（如围棋棋子）。
    To illustrate this process-relational approach, Lenco (2018) builds on the example provided by Deleuze and Guattari (1987, pp. 352-3) and compares two board games: chess and Go.

第四章：贡献 (Contributions)

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章是论文的核心贡献部分。作者首先系统地综合了前述理论，凝练出“过程-关系”方法论的三个核心 conceptual moves：1) 将农民重构为“后人类主体”，并将物质视为具有能动性；2) 将农业理解为不断展开的过程，而非静态实体；3) 强调这些过程是涌现的、开放的“生成”。这套理论旨在瓦解现代主义科学观下那个可被预测和控制的“科学可知农场”形象。随后，作者简要回顾了自己已发表的三篇论文，将它们定位为这一宏大理论框架在具体研究议题（如变革的可见性、农业韧性、有机部门动态）中的应用与探索，从而论证了该框架的分析潜力与实践价值。

  • 对Campbell观点的总结：作者指出，科学在稳定和捍卫“现代主义农场”中扮演了角色。本研究旨在通过提出“过程-关系”方法，来打破这种由机械关系塑造的、技术上可定义、经济上可控的农场形象。
    This points towards the role of science in stabilizing and defending the modernist farm (see Campbell, 2020). Indeed, as researchers, we are active participants in the world we are studying; we thus play a role in enacting modernist farming, or in disrupting it (Campbell, 2020, p. 28).
  • 对Braidotti观点的总结：在阐述第一个核心观点时，作者引用Braidotti对后人类主体的定义，即主体并非统一或自主的，而是“具身的、嵌入的、关系的和情感性的协作实体”。
    It builds on the notion of posthuman subjects, which Braidotti (2019b, p. 46) defines as neither unitary nor autonomous, but as “embodied and embedded, relational and affective collaborative entities”.
  • 对Coole & Frost, Haraway, Pickering观点的总结：作者综合这些观点，强调农业是一种由人、生物和自动装置组成的纠缠和协作，人类是在“与…共同行动”（acting-with）。
    Building on the new materialist conception of matter as having agential qualities (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 7), farming becomes a collaborative endeavour. Farming is an entanglement, a collaboration of people, critters, and automatons (see Haraway, 2016). Humans are understood as ‘acting-with’ (Pickering, 2021).
  • 对Bennett观点的总结：作者引用Bennett的观点，指出“人类”能动性本身就是一个根本性的集体、多物种的共同努力的结果，从而强调能动性在整个农业生成体中的分布性。
    In other words: “‘Human’ agency is itself always a radically collective, multi-specied endeavor effect” (Bennett, 2015, p. 86, italics in original).
  • 对Barad和Latour观点的总结：在阐述第二个核心观点时，作者引用Barad，强调要“给予物质其作为世界生成过程中积极参与者的应有地位”，并引用Latour，指出研究视角应从物质“是什么”转向其潜能和作用。
    By giving “matter its due as an active participant in the world’s becoming” (Barad, 2007:136), the image of a farm, of crops, animals, buildings, machines as passive ‘things’ shaped by human will shifts towards farming as a lively assemblage of heterogeneous and interacting processes. This invites a shift from understanding matter in terms of what it is, towards examining its potentialities and the ways in which it makes possible, mediates, supports, prevents, resists our actions (Latour, 2004).
  • 对Law观点的总结：在阐述第三个核心观点时，作者引用Law的观点，认为现实是复杂的、流动的、不确定的、多重的和零散的，以此来强调变化过程的涌现性和不可预测性。
    Reality is thus understood as complex, fluid, indeterminate, multiple, fractional (Law, 2004).

第五章：代结论：未来的开放性 (In lieu of a conclusion: future openings)

大意、细节与原文引用 (Main Idea, Details & Original Quotes) 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类) (Cited Literature (Categorized by Argument Theme))

本章作为结论，并非对研究进行封闭性总结，而是指向未来的开放性。作者重申，“过程-关系”方法论的核心在于转变视角——从关注人类认知意愿转向关注具身体验与情感，从承认人类中心主义转向捕捉更广泛的（非）人类能动性，从聚焦静态结构转向描绘动态的生成过程。这种转变不仅是概念上的，更对未来的研究方法提出了挑战。作者呼吁研究者要勇于实验，探索能够捕捉并呈现这些流动过程与物质能动性的新方法。最终，研究的目标不再是“再现”一个客观真理，而是积极参与到“让不同的世界成为可能和可见”的实践中。这是一种对研究者自身创造力的呼唤，旨在通过学术写作激发新的思维、感知和行动方式，从而不断揭示“事情本可以不同”的多种可能性。

  • 对核心论点的总结与升华：作者最后强调，采纳过程-关系方法论是对研究者自身的一次呼吁，要求我们变得实验性和大胆，去拥抱变化、运动和惊喜，并通过我们的研究去影响、展演和行动。
    Following a process-relational approach is thus a call to be experimental and daring. It is about change, movement, surprise; about affecting, enacting, doing; about a vibrant, vital, ever-unfinished becoming.
