Framing Agri-Digital Governance: Industry Stakeholders, Technological Frames and Smart Farming Implementation

Higgins and Bryant

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，在农业数字化治理（agri-digital governance）的背景下，中观尺度行动者（meso-scale actors）对智能农业技术的阐释框架（technological frames）远比现有文献所描述的“框架不一致（frame incongruence）”更为复杂。作者通过引入“框架矛盾（frame ambivalence）”和“框架转换（frame switching）”这两个新的分析概念，批判性地指出不同的框架关系对产业主权（industry sovereignty）产生着多样化的、甚至相悖的影响。文章进一步论证，增强产业主权（例如，通过维持现状的框架矛盾和转移责任的框架转换）的框架动态，可能并不会促进，甚至会限制顾问和推广人员个体层面的“数字能动性（digital agency）”，从而揭示了在智能农业推行过程中一个深刻的内在张力。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

本文的论据建立在对澳大利亚新南威尔士州水稻产业的质性研究之上。研究者对20位中观尺度的“产业利益相关者”进行了半结构化访谈，这些受访者涵盖了管理者、咨询顾问、农艺师和研究人员。通过对这些一手访谈资料的深入编码和主题分析，作者提炼出三个核心的框架领域（core domains）：技术推广（technology extension）、技术优先级（technology priorities）和技术可采纳性（technology adoptability）。在这三个领域中浮现出的不同阐释、矛盾态度和归因转换，构成了支撑其核心论点的主要经验证据。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

文章的论证逻辑层次清晰。首先，作者回顾了农村社会学对智能农业的批判（主要集中于宏观的政治经济和微观的农民适应），并精准定位了研究空白——对中观尺度行动者的关注不足。其次，文章系统梳理了“技术框架”理论，特别是源自技术社会建构（SCOT）和信息系统研究的Orlikowski与Gash的模型，但批判性地指出该理论过分聚焦于“框架一致性”问题。随后，通过对访谈数据的分析，文章在“技术推广”领域验证了传统的“框架不一致”现象；接着，在“技术优先级”领域创新性地识别出“框架矛盾”；在“技术可采纳性”领域则发现了“框架转换”的行为。最后，在讨论部分，文章将这三种框架关系与Carolan的“产业主权”概念进行勾连，系统阐释了每种关系如何以不同方式（或增强或削弱）产业对技术变革方向的控制力，并最终揭示了产业主权与个体数字能动性之间的复杂张力，从而完成了从经验发现到理论贡献的升华。

4. 方法 (Method)

研究采用了一种社会建构主义（social constructionist）的研究范式，视访谈为共同建构现实的互动事件。核心方法是质性研究中的半结构化访谈（semi-structured interviews）。研究者运用了混合抽样策略，结合了立意抽样（purposive sampling）以确定关键信息提供者和滚雪球抽样（snowball sampling）以拓展参与者网络。数据分析遵循了严谨的质性分析流程，包括开放编码（open coding）、主轴编码（axial coding）以及最终的主题分析（thematic analysis），从而系统性地从访谈文本中归纳出三个核心的技术框架领域。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文的案例研究聚焦于澳大利亚的水稻产业。这是一个高度依赖灌溉、地理位置集中且产业结构垂直整合的特定农业部门。该案例的选择具有典型性，因为它正经历着公共农业推广服务削减、向私营农艺师咨询模式转型的制度变迁。这一背景凸显了中观尺度行动者在技术推广中的关键“隘口”作用。水资源短缺（如“千年干旱”）等环境压力也使得技术选择（如节水品种 vs. 精准农业）的讨论变得尤为迫切，为观察和分析不同技术框架的冲突、矛盾与转换提供了绝佳的场域。

Introduction

本章首先定义了“智能农业”及其主流论述中的“双赢”前景，即同时解决生产力、粮食安全和环境问题。随后，迅速转向农村社会学的批判视角，指出智能农业带来了权力不对等、信息商品化和数据主权等问题。在此基础上，作者精准地识别出现有研究的空白：以往研究多聚焦于宏观（大公司与农民）和微观（农民的适应策略）层面，而忽视了负责技术实施的中观尺度行动者（meso-scale actors），如农业服务提供者和农艺师。最后，本章提出以“技术框架”（technological frames）理论为分析工具，旨在探究这些中观行动者如何构建对智能农业的阐释，以及这些框架如何影响产业对技术变革的“主权”（sovereignty）。

  • 对智能农业的定义: 作者引用了多个来源来界定智能农业，即应用信息通信技术（ICTs）以更有效地管理农场系统，涵盖传感器、机器人、大数据等技术。
    In encompassing data-driven and data-enabled technologies such as sensors, robotics, drones, global positioning systems (GPS) and the use of Big Data, smart farming extends ‘conventional tools (e.g., rain gauge, tractor, notebook) by adding autonomous context-awareness by all kinds of sensors, built-in intelligence, capable to execute autonomous actions or doing this remotely’ (Wolfert et al. 2017, p. 70).
  • 智能农业的“双赢”论述: 引用文献指出，科学家和政策制定者将智能农业视为解决农业生产力、全球粮食安全和环境影响等多种挑战的双赢方案。
    In doing so, smart farming technologies are viewed by scientists and policymakers as a win-win solution to diverse challenges such as farm productivity, global food security and the environmental impacts of farming (Bongiovanni and Lowenberg-DeBoer 2004; Oliver 2013; Talebpour et al. 2015; Lindblom et al. 2017).
  • 农村社会学对权力关系的批判: 引入社会学视角，指出智能农业可能加剧大型公司与农民之间不平等的权力关系。
    For example, scholars have drawn attention to the implications of smart farming in terms of the development of unequal power relationships between large corporations and farmers (Wolf and Wood 1997; Bronson and Knezevic 2016; Carolan 2017).
  • 对信息商品化的批判: 指出上述不平等关系可能导致信息商品化问题。
    These unequal relationships can contribute to commodification of information (Wolf and Buttel 1996; Wolf and Wood 1997), transfer of data ownership from farmers to digital agricultural companies leading to associated issues of data access (Bronson and Knezevic 2016; Carolan 2018), and problems with data privacy and security (Rotz et al. 2019).
  • 农民层面的适应与协商策略: 提及社会学家也研究了农民如何通过实验和修补（tinkering）来协商不对称的权力关系，使技术在地方上可行。
    Sociologists have also investigated how farmers negotiate asymmetrical power relations and find novel strategies to make smart farming technology locally workable through experimentation (Carolan 2018) and tinkering (Higgins et al. 2017).
  • 中观行动者的核心作用: 指出中观行动者，特别是农场顾问，在管理农民实施智能农业相关的不确定性方面扮演着核心角色。
    Meso-scale actors – and farm advisors in particular – are increasingly recognised as playing a potentially central role in smart farming, especially in managing farmer uncertainty associated with its implementation (Eastwood et al. 2017; Rijswijk et al. 2019).
  • 对顾问角色的挑战: 强调智能农业对农技推广和顾问人员的常规实践构成了重大转变，带来了提供有效支持的挑战。
    Smart farming technology, however, represents a substantial shift from routine practices for those in farm extension and advisory positions (Nettle et al. 2018) and this poses challenges in providing effective support to farmers.
  • 顾问的“意义构建者”角色: 引用文献将顾问的角色定位为帮助农民从智能农业工具中实现更大价值的“意义构建者”（sensemakers），而非简单的技术推广者或障碍。
    it is argued that advisors play an important role as ‘sensemakers’ (Eastwood et al. 2019a) in helping farmers achieve greater value from smart farming tools.
  • 提升顾问的“数字能动性”: 强调通过协同设计和合作来支持农业知识提供者的“数字能动性”或“数字领悟力”（digi-grasping）是当务之急。
    As such, supporting agricultural knowledge providers’ ‘digital agency’ or ‘digi-grasping’ capacities through processes of co-design and collaboration is considered to be a priority so that they are better able ‘to both recognise and act upon the systemic nature of digital innovation in smart farming’ (Ayre et al. 2019, p. 9; see also Rijswijk et al. 2019).
  • 现有研究的局限性: 指出现有关于中观行动者的研究尚未系统地与更广泛的社会学辩论相结合，特别是关于行动者如何在特定行业内协商技术实施，以及个体能动性如何与产业主权问题相关联。
    However, this research has not yet engaged in a systematic way with existing sociological debates concerning the ways in which actors seek to navigate and negotiate smart farming implementation within specific industries or commodity sectors, and how facilitating or constraining individual agency is linked to broader questions of sovereignty for those industries.
  • 引入“产业主权”概念: 明确本文中“主权”的定义，即农业产业或社区对其数据景观进行民主控制，从而规划自身未来的能力。
    We define sovereignty in this article as being the capacity of farming industries or communities to exercise ‘democratic control of datascapes so as to empower [them] to chart their own futures and define their aims’ (Carolan 2018, p. 759).
  • 技术框架理论的适用性: 解释为何选择技术框架理论，因为它能帮助识别中观行动者理解智能农业技术的诠释图式，这些图式塑造了他们后续的行动。
    A theoretical focus on frames and framing provides a conceptually coherent approach for engaging with these questions by enabling researchers to identify the interpretative schemas through which meso-scale actors develop ‘particular assumptions, expectations, and knowledge’ of smart farming technology, ‘which then serves to shape subsequent actions toward it’ (Orlikowski and Gash 1994, p. 175).
  • 选择互动主义的框架方法: 说明选择技术框架方法是因为它符合社会学中更具“互动性”的框架研究路径，并明确将技术视为核心构成部分。
    While there are numerous theoretical approaches to the construct of framing (see e.g., Dewulf et al. 2009), we have selected a technological frames approach as it accords with a more sociological ‘interactional’ approach to framing (Dewulf et al. 2009), and it explicitly recognises technology as a core component in the construction of interpretative schemas across different domains.
  • 技术框架的三个领域: 介绍Orlikowski和Gash提出的技术框架所涵盖的三个领域：技术本质、技术策略和使用中的技术。
    These domains include: the nature of technology including its capabilities and functionality; technology strategy, which includes the likely value of the technology to the organisation or industry; and, technology in use, which refers to the likely or actual conditions and contexts associated with how technology will be used (Orlikowski and Gash 1994, pp. 183–184).

Technological frames

本章系统地阐述了本文的核心理论工具——“技术框架”。它首先追溯了“框架”概念的社会学起源，归功于Goffman，并强调框架不仅是个人理解世界的图式，也是动员他人行动的工具。接着，文章聚焦于“技术框架”这一特定概念，指出其源于技术的社会建构（SCOT）理论，并在信息系统文献中得到广泛发展和应用。作者详细界定了Orlikowski和Gash的技术框架定义，并阐述了其两个关键特征：组织意义和情境灵活性。最后，本章重点讨论了“框架一致性”（frame congruence）的概念，认为它是技术成功实施的关键，并列举了达成一致的四种方式。同时，作者批判性地指出，仅仅发现“框架不一致性”（frame incongruence）是不够的，研究需要超越这一起点，探索不同框架之间可能产生的更复杂效应及其对产业主权的影响。

  • 框架理论的起源: 将框架理论追溯至Goffman，他认为框架是个人用以解读和理解社会情境的图式。
    Framing is largely attributed to the work of Goffman (1974, pp. 10–11) who proposes that frames are the schema that individuals develop and draw upon to interpret and make sense of social settings.
  • 框架的功能: 引用Benford和Snow，说明框架使事件变得有意义，从而组织经验并指导行动。
    Frames enable individuals to ‘render events or occurrences meaningful and thereby function to organize experience and guide action’ (Benford and Snow 2000, p. 614).
  • 框架与能动者的关系: 强调框架研究不仅关乎框架本身，还关乎框架与行动主体（agents）之间的关系，框架可被用来动员他人。
    As Kwan (2009, p. 26) argues, exploring frames is ‘not just about the frames themselves and the interplay between competing and complementary frames … they are also about the relationships between frames and agents’.
  • 技术框架的学术应用: 指出技术框架作为概念工具，在组织和农业产业中被广泛用于理解社会群体如何理解技术。
    Technological frames are widely recognised as a useful conceptual tool for understanding how members of social groups understand and attribute meaning to technological artefacts within organisations (e.g., Bijker 1995; Klein and Kleinman 2002; Davidson and Pai 2004; Lin and Silva 2005; Khoo and Hall 2013) and, significantly for this article, farming industries (Jakku and Thorburn 2010; Thorburn et al. 2011).
  • 技术框架的SCOT渊源: 阐明技术框架最初是在技术的社会建构（SCOT）方法中发展的，强调社会文化过程。
    Technological frames were originally developed within a social construction of technology (SCOT) approach (Pinch and Bijker 1987; Bijker 1995), in which emphasis is placed on the socio-cultural processes through which members of a social group develop interpretive schema around an artefact.
  • 信息系统领域的定义: 采用信息系统文献中Orlikowski和Gash对技术框架的权威定义，即组织成员用以理解技术的假设、期望和知识的子集。
    From this perspective, technological frames are conceptualised as: … that subset of members’ organizational frames that concern the assumptions, expectations, and knowledge they use to understand technology in organisations. (Orlikowski and Gash 1994, p. 178)
  • 技术框架的特征: 总结技术框架的两个关键特征：一是用于组织和赋予技术意义；二是具有诠释性、灵活性和情境特定性。
    Second, frames are ‘interpretative, flexible, and context-specific’ (Lin and Silva 2005, p. 50).
  • 框架一致性的重要性: 指出框架一致性，即不同群体框架在关键元素上的对齐，被认为是技术成功实施的核心。
    Frame congruence is argued to be central to the successful implementation of technology in organisations and industries (e.g., Lin and Silva 2005; Davidson 2006; Jakku and Thorburn 2010; Olesen 2014) by creating a set of ‘similar expectations around the role of technology in business processes, the nature of technological use, or the type and frequency of support and maintenance’ (Orlikowski and Gash 1994, p. 180).
  • 达成一致性的四种方式: 列举了文献中提到的达成框架一致性的四种途径，包括有效沟通、重塑框架、边界对象和强加主导框架。
    Congruence is typically achieved in one of four ways, through: (a) effective communication among organisational stakeholders (Orlikowski and Gash 1994); (b) reframing stakeholder’s existing technological frames (Lin and Silva 2005); (c) conceptualising technologies as ‘boundary objects’ that can facilitate co-learning so that those involved ‘arrive at an increasingly shared understanding of the problem’ (Jakku and Thorburn 2010, p. 677); and, (d) the imposition of a dominant frame that persuades other groups into accepting the dominant viewpoint (Davidson 2006; Olesen 2014).
  • 框架不一致性的后果: 说明框架不一致性会围绕技术的开发、实施和使用引发困难和冲突。
    In contrast, frame incongruence can inhibit implementation of technology by causing ‘difficulties and conflicts around developing, implementing, and using technologies’ (Orlikowski and Gash 1994, p. 180).
  • 对旧技术框架的依赖: 指出现有研究注意到，人们倾向于用与现有“旧”技术相关的框架来解释“新”技术。
    However, McLoughlin et al. (2000, p. 21) note that there is often a tendency to interpret ‘new technology through frames associated with existing “old” technologies’.
  • 超越“不一致性”的批判性立场: 引用Davidson和Pai的观点，认为仅仅指出不同群体想法不同会导致问题在长远来看并不令人满意，需要更深入的分析。
    However, we also acknowledge Davidson and Pai (2004, p. 486) who observe that ‘merely noting that different groups think differently about information technologies, and that differences can cause problems, is not very satisfying in the long run’.
  • 本文的研究目标: 明确指出本文旨在基于Carolan的研究，探索框架不一致性之外的其他可能效应，以及这些效应如何限制或赋予产业对技术变革的主权。
    As such, through our analysis we build on the work of Carolan (2017, 2018) to investigate, (a) other effects, in addition to incongruence, that may be possible from different technological frames, as well as (b) how these effects variously restrict or afford industry sovereignty over technological change, including smart farming technology implementation.

Research methodology

本章详细介绍了研究设计与执行过程。首先，作者界定了研究的地理和产业背景——澳大利亚新南威尔士州的Murrumbidgee和Murray河谷地区的水稻产业，并简要说明了其生产概况和对灌溉的依赖性。接着，作者明确了本研究的认识论立场为社会建构主义，并因此采用质性研究方法。研究核心是与20位来自四个职业群体的参与者进行的半结构化访谈。为了招募受访者，研究采用了立意抽样和滚雪球抽样相结合的混合策略。访谈内容围绕参与者在产业中的角色、对技术的看法、变革的障碍与驱动因素等展开。最后，作者阐述了数据分析的步骤，即通过开放编码、主轴编码和主题分析，最终从数据中提炼出构成参与者技术框架的三个核心领域。

  • 案例背景介绍: 描述了澳大利亚水稻产业的地理位置、农场数量、产量以及对灌溉水的高度依赖性。
    There are approximately 1,500 farms across these regions, which since 2011 have collectively produced an average of 900,000 tonnes of rice per annum (Clarke 2016).
  • 研究范式: 明确采纳社会建构主义方法，关注人类行动和互动如何构建现实。
    As the project was focused specifically on social interactions with technology, we adopted a social constructionist approach in which the focus is on understanding how ‘human action and interaction constitutes reality’ (Richey and Ravishankar 2017, p. 3).
  • 抽样策略: 描述了如何通过立意抽样识别关键信息提供者，并结合滚雪球抽样来扩大样本。
    First, purposive sampling was used to identify key informants across the different occupational groups (Patton 2015). Each of the key informants then provided opportunities to snowball sample through identification of further participants (Ritchie et al. 2014).
  • 访谈的性质: 将访谈视为“现实建构和互动的事件”，访谈者和参与者是共同建构者。
    As appropriate to the social constructionist approach, the interviews were treated as ‘reality-constructing and interactional events’ (Koro-Ljungberg 2008, p. 430) in which the interviewer and participant act as co-constructors.
  • 数据分析流程: 阐述了数据分析的编码过程，即开放编码、主轴编码，最终进行主题分析以得出主题。
    Data from the interviews were initially analysed using open coding, followed by axial coding to seek out possible contextual relationships between open codes (Miles et al. 2014). Finally, a thematic analysis (Hennink et al. 2011) was conducted across the 20 interview transcripts to derive a set of themes.
  • 分析结果: 明确指出分析产生了三个广泛主题——技术推广、技术优先级和技术可采纳性，这些主题构成了参与者技术框架的核心领域。
    Three broad themes were derived from this analysis – technology extension, technology priorities, and technology adoptability – which, similar to Orlikowski and Gash (1994, p. 183), were identified as forming ‘core domains of the participants’ technological frames’.

Technological frames in the Australia rice industry

本章是研究的核心发现部分，详细阐述了从访谈数据中提炼出的三个技术框架领域。第一领域是“技术推广”，揭示了关于商业农艺师在智能农业实施中角色的两种截然相反的框架，体现了典型的“框架不一致性”。第二领域是“技术优先级”，展现了利益相关者在应优先发展易于采纳的新品种，还是应加大投资推广更复杂的智能农业技术之间存在的犹豫和矛盾，作者将此现象识别为“框架矛盾”。第三领域是“技术可采纳性”，分析了利益相关者在解释智能农业采纳障碍时，如何在个体因素（如农民年龄和态度）和结构性因素（如缺乏技术支持、气候限制）之间来回切换归因，作者将此命名为“框架转换”。这部分通过大量引用原始访谈数据，生动地展示了这三种不同的框架关系。

  • 对案例背景的引用: 在讨论新品种的重要性时，引用了 Clarke 的报告以说明垂直整合的产业结构（由SunRice公司主导）如何通过提供有质量保证的种子来促进新品种的快速采纳。
    The primary commercial rice company located in the region (SunRice) annually contracts growers to trial new varieties on their farms, providing seed that is deemed to be clean and quality assured (Clarke 2016).

Discussion

本章对研究发现进行了深入的理论对话和阐释。首先，它回应了研究问题一，总结了研究识别出的三个框架领域及其内部存在的不同框架关系：技术推广领域的“不一致性”，技术优先级领域的“矛盾性”，以及技术可采纳性领域的“转换性”。作者强调，这项发现挑战了技术框架文献中对“框架不一致性”的过度关注。其次，文章创新性地阐释了“框架矛盾”和“框架转换”这两个概念，认为它们是理解中观行动者复杂性的新工具。接着，文章回应研究问题二，将三种框架关系与“产业主权”联系起来，分析了每种关系对产业控制技术变革方向的不同影响。例如，不一致性会阻碍实施，而矛盾性则可能在维持现状（从而保持控制）和推动变革间摇摆，转换性则通过转移责任来定位产业的控制范围。最后，文章提出了一个核心的洞见：那些最能维护产业主权的框架关系（矛盾和转换），可能恰恰不利于提升个体行动者的数字能动性，揭示了两者之间的潜在冲突。

  • 回应中观行动者研究: 指出本研究的发现为理解中观行动者在应对智能农业的多样性、复杂性和不确定性时所面临的挑战提供了进一步证据。
    Identification of these frames provides further evidence of the challenges experienced by meso-scale actors in ‘managing the diversity, complexity and uncertainty’ (Ayre et al. 2019, p. 2; see also Nettle et al. 2018) associated with smart farming technology.
  • 批判框架文献的“不一致性”偏见: 指出技术框架文献和智能农业的中观行动者研究都倾向于关注框架不一致性，并致力于达成统一的战略愿景。
    An emphasis on frame incongruence is also evident in the literature on meso-scale actors in smart farming, in which understandings of and responses to smart farming are characterised as ad-hoc (Rijswijk et al. 2019) and lacking in a shared and strategic vision (Fielke et al. 2019).
  • 提出整合性方法的局限: 认为现有文献提倡的系统性、整合性方法可能只对解决框架不一致性有效，而对处理框架矛盾或转换则效果不佳。
    While the development of a more systematic and integrated approach to smart farming advocated in the literature provides a way of addressing incongruent, conflicting or contrasting frames, it may not be as effective in managing frame ambivalence or switching.
  • 将框架关系与产业主权挂钩: 明确讨论部分的目标是建立在现有研究之上，反思每种框架关系对产业主权产生的更广泛影响。
    In addressing Research Question 2, we build on current research that explores the possibilities for enhancing the digital agency of farm advisors, by reflecting on the broader effects that each frame relationship engenders for the sovereignty of the rice industry to ‘chart their own futures and define their aims’ (Carolan 2018, p. 759) in relation to technological change.
  • 框架不一致性的后果: 分析指出，框架不一致性会阻碍技术变革，可能导致“知识碎片化和信息不对称”。
    From this perspective, if commercial agronomists and advisors are not promoting, or have doubts about, the use of specific technologies, the Australian rice industry will face growing ‘knowledge fragmentation and information asymmetry’ (Sutherland et al. 2013, p. 97) that is likely to be counter-productive in enabling more widespread implementation of smart farming technology.
  • 框架矛盾的双重效应: 一方面，框架矛盾允许对模糊情况进行解释，为不确定性留出空间；另一方面，它可能导致利益相关者“两面下注”，固守旧有技术路径。
    On the other hand, ambivalence can lead to industry stakeholders hedging their bets (see e.g., Sillince and Mueller 2007) on new and unproven technology, adhering to existing priorities of improving productivity through the promotion of new, and easily adoptable, rice varieties, which have worked well for the industry in the past.
  • 框架转换的责任重构功能: 认为框架转换不仅是策略转换，更是在个体和结构归因之间持续切换，这使得利益相关者能够界定责任范围并转移焦点。
    Switching not only allows ‘wide scope for interpretation of responsibility’ (Sillince and Mueller 2007, p. 155), it also enables growers to be positioned as critical to technological change outcomes within the rice industry.
  • 主权与数字能动性的张力: 提出核心论点，即最能赋予产业主权的框架关系（矛盾与转换）可能并不总是有利于提升中观行动者的数字能动性，甚至可能对其产生负面影响。
    This in turn can have negative implications for the capacity of growers in experimenting and tinkering with new technology, which is central for working with and working ‘around technology to make it adoptable on-farm’ (Higgins et al. 2017, p. 201).
  • 对未来研究的启示: 提出一个重要的研究启示，即支持产业主权的框架关系可能与促进数字能动性的努力相悖，未来研究需要关注这种张力。
    One significant implication of our analysis for smart farming research is that frame relationships supporting industry sovereignty may not always be conducive to the promotion of digital agency among meso-scale actors, and could in fact impose limits on smart farming implementation within particular industries.
  • 引入责任式创新（RRI）: 建议未来的智能农业研究应采用责任式研究与创新（RRI）原则，以确保在支持数字能动性的同时考虑到产业主权问题。
    Future smart farming research underpinned by Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) principles – anticipation, inclusion, reflexivity and responsiveness (Bronson 2018; Regan 2019; Eastwood et al. 2019b) – will be critical in ensuring that industry sovereignty is taken into account by researchers as part of efforts to support agricultural knowledge providers’ digital agency.

Conclusion

本章对全文进行了总结，并强调了其核心贡献。首先，文章重申了技术框架方法对于探索中观行动者在智能农业实施中的作用具有巨大潜力，特别是在分析他们如何构建对技术优先级、推广能力和采纳驱动因素的理解方面。其次，文章强调了本文的两大核心贡献：第一，揭示了中观行动者中存在“框架矛盾”和“框架转换”等比“框架不一致”更复杂的框架关系，这对当前寻求整合性解决方案的研究提出了挑战；第二，将研究焦点从个体层面的“数字能动性”提升到产业层面的“主权”，并论证了两者之间可能存在的紧张关系。作者认为，未来需要引入责任式研究与创新（RRI）的原则来协调这两者。最后，文章承认了研究的局限性，指出需要在更多商品部门和国家背景下进行进一步研究，以检验本文提出的概念框架的普适性，并建议可以运用其他框架理论来深化理解。

  • 对数字能动性研究的贡献: 指出当前文献关注于提升中观行动者的数字能动性，以实现数字工具的价值。
    Second, the current literature focuses on enhancing the digital agency of meso-scale actors so that they are better able to ‘realise the value of digital tools and services’ (Ayre et al. 2019, p. 11) and thereby support more effective uptake of smart farming innovations.
  • 提出关注产业主权: 论证在关注个体能动性的同时，研究人员也需要关注产业层面的能动性，即遵循Carolan的工作称之为的“主权”。
    We argue that while supporting the digital agency of meso-scale actors is important, researchers also need to give attention to agency at an industry scale – what we have termed ‘sovereignty’ following the work of Carolan (2018).
  • 对未来研究的建议: 建议未来的研究可以采用其他理论方法，如基于诠释或认知范式的框架理论，来探讨本文未深入或未涉及的方面。
    In doing so, other theoretical approaches to framing based upon interpretative or cognitive paradigms (Dewulf et al. 2009) might be applicable in teasing out aspects of meso-level actors’ role in smart farming implementation that have been touched on only briefly, or not considered at all in this article.
  • 最终展望: 强调无论采用何种理论方法，理解框架关系的多样性及其产生的复杂政治本体论，将是社会科学研究者未来工作的核心。
    Regardless of research context or theoretical approach to framing, understanding the diversity of relationships between frames, and the complex political ontologies they engender, will need to be a central focus of inquiry for social researchers as they seek to further understand how to enhance farmer ‘access to outcomes and opportunities from digital innovation in smart farming’ (Ayre et al. 2019, p. 11).
