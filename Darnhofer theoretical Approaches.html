全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点在于，为了深刻理解“农业”这一复杂现象，必须采用一种“过程-关系”的理论路径。作者主张，这种路径需要超越传统社会学中主客二分、能动与结构、人与自然的对立框架，将农业视为一个由人类、非人类（动植物、微生物）、物（技术、土壤、数据）和观念（知识、规范、政策）等异质性要素持续不断地“生成”（becoming）和“纠缠”（intermingle）的过程。为了构建这一分析框架，作者系统性地综合了四个虽有不同侧重但本体论和认识论上高度契合的后人类主义理论流派：行动者网络理论（ANT）、生成组合理论（Assemblage Thinking）、新物质主义（New Materialism）和关系社会学（Relational Sociology）。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据主要通过对四个核心理论的文献梳理和其在农业食品研究领域的应用实例来展开。首先，通过引介ANT，作者展示了如何将农场视为一个由土壤、机械、动物、知识、法规等组成的异质性网络，强调了非人类的能动性（如水稻的物质性）。其次，通过生成组合理论，作者强调了农业实践的暂时性、脆弱性和涌现性，指出任何农业组合（assemblage）都可能被重新组合，从而为“另类”实践开辟空间。再次，通过新物质主义，作者进一步深化了对“物”的能动性（agency）的探讨，将土壤、塑料、度量标准（metrics）等视为具有自身活力、能够塑造甚至创造社会经济关系的行动者。最后，通过关系社会学，作者从根本上解构了实体主义的思维，提出社会现象（如农民身份）并非源于实体属性，而是在关系网络中被构成的，强调了过程性和相互依存性。所有这些理论论据都通过引用具体的农业研究案例（如口蹄疫、苹果矮化技术、新西兰葡萄园可持续性审计）得到了支撑。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑层次清晰，层层递进。首先，在引言部分，作者明确了现有理论的不足，并提出了整合四个理论流派的必要性，指出它们的共同本体论基础——即过程哲学和关系实在论。随后，文章分四个小节逐一剖析每个理论。对每个理论的阐述都遵循“核心概念介绍 → 农业领域应用案例 → 理论贡献与局限性”的模式。例如，在介绍ANT时，作者先阐明其“对称性原则”和“翻译”等核心概念，然后列举其在分析口蹄疫、农民决策等方面的应用，最后也指出了ANT在处理动物“他者性”（otherness）上的不足。这种结构使得论证既有理论深度，又有经验研究的支撑。通过对四个理论的依次阐述和比较（如比较ANT对网络稳定性的关注与Assemblage对持续生成过程的关注），作者最终构建了一个更为全面和动态的分析框架，成功地论证了采用“过程-关系”视角研究农业的必要性和有效性。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文采用的核心研究方法是理论综合（Theoretical Synthesis）与批判性文献综述（Critical Literature Review）。作者并非进行一手经验研究，而是通过对现有理论资源进行系统性的梳理、比较和整合，构建一个新的、适用于农业食品研究领域的理论框架。作者选择的四个理论流派本身就是复杂的学术思潮，他/她精准地抓住了它们在本体论上的共通之处（反实体主义、过程性、分布式能动性），并清晰地辨析了它们各自的侧重点和分析优势。这种方法论的贡献在于为该领域的经验研究者提供了一套丰富而深刻的分析工具，使他们能够更敏锐地捕捉农业实践中人与非人之间复杂的相互作用和动态演变。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

虽然本文是理论探讨，但作者巧妙地运用了大量来自农业食品研究的二手案例来阐释抽象的理论概念。这些案例包括：1) 将农场本身视为一个行动者网络（Noe & Alrøe, 2006）；2) 一头“小牛”的身份是如何被标签、文件、法规等一系列实践所共同“生成”的（Singleton & Law, 2013）；3) 英国口蹄疫危机被分析为一个混合了自然、社会、经济和政治要素的事件（Murdoch, 2003）；4) 印度尼西亚水稻的物质性（流动性）如何影响了人类的适应策略（Dwiartama & Rosin, 2014）；5) 苹果园中的矮化技术如何创造了新的经济关系（Legun, 2015）；6) 可持续性评估中的“度量标准”（metrics）如何主动地重塑了农民的主体性和实践（Rosin et al., 2017）；7) 瑞士两种区域奶酪的生产如何体现了“另类”与“传统”的共存（Forney, 2016）；8) 国际象棋与围棋的对比，用以说明实体论与关系论的本体论差异。这些案例极大地增强了文章的说服力和可读性，清晰地展示了理论如何应用于具体的农业情境分析。

3.1 Actor Network Theory
大意、细节与原文引用 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章概述了行动者网络理论（ANT）的核心思想，强调其如何通过消解人与非人、社会与自然的二元对立，来分析现象是如何通过异质性要素（人类、非人类、技术、文本等）的网络关系构建起来的。ANT拒绝将“社会”视为一个独立的领域或背后存在的驱动力，而是主张社会性是各种联系和互动的“结果”而非“原因”。在农业研究中，ANT被用来揭示农场实践如何是异质性网络的产物，凸显了非人类行动者（如动物、作物、疾病、技术）的能动性，并挑战了将农民视为孤立自主决策者的传统观念。同时，本章也探讨了ANT的局限，特别是其“对称性原则”在处理动物等生命体的特殊能动性和伦理地位时可能存在的不足。

  • 作者总结ANT和生成组合理论的共同点，即它们都关注由人类、非人类和物质构成的互动网络，并认为这对于理解社会性与物质性的交织具有分析价值。
    Both help to think how sociality and materiality are intertwined, and highlight that paying attention to material process in the social world can be analytically useful (Legun & Henry, 2017, p. 77).
  • 指出ANT和生成组合理论的相似性源于其共同的知识谱系。
    And indeed, there are numerous similarities between ANT and assemblage theory, since they have a similar intellectual trajectory (Müller, 2015).
  • 提及拉图尔在其著作中使用“assemblage”一词，说明了ANT和生成组合理论术语上的重叠。
    Indeed Latour (2005) also uses the term in his book ‘Reassembling the social’, and a number of researchers use the term ‘assemblage’ rather than ‘network’ in the context of ANT.
  • 比较ANT和生成组合理论的侧重点差异，前者关注网络的稳定化过程，后者关注持续进行的、充满可能性的“组合”过程，并且后者对情感（affect）和不同能动性（如动物与物质）的区分更敏感。
    Moreover, while ANT is particularly well suited to capture aspects of materials, assemblage thinking may have a greater sensitivity in differentiating between capacities, e.g. of animals and materials; and it may have greater sensitivity for the productive role of affect (Müller & Schurr, 2016).
  • 指出后人类主义方法论（如新物质主义）强调非人类行动者超越人类意图和控制，从而开启了事件涌现而非必然发生的可能性。
    Indeed, a posthuman approach foregrounds that options shift without human will or consent, so that the unfolding of events is not inevitable, but rather emerges, often surprisingly (Legun & Henry, 2017).
  • 引用拉图尔和伍尔加的早期研究，说明ANT起源于对科学知识社会学中“过度社会化”观念的批判，强调了物质条件在科学实践中的重要性。
    However, authors such as Bruno Latour and Steve Woolgar critiqued this oversocialised conception of science (Latour & Woolgar, 1979).
  • ANT通过强调人与非人之间的复杂网络，扁平化了现代主义的等级秩序，克服了二元论思维。
    ANT thus flattens the modernist hierarchy between humans and nonhumans, it overcomes dualistic thinking by highlighting the complex networks that link social, natural, and technical elements (Murdoch, 2001).
  • ANT通过揭示被遗忘的、使现象成为可能的关系，有助于重新开启对其他可能性的想象。
    By making them visible, it enables reengaging with foreclosed alternative possible imaginings (Legun & Henry 2017).
  • 引用拉图尔的观点，ANT将“社会”视为关联的结果而非一个预设的领域，从而消解了“社会结构”等概念。
    As a result, ‘the social’ is not seen as a distinct domain of reality (Latour, 2005, p. 4), and with it comes a dissolution of the concepts of ‘social structure’ and ‘underlying mechanisms’.
  • 拉图尔反对存在“幕后”社会力量的观点，主张以局部的、短暂的互动或关联来取而代之。
    Latour (2005, pp. 65-66) rejects any sense of social forces working ‘behind the scenes’, replacing these entirely with localized, more or less short-lived interactions or associations.
  • ANT中社会学家的任务是解释异质性元素如何被组装成网络，网络是互动的结果而非原因。
    These networks are the outcomes, not the causes of interactions, so the focus is on the relational micropolitics of events, activities and interactions themselves (Fox & Alldred, 2018, pp. 320-1).
  • 拉图尔将“行动”定义为在特定情境下与他者共同完成的事件，而非仅仅是人的行为。
    Within ANT, “action is not what people do”, but what is “accomplished along with others in an event, with the specific opportunities provided by the circumstances” (Latour, 1999, p. 288).
  • 技术系统的“效率”并非其固有属性，而是通过持续的“翻译”过程在异质性行动者网络中被“执行”和赋予连贯性的结果。
    Indeed “their apparent solidity or obviousness only holds inasmuch as this ceaseless work of translation stabilizes a heterogeneous actor network which ‘performs’ them and gives them their coherence” (Licoppe, 2010, p. 182).
  • 在农业情境下，将农场理解为一个行动者网络，可以揭示其由土壤、机械、动物、知识等异质性实体构成的系统。
    When Noe and Alrøe (2006) propose to understand a farm as an actor-network, it allows them to highlight that a farm is a heterogeneous system composed of many different kinds of entities, including soil, machinery, animals, people, knowledge, regulations, etc.
  • 牛的特性和产奶量取决于它所处的网络关系（放养或舍饲），说明了实体通过关系获得其形式和性能。
    Seeing farms from an ANT approach highlights that the entities get their forms and performance through the relations in which they are located, e.g. whether a cow grazes or is stable-fed with concentrates, will affect what kind of cows it is, incl. how much milk she produces (Noe & Alrøe, 2012).
  • 农业实践是对异质性要素的排序；事物的稳定性来源于实践的重复性，否则事物不会保持不变。
    They point out that we need to avoid imagining that a calf is a calf is a calf: if things “are stable this is because the practices themselves are repetitive. Without such repetitions things don’t hold steady” (Singleton & Law, 2013, p. 262).
  • 多位学者运用ANT分析了2001年英国口蹄疫事件，揭示其为一个混合事件。
    Several authors (Donaldson, Lowe, & Ward, 2002; Law, 2006; Law & Mol, 2011; Law & Singleton, 2009; Murdoch, 2003) have used ANT to analyse the foot and mouth disease, which broke out in the sheep population in the UK in 2001.
  • 口蹄疫事件被视为一个复杂的异质性网络，其中自然、社会、经济、政治和技术过程相互交织。
    They showed that the unfolding of the disease was a hybrid event, which “aligned natural, social, economic, political and technological processes within a complex and heterogeneous network of effects” (Murdoch, 2003, p. 266).
  • ANT有助于打破农民是自主决策者的印象，揭示农民的选择、行动和身份是如何被网络中的人、技术和制度所共同构成、扩展和限制的。
    They highlighted that networks of people, technologies, and institutions expand and constrain farmers’ choices in ways that modify their relationships, actions, and identity (Gray & Gibson, 2013, p. 85).
  • 通过印尼水稻的案例，作者强调了非人类的能动性：水稻的物质特性能够赋能或制约人类目标的实现，从而塑造人类的适应性策略。
    Rice is thus not a mere passive object to be used by humans, it shapes the manner in which the adaptive capacity of humans is expressed. The resulting adaptation strategies are influenced by the materiality of rice, in particular its fluidity; and by its capacity, as an object and symbol, to connect to a wide variety of entities (Dwiartama & Rosin, 2014).
  • 变革的发生是人类与非人类行动者之间复杂互动的结果，而非仅仅是人类意愿的产物。
    How change unfolds is thus not just the result of the agency and intentionality of humans, but also of the materiality of nonhumans; it is the result of a complex interplay between human and nonhuman actants (Dwiartama, 2016).
  • 虽然现实和身份在异质性实践中生成，但这些实践是可变的，存在着 enacting 另类现实的空间。
    However, while “realities and identities are generated in patterned and heterogenous practices” (Singleton & Law, 2013, p. 262), these practices are also malleable, there is variation.
  • 另类农业世界不仅是可能的，而且是多元地、持续地存在的，这体现了对“别无选择”观念的抵制。
    “It is not simply that other farming worlds are possible, though this is certainly true. It is also the case that multiple farming worlds exist – and are endlessly coming into existence” (Singleton & Law, 2013, p. 272).
  • 通过对数字农业平台的研究，Carolan揭示了不同的平台构建了不同的人-非人-产品-实践关系，从而编排了不同的政治、规范和社会秩序。
    Carolan (2020), in a study on big data and digital farming platforms, retraced farm data value chains. He shows the orchestrated relations among humans, non-humans, products, spaces, places and practices, highlighting the widely distributed nature of these digital farming platforms.
  • Jones批判ANT的对称性原则，认为虽然它突出了被压抑的主体，但也可能削弱了它们作为独特性主体的身份，尤其是在动物议题上。
    However, Jones (2003) notes that while this theoretical approach brings out oppressed subjectivities from the shadows, it may well undermine their identity as distinct subjects.
  • Whatmore和Thorne指出ANT中的非人类能动性带有一种“技术性倾向”。
    Indeed, Whatmore and Thorne (2000, p. 186) note that nonhuman agency in ANT has a ‘technical inflection’.
  • Jones质疑将动物与机器同等对待的合理性，认为动物拥有不同于工具的、独特的能动性和他者性。
    This led Jones (2003, p. 294) to ask whether “it is reasonable to treat animals symmetrically, as blanks, as things that take their identity from their relational interconnectivities alone”.
  • 对植物能动性的理解仍处于初级阶段，需要更多关注。
    Yet our understanding of the specific capacities of plants’ agency is at its infancy (Pitt, 2015).
  • ANT通过不预先区分人与物，提供了社会-物质视角，并解构了“实体化的总体性”。
    Overall, by not distinguishing a priori between humans and materials, ANT offers a socio-material perspective and decentres ‘reified totalities’ (Müller, 2015, p. 28).
  • ANT强调网络中要素产生的效应并非预先决定，而是依赖于它们所属的共同构成关系。
    ANT takes into account that the effects they produce are not predetermined, but dependent on the co-constitutive relational arrangement of which they are part (Jones, 2006, p. 187).
  • ANT强调社会-物质的过程性，引用Law的观点，“没有社会秩序，只有无尽的排序尝试”。
    ANT also insists on the processual nature of the socio-material: ‘there is no social order. Rather, there are endless attempts at ordering’ (Law, 1994, p. 101, in Müller, 2015, p. 30).
  • 再次强调ANT可能忽略了动物的“他者性”及其在被纳入网络时的伦理后果。
    Indeed, it can seem bereft of a sensitivity to the otherness of animals (Jones, 2003).
  • 随着环境和动物福利问题的增长，将动物归入宽泛的非人类类别已无法满足伦理要求。
    Animals have a particular form of agency and particular demands for ethics, demands which cannot be satisfied if animals are subsumed in a general category of nonhumans, especially in the context of the growth of environmental concerns and questions of animal welfare (Jones, 2006).

3.2 Assemblage thinking
大意、细节与原文引用 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章介绍了源自德勒兹和加塔利、后由德兰达系统化的“生成组合理论”（Assemblage Theory）。与ANT相比，它更强调过程的“正在进行时态”，即“组合”（assembling）这一持续的、不稳定的、充满偶然性的劳动。核心概念是“关系的外部性”，即组合中的各个部件拥有一定的自主性，可以被拆解并重新接入其他组合，因此任何组合都不是一个无缝的整体，而是脆弱、暂时且可变的。这一理论鼓励研究者关注系统的涌现性、多样性和不确定性，而非寻找固定的结构或决定性力量。在农业研究中，它被用于分析农民身份的形成、未来技术（如机器人）对当下实践的影响，以及在主流体系中寻找“另类”实践的空间，强调任何农业现实都只是众多可能性中的一种，并始终处在被重新组合的过程中。

  • 指出“Assemblage”是德勒兹和加塔利使用的法语词“agencement”的英译。
    The term ‘assemblage’ may sound French, but it is actually an English translation of the French word ‘agencement’ used by Deleuze and Guattari, who only rarely use the French term ‘assemblage’ (Phillips, 2006).
  • 说明许多使用“assemblage”概念的实证研究与德兰达的理论发展密切相关。
    Much empirical work using assemblages is linked to the specific development of assemblage thinking by Manuel DeLanda (2006, 2016).
  • 引用德兰达的观点，阐述“关系的外部性”是德勒兹理论的核心，即关系外在于其构成项，关系可以改变而构成项不变。
    For DeLanda (2006b, p. 253) the key idea in Deleuze’s theory is the exteriority of relations, i.e. that not only “relations are external to their terms, but also ‘a relation may change without the terms changing’ (Deleuze & Parnet, 2002, p. 55)”.
  • 部件的属性不能解释构成组合的关系，因为组合是部件“能力”实际发挥的结果，而非部件属性的简单聚合。
    The exteriority of relations also implies that the properties of components cannot explain the relations which constitute an assemblage, as the assemblage is not the result of an aggregation of the components’ properties, but the actual exercise of the component’s capacities (DeLanda, 2006a).
  • 部件的“能力”依赖于其属性，但不能还原为其属性，因为它涉及到与其他互动实体的属性的参照。
    These “capacities do depend on a component’s properties but cannot be reduced to them since they involve reference to the properties of other interacting entities” (DeLanda, 2006a, p. 11).
  • 一些研究将“assemblage”用作描述预设社会空间组织的标签，这残留了本质主义的痕迹。
    Some use it as a label to describe pre-given socio-spatial organisations and relations, thus displaying remnants of essentialism (Allen, 2012).
  • 概括了生成组合思维通常强调的四类过程：组合劳动的历史性、能动性的分布性、结果的涌现性和多元性，以及关系的脆弱性和不确定性。
    However, generally it is deployed to emphasise four types of processes (see Anderson & MacFarlane, 2011; Baker & McGuirk, 2017; McFarlane, 2009): (1)... (2)... (3)... (4)...
  • 组合往往会产生意想不到的间接后果，Bentia称之为“意外的能动性”。
    Indeed, assemblages tend to have unexpected and indirect outcomes, where things do not develop as planned, which Bentia (2021, p. 15) referred to as “the agency of the unexpected”.
  • 在人文地理学中，“assemblage”指异质性元素构成的暂时性社会空间形态。
    In human geography, assemblage often refers to the composition of heterogenous elements into some form of provisional socio-spatial formation (Anderson, Kearnes, McFarlane, & Swanton, 2012; Anderson & MacFarlane, 2011).
  • 一个组合可以包含人、植物、动物、技术、规范等，所有这些元素在组合内外都具有能动性。
    An assemblage may include a gathering of humans, plants, animals, materials, technologies, techniques, norms and events, all of which have the capacity for agency, within and beyond the assemblage (Baker & McGuirk, 2017).
  • 生成组合理论旨在模糊社会与物质、结构与能动性之间的界限。
    Assemblage is thus part of a reconstitution of the social that seeks to blur divisions between the social and the material, between structure and agency (DeLanda, 2006a).
  • 该理论旨在抵制社会秩序的“自然化”，转而关注组合的“构成过程”，即它们如何被维系，同时又保留被另类组合的潜力。
    The aim is to resist the ‘naturalisation’ of social orders and instead become attentive to the ‘processes of composition’, how assemblages are “held together so as to endure while always also retaining the potential to be assembled otherwise” (Greenhough, 2012, p. 203).
  • 研究焦点从描述“一个”组合转向关注“组合的实践”（practice of assembling），即汇集异质性元素的持续劳动。
    Rather than describing ‘an’ assemblage, i.e. describing a grouping, the focus is thus on the practice of assembling, on the ceaseless labour involved in bringing heterogeneous elements together, arranging them, forging connections between them (Li, 2007).
  • 组合是一个持续被制造和重塑的过程，是“安排、组织、拼凑”不同行动者、物体、技术等的过程。
    Indeed, an assemblage is continually in the process of being made and remade, an on-going process of “arranging, organizing, fitting together of disparate actors, objects, techniques, organizations, representations” (Baker & McGuirk, 2017, p. 431).
  • 强调任何特定的构型都必须通过努力来维持，包括协调动机、翻译思想、发展实践、调动资源等。
    Any particular configuration must be worked at, a work that includes aligning motivations, translating ideas, developing new practices, marshalling resources, developing expertise, building relationships (Baker & McGuirk, 2017).
  • 这种对组合工作的强调凸显了组合的偶然性、暂时性、脆弱性和可修正性。
    This disparate work puts the emphasis on how the composite, the grouping is contingent, which implies that it remains provisional, uncertain, fragile, and thus revisable (Anderson & MacFarlane, 2011).
  • 生成组合思维鼓励对看似稳固和不变的事物持怀疑态度，并关注其随时间演变的过程。
    It encourages a sceptical attitude towards the appearance of unity, coherence, permanence, and invites to foreground how it arose over time. (Lather, 2016).
  • 组合的“影响能力”是其构成元素共同作用的结果，离开组合则无法实现。
    “An assemblage is a collection of elements that come together to affect, and this capacity to affect could not be realised without the assemblage” (Mancilla Garcia, Hertz, & Schlüter, 2020, p. 235).
  • 能动性位于人与多重非人行动者的复杂相互牵涉之中，它们共同形成一个有效的组合。
    Agency is thus “located in the complex interinvolvement of humans and multiple nonhuman actants, which together form an effective assemblage” (Bennett, 2015, p. 88).
  • 以全球羊毛产业为例，说明组合可以跨越地理距离，将远隔的部件组装在一起，从而理解全球经济重构对地方农村社会的影响。
    For example Jones, Heley, & Woods (2019) applied assemblage reading to the global wool industry, showing how global economic restructuring impacts local rural societies in Wales.
  • 在农场层面，生成组合理论揭示了农业生产是由人、物质、非人及无形要素共同组合而成的。
    A farmer thus attempts – more or less successfully – to coax together a variety of entities, each with their own agency and capabilities (Sutherland & Calo, 2020).
  • 引用van der Ploeg关于“耕作风格”的研究，说明不存在技术或经济决定论，农民的世界观塑造了生产组织方式。
    Indeed, the work of Jan Douwe van der Ploeg (1985, 2000) on ‘farming styles’ has amply shown that there is no technological or economic determinism, but that the farmer’s world view shapes how production is organised, delimiting a rather wide space of possibility.
  • 生成组合理论的补充价值在于，它关注元素间的关系，并强调组合因构型不断变化而具有不稳定性。
    What assemblage theory adds, is a focus on the relations between the elements, thereby highlighting that assemblages are precarious since the configurations are constantly changing (Müller, 2015).
  • 组合中的所有元素都拥有未被发掘的潜能，如果被不同地组合，将会产生不同的效果。
    Indeed, all the elements of the assemblage have untapped skills, abilities, capacities that could be used, that would have different effects, if they were assembled differently (Sutherland & Calo, 2020).
  • 通过对苹果园未来机器人技术的研究，说明农场不仅是农民能动性的被动背景，而是主动参与塑造想象和约束行动。
    As such the farm is not a passive backdrop of the agency of the farmer, but the “farm participates in enabling and constraining action while shaping what kinds of worlds become imagined” (Legun & Burch, 2021, p. 381, italics in original).
  • Forney的研究指出，一个组合（如农民组织）内部同时存在多种过程，包括来自零售商的压力、官僚化的影响以及农民的集体赋权等。
    Indeed, within an assemblage such as the farmer organisation ‘IP Suisse’, there are multiple processes that are unfolding simultaneously. These include pressures by retailers, the impact of bureaucratisation, and a collective empowerment of farmers through learning, collaborations, and experiments. (Forney, 2021).
  • 通过对两种区域奶酪的研究，Forney展示了“市场化”与“合作社”模式如何同时存在，打破了二元对立，为“另类”在“传统”内部开辟了空间。
    Both cheeses are enacted in networks of relations through repeated processes of meaning, attribution and translation; and importantly, they exist simultaneously, casting away binary oppositions and opening up new spaces for the ‘alternative’ within the ‘conventional’ (Forney, 2016).
  • 生成组合理论有助于识别差异空间，从而“使不可思议之事变得可想，使不可为之事成为常态”。
    By making their presence visible, studies contribute to “making the un-thought thinkable and the undoable routine” (Carolan, 2013a, p. 148).
  • 生成组合启发的农业研究表明，农民如何通过重塑实践扩大了“可能”与“可行”的边界。
    Assemblage-inflected studies, by looking for novelty, rather than just dominant practices, have shown how farmers have enlarged ‘the possible’ and ‘the doable’ (see Carolan, 2013).
  • 实践的关系不稳定性为重新想象和创造新行动开辟了空间。
    Indeed, the relational instability of practices creates openings for re-imagining practices, and spaces for new doings (Carolan, 2013b; Lewis & Rosin, 2013).
  • 研究者自身也是“研究-组合”的一部分，需要对此保持反思。
    Importantly, assemblage thinking goes beyond informing empirical research. It is also about becoming aware that we, as researchers, are part of the research-assemblage (Fox & Alldred, 2015a).
  • 新西兰有机农业的研究案例表明，研究项目本身具有塑造现实的力量，可能强化了某些话语（如“以度量为中心”的环境审计）。
    As Campbell and Rosin (2011) pointed out, two long-term research programmes had important enactive power in shaping the organic agricultural sector in New Zealand: they rendered ‘thinkable’ particular trajectories, while also reinforcing ‘metric-centric’ environmental audit systems.

3.3 New materialism
大意、细节与原文引用 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章深入探讨了“新物质主义”思潮，它进一步强化了前述理论中对非人类能动性的关注。新物质主义的核心观点是，物质（matter）本身并非被动、静止的，而是具有内在的“活力”（vitality）和自我组织、自我转化的能力。它挑战了将物质世界仅仅视为人类意志投射对象的观念，也与仅关注物质的经济属性的马克思主义历史唯物主义相区别。新物质主义强调世界是由人与非人共同构成的、不可分割的网络，其中“物”（如技术、身体、自然力）与人类一样是行动者。在农业研究中，这一视角被用来分析塑料包装、可持续性度量标准、矮化技术乃至土壤本身如何作为积极的行动者，参与并塑造农业实践、经济关系乃至伦理观念。该理论不仅是一种分析工具，更是一个政治和伦理项目，旨在通过承认物质的活力来促进更可持续、更尊重生态的生产方式，并与原住民宇宙观等思想建立对话。

  • 指出新物质主义是后人类主义内部的一个运动，对表象主义和建构主义的激进化倾向做出了回应。
    New materialism (Bennett, 2010; Coole & Frost, 2010; Dolphijn & van der Tuin, 2012) was initially a reaction to the representationalist and constructivist radicalizations, which somehow lost track of the material realm.
  • 在新物质主义看来，人类并非施加自由意志于被动物质世界的自主行动者，而是身处一个广泛的关系系统之中，“物”与人一样是行动者。
    As a result, humans are not seen as autonomous agents imposing their free will on a passive material world, but rather humans are seen as located within an extensive system of relations, within which ‘things’ are agents along with humans.
  • 新物质主义中的“物质性”涵盖了话语、身体、文本、图像、机器等异质性元素。
    These ‘things’ are heterogeneous; materiality takes many forms: talk, bodies, texts, images, machines, architectures, etc. (Higgins, Bryant, Howell, & Battersby, 2017).
  • 新物质主义与马克思的历史唯物主义不同，后者将物质性与经济理性捆绑，而前者认为物质性是多元、开放、复杂和偶然的。
    In new materialism materiality is plural, open, complex, uneven, contingent, and should be understood in a relational, emergent sense (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 29; Fox & Alldred, 2018, p. 317).
  • 新物质主义关注非人类力量的能动贡献，这些力量可能在自然、人体或人造物中运作，强调的不是认知而是情感（affect）。
    It thus builds on a different account of how materiality matters, one that focuses on the agentic contribution of nonhuman forces, which may be operating in nature, in the human body, or in human artifacts. (Coole & Frost, 2010).
  • 物质被视为拥有自我转化、自我组织和方向性的模式，不再是被动和惰性的。
    New materialism conceives of matter as “possessing its own modes of self-transformation, self-organization, and directedness, and thus no longer as simply passive and inert” (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 10).
  • 引用Bennett对“活力”（vitality）的定义，即物质不仅能阻碍人类意志，还能作为具有自身轨迹和倾向的准行动者。
    Materials are seen as having vitality, by which Bennett (2010, p. viii) means “the capacity of things – edibles, commodities, storms, metals – not only to impede or block the will and designs of humans but also to act as quasi agents or forces with trajectories, propensities, or tendencies of their own.”
  • 物质的能动性并非源于我们的“注意”，而是它们所在的网络赋予了这种“注意”发生的可能。
    Rather, these things are assembled within a web that itself allows for the ‘noticing’ to occur; this web is not empowered by our noticing it, but rather empowers those within its frame (Coole & Frost, 2010).
  • 以另类食品网络中的塑料为例，说明塑料如何发挥作用，塑造、影响和干扰实践，并成为人们关注、协商和实验的对象。
    She has shown that they perform a very specific work that shapes, influences, and disrupts practices in these alternative food networks. Plastics thus influenced the “situated practices of re/making markets with differentially valued materials” (Phillips, 2016, p. 215).
  • 自然被视为一种能动的力量，与文化、历史、话语、技术等要素互动，其行动对人类和非人类世界都产生后果。
    Nature acts and these actions have consequences for both the human and nonhuman world (Alaimo & Heikman, 2008, p. 5).
  • 在农业情境中，新西兰葡萄园可持续性度量标准的研究显示，度量标准本身具有能动性，通过参与各种关系影响农民的主体性和实践，导致社会和环境关系的重组。
    In the context of farming, Rosin, Campbell, and Reid (2017) highlighted the agency of metrics in showing how the sustainability of vineyards is understood in New Zealand...
  • 度量标准（metrics）被证明在驱动实践和思维模式变革中是活跃的。
    Similarly, Alrøe et al. (2017) have shown how metrics are active in driving changes in practices and mindsets.
  • 对苹果园矮化技术的研究表明，技术不仅促进或固化经济关系，还能创造新的关系、产生意想不到的效果并激发实验。
    Legun (2015), based on her analysis of dwarfing technologies in apple orchards, highlighted how using a materialist perspective, technologies do not just facilitate or congeal economic relations by influencing which practices are selected or what actions seem possible; they can also create them.
  • 新物质主义挑战了人类有权或有能力主宰自然的假设。
    In the context of natural resource management, new materialism not only disturbs the conventional sense that agents are exclusively human, it also deeply questions the corollary presumption, that humans have the right or the ability to master nature (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 10).
  • 将自然视为可塑资源的观念不再成立。
    As such that “Nature can no longer be imagined as a pliable resource of industrial production or social construction” (Alaimo & Heikman, 2008, p. 4).
  • Bennett指出，将物质视为死的或完全工具化的形象助长了人类的狂妄自大和破坏地球的幻想，因此倡导物质的活力。
    As Bennett (2010, p. ix) puts it: “Why advocate the vitality of matter? Because my hunch is that the image of dead or thoroughly instrumentalized matter feeds human hubris and our earth-destroying fantasies of conquest and consumption”.
  • 承认物质的活力可能有助于产生更生态、更可持续的生产模式。
    Thus, acknowledging that matter is not dead and inanimate may contribute to the emergence of more ecological and more materially sustainable modes of production (Bennett, 2010, p. ix).
  • 新物质主义是一个政治项目，鼓励与充满活力的物质进行更智能和可持续的互动，并重新探索日常生活物质细节与更广泛的社会经济结构之间的关系。
    As such it is linked to a critical reengagement with political economy, a new exploration of the nature of and relationship between the material details of everyday life and broader geopolitical and socioeconomic structures (Coole & Frost, 2010, p. 7).
  • 新物质主义可以与原住民宇宙观建立联系，后者强调土地的能动性及其与人类的共同构成。
    For example Bawaka Country et al. (2016) show an indigenous-led understanding of place which emphasizes the agency of the land, its co-constitution with and as human.
  • 通过将土壤想象为“活的”，可以培养与土地的连接感，克服以人类为中心的生产主义精神。
    Similarly, Puig de la Bellacasa (2019) explores how altering the imaginaries of soils – as being alive rather than as inert matter subjected to human use – may allow to develop a sense of earthy connectedness and shared aliveness, and thus help overcome the human-centred productionist ethos.
  • 土壤不仅塑造农民的选择，还可以成为构建公正和可持续食物系统的重要盟友。
    Ferguson et al. (2016) use new materialism to argue that soil not only shapes farmers’ choices and possible actions, it can also be an important ally in shaping a just and sustainable food system.
  • 理解奶牛和真菌等非人类自身的特质和能动性，有助于建立新的关系，并克服人类例外论和物种等级制。
    Understanding cows – or fungi (see Sheldrake, 2020) – on their own terms may allow to build new relations with them, to appreciate their agency, and to overcome assumptions of human exceptionalism and species hierarchy. (Cornips and van den Hengel, 2021).

3.4 Relational sociology
大意、细节与原文引用 引用的文献 (按论点主题分类)

本章将理论视角转向“关系社会学”，将其作为对“实体主义”思维方式的根本性替代。关系社会学主张，社会分析的起点不应是预先存在的实体（如个人、结构），而应是连接这些实体的“关系”本身。作者对比了关系社会学内部的两种路径：一种是以Donati为代表的“关系实在论”，它承认关系的实在性，但仍将行动者视为可以进入关系的实体；另一种是以Dépelteau为代表的、更彻底的“过程-关系”路径，它认为实体本身就是由关系所构成的，没有内在于关系之外的属性。作者用围棋和国际象棋的对比生动地阐释了后一种观点：棋子的价值完全取决于它在棋盘上的关系网络，而非其固有属性。这种视角将社会现象（如阶级、文化）理解为持续流变的关系过程，而非静态的“物”或“结构”。尽管作者指出尚未发现关系社会学在农业研究中的直接应用，但它为理解农业中的各种社会关系（家庭内部、知识网络、政策互动等）提供了一个极具潜力的、动态的、反本质主义的理论框架。

  • 指出关系社会学共同的思想渊源，包括塔尔德、齐美尔、莫斯和埃利亚斯。
    Relational sociology builds among other on Gabriel Tarde, Georg Simmel, Marcel Mauss, and Norbert Elias (Guy & Selg, 2019).
  • 列举了在关系社会学内部发展出不同路径的现代学者。
    Some of their ideas have been taken up and developed by authors who developed different approaches within relational sociology, e.g. Nick Crossley (2010), Andrew Abbott (2016), Scott Eacott (2018), François Dépelteau (2018), and Pierpaolo Donati (2021).
  • 关系社会学的共同点是关注互动者之间的相互依赖，将社会关系视为社会现象产生的引擎，从而超越能动性/结构、微观/宏观等二元对立。
    They thus move away from co-deterministic distinctions between agency and structure, micro- and macro-level, individuals and society, and give more weight to social relations as the engine of production of social phenomena (Dépelteau, 2018).
  • Donati的关系实在论认为，社会“是”关系，而非“拥有”关系，社会形态发生是通过社会关系实现的。
    Donati has developed a ‘relational realist’ theory of society, where the basic idea is that society is not a space ‘containing’ relations, rather relations are the very stuff of what we call ‘the social’, i.e. “society ‘is relation’ and does not ‘have relations’” (Donati, 2015, p. 87, italics in original).
  • Donati的理论建立在Archer的“形态发生方法”之上，认为社会和文化结构本质上是可变的。
    He thus builds on Margaret Archer's (1995) ‘morphogenetic approach’. As such social and cultural structures are inherently transformable, but this transformation is historical.
  • 对Archer的“形态发生”与吉登斯的“结构化”理论进行了区分，指出两者在概念化和整合时间维度上存在差异。
    Morphogenesis agrees with Giddens’ structuration that action and structure presuppose one another; however they differ in how they conceptualize it and how they theorize the structuring of social systems, not least how they integrate time (Archer, 1982).
  • 社会关系是形态发生过程的涌现效应，是结构、文化和能动性维度的交织。
    For Donati (2015), social relations are emergent effects of morphogenetic processes, i.e. the structural, cultural, and agential dimensions of morphogenesis are intertwined within a social relation.
  • 以“领导者”为例，说明关系的涌现是多重因素（个人魅力、追随者、情境）复杂交织的结果。
    Donati (2015, p. 89) illustrates this by using the example of a leader: the leader does not exist only because there are followers, but also because of the charismatic qualities of that person, as well as contextual factors within and outside of the relational network.
  • 基于齐美尔的“相互作用”（Wechselwirkung）概念，Donati认为社会关系并非“物”，而是相互性或互惠性的特殊效应。
    Building on the notion of ‘Wechselwirkung’ by Georg Simmel, Donati (2021, p. 3) does not treat social relations as a ‘thing’, since they are a peculiar effect of mutuality or reciprocity between the terms that they links.
  • 在Donati的理论中，社会关系是人类所特有的，区别于非人类关系。
    Social relations – a term used to refer to both process and outcome – are thus proper to human beings, and different from nonhuman relations (Donati, 2015, p. 88).
  • Dépelteau批判Donati等人的方法仍保留了实体主义的承诺，因为行动者是预先存在的，然后才进入关系。
    For Dépelteau (2008, 2017b), approaches such as the one proposed by Donati retain some modernist-substantialist commitments, since the human actors are pre-given and then subsequently engage in relations.
  • Dépelteau受Emirbayer启发，提出一种完全采纳过程-关系哲学的社会学，其中实体本身由关系构成。
    Dépelteau, building on Emirbayer (1997), proposes a relational sociology which fully adopts a process-relational philosophy. Here, the entities themselves are constituted by relations.
  • 引用德勒兹和加塔利的例子，说明国际象棋与围棋的对比可以阐释过程-关系方法。
    To illustrate this process-relational approach, Lenco (2018) builds on the example provided by Deleuze and Guattari (1987, pp. 352-3) and compares two board games: chess and Go.
  • Lenco指出，一个更受围棋启发的社会学将摒弃任何对预设实体的依赖，转而关注构成流动的、纯粹关系性特征的关系方面。
    Lenco (2018, p. 154) points out that “a more Go-inspired relational sociology would disavow any reliance on pre-given entities, focusing rather on the relational, often chaotic, aspects that in fact constitute fluid, purely relational characteristics.”
  • Dépelteau认为社会现象（如阶级、经济）不是能够行动或约束的“社会之物”，因为它们本身无法行动。
    Social phenomena such as social classes, the economy, pressure groups, culture, or gender are thus not the causal effects of ‘social things’, as these social phenomena cannot act, enable, or constrain, they are not solid ‘structures’ (Dépelteau, 2008).
  • 社会现象被理解为通过相互依赖的行动者之间的情境化关系而涌现、变化和消失的流变社会过程。
    Rather, social phenomena are understood as fluid social processes emerging, changing, disappearing through contextualized relations between various interdependent actors (Dépelteau, 2017).
  • 社会结构被理解为相互依赖的行动者之间关系的或多或少稳定的效应，而非决定行动的“外部”实体。
    As such a social actor is not determined by an ‘external’ social structure, as this ‘structure’ is understood as the more or less stable effect of relations between interdependent actors (Dépelteau, 2008).
  • Vandenberghe总结道，关系社会学通过与各种将世界视为独立实体偶然聚合的实体主义方法（如理性选择、规范功能主义）的对立来定义自身。
    As Vandenberghe (2018, p. 636) puts it, this approach in relational sociology is “defined relationally in its opposition to categorical, essentialist, substantialist, atomist and fixist approaches, like rational choice, normative functionalism, and positivist variable analysis, which conceive of the world as a hapless conglomerate of contingently related, free-standing entities.”
  • 关系社会学关注的互动过程可以扩展到非物质维度，如社会、文化、权力、知识关系。
    This process is often conceptualised as interaction, i.e. as material or energy flow (e.g. in social ecology), and can be extended to non-material dimensions such as social, cultural, power, knowledge relations (see Lejano, 2019).
  • 关系性同样关注意义，即事物的意义或身份如何依赖于其与其他事物的关系。
    Indeed, relationality also focuses on meaning – i.e. how the meaning or identity of something depends on others it is in relation with (Lejano, 2019).
  • Dépelteau强调了时间在关系中的重要性，认为当下的互动总是通过记忆、知识等与过去的经验相连。
    The approach also foregrounds time, since interactions at time T1 are always somehow connected to various past experiences through dynamic, heterogenous memories, knowledge, views, involving long chains of interactions (Dépelteau, 2015, p. 14).
