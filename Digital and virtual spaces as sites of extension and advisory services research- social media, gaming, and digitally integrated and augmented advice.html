Digital and virtual spaces as sites of extension and advisory services research: social media, gaming, and digitally integrated and augmented advice

Laurens Klerkx

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点是，农业领域的数字化转型正在催生全新的、混合的数字与虚拟空间（如社交媒体、游戏、增强现实等），这些空间正从根本上重塑农业推广与咨询服务（AEAS）的形态、实践和权力关系。因此，作为农业教育与推广研究领域的学者，必须紧急地将研究议程扩展至这些新兴空间，将其作为知识交流、创新学习、决策形成以及新行动者涌现的关键场域进行批判性审视。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

作者的论据建立在对现有文献的广泛征引和对现实世界趋势的敏锐观察之上。首先，他引用了农村社会学和创新研究中关于农场作为人类与非人类行动者“集结体”（assemblages）的理论，为理解技术与农业实践的融合提供了理论基础。其次，他系统梳理了数字化在农业中已有的应用研究，如ICT信息亭、决策支持系统等，以证明数字化并非全新现象。最核心的论据来自对三个新兴领域的具体案例和初步研究的列举：1) 社交媒体上涌现的农业“影响者”和咨询播客；2) 农业模拟游戏（如《Farming Simulator》）对塑造农业想象的潜在影响；3) 整合了大数据、传感器和AI的数字咨询平台与代表未来趋势的“数字孪生”和增强现实技术。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证逻辑层层递进。他首先从一个后人类主义的视角出发，将农场定义为一个动态的、由多元行动者构成的“集结体”，打破了传统上以人类为中心的认知。接着，他指出数字化并非简单地作为外部工具被“应用”于农场，而是深度“纠缠”（entangled）于现有的集结体之中，从而创造出全新的“社会-网络-物理系统”（socio-cyber-physical systems）。在此理论框架下，他识别出现有研究的不足，并系统性地划分出三个亟待探索的研究前沿：社交媒体、游戏化以及整合与增强型咨询。在每个前沿领域，他都提出了具体、深刻的研究问题，例如行动者角色变迁、知识权威的重构、技能的“去技能化”与“再技能化”、数据政治以及专业身份的转变等，从而为该领域构建了一个清晰的未来研究议程。

4. 方法 (Method)

作为一篇期刊社论（Editorial），本文采用的核心研究方法是议程设定型文献综述（Agenda-Setting Literature Review）与概念综合（Conceptual Synthesis）。作者通过批判性地回顾和整合农业创新研究、农村社会学、科技研究（STS）以及数字农业等多个领域的文献，识别出现有研究的焦点与盲点。他并非进行一项经验主义研究，而是通过理论思辨和对前沿现象的观察，构建一个分析框架，并在此基础上为《农业教育与推广杂志》乃至整个学术社群提出一个具有前瞻性的研究方向和一系列核心研究问题。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

本文没有采用单一的深度案例研究，而是策略性地使用了大量前沿的、说明性的微案例（illustrative examples）来支撑其论点。在社交媒体部分，他提及了“Future of Agriculture”等播客、专注于城市农业的咨询公司“Agritecture”以及农民在Twitter和Facebook上的个人品牌（如“LeanFarm”）。在游戏部分，他以《Farming Simulator》和《Stardew Valley》为例，探讨休闲游戏的影响，并以“Veterinary Dialog Trainer”为例说明严肃游戏在专业培训中的应用。在数字咨询部分，他提到了欧盟的FAIRshare项目和农业咨询撮合应用“Agvisorpro.com”，并通过“Cyborg farmer”（赛博格农民）这一概念形象地描绘了增强现实技术带来的未来图景。

导论：作为集结体的农场与数字纠缠

本部分首先为文章奠定理论基础，提出应将农场理解为由人类与非人类行动者（如土壤、技术、动物等）共同构成的复杂网络，即“集结体”（assemblages）。这些集结体是农民决策、学习和创新的空间。随后，作者指出数字化并非一个全新的现象，而是作为一种媒介和技术，正日益深度地“纠缠”于这些集结体之中，从而催生出新的“混合物质情境”与“社会-网络-物理系统”，这为农业推广与咨询服务的研究开辟了新的场域和挑战。

  • 总结：作者引用Darnhofer的观点，将农场视为一个由各种元素和环境共同构成和塑造的动态过程，为后文讨论数字元素的融入提供了理论起点。
    The ﬁeld of research on agricultural and rural extension and education... has always engaged with diﬀerent aspects of the spaces in which agricultural producers (farmers, growers, ranchers) are situated and operate. In this editorial, I will reﬂect on the elements and environments which jointly constitute and shape the farm (Darnhofer 2020)...
  • 总结：指出推广与咨询服务在当前关于农业食品部门数字化转型的辩论中占据核心位置，并已成为研究、实践和政策的焦点。
    Extension and advisory services receive ample consideration in current debates on digitalization and digital transformation of the agrifood sector, and are an important focus of attention for agriculture and food systems research, practice and policy (Ehlers, Huber, and Finger 2021; Ingram and Maye 2020; Klerkx 2020).
  • 总结：将农场及其环境定义为人类与非人类行动者和实体的网络或“集结体”，这些集结体共同塑造农民的决策、创新行为及学习过程。
    Farms and their environments can be seen as networks of human and non-human actors and entities, also referred to as assemblages (Higgins et al. 2017; Darnhofer 2020; Comi 2020), which shape farmers’ decision making and innovative behavior and provide spaces for experimental, experiential and social learning (Cofré-Bravo, Klerkx, and Engler 2019; Comi 2020; Crawford et al. 2007; Eastwood, Klerkx, and Nettle 2017; Fieldsend et al. 2021; Higgins and Bryant 2020; Leeuwis and Aarts 2011; Pigford, Hickey, and Klerkx 2018; Šūmane et al. 2018).
  • 总结：强调农民的社会网络（包括其他农民、顾问、供应商等）既能支持也能限制其决策、学习和创新过程。
    These social networks support and enable but may also constrain and condition farmers’ decision making and learning and innovation processes, for example in terms of how and what is produced, and what technologies and practices are created and implemented on the farm (Fieldsend et al. 2021; Legun and Bell 2016).
  • 总结：引入后人类主义视角，指出非人类行动者网络（土壤、植物、技术设备等）与人类网络相互作用、共同演化。
    Networks of non-human actors and entities (e.g. soils, plants, animals, landscapes, technological artifacts and infrastructures) interact with networks of humans, and these co-evolve and inﬂuence each other (Finstad, Aune, and Egseth 2021; Higgins et al. 2017; Pigford, Hickey, and Klerkx 2018).
  • 总结：说明传统的咨询活动，如田间参观和示范农场，本身就是一种主动调用和组合人类与非人类网络（集结体）的实践。
    Both human and non-human networks (or assemblages) are actively invoked in advisory activities through for example ﬁeld visits and demo-farm settings (Adamsone-Fiskovica et al. 2021.; Burton 2020; Cooreman et al. 2020; Cowan et al. 2015; Vaarst et al. 2007).
  • 总结：指出各类传统媒体（书面、音视频）在人类与非人类网络之间扮演了中介和接口的角色，支持着决策与创新。
    Several sorts of media (e.g. written, audio-visual) mediate and provide interfaces between these human and non-human networks and support decision making, learning and innovation (Chowdhury et al. 2015; Moussa et al. 2011; Pasiona, Nidoy, and Manalo Iv 2021; Ragasa et al. 2021; Rust et al. 2021; Silvestri et al. 2020; van Mele, Wanvoeke, and Zossou 2010).
  • 总结：论述了数字化并非一个全新的现象，学术界已有大量关于早期数字媒介（如ICT信息亭、手机短信、决策支持系统、社交媒体）在农业知识传播中作用的研究积累。
    Though there seems to be an acceleration of technological developments lately, digitalization is not a new phenomenon and there is substantial experience with digitally mediated learning and innovation spaces (Klerkx, Jakku and Labarthe 2019). Scholars have, for example, looked at the strengths and limitations of ICT in information provisioning to farmers... (Baumüller 2017; ...; Tata and McNamara 2018), the role of computer-mediated decision support systems and learning tools (Antle, Jones, and Rosenzweig 2017; ...; Rose et al. 2016), as well as the role of social media in knowledge exchange and advice provisioning (Aguilar-Gallegos et al. 2021; ...; Singh Nain, Singh, and Mishra 2019).
  • 总结：随着数字技术与现有集结体的深度纠缠，新的“混合物质情境”正在涌现，使得推广服务成为“社会-网络-物理系统”的一部分。
    With digital technologies becoming more pervasive and entangled with existing networks or assemblages of human and non-human actors and entities, new ‘hybrid material contexts’ emerge (Klerkx 2020). Extension and advisory services become part of ‘socio-cyber-physical systems’ (Lioutas et al. 2019; Rijswijk et al. 2021)...
  • 总结：这些新出现的“数字农业知识与创新系统”中的情境，需要学术界给予更多、更深入的关注。
    ...and thus new contexts and empirical situations emerge in ‘digital agricultural knowledge and innovation systems’ (Ayre et al. 2019; Fielke et al. 2019; Ingram and Maye 2020; Klerkx, Jakku, and Labarthe 2019) which merit further and perhaps also more attention.

研究前沿一：作为咨询空间的社交媒体

本节聚焦于第一个需要深入研究的新兴领域：社交媒体。作者认为，尽管已有研究关注社交媒体，但对其作为咨询工具的具体运作机制——由谁、在何处、针对何事、在何种制度条件下使用——仍需更多关注。特别地，他强调了在社交媒体上涌现出的新型知识权威，如农业“影响者”和“名人”，并呼吁研究这些新行动者的特征、影响力，以及线上活动如何与传统咨询互动。最后，他提出了组织层面如何嵌入社交媒体，以及如何进行质量控制和应对错误信息等关键问题。

  • 总结：指出在社交媒体平台上，农民和顾问自身成为了知识流动的关键生成者、促进者甚至守门人，其中一些人获得了“影响者”和“名人”的地位。
    On these social networks, farmers and advisors are key generators and facilitators of knowledge ﬂows (and perhaps also gatekeepers), sometimes getting the status of inﬂuencers and celebrities (Goodman and Jaworska 2020; Klerkx 2020; Phillipov and Goodman 2017; Phillips, Klerkx, and McEntee 2018; Rust et al. 2020).
  • 总结：呼吁需要更系统地研究社交媒体上的这些行动者是谁、在哪里、做什么（例如，覆盖范围、使用的语言、目标农民类型），他们的社交媒体活动如何与他们的咨询业务互动，以及这对农业社区和传统知识分享模式产生何种影响。
    ...there is a need to gain a better insight into who these actors on social media are, where they are and what they do..., how their social media presence interacts with their advisory activities, and how this impacts on the farming community and interacts with more traditional (non-digital and non-virtual) forms of knowledge sharing.
  • 总结：提出需要进一步研究咨询组织如何将社交媒体整合到其运营中，这是对Aguilar-Gallegos等人工作的延续。
    Furthermore, more research is needed on how social media are embedded in advisory organizations (following Aguilar-Gallegos et al. 2021)...
  • 总结：强调了研究如何将咨询的质量控制和内容策划扩展到社交媒体领域，以应对错误信息等问题的必要性。
    ...and how quality control and curation of advice extends to social media (to deal with misinformation for example - see Chowdhury and Abdulai 2020).

研究前沿二：农业学习与创新的游戏化

本节将游戏（Gaming）界定为第二个值得关注的研究主题，并将其分为休闲游戏和目的性游戏两类。作者认为，像《Stardew Valley》这样的休闲农业游戏，因其流行而可能深刻影响现有的和下一代的农民，塑造他们对农村生活和田园牧歌的想象，并影响技能和知识的代际传递，游戏在某种意义上扮演了顾问和实验空间的角色。对于目的性游戏（严肃游戏），作者指出其在农业领域已有应用传统，可用于探索复杂问题、设想未来转型、甚至通过虚拟化身来培训顾问。最后，他呼吁要更好地理解游戏在农业食品创新中的多面性，以及如何促进农业部门与游戏产业的互动。

  • 总结：引用Sutherland的研究，说明《Stardew Valley》这类游戏如何创造了关于农村生活和农耕的想象，并可能对当前和下一代农民（包括新进入者）在技能习得和知识传递方面产生影响。
    Sutherland reports on the game Stardew Valley (Sutherland 2020a, b) and how this game enables the creation of imaginaries of rural life and farming. Given their popularity, farming games may have an inﬂuence on current farmers but also on next generation farmers, which would include both children of current farmers and new entrants (Sutherland and Calo 2020; Zagata and Sutherland 2015), in terms of skills acquirement and (intergenerational) knowledge acquisition and exchange.
  • 总结：指出目的性/严肃游戏在农业中已有应用历史，包括传统的桌面游戏和数字视频游戏。
    Regarding purposeful gaming, this builds on a tradition of use of serious games in agriculture including both classic board games and digital video games (Gómez-Prada, Orellana-Hernández, and Salinas-Ibáñez 2020; Martin, Felten, and Duru 2011; Mushtaq et al. 2017; Salvini et al. 2020; Shaban and Merkley 2016).
  • 总结：严肃游戏可用于探索问题情境与解决方案，甚至可以面向未来，探索更广泛的食物系统转型。
    Serious games can be used to explore problem situations and solutions and can also be future oriented and serve to explore broader food systems transformation (Mangnus et al. 2019).
  • 总结：举例说明目的性游戏可以应用于顾问培训，例如通过使用虚拟形象模拟咨询场景。
    Purposeful gaming can also be applied to advisor training through simulations of advisory encounters using avatars (Mushtaq et al. 2017), for example, a ‘Veterinary Dialog Trainer’...
  • 总结：借鉴Vervoort关于游戏产业与转型思维联系的观点，主张需要更好地理解游戏在农业食品创新中的多重作用，如何优化游戏设计，以及农业部门与游戏产业的潜在互动。
    In line with ideas by Vervoort (2019) who reﬂects on game sector development and its connection with support of transformational thinking and action, there is a need to better understand the diﬀerent facets of the use of games and gaming in agri-food innovation and transformation, how games can be best designed, and better understanding of the actual or potential interactions between the agricultural sector and the games industry.

研究前沿三：数字集成与增强型咨询

本节探讨了第三个研究前沿，分为“数字集成咨询”和“增强型咨询”两个层面。前者关注咨询组织如何将数字工具整合到面向客户的“前台”业务和内部研发的“后台”活动中。这引发了对商业模式、数据政治经济、供需匹配、服务公平性以及将农场工人纳入考虑等一系列研究议题。后者则展望了更具颠覆性的未来，即虚拟现实（VR）、增强现实（AR）和“数字孪生”等技术如何“增强”乃至取代人类的分析能力，可能催生出“赛博格农民/顾问”。这提出了关于技能变迁（去技能化/再技能化）、职业身份认同、人机交互的最优平衡以及“混合型”顾问的出现等深刻的社会技术问题。

  • 总结：将数字集成与增强型咨询的研究议题分为咨询组织的“前台”（面向客户）和“后台”（研发与服务开发）活动。
    ...digitally integrated and augmented advice, this concerns both ‘front-oﬃce’ (customer facing services) and ‘back-oﬃce’ activities (R&D and new service development) in advisory organizations (Labarthe and Laurent 2013; Rijswijk, Klerkx, and Turner 2019).
  • 总结：指出已有学者开始探索顾问在前台活动中如何整合数字工具并与数字农业产品（如精准设备、传感器、大数据分析）互动。
    In terms of digitally integrated advice, scholars have started to explore how advisors in their front-oﬃce type activities have started to integrate digital tools and interact with digital agriculture artifacts and aﬀordances (e.g. precision equipment, sensors, robots, big data analysis) (Ayre et al. 2019; Eastwood et al. 2019; Ingram and Maye 2020; Newton, Nettle, and Pryce 2020; Higgins and Bryant 2020).
  • 总结：提及一些学者关注数字服务的经济属性、商业模式（开源vs专有）及其更广泛的政治经济学意涵。
    Some scholars have focused on the economic good nature of digital services and business models (Birner, Daum, and Pray 2021), the sorts of open source and proprietary models and how they may form part of larger services and good packages provided to farmers and the political economy of those (Clapp and Ruder 2020; Daum et al. 2021; Gardezi and Stock 2021; Klerkx, Jakku, and Labarthe 2019; Wittman, James, and Mehrabi 2020).
  • 总结：尽管十多年前已有学者注意到基于ICT的撮合中介，但技术已发展出如应用程序这样的交互式供需匹配工具，需要对此进行更多研究。
    ...though Klerkx and Leeuwis (2008) have already noted ICT based brokers over a decade ago, these have technologically evolved and there are now interactive apps for such matchmaking (e.g. https://www.Agvisorpro.com).
  • 总结：呼吁更多研究关注数字推广服务的获取和使用权的分配与赋能机制，以及如何平衡数据价值创造与客户数据所有权。
    More research could be done on how access to and use of digital extension is distributed and enabled (Mehrabi et al. 2021; Steinke et al. 2020), and on how advisory service providers create value propositions on basis of data, whilst respecting the data ownership of their clients.
  • 总结：强调需要将研究视角从农民扩展到农场工人和其他食品系统工作者，因为他们同样受到数字化的影响并需要发展数字技能。
    Additionally, we need more focus on how digital services connect with the roles and needs of other audiences on-farm and oﬀ-farm, as it has become increasingly realized that farm workers and more broadly food system workers need to considered (Cofre-Bravo et al. 2019; Dockès et al. 2019; Pitt 2021). This is because they, just like farmers, are aﬀected by digitalization (Rotz et al. 2019) and need to be supported in developing digital skills (KPMG and Skills Impact 2019).
  • 总结：引入“增强型咨询”概念，指出VR/AR等新技术扩展了人类的分析能力，可能导致“赛博格农民”或“无人机农民”的出现，这也同样适用于顾问。
    As regards augmented advice, it has been noted that new digital technologies (virtual reality, augmented reality) aﬀord new possibilities and extend human analytical capacities and that this may lead to ‘cyborg farmers’, ‘joystick farmers’ or ‘drone farmers’ (Klerkx, Jakku, and Labarthe 2019...), and this also extends to their advisors.
  • 总结：实时建模和“数字孪生”技术创造了新的决策、创新和学习空间，这可能改变对人类顾问（甚至农民）的需求。
    Real-time modeling and the creation of digital twins of crops, animals or even whole farms (Pylianidis, Osinga, and Athanasiadis 2021; Verdouw et al. 2021) create new decision, innovation and learning spaces... which may have implications for the analytical capacities of farmers and advisors or even reduce a need for human advisors (and maybe even farmers).
  • 总结：以无人机引入“立体农业”为例，说明新技术在增强感官范围的同时，也要求新的解读技能。
    Other new spaces and dimensions, such as drones which would introduce ‘volumetric agriculture’ (Klauser and Pauschinger 2021), augment the sensory reach of farmers and advisors but also require new interpretation skills.
  • 总结：指出数字化可能导致农民和农场工人的“去技能化”和“再技能化”，以及关于“好农夫”身份认同的改变，并呼吁理解这对顾问和教育者意味着什么。
    For farmers and farm workers, processes of deskilling and reskilling and identity change as regards what is ‘good farming’ have been noted as a (potential) result of digitalization (Brooks 2021; Gardezi and Stock 2021; Ingram and Maye 2020; Klerkx, Jakku, and Labarthe 2019) or perhaps even more pervasive ‘de-agrarianization’ (Barrett 2021). We also need to understand what this means for advisors and educators.
  • 总结：最后呼吁研究增强型咨询界面如何影响顾问的职业身份及其组织的认同，以及如何进行相关的顾问培训和发展，以找到数字与模拟互动的最佳平衡点，并探索未来可能出现的“混合型”顾问形态。
    This includes studying eﬀects of emerging augmented advisory interfaces... how augmented advisory interfaces aﬀect advisors’ professional identity and the identity of their organizations (Eastwood et al. 2019; Ingram and Maye 2020; Rijswijk, Klerkx, and Turner 2019), and how advisor training and development in these aspects takes place... focusing on what an optimal balance between digital and analog interaction is... and what sorts of hybrid advisors and advisory encounters will emerge (Ingram and Maye 2020).
