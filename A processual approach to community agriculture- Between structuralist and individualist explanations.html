A processual approach to community agriculture: Between structuralist and individualist explanations

Irina Aguiari

全文概要总结

1. 论点 (Argument)

本文的核心论点在于，对当代“社区农业”（community agriculture）的理解不应局限于新自由主义框架下的两种对立解释——即视其为对全球环境退化的结构性反应，或是个人化的生活方式政治。作者 Irina Aguiari 认为，这种二元对立的视角忽视了其深厚的历史根源。因此，她主张采用一种“过程社会学”（processual sociology）的视角，将社区农业（在此被定义为为了粮食生产而进行的土地公有化实践）理解为一个历经数百年、在不同历史情境下不断演变的“行动剧目”（action repertoire）。这个行动剧目既受到宏观结构性力量的塑造，也反过来通过能动性的集体行动影响着历史进程，从而超越了结构与能动性（structure vs. agency）的简单二分法。

2. 论据 (Evidence)

文章的主要论据来源于对意大利半岛自古典时代晚期至今长达两千年的二手历史文献的系统性梳理和比较分析。作者通过整合历史学、社会学和农业史的文献，重构了土地公有化实践的长期轨迹。具体论据包括对罗马时期土地制度的分析、中世纪庄园经济下“公民用益”（civic uses）的演变、近代资本主义兴起过程中的“圈地运动”（enclosures），以及现代工业化和全球食物体系（food regimes）形成过程中农民的无产阶级化。这些历史证据共同指向一个核心事实：尽管形式和功能不断变化，但以社区为基础的粮食生产实践始终作为一种应对剥夺和控制的策略而顽强存在。

3. 论证过程 (Reasoning Process)

作者的论证过程是一个宏大的历史叙事重构，遵循了三个清晰的“时间宏大序列”（temporal macro-sequences）。首先是“粮食生产的委托”（delegation），从古典时代晚期到封建社会，粮食生产被委托给从属的农民阶级，社区农业从主流模式转变为私有制的补充。其次是“粮食生产的私有化”（privatization），从中世纪晚期到近代早期，随着商业资本主义和城市化兴起，圈地运动系统性地瓦解了公地，将社区农业边缘化。最后是“粮食生产的商品化”（commodification），从15世纪至今，特别是在全球食物体系建立后，粮食彻底沦为商品，而土地公有化在被压抑数世纪后，重新作为一个对抗资本主义食物链的替代性范式和行动剧目浮现。通过这一长时段（longue durée）的分析，作者展示了剥夺、私有化等结构性压力如何持续存在，而农民的集体行动又是如何在这种压力下不断调整和重塑“社区农业”这一斗争形式。

4. 方法 (Method)

本文在方法论上明确采用了“过程社会学”（processual sociology）框架，强调关系、过程和非线性的时间观。研究方法是基于二手文献的历史比较分析（historical-comparative analysis）。作者依据 Bosi & Reiter (2014) 的指导方针，系统地筛选了三种类型的二手资料：(1) “国家文本”（country texts），即关于意大利半岛的宏观历史专著；(2) “主题文本”（topic texts），即专门研究土地公有化的文献；(3) “因果文本”（cause texts），即那些支持本文核心论点（即公有化实践的连续性）的文献。这种方法不旨在发现新的历史事实，而是如 Skocpol (1979) 所言，对已有的社会现象提供一种新的解释框架，通过叙事性地呈现历史过程来揭示社区农业作为行动剧目的演变动态。

5. 案例 (Case/Example)

整个意大利半岛的历史构成了本文的核心案例研究。作者之所以选择意大利，是因为其地理、文化和农业实践的多样性为检验一个具有普遍解释力的过程性框架提供了丰富的实证土壤。具体的历史案例贯穿全文，例如：罗马时代的“无产者”（proletarii）因失去土地而产生；中世纪封建领主通过授予“公民用益”（civic uses）来收编和控制古老的社区实践；近代自由主义和理性个人主义思潮如何为彻底废除公地提供了意识形态基础；以及在当代新自由主义食物体系下，社区农业作为一种具有“食物主权”（food sovereignty）诉求的抵抗形式而复兴。这些案例共同描绘了一幅动态的、充满冲突的历史图景，展示了社区农业在具体时空下的不同面貌。

第一部分：导论、概念界定与理论框架 (Introduction, Conceptualization & Theoretical Framing)

本部分界定了核心概念“社区农业”，并将其置于当代社会学关于农业食物运动的理论辩论中。作者批判了将此类运动简单归因于对新自由主义的反应（结构主义）或个人生活方式选择（个体主义）的二元对立观点。为超越此局限，作者引入了“过程社会学”作为核心理论工具，强调关系、非线性的时间观，并提出将社区农业视为一种随时间演变的“行动剧目”。

  • 作者总结了当前对食物行动主义的两种主流解释：一者视其为对新自由主义的抵抗。
    Such an increase in practices of food activism has been either interpreted as a reaction to neo‐liberalisation (Apostolopoulou & Cortes‐Vazquez, 2018; Vivero‐Pol et al., 2019)...
  • 二者则将其归入个人化的生活方式政治范畴。

    The present contribution challenges the exhaustiveness of these sociological explanations through a processual approach to community agriculture (Darnhofer, 2020; McMichael, 2014; Schiavoni, 2017).
  • 作者强调，采用长时段视角是一种有意识的分析选择，旨在识别历史的重复模式。
    Rather than a limitation to the study, this represents a deliberate analytical choice motivated by the research question (Pettigrew, 1990; della Porta, 2002).

    The analysis builds on three temporal macro‐sequences (Bidart et al., 2013): (i) delegation, (ii) privatization and (iii) commodification of food production.
  • 作者指出，全球食物体系的建立与食物生产的工业化和商品化同步。
    The third and last sequence (15th century onwards) witnesses the industrialization and commodification of food production together with the establishment of global food regimes (McMichael, 2009).

    The analysis also accounts for the role of peasant agency in uncovering how structural mechanisms are in turn shaped by processes of collective action through which peasants do make their own history (Marx, 1852; McMichael, 2008).

  • 作者进一步阐释了过程社会学的本体论前提，即关系是社会的基础。
    While various sociological approaches recognize the significance of relations, processual sociology goes further by considering them as the very foundation of its ontological premises (Emirbayer, 1997).
  • 过程社会学的时间观被描述为非线性和开放式的。
    Since processes are always transforming, processuality embraces a non‐linear and open‐ended conception of time (Dépelteau, 2015) which is the basis of the present historical account.

    In the absence of overarching definitions, I advance a practice‐based conceptualization of community agriculture (Reckwitz, 2002; Schatzki, Knorr Cetina, & Von Savigny, 2001).

    The focus of community agriculture is on the practices of land commoning defined as the activities carried out by individuals within a community to oversee and restore resources that are collectively shared (Linebaugh, 2008; Varvarousis et al., 2021)...

In other words, it serves as an emancipatory practice aimed at potentially transformative changes in the everyday lives of its practitioners (de Angelis, 2017).

第二部分：历史序列之一：粮食生产的委托 (Delegation of food production)

本章追溯了社区农业的第一个转变阶段，即“委托”。在古典时代，随着大型文明（特别是罗马）的崛起，为了确保充足的粮食剩余，粮食生产被系统性地“委托”给了被征服和被剥夺土地的农民阶级。社区农业从原始村社的主导生产方式，逐渐转变为对私有土地的补充，并最终在大型庄园（latifondia）的体系内被收编，成为一种服务于地主精英的、受控制的生产安排。

  • 作者指出，古典文明为了养活大规模人口，必须将粮食生产责任分配给特定社会群体。
    In classical antiquity, the imperative to assign specific social groups with the responsibility of food production arose from the necessity to ensure an ample food surplus for ever‐larger communities (Garnsey, 1999).
  • 原始村社要求所有成员完全投入农业，这限制了社会分工。
    Ancient village societies relied on the full commitment of all members to agriculture as their primary occupation (Gomme, 1890).
  • 复杂经济系统的建立是基于对农民的征服，他们被剥夺了公地，被迫为非生产者精英服务。
    As the Italian peninsula witnessed the emergence of more complex economic and social systems, their foundations were built upon the subjugation of working peasants. ... Consequently, they were coerced into growing and harvesting crops primarily for the benefit of non‐food‐producing elites (Isett & Miller, 2017; Magdoff, 2012).
  • 作者以罗马文明为例，说明其对农民的控制是委托粮食生产的关键。
    The Roman civilization is perhaps the most prominent example of how civilizations depended on the control of the peasantry for the delegation of food production (Gauckler, 1883).
  • 在罗马地区城市化过程中，大地产被整合为较小的家庭单位。
    During the urbanization of the Rome region in the 8th century BCE, there was a consolidation of larger properties into smaller family units (Kovalevsky, 1890).
  • 虽然社区农业在意大利各民族中普遍存在，但私有产权的确立使其逐渐边缘化。
    While community agriculture was prevalent among Italic peoples (Curis, 1917; Schupfer, 1885), the challenges of adapting land commoning to larger groups were often resolved by assigning property rights and establishing differential social statuses.
  • 这种私有财产与公地并存的模式在欧洲其他地区也可见到。
    This pattern of coexistence was also observed across regions (de Laveleye, 1891) such as the German mark and allmend in Germany, the Scandinavian almenning, the Scottish townships (de Laveleye, 1885) and the Russian mir (Kovalevsky, 1888).
  • 罗马帝国主义的扩张以牺牲帝国境内外的社区农业和公地为代价。
    Roman imperialistic ambitions (ca. 27 CE) had a significant impact on community agriculture. Territorial expansion ... often came at the expense of community agriculture and common lands...
  • 罗马的公地实际上被帝国当局垄断，以授予富裕的牧主。
    However, in practice, the imperial authorities ensured a monopoly over pastures for wealthier herds by granting them perpetual and free hereditary possession (Cassandro, 1943).
  • 尽管有土地改革尝试，但大多被规避。
    Several agrarian laws were attempted to establish a fairer redistribution of common pastures, but they were largely circumvented (Gauckler, 1883).
  • 土地所有权的家族继承限制了大众获得土地的机会，使其成为财富和权力的象征。
    Familiar inheritance among landowner elites significantly limited access to land, transforming it into a privilege that symbolized wealth and power, thereby making it unattainable for the majority of the population (Battaglia, 1897).
  • 基督教教会的崛起在大型庄园的形成中扮演了重要角色。
    The ascent of the Christian Church as a religious and political power played a significant role in the emergence of large estates (Laurent, 1865).
  • 教会通过获得土地所有权来巩固其经济和政治影响力。
    Following the legalization of Christianity and its establishment as the official state religion in the 4th century, the Church acquired control over numerous properties (Milella, 1880).
  • 在罗马帝国解体初期，土地所有权与政治权力深度交织。
    Under the incipient disaggregation of the Roman Empire, land ownership became deeply intertwined with political power (Baudi di Vesme, 1876; Beaudouin, 1899).
  • 地主允许庄园内的农民集体耕作部分土地，但这主要有利于地主自身，通过税收和转嫁供养责任获利。
    The local lords took advantage of this arrangement by imposing taxation in the form of a share of the peasants' harvest, even from common lands, thus ensuring a stable income for the estate. ... a cohabitation that often resulted in abuses and oppression limiting local autonomy (Winspeare, 1883).

第三部分：历史序列之二：粮食生产的私有化 (Privatization of food production)

本章探讨了社区农业的第二个历史阶段：系统性的私有化。从后古典时期到中世纪晚期，随着封建庄园制度的巩固和城市公社的兴起，私有产权和货币经济得到强化。社区农业被进一步边缘化，推向生产力较低的山区。古老的公有实践被领主改造为受严格控制和征税的“公民用益”（civic uses）。城市化加剧了对乡村的控制，公地被城市精英吸收和规制，农民被剥夺土地，沦为无地的“臂膀劳动者”（braccianti），而这整个过程伴随着激烈的社会冲突和对自然环境的破坏。

  • 西罗马帝国崩溃后，大型庄园成为恢复私有土地所有权和领土控制的中心。
    Following the collapse of the Western Roman Empire in 476 CE, the Italian peninsula faced invasions by foreign populations. In an agricultural landscape reduced to a semi‐natural economy, large estates became the places designated to reinstate private land ownership and assert territorial control (Sereni, 1982).
  • 封建社会关系巩固了私有土地所有权，并通过严格的继承规则使其僵化。
    The crystallization of feudal social relations solidified private landownership and tied its rights to rigid rules of inheritance (de Coulanges, 1873).
  • 后古典时期，大型庄园（latifondia）在经济和行政生活中仍发挥着决定性作用。
    During this period, latifondia still performed a decisive function as centres of economic and administrative life (Poggi, 1857).
  • 在领主的严格控制下，农民之间却发展出基于空间邻近和互助的协会关系。
    Peasants lived in a condition of strict territorial control and dependence on the lord, yet this context surprisingly fostered the development of associational and consortium relationships based on spatial proximity, economic support, and a shared desire to participate (Calisse, 1893; Thibault, 1904).
  • 中世纪精英阶层的饮食开始引入来自中东的香料等外国产品。
    Still, a modest variety if compared to the diets of the elites who started incorporating foreign products like spices from the Middle East (Freedman, 2012).
  • 法兰克王国通过采邑制度加强了公权与私有财产的结合，教会也享有类似特权。
    The Carolingian fief system exemplified the merging of private property and public authority, as these estates enjoyed special immunity and privileges. This prerogative extended to the Church as well because bishops held similar benefits and shared territorial control with secular lords (Luzzatto, 1910).
  • 农民反抗公地税收的起义频发。
    Revolts by settlers against taxes on common lands were frequent occurrences, as they sought to preserve community agriculture and resist oppressive practices (Salvioli, 1913).
  • 领主通过授予“公民用益”（civic uses）这一自上而下的公地形式来应对起义，这实际上是对社区农业残余的控制。
    These top‐down commons ultimately represented the only residue of community agriculture in the Middle Ages and persisted until contemporaneity (Grossi, 2017).
  • 这些被操控的“公民用益”旨在消灭真正的社区农业。
    However, it is crucial to acknowledge that these forms of control were already residual, limited, and manipulated, serving as a means of exerting reproductive control over the peasantry to eradicate community agriculture (Venezian, 1887/1990; Cencelli, 1920).
  • 城市公社运动的兴起 fosters a sense of unity and corporatism.
    Urban communities began to form associations and embrace the cooperative spirit of the communal movement, fostering a sense of unity and corporatism (Lanzani, 1881).
  • 随着城市公社运动的发展，土地所有者被迫城市化并将其财产转移到城市遗产中。
    As the communal movement gained momentum, landowners were compelled to urbanize and transfer their properties into the city's heritage (Roberti, 1903).
  • 乡村地区的骚乱遭到暴力镇压，导致农民财产被毁，家园被烧。
    Rural unrest was met with violent repressions, destroying peasants' belongings, and burning of their homes and lands (Casanova & del Vecchio, 1894).
  • 城市化进程对社区农业产生了深远影响，公地被城市权力吸收。
    The reconfiguration of urban‐rural dynamics had profound implications for community agriculture (Salvioli, 1901).
  • 城市权力通过成文法典对公地进行严格规制和分类。
    Urban centralization resulted in the absorption of common lands, as political power and territorial control tightened their grip on these commons, imposing strict regulations and categorizations through written statutes (Poggi, 1845).
  • 在中世纪早期，土地是所有权利和义务的基础，无人能与土地分离。
    In the Early Middle Ages, land served as the foundation for all individual's rights and obligations, regardless of their social status. Its political, economic, and social value meant that it was impossible to conceive of anyone as separated from the land (Leicht, 1903).
  • 农民最终被边缘化，沦为仅靠体力劳动的无地贫困农业工人（braccianti）。
    Peasants, who had once enjoyed land access and its associated privileges, were now marginalized and reduced to being braccianti – pauper, dispossessed rural workers who only relied on their arms and physical labour to survive (Bianchi, 1891).

第四部分：历史序列之三：粮食生产的商品化 (Commodification of food production)

本章分析了社区农业历史的第三个阶段：商品化。从近代早期开始，圈地运动、殖民主义和工业化进程加速了粮食生产的委托和私有化，最终将粮食转变为一种可以在市场上交易的商品。国际政治关系和资本积累逻辑开始主导农业。自由主义和理性个人主义的兴起为彻底清除社区农业的残余提供了意识形态武器。农民被进一步剥夺，沦为完全与生产资料和劳动成果异化的农业无产者。尽管意大利统一后的国家立法试图处理“公民用益”问题，但其根本目标是巩固私有财产权和压制民众骚乱。最终，在20世纪的法西斯主义和战后资本主义食物体系下，社区农业几乎被完全清除，直到近几十年来，随着人们对工业化农业的反思，它才作为一种另类实践和抵抗形式重新出现。

  • 系统性的圈地运动旨在根除公地。
    In the 17th century, enclosures became a systematic strategy to eradicate land commons (Marx, 1867).
  • 食物商品化受到资本积累和国际政治关系的调控。
    It turned into a tradable commodity in the market, subject to the regulation of capitalist accumulation and influenced by international political relations (Friedmann, 1987; Friedmann & McMichael, 1989).
  • 14世纪的小冰河时期导致粮食短缺，推动了农业专业化。
    The 14th century witnessed a significant shift in climatic conditions, marked by a period known as the Little Ice Age (Le Roy Ladurie, 1971).
  • 在重商主义农业时代，一些地区维持封建生产模式，而另一些则走向资本主义农业。
    While some regions maintained feudal patterns of production (Rinaldi, 1886), others embraced capitalist agriculture through land reclamation and irrigation systems. In the southern regions and Papal domains, feudal structures remained prevalent... (Dragonetti, 1788).
  • 威尼托和伦巴第通过技术改良和灌溉系统成为意大利半岛资本主义农业的先行者。
    Wider extensions of available lands and the implementation of innovative irrigation systems (Bianchi, 1989) consolidated two forerunners of capitalist agriculture in the peninsula: Veneto and Lombardy.
  • 过去，农业生产中心位于南方，特别是西西里。
    In the past, pivotal centres of agricultural production were located in Southern regions (Lizier, 1907), particularly in Sicily...
  • 法国大革命的反封建动力也席卷了农村社群主义的残余。
    Fuelled by the antifeudal impetus of the French Revolution that trampled down large estates and barons, modernity dragged along the residuals of rural communitarianism too (Bloch, 1978; Bourgin, 1908; Ikni, 1982).
  • 现代农业无产者与他们的古代前辈不同，完全无法获得土地。
    In contrast to the classical and early modern peasantry who relied on common lands to secure a basic portion of their sustenance (Magdoff, 2012) modern agricultural proletarians had no access to land whatsoever and were stripped of their means of subsistence.
  • 现代农民完全与自己的劳动和收获成果异化。
    Whatever they harvested exclusively belonged to the landowner, and they were compelled to spend their wages on buying back the essential yet privatized goods they had produced (Holt‐Giménez, 2017).
  • 食物的价值从其文化、社会和政治象征意义中脱离，被市场价格所掩盖。
    With this detachment from any socio‐cultural embeddedness, the value of food as a cultural, social, and political symbol was overshadowed by its market price (Vivero‐Pol, 2017).
  • 食物商品化为基因改造和种子专利等大规模干预铺平了道路。
    Framed in wider strategies to produce nature (Castree, 2005; McCarthy & Prudham, 2004), the commodification of food paved the way for massive interventions in cropping techniques, experiments on genetics, and seed patenting (Jansen, 2014).
  • 1888年的国家法律废除了意大利半岛上所有的公民用益和公地，引发了广泛的民众骚乱。
    In 1888, the Chambers approved the first national law that abolished all civic uses and common lands in the peninsula. Common lands were purchased by the State, but the monetary restitution offered to local communities was grossly inadequate, leading to widespread popular unrest (Cinanni, 1962).
  • 20世纪初，农民运动的焦点从收回公地转向土地再分配或改善工作条件。
    The focus shifted away from the reclamation of common lands while strikes, protests and occupations were aimed at land redistribution or the improvement of working conditions (Caracciolo, 1950).
  • 法西斯主义通过支持大企业和破坏农村劳工的团结纽带，来巩固其政治统治。
    Fascism encouraged the formation of large enterprises where labourers were placed in entirely subordinate and vulnerable positions... These measures were part of the broader objective... to manage popular discontent and disrupt the communitarian and solidarity bonds among the rural workforces (Cartiglia, 1979).
  • 尽管意大利共和国的立法承认公民用益，但这些条款主要起到了保守作用。
    While the legislation of the Italian Republic recognized civic uses, these provisions primarily served a conservative function (Cristoferi, 2016).
  • 后工业化社会和新自由主义食物供应链推动了对食物本身的直接干预。
    What the Green Revolution brought about has been the normalisation of a direct intervention in food, on the productive outcome itself... flooding the market with processed foods of nearly no nutritional value and nefarious ecological effects (Giraldo, 2019).

第五部分：结论 (Conclusive Remarks)

本结论部分总结了文章的核心贡献，并指出了未来的研究方向。作者强调，本文通过“行动剧目”这一概念成功地超越了结构与能动性的二元对立。其次，通过历史化社区农业，文章重申了粮食和土地使用问题在资本主义发展理论中的核心地位，将当代的土地掠夺与历史上的剥夺机制联系起来。最后，作者倡导将过程社会学框架作为一种适应性强的分析工具，应用于更广泛的社会现象研究，例如将土地公有化的历史化研究扩展到意大利之外，或分析其他自然公地（如水资源）的私有化过程。

  • 作者认为，过程性方法有助于理解当代农业食物运动的理论前提和经验表现。
    The analysis has advanced a more comprehensive perspective on the theoretical premises and empirical manifestations of community agriculture through a processual approach (Bosi & Malthaner, 2023).
  • 行动剧目会随着社会行动者调整方法和适应变化而随时间变动。
    A repertoire of action shows both adaptability to specific contingencies and versatility for agential preferences which culminate in the transformation of collective action over the long term (Tarrow, 1993).
  • 作者认为，结构性和能动性的转变共同促使集体行动将土地公有化采纳为一种抵抗形式。
    In conclusion, it is both structural and agential transformations that have determined significant shifts in material and cultural references, enabling collective action to adopt land commoning for food production as a form of resistance (Tilly, 2006).
  • 文章将当代土地掠夺与历史上的剥夺策略联系起来。
    For instance, bridging past and present strategies of land grabbing and peasant dispossession. Deterritorialization, enclosures, and underconsumption are all persistent mechanisms in the history of food (Braudel, 1961)...
  • 作者指出，结构性过程的本质是充满争议的，地方社区一直在为非商品化的食物系统而斗争。
    On the other side, the analysis has premised on the contested nature of structural processes (Polanyi, 2001; Radin, 1996) by emphasizing how local communities have persistently struggled for decommodified food systems (Segers & Van Molle, 2022; Thirsk, 2000).
  • 作者将社区农业的历史演变与社会运动的宏观历史变迁联系起来。
    The evolution of land commoning for food production from the Roman era to the present day, now inspired by principles of food sovereignty (Nyéléni, 2007), for example, reflects the broader shift from industrial capitalism to advanced neoliberalism...
  • 结论指出，集体行动不能仅通过结构性机会理论来研究，也不能仅仅归结为个人偏好。
    In this sense, while collective action cannot be studied only through structural theories of political opportunities, it is not only about individual preferences and lifestyle choices either.
  • 食物作为一种社会和政治裂痕，既是冲突的焦点，也融入了后工业社会中另类生活方式的理想。
    In so doing, land commoning for food production implies both the identification of food as a social and political cleavage to engage in political, social, and ecological conflicts (Goodman, 1999) and often merges into ideals of alternative ways of life in post‐industrial societies too (Edelman, 2001; Touraine, 1997).
